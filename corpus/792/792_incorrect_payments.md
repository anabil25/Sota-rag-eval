792            INCORRECT PAYMENTS

When a family receives a payment that is more or less than the amount for which they are actually eligible, an incorrect payment has occurred. This chapter provides policy for correcting payments.

Any [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance staff member who discovers an incorrect payment is responsible for initiating action to correct the error.
