807            NON-FINANCIAL FACTORS OF ELIGIBILITY

The following factors of eligibility generally apply to all Medicaid categories unless there is a specific exception provided.

General non-financial factors of eligibility include:

1.  U.S.United States Citizenship or Qualified Alien status ([section 808](../808_us_citizenship_and_eligible_alien_status/808_us_citizenship_and_eligibile_alien_status.md));

2.  Alaska Residency ([section 809](../809_alaska_residency/809_alaska_residency.md))

3.  Social Security Enumeration ([section 810](../810_social_security_enumeration/810_social_security_enumeration.md))

4.  Assignment of Rights ([section 811](../811_assignment_of_rights/811_assignment_of_rights.md))

5.  Cooperation with Child Support ([section 812](../812_medical_support_assignment/812_medical_support_assignment.md))
