STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #27

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  December 1, 2010

This manual change includes new  Interim Assistance Reimbursement procedures (described in the November 19 broadcast to staff) and moves information about contacting the Medical Reviewer to its own subsection.

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights to policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 426-2 F](../../426/426-2_eligibility_requirements_for_interim_assistance.md#426_2_F_CONTACTING_THE_MEDICAL_REVIEWER)  Contacting the Medical Reviewer

- Adds new sub-section about how to contact the Medical Reviewer.
- Adds the Medical Reviewer email address for staff inquiries.

[MS 426-3](../../426/426-3_interim_assistance_reimbursement_(iar).md)  Interim Assistance Reimbursement (IAR)

- Explains changes to Interim Assistance Reimbursement procedures that went into effect on November 20, 2010.

[MS 441-1 B](../../441/441-1_earned_income.md#441-1_B_FLEXIBLE_HEALTH_BENEFITS)  Flexible Health Benefits

- Adds new policy that only the contribution amount that goes directly to the employee (benefit contribution excess) is countable income.  Any amount the employer pays for mandatory health insurance benefits is not counted as income to the employee.
- Reorganizes the earned income section to align more closely with the organization of the other program manuals.

[MS 460-2 B](../../460/460-2_financial_responsibility.md#460_2_B____INCOME_AND_RESOURCES_OF_ALIEN_SPONSORS) Income and Resources of Alien Sponsors

- Clarifies that the deeming period for alien sponsors  lasts for 3 years.
