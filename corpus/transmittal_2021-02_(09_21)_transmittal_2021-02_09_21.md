STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2021-02

MANUAL: Heating Assistance Program

DATE: September 1, 2021

Several sections of the Heating Assistance Program Policy Manual have been revised in order to incorporate new policy, clarify existing policy, and align policies within our policy manuals.

If you have questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto: dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 3000](../3000/3000_introductions_and_definitions.md)            INTRODUCTIONS AND DEFINITIONS

- Added direct pay as allowable for clients who self-harvest or purchase wood as their primary heat source.

[MS 3000-1B](../3000/3000-1_responsibilities_in_administration.md#3000-1_B._RECORDS_AND_REPORTS)      RECORDS AND REPORTS

- Updated location of client records from the case file to ILINX.

[MS 3000-1E](../3000/3000-1_responsibilities_in_administration.md#3000-1_E._DISCLOSURE_AND_USE_OF_INFORMATION)      DISCLOSURE AND USE OF INFORMATION

- Added weatherization services as an agency [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance obtains information from and discloses information to.

[MS 3000-2](../3002/3002_households.md)         DEFINITIONS

- Made known that the Head of Household is commonly referred to as the Primary Information Person (PI) and is the first person listed on the application.
- Removed Alaska Native/American Indian as part of the "Mixed" Household definition. This type of household is now explained in section [3002-1](../3002/3002-1_households.md).
- Updated the "Vendor" definition to include an explanation of an "Approved" Vendor" and direction on how a vendor can contact [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance to become an approved vendor.
- Corrected [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income definition.

[MS 3001-1](../3001/3001-1_application_form_availability.md)        APPLICATION FORM AVAILABILITY

- Updated the [HAP](#)Heating Assistance Program season start-up application mailing dates for the elderly, disabled, families with young children, and all other previous season participants.

[MS 3001-2G](../3001/3001-2_the_application_form.md#3001-2_G._TRIBAL_APPLICATIONS)     COMPLETE APPLICATION

- Removed reference to the number associated with questions on the application as these may change from year to year when updates to the application are made.
- Removed requirement for all adults in the household to sign the application.

[MS 3001-2H](../3001/3001-2_the_application_form.md#3001-2_H._VOTER_REGISTRATION_APPLICATIONS)      TRIBAL APPLICATIONS

- Replaced reference to "mixed" household with "household containing both Native and non-Native members." This aligns with [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance's other program manuals.
- Removed definition "head of household" since this term is defined in section [3000-2](../3000/3000-2_definitions.md).
- Removed reference to obsolete eligibility system codes.

[MS 3001-4A](../3001/3001-4_verification.md#3001-4_A._VERIFICATION_REQUIREMENTS_FOR_INITIAL_APPLICATIONS)      VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS FOR INITIAL APPLICATIONS

- Updated note with explanation of why [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance requires both the fuel and electric billing statements.

[MS 3001-4C](../3001/3001-4_verification.md#3001-4_C._HEAT_OR_RENT_COST)      HEAT OR RENT COST

- Removed reference to not needing a copy of the rental agreement for the client's file, as all submitted documentation is now scanned into ILINX.
- Changed reference from "[HAP](#)Heating Assistance Program staff" to "caseworker" to align with [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance's other program manuals.

[MS 3001-4H](../3001/3001-4_verification.md#3001-4_H_INFORMATION_FROM_DATA_SYSTEMS_AND_EIS_INTERFACES)      INFORMATION FROM DATA SYSTEMS AND [EIS](#)Eligibility Information System INTERFACES

- Changed section title from "Use of Interface[DPA](#)Division of Public Assistances" to "Information from Data Systems and [EIS](#)Eligibility Information System Interfaces" to align with [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance's other program manuals.
- Changed reference from "worker" to "caseworker" to align with [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance's other program manuals.
- Updated section to mirror [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance's other program manuals.

[MS 3001-4J](../3001/3001-4_verification.md#3001-4_J._QUESTIONABLE_INFORMATION)       QUESTIONABLE INFORMATION

- Changed reference from "[ET](#)Eligibility Technician" to "caseworker" to align with [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance's other program manuals.

[MS 3001-5A](../3001/3001-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#3001-5_A._ENTERING_APPLICATIONS)      ENTERING APPLICATIONS

- Referred to and provided link to Administrative Procedures Manual, [Section 101-7](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/admin/101/101-7_timeliness_standards.htm).

[MS 3001-5C](../3001/3001-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#3001-5_C._EXPEDITE_PROCESSING_OF_THE_APPLICATION)      EXPEDITE PROCESSING OF THE APPLICATION

- Inserted new subsection to address changes reported and additional applications received before a case decision is made, to align with [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance's other program manuals.

- Renumbered subsection from [3001-5D](../3001/3001-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#3001-5_C._EXPEDITE_PROCESSING_OF_THE_APPLICATION#3001-5_D_CHANGES_REPORTED_TO_DPA) to [3001-5E](../3001/3001-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#3001-5_E_APPROVING_THE_APPLICATION) to accommodate the insertion of new subsection "Changes Reported to [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance."

[MS 3001-5F](../3001/3001-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#3001-5_F._PENDING_THE_APPLICATION)      PENDING THE APPLICATION

- Renumbered the subsection from [3001-5E](../3001/3001-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#3001-5_E_APPROVING_THE_APPLICATION) to [3001-5F](../3001/3001-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#3001-5_F._PENDING_THE_APPLICATION) to accommodate the insertion of new subsection "Changes Reported to [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance."
- Added reference to submitted documentation being filed electronically in ILINX.
- Added incorrect timeframe for the initial pend notice as a reason for re-pending the application.

[MS 3001-5G](../3001/3001-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#3001-5_G._DENYING_THE_APPLICATION)      DENYING THE APPLICATION

- Renumbered the subsection from [3001-5F](../3001/3001-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#3001-5_F._PENDING_THE_APPLICATION) to [3001-5G](../3001/3001-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#3001-5_G._DENYING_THE_APPLICATION) to accommodate the insertion of new subsection "Changes Reported to [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance."

[MS 3001-5H](../3001/3001-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#3001-5-_H._REASONS_FOR_DENYING_AN_APPLICATION)      REASONS FOR DENYING AN APPLICATION

- Renumbered the subsection from [3001-5G](../3001/3001-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#3001-5_G._DENYING_THE_APPLICATION) to [3001-5H](../3001/3001-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#3001-5-_H._REASONS_FOR_DENYING_AN_APPLICATION) to accommodate the insertion of new subsection "Changes Reported to [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance."
- Removed reference to obsolete eligibility system codes.
- Removed example of wording used in a denial notice for a client living in a temporary shelter. This information should be referenced in a job aid.
- Removed example of wording used in a denial notice for a client who is eligible to be served by Tribal [LIHEAP](#)Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program. This information should be referenced in a job aid.
- Changed reference from "worker" to "caseworker" to align with [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance's other program manuals.

[MS 3002-1A](../3002/3002-1_households.md#3002-1_A._TYPES_OF_HOUSEHOLDS)      TYPES OF HOUSEHOLDS

- Changed reference from "worker" to "caseworker" to align with [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance's other program manuals.

- Removed reference to obsolete eligibility system codes.

- Removed reference to the number associated with questions on the application as these may change from year to year when updates to the application are made.
- Removed the option for parents to choose who will claim the children in a shared custody situation because children are included in the first application received for the program year.
