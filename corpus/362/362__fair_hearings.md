362      FAIR HEARINGS

Any Senior Benefits applicant
or recipient whose application is not acted on within 30 days after its
receipt, or who is affected by any adverse action taken on his or her
case may request a fair hearing.  The
fair hearing request must be made in writing to any employee of the Division
of Public Assistance. 

Hearing procedures are
detailed in the  [Administrative Procedures
Manual Chapter 117-2.](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/admin/117/117-2.htm)
