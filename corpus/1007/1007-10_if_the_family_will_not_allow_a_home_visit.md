1007-10          IF THE FAMILY WILL NOT ALLOW A HOME VISIT

In some cases, the family may not agree to or allow the [PR](#)Pre-Employment Skills visit to be made. They may not respond to contacts to arrange a [PR](#)Pre-Employment Skills visit, not be home at the pre-arranged time, or refuse entry to the home. In these situations, ensure that all attempts to arrange and to complete the [PR](#)Pre-Employment Skills visit are documented in [CMS](#)Case Management System. Determine, based on information available in [CMS](#)Case Management System, whether to request an additional reduction in benefits and document the decision on the [PR](#)Pre-Employment Skills form, [WS40](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/e-forms/pdf/TA-40.pdf).
