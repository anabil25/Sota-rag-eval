Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #19

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: September 1, 2022

We have made several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field. An overview of the changes are listed below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov)

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[805 A](../../805_medicaid_recipient_id_card/805_medicaid_recipient_id_card.md#805_A._________ISSUING_MEDICAID_TO_ELIGIBLE_RECIPIENTS)   Issuing Medicaid to Eligible Recipients

- Clarified that homeless individuals can have their Medicaid ID cards mailed to the local [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance office.

[806-3](../../806_application_and_renewal_process/renewal_requirements.md)  Renewal Requirements

- Clarified that MAGI Medicaid must be renewed when an Application for Services (GEN 50C) or Eligibility Review Form (GEN 72) is received by DPA.

[808-5(A)](../../808_us_citizenship_and_eligible_alien_status/808-5_non_qualified_aliens.md#808-5_A.___LEGAL__NON-QUALIFIED_ALIENS)   Legal, Non-Qualified Aliens

- Added note clarifying that an individual with Temporary Protected Status (TPS) is a legal, non-qualified alien.

[816(A)](../../816_magi_medicaid_categories/806_magi_medicaid_categories.md#816_A._INDIVIDUALS_THAT_MAY_MEET_ELIGIBILITY_FACTORS_FOR_BOTH_MAGI_MEDICAID_AND_ADLTC_MEDICAID)   Individuals That May Meet Eligibility Factors For Both MAGI Medicaid and ADLTC Medicaid

- Revised section for clarity.

[819(A)](../../819_types_of_income/819_types_of_income.md#819_A._EARNED_INCOME)  Earned Income

- Added information regarding virtual currency/cryptocurrency (ex: bitcoin).

[827](../../827_retroactive_medicaid/825_retroactive_medicaid.md)    Retroactive Medicaid

- Added examples of when [DOL](#)Department of Labor and Workforce Development data can be used to determine eligibility for retroactive Medicaid.

[Addendum 3](../../addenda/addendum-3_countable_and_excluded_income_for_magi_medicaid.md)   Countable and Excluded Income for MAGI Medicaid

- Added note clarifying that virtual currency/cryptocurrency (ex: bitcoin) received as payment for goods or service is countable income.

Addendum 7   Social Security Enumeration Request Letter Template

- Revised signature line by removing "Eligibility Technician" as the Social Security Administration will not accept the form if it isn't signed by a member of management. Also, updated phone number to the [VCC](#)Virtual Contact Center.
