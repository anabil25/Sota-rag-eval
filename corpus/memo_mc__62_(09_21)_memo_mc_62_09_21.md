APA - Alaska Adult Public Assistance Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   [MC](#)Manual Change #62

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  September 1, 2021

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy, clarify existing policy, and align policies within our program policy manuals. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these policy revision are described below.  If you have any questions, contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

[410-2E](../../410/410-2_the_application_form.md#410_2_E__WHO_CAN_SIGN_THE_APPLICATION_FORM?)     WHO CAN SIGN THE APPLICATION FORM

- Clarified who can sign an application for [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance benefits.

[410-3B](../../410/410-3_the_interview.md#410_3_B____WHO_MUST_PARTICIPATE_THE_INTERVIEW_)     WHO MUST PARTICIPATE IN THE INTERVIEW

- Clarified who can participate in an [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance interview.

[410-3C](../../410/410-3_the_interview.md#410_3_C____WHO_CAN_CONDUCT_THE_INTERVIEW_)      WHO CAN CONDUCT THE INTERVIEW

- Removed [NFAP](#)Native Family Assistance Program agency as an entity who could conduct an [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance interview.

[410-4](../../410/410-4_verification.md)     VERIFICATION

- Added [IEVS](#)Instant Eligiblity Verification System to interfaces that must be checked when adding a spouse.
- Removed [INGENS](#)Public Information Database from interfaces that are checked if questionable or under certian conditions.

[420-2D](../../420/420-2_concurrent_participation_in_the_ssi_program.md#420_2_D__BENEFITS_TO_APA_RECIPIENTS_PENDING_AN_SSI_DECISION)     BENEFITS TO [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance RECEIPIENT PENDING AN [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income DECISION

- Clarified that if an [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance recipient is still deemed disabled and meets all other factors of eligibility, the recipient should be given 30 days to provide verficiation that they are pursuing [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income.

[433-4](../../433/433-4_able_accounts.md)     [ABLE](#)Achieving a Better Life Experience ACCOUNTS

- Clarified that an [ABLE](#)Achieving a Better Life Experience account can be administered by any state that allows an [ABLE](#)Achieving a Better Life Experience account.

[441-1E](../../441/441-1_earned_income.md#441_1_E_NET_EARNINGS_FROM_SELF_EMPLOYMENT)     NET EARNINGS FROM SELF-EMPLOYMENT

- Updated section to clarify what are allowable business expenses.
