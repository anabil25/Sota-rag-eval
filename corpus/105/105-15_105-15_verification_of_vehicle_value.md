105-15         VERIFICATION OF VEHICLE VALUE

Find the fair market value of countable vehicles by using the NADA Appraisal Guides on the Internet at [www.jdpower.com/cars](https://www.jdpower.com/cars).

1. On the website, click on the appropriate selection – Automobiles, Classic Cars, Motorcycles, Boats, Recreational Vehicles, or Aircraft.
2. If prompted, enter the local Zip Code and click on the SUBMIT button.
3. When prompted, select a Category, Make, Year, and Model.
4. If prompted to enter mileage or optional equipment, leave blank.
5. Click on the "Get Pricing Report" or "Get Price" button.

The fair market value is the base amount quoted as "Average Trade-in" or "Low Retail."

The actual prompts may differ depending on the category of vehicle selected.

If the household questions the NADA value, they may provide proof of a lower value from a reliable source such as a car dealership.
