105-5          INCOMPLETE
VERIFICATION

In very limited circumstances, Temporary Assistance
eligibility may be found for the first two calendar months without complete
verification being obtained. See the Temporary Assistance Manual.

SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program
also allows certain verification to be temporarily waived for household
entitled to expedited services. See the SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program
Manual.
