**STATE OF ALASKA**

**Department of Health and Social Services**

**Division of Public Assistance**

**TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:**2015-03

**MANUAL:**Alaska Food Stamp Program

**DATE:**June 1, 2015

We have made a change regarding expungement verification requirements and updated the incarcerated household example.

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at email: [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

**OVERVIEW OF CHANGES**

**[MS 602-1A(5)](../../602/602-1_a.md)** **Excluded Household Members**

- Removed the word written from the ***Note*** regarding expunged felony verification requirements.

**MS 604-3A(4)** **Semi-Annual Reporting**

- Aligned the “Report of Household Member Incarcerated” example with adequate notice requirements.
