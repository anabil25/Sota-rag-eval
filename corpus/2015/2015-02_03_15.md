**STATE OF ALASKA**

**Department of Health and Social Services**

**Division of Public Assistance**

**TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:** 2015-02

**MANUAL:** Alaska Food Stamp Program

**DATE:** March 2, 2015

We have made changes to the self-employment annualization table based on changes in the Federal Poverty Guidelines ([*FPG*](javascript:TextPopup(this))*Federal Poverty Guidelines*) for Alaska. In addition, we’ve updated the exception for battered shelter residents to include men.

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at email: [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

**OVERVIEW OF CHANGES**

**MS 601-1 A** **Eligibility in One Food Stamp Household**

- Updated exception to include men as a resident of a shelter for battered persons.

**[MS 602-1 A](../../602/602-1_a.md)** **Nonfinancial Eligibility - Households**

- Updated terminology to include men as a resident of a shelter for battered persons.

**[MS 604-3 G](../../604/604-3_g.md)** **Adding a Member to the Household**

- Updated exception to include men as a resident of a shelter for battered persons.

**[MS 605-1 D](../../605/605-1_d.md)** **Institutions**

- Updated terminology to include men as a resident of a shelter for battered persons.

**[MS 605-2 D (7)](../../605/605-2_d.md)** **Annualization Determination**

- Updated self-employment annualization standards with release of Federal Poverty Guidelines for Alaska, effective March 1, 2015.
