STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #62

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: April 1, 2019

We have updated the self-employment annualization table based on changes in the Federal Poverty Guidelines (FPG) for Alaska effective April 1, 2019. We have also made several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 712-1](../712/712-1_definition_of_a_caretaker_relative.md)      DEFINITION OF A CARETAKER RELATIVE

- Clarified that [NSTAR](#)Northern Support through Automated Resources interface screens FD50, FD52, and FD55 via the Non-Custodial Parent (NCP) field can be used to verify the paternal relationship to a child and should be the first resource used by a caseworker to verify the relationship.

[MS 712-3 A](../712/712-3_parental_relationship.md#712-3_A._DEFINITIONS)      PARENTAL RELATIONSHIP DEFINITIONS

- Clarified that a court or [CSSD](#)Child Support Services Division can determine parental relationship.

[MS 712-3 B](../712/712-3_parental_relationship.md#712-3_B._VERIFYING_PARENTAL_RELATIONSHIP_FOR_CHILDREN_BORN_IN_ALASKA)      VERIFYING PARENTAL RELATIONSHIP FOR CHILDREN BORN IN ALASKA

- Clarified that a court or [CSSD](#)Child Support Services Division can determine parental relationship.
- Clarified that the [NSTAR](#)Northern Support through Automated Resources interface via the Non-Custodial Parent (NCP) field should be the first resource used by a caseworker to verify the relationship between a parent and child.

[MS 712-3 C](../712/712-3_parental_relationship.md#712-3_C._VERIFYING_PARENTAL_RELATIONSHIP_FOR_CHILDREN_BORN_IN_ANOTHER_STATE)      VERIFYING PARENTAL RELATIONSHIP FOR CHILDREN BORN IN ANOTHER STATE

- Clarified that a court or [CSSD](#)Child Support Services Division can determine parental relationship.
- Clarified that the [NSTAR](#)Northern Support through Automated Resources interface via the Non-Custodial Parent (NCP) field should be the first resource used by a caseworker to verify the relationship between a parent and child.

[MS 723-1 C](../723/723-1_good_cause.md#723-1_C._GOOD_CAUSE_FOR_NON-COMPLIANCE_WITH_THE_FSSP_OR_WORK_ACTIVITIES_REQUIREMENTS)      GOOD CAUSE FOR NON-COMPLIANCE WITH THE [FSSP](#)Family Self-Sufficiency Plan OR WORK ACTIVITIES REQUIREMENTS

- Revised "New Baby" good cause category to clarify that only one parent of a two parent household can claim good cause for this reason.

[MS 759-6 E](../759/759-6_income_from_self-employment.md#759-6_E._BUDGETING_SELF-EMPLOYMENT_INCOME)      ANNUALIZATION DETERMINATION

- Updated the Self-Employment Annualization Standards due to [FPL](#)Federal Poverty Limit changes effective April 2019.

[MS 759-7 A](../759/759-7_income_from_subsidized_employment_and_employment_based_training.md#759-7_A._EARNED_INCOME_FROM_AN_ON-THE-JOB_TRAINING)      EARNED INCOME FROM AN ON-THE-JOB TRAINING

- Updated example to reflect change to case manager reporting methods.

[MS 759-7 B](../759/759-7_income_from_subsidized_employment_and_employment_based_training.md#759-7_B._EARNED_INCOME_FROM_A_JOB_START)      EARNED INCOME FROM A JOB START

- Updated example to reflect change to case manager reporting methods.

[Addendum 4](../addenda/addendum_4_-_the_leave_and_earnings_statement.md)      ENTITLEMENT DEFINITIONS ‒ FAMILY SUBSISTENCE SUPPLEMENTAL ALLOWANCE (FSSA)

- Changed Food Stamp reference to [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program for consistent terminology throughout the policy manuals.
