793-2          FRAUD REFERRALS

When a caseworker believes an individual is withholding or concealing information, or receives a complaint alleging an applicant or recipient may be fraudulent, the caseworker will refer the case to the Fraud Control Unit. The Fraud Control Unit will investigate and decide whether to:

- Proceed against the individual through an Administrative Disqualification Hearing, or
- Conduct a fraud investigation, which may result in referring the case to the Department of Law for criminal prosecution.

See [MS 112](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/admin/112/112_fraud_control.htm) of the Administrative Procedures Manual for additional information about fraud referral procedures.
