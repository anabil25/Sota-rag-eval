758-9          REFUGEE CASH ASSISTANCE PAYMENTS

Monthly refugee cash assistance payments made to refugees in Alaska through the Refugee Assistance & Immigration Services program administered by Catholic Social Services are counted when determining eligibility for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program.

Refugees who qualify for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program are not eligible to receive the monthly refugee cash assistance payments from the Refugee Assistance & Immigration Services Program. Caseworkers must notify and coordinate with Catholic Social Services staff when a Refugee Assistance & Immigration Services Program recipient becomes eligible for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program benefits. See Administrative Procedures [MS 128-4](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/admin/128/128-4_refugee_assistance.htm) for more information about refugee assistance.
