758-10          CROWDFUNDING ACCOUNTS

Crowdfunding accounts (GoFundMe, Kickstarter, etc.) are considered unearned income if they were set-up by the household.

If a Crowdfunding account was set-up by someone outside of the household, it would be considered unearned income once the funds have been released and given to the household.
