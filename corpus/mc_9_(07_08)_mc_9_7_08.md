ADLTC - Alaska Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #9

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  July 15, 2008

We have updated program standards to reflect changes for 2008 and added a new addendum to describe Working Disabled Medicaid Buy-in Procedures.  We have also made numerous technical changes.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3347 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:DPApolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 507](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/507/507_estate_recovery.md)  Estate Recovery

- Removes the name of Public Consulting Group (PCG).  This organization no longer helps to administer the Estate Recovery program.

[MS 520-D](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/520/520_elig_req_apa_policy.md#520_D__DISABILITY_DETERMINATIONS)  Disability Determinations

- Changes the cross reference from Administrative Procedures MSManual Section 105-12 to MSManual Section 115-9.

[MS 523-D](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/523/523_medicaid_except_to_income.md#523_D_______COST_OF_CARE_DETERMINATION)  Cost of Care Determination

- Clarifies that Senior Benefits Payments are countable in the cost-of-care determination.

[MS 533-C](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/533/533_tefra.md#533_C_______REFERRAL_AND_INTERAGENCY_COMMUNICATIONS)  Referral and Interagency Communications

[MS 533-D](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/533/533_tefra.md#533_D_CASE_PROCESSING)  Case processing

[MS 533-E](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/533/533_tefra.md#533_E_______TEFRA_REVIEWS)  TEFRA  Reviews

- Removes Arbitre Consulting as  a contracted care coordination agency for disabled child at home (TEFRA ) determinations. DSDSDivision of Senior and Disabilities Services now does the ICAPInventory for Client and Agency Planning Evaluation evaluations previously done by Arbitre Consulting.
- Clarifies the role of the Division’s Long Term Care Coordinator in the administration of TEFRATax Equity and Fiscal Responsibility Act, P.L. 97-248 cases.

[MS 534](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/534/534_working_disabled_medicaid_buy-in.md)  Working Disabled Medicaid Buy-In.

- Adds a cross reference to new [Addendum 8](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/Addenda/ADDENDUM_8.md) on Working Disabled Medicaid Buy-in Procedures.

[MS 554-K](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/554/554_Transfer_of_Assets.md#554_K_Annuities)  Annuities

- Adds a note to clarify that transfer of asset provisions do not apply to revocable and/or assignable annuities.

[MS 554-L](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/554/554_Transfer_of_Assets.md#554_L_Transfer_Penalty_and_Partial_Months)  Transfer Penalty and Partial Months.

- Updates the email address for Division of Senior and Disability Services in [MS 554-L(7)](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/554/554_Transfer_of_Assets.md#554_L_7_Notification_When_a_Transfer_of_Assets_Penalty_is_Imposed).

[MS 580-D](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/580/580_medicare_premium_assistance.md#580_D___LOW_INCOME_SUBSIDY__LIS__PROGRAM)  Low-Income Subsidy (LIS  ) Program

- Updates this section to reflect 2008 resource limits for low-income subsidy (extra help) categories.
- Removes information about LISLow Income Subsidy subsidy  benchmark amounts for Alaska and a cross reference to new table in [Addendum 1](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/Addenda/ADDENDUM_1.md) that includes this information.

[Addendum 1](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/Addenda/ADDENDUM_1.md)  Program Standards

- Updates Federal Poverty Guidelines for Alaska that became effective March 1, 2008.
- Adds information about maximum Low Income Subsidy amounts for Alaska.
- Updates Pickle Amendment Table for 2008.

[Addendum 6](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/Addenda/ADDENDUM_6.md)  Medicare Part D Low Income Subsidy Guide

- Updates Low Income Subsidy resource limits for 2008.

[Addendum 8](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/ADLTC_Manual/Working_Copy/Robohelp/Addenda/ADDENDUM_8.md)  Working Disabled Medicaid Buy-In Procedures

- Provides information about processing procedures for Working Disabled Medicaid Buy-In cases.
