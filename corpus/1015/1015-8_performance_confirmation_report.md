1015-8          PERFORMANCE CONFIRMATION REPORT

Work Services Providers must adhere to the Alaska Work Verification requirements listed in [WSPM](#)Work Services Procedure Manual [section 1003-3](../1003/1003-3_work_verification.md).

Work Verification accuracy results will be reviewed during each provider's [WSQR](#)Work Services Quality Review. If a provider's Work Verification of Core and Work Support activities demonstrates an accuracy rate of lower than 80% during a review the following will be implemented prior to issuing the next payment:

1. The Management Evaluation Team will provide a report template with a sample of cases that include the reported work activity for the prior month.
2. The Provider will verify the accuracy of the work verification utilizing information documented by their staff and respond to all the sample cases with the following confirmation:

1. [CMS](#)Case Management System Work Activity Screen for each adult with reported activities (both adults in a selected 2P family)
2. [CMS](#)Case Management System Client Note which describes the data entered and how it was verified
3. Provide the hard copy verification, e.g., pay stubs, work statement, activity logs, or the third party verification documentation
4. The provider will correct verification errors and write a [CLNO](#)Client Notes describing the corrective actions taken, and
5. send the completed report to [cspa@hss.soa.directak.net](mailto:http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/ta.md#t=740%2F740-2_pass_ii_child_care.htm&rhsearch=child%20care&rhhlterm=child%20care) with all supporting verification documents within five (5) business days.

1. The Management Evaluation Team will independently review this sampled case data. A finding of lower than 80% accuracy will result in a 20% (up to a monthly maximum of $20,000) reduction in the provider's payment for the month.
2. If the provider does not respond to the Management Evaluation Team's request for verification, the payment reduction will be applied.
3. Any penalty assessed in numbers 2 or 3 above may be resolved through demonstrated corrective action.

Participation in the Performance Confirmation Report will continue until the provider's [WSQR](#)Work Services Quality Review demonstrates 80% Work Verification accuracy or the provider has three consecutive months of 80% or higher Work Verification following this process.  If the Work Verification accuracy drops below 80% in the next [WSQR](#)Work Services Quality Review, the process will begin again.

Work Services Providers may choose to review Work Verification data entry using the sample provided by [PI&A](#)Program Integrity & Analysis/[R&A](#)Research & Analysis on the participant report as they see fit if they are not required to do so under the terms  of this report requirement.  Some reasons to review Work verification include:

- Monthly case file review
- Supporting or coaching for staff's individual performance
- New staff's understanding and application of Work Verification standards
- Other application according to leadership's identified need

Technical assistance resources will be made available as requested to assist providers develop their expertise in Work Verification expectations.
