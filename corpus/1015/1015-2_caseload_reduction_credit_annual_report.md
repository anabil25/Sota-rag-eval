1015-2          CASELOAD REDUCTION CREDIT ANNUAL REPORT

Section 407(b)(3) of the Social Security Act, as amended by the Deficit Reduction Act of 2005 (DRA), requires [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance to reduce a state's required work participation rate for a fiscal year by the state's caseload reduction rate for that fiscal year. The caseload reduction credit gives a state credit for reducing its caseload between a base year and a comparison year.

1. The Caseload Reduction Credit Annual report is a federally required report and is a [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance internal report only.
2. This report is not readily accessible to Work Services providers.
3. This report is an internal document that is generated by [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance's Research and Analysis unit and is verified and submitted to the Federal Administration of Children and Family Services by the department's Administrative Operations Manager.
