1015-7          PENALTY REPORT

The Penalty Report is available to Work Services case managers. This report includes clients whose [TA](#)Temporary Assistance cases have been reduced due to penalty. Supervisors use this report to monitor and guide their staffs' efforts to reengage penalized clients in appropriate activities.

1. The Penalty Report of generated by [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance's Research and Analysis unit and is emailed monthly to Work Services providers. This report is not available on the DPAweb.
2. There are no instructions or a data dictionary associated with this report.
3. There are no [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance reporting requirements associated with this report. The Penalty Report is primarily used as a management tool to monitor [TA](#)Temporary Assistance clients in penalty status and to guide efforts in re-engagement of clients.
