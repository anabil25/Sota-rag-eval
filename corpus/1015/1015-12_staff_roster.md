1015-12          STAFF ROSTER

Work Services providers will submit an updated Staff Roster to [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov) by the last business day of the month. At a minimum, the roster will include:

- Staff first and last name;
- Job title;
- Business phone number;
- Business email address;
- Physical address (mailing address if different).
