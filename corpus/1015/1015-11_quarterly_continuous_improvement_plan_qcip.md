1015-11          QUARTERLY CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT PLAN (QCIP)

This report is a quarterly narrative report describing the performance achieved for the quarter recently ended and plans to improve performance moving forward.

1. The [QCIP](#)Quarterly Continuous Improvement Plan is available on eForms at <http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/e-forms/pdf/TA-41.pdf>.
2. Performance data is copied from <http://soar.hss.state.ak.us/performance/index.php>. The [WSP](#)Work Services Provider projects the performance goal for the next quarter based on the conditions within their service delivery area and their continuous improvement strategies proposed on this report.
3. Work Services providers complete the [QCIP](#)Quarterly Continuous Improvement Plan by the 30th of the month following the end of the quarter. This narrative report is submitted through [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).
