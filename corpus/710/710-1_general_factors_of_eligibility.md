710-1          GENERAL FACTORS OF ELIGIBILITY

All of the following factors of eligibility must be met for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program eligibility to exist:

1. Citizenship and Eligible Alien Status
2. Residency in Alaska
3. Age
4. Living in Home of a Caretaker Relative
5. Living Arrangement of a Minor Parent
6. Child Support-Assignment
7. Child Support-Cooperation
8. Social Security Enumeration
9. Family Self Sufficiency Planning and Participation in Assigned Work Activities
10. Strikers
11. Financial Need-Resources
12. Development of Income
13. Financial Need-Income
14. 60-Month Time Limit on Assistance
