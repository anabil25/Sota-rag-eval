128-3          REPATRIATION
PROGRAM - MENTALLY ILL NATIONALS

This program is exactly like the repatriation program,
except it provides extra social and psychological services to U.S. citizens
who are returned to the U.S. because of mental illness. Notification of
the need for cash and medical assistance will be made by the AFDCAid to Families with Dependent Children Program Officer to the servicing
District office, and detailed instructions will be provided.
