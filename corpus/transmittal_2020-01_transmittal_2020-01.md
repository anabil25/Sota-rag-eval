Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2020-01

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: April 1, 2020

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have any questions, please contact Field Services at [dpafield@alaska.gov](mailto:dpafield@alaska.gov) or the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 100-3 (E)](../100/100-3_confidentiality.md#100-3_E._SUBPOENAS_AND_COURT_ORDERS)      SUBPOENAS AND COURT ORDERS

- Updated email address for Assistant Attorney General at [alexander.hildebrand@alaska.gov](mailto:alexander.hildebrand@alaska.gov).

[MS 100-3 (H)](../100/100-3_confidentiality.md#100-3_H._REQUESTS_FOR_INFORMATION_FROM_OTHER_AGENCIES)      REQUESTS FOR INFORMATION FROM OTHER AGENCIES

- Added the Social Security Administration to the list of agencies that we can release information to without a signed release from the client due to clarification from Department of Law, and renumbered the list of agencies

[MS 101-2](../101/101-2_forms.md)      APPLICATION FORMS

- Added that Senior Benefits will accept the GEN 50C as an initial application
- Added that Senior Benefits will accept the GEN 72 to determine continued eligibility. This change is effective April 1, 2020.

[MS 103-6 (C)](../103/103-6_retention_of_case_records.md#103-6_C._RECORD_STORAGE_PROCEDURES)      RECORD STORAGE PROCEDURES

- Updated Record Officer's contact information to Marie Speegle

[MS 103-6 (D)](../103/103-6_retention_of_case_records.md#103-6_D._INSTRUCTIONS_FOR_FIELD_OFFICES_WHEN_REQUESTING_STORED_RECORDS)      INSTRUCTIONS FOR FIELD OFFICES WHEN REQUESTING STORED RECORDS

- Updated Record Officer's contact information to Marie Speegle

[MS 105-14](../105/105-14_systematic_alien_verification_for_entitlements_-_electronic_verification_process.md)      SYSTEMATIC ALIEN VERIFICATION FOR ENTITLEMENTS – ELECTRONIC VERIFICATION PROCESS

- Clarified that checking for an alien sponsor is required whenever SAVE is used
- Added instructions for how to see if an alien has a sponsor and link to policy manuals for how to handle alien sponsor deeming

[MS 108-2 (B)](../108/108-2_content_of_a_notice.md#108-2_B._REQUIRED_NOTICE_CONTENT)      REQUIRED NOTICE CONTENT

- Clarified that all notices must contain or clearly state certification period for approved cases, which can be written in the free form section of the notice

[MS 109-5](../109/109-5_editing_and_deleting_documentation_from_cano.md)      EDITING AND DELETING DOCUMENTATION FROM CANO

- Updated online link to Sysops page for requesting CANO deletion

[MS 121-15](../121/121-15_returned_warrants___checks.md)      RETURNED WARRANTS / CHECKS

- Changed title to include "checks" for clarity
- Revised note to clarify that warrants / checks payable to the State of Alaska should not be voided or canceled. These should be routed to the BIRU for processing.
