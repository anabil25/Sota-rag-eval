756-3          DEFINITION OF TOTAL GROSS MONTHLY INCOME

"Total gross monthly income" consists of the total sum of countable earned, in-kind (as described in [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 758-2](../758/758-2_in-kind_income_and_vendor_payments.md)), and unearned pre-taxed income of all persons in the assistance unit.
