APA - Alaska Adult Public Assistance Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   MC #57

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE: December 2, 2019

This year Social Security announced a 1.6% Cost of Living Allowance (COLACost of Living Allowance) increase effective January 2020. We have incorporated the 2020 standards into the examples and [Addendum 1](../../addendum/addendum_1.md).  These standards become effective with the benefit month of January 2020. The COLACost of Living Allowance adjustment also causes:

- An increase in the income limit for Substantial Gainful Activity; and
- An increase in the student earned income exclusion.

Due to a recent clarification from the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMSCenters for Medicare and Medicaid Services), we have simplified our Adult Public Assistance (APAAdult Public Assistance) benefit calculation to be the difference between the individual's countable income and the need standard for their household type. In addition to this change, the APAAdult Public Assistance need and payment standards were reduced based on the State budget. Both of these changes are effective with the benefit month of January 2020.

We also made several changes to incorporate new policy, clarify existing policy, and align policies within our program policy manuals. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these policy revision are described below.  If you have any questions, contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).
