STATE OF ALASKA

DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #15

MANUAL: Work Services Procedure manual

DATE: April 1, 2025

We have made technical corrections and incorporated the changes outlined below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Work Services Policy team at WSTA@alaska.gov or the Policy & Program Development team  at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 1001-2](../1001/1001-2_employability_assessment.md)       EMPLOYABILITY ASSESSMENT

- Clarified that the Work Services intake and initial [FSSP](#)Family Self Sufficiency Plan must be completed within 30 calendar days of the referral to Work Services.

[MS 1004-4](../1004/1004-4_supportive_service_codes_and_definitions.md)       SUPPORTIVE SERVICE CODES AND DEFINITIONS

- Described circumstances and timeframes for which Youth Enrichment (YE) can be used to pay for above and beyond childcare costs.

[MS 1013-2](../1013/1013-2_pass_ii___iii_child_care.md)       PASS II/III CHILDCARE

- Clarified roles and responsibilities in sending the W150 notice.
- Provided example for language for when requesting the W150 to be sent by eligibility.

[MS 1015-8](../1015/1015-8_performance_confirmation_report.md)       PERFORMANCE CONFIRMATION REPORT

- Added participation timeframe requirements.
