101-8          REGISTERING
HOUSEHOLD MEMBERS LISTED ON THE APPLICATION

When registering an application, staff must register
everyone listed on the application as household members. Although not
all household members may be included in the case, there is a possibility
that the household member could affect a related case.

Exception:

When registering a recertification, clerical staff are not required to
add household members to an existing case. The caseworker will determine
who needs to be added to the case when processing the recertification.

Refer to [Eligibility Information
System (EIS) Procedure 1999-8](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/eis/1999-8regclientinquiry.pdf) for additional information on application
registration and client inquiry.
