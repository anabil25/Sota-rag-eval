MEMORANDUM

DATE:         March 21, 2005

TO:               All Adult Public Assistance Manual Holders

FROM:         Policy and Program Development Team

                      Ellie Fitzjarrald, Chief

SUBJECT: Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Manual Change #1

This manual change updates the Income Standards in Addendum 1 with the 2005 Federal Poverty Guidelines for Alaska, and the 2005 Reduction Factors for the Pickle Amendment Table. It also adds the EISEligibility Information System Qualified Medicare Beneficiaries (QMB ) eligibility codes and the assisted living "AS " subtype for use in home and community-based waiver determinations, and the "IC" and "IF" eligibility codes that were inadvertently omitted.

If you have any questions, please contact any member the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3347 or email us at dpapolicy@health.state.ak.us.
