808            U.S. CITIZENSHIP AND ELIGIBLE ALIEN STATUS

To receive Medicaid, a person must be:

- A United States citizen or U.S.United States National;

- A qualified alien, as described in [MS 808-2](808-2_qualified_aliens.md). Qualified aliens may be subject to limited eligibility as explained in [MS 808-4](808-4_five_year_waiting_period.md).

- A Native American born in Canada or Mexico who has treaty rights to cross the U.S./Canada and U.S./Mexico borders as described in [MS 808-3](808-3_native_americans_born_outside_the_united_states.md).

- A Compact of Free Association (COFA) migrant, as described in [MS 808-5(B)](808-5_non_qualified_aliens.md#808-5_B.___NON-IMMIGRANTS).
