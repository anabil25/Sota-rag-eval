ADLTC - Alaska Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #12

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

Date:  April 1, 2009

Beginning April 1, 2009, all children under the age of 19 who are eligible for Medicaid (except BANewborn Medicaid, TMTransitional Medicaid, 4MFour Month Post-Medicaid Eligibility, and Retroactive Medicaid categories) remain eligible for those benefits for a continuous 12-month period.  This change applies to all actions taken on or after April 1, 2009.

The manual has been updated to change all references to 6-month continuous eligibility  to 12 months, and language that no longer applies has been removed.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:DPApolicy@alaska.gov).
