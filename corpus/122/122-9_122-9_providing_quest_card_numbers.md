122-9          PROVIDING
QUEST CARD NUMBERS

Federal regulations require that the Quest
card must be present
at the time of the transaction/purchase. Caseworkers should never provide
card numbers to clients. Should an office receive such a request, refer
the client to the EBTElectronic Benefit Transfer
Helpdesk.

FNSFood and Nutrition Service
can review all Quest card transactions and can easily identify clients
and retailers that are transacting without swiping the card. FNSFood and Nutrition Service has the authority to de-authorize
any retailer that is not following the policies and procedures. If a caseworker
provides card numbers to clients, it could potentially jeopardize the
retailer's authorization to accept Quest card transactions. This would
be a severe penalty to the retailer and a detriment to the client and
the community.
