122-6          ALTERNATE
PAYEE

Client's alternate payee can request an alternate
payee card. The Eligibility Technician should make sure that the EISEligibility Information System fields "Authorized to Use Cash"
or "Authorized to Use Food" on the EBPMEBT Payee Maintenance Screen
screen is coded correctly. Also the Eligibility Technician should advise
the alternate payee that they need to know the PI's SSNSocial Security Number
and DOBDate of Birth if they need
to contact JPMorgan Customer Service for any reason.
