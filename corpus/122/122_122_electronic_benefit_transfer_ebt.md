122            ELECTRONIC BENEFIT TRANSFER (EBTElectronic Benefit Transfer)

The Benefit Issuance & Recovery unit is responsible for all EBTElectronic Benefit Transfer management, EBTElectronic Benefit Transfer Helpdesk, and direct deposit functions.

All SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program benefits are placed on the Quest (EBTElectronic Benefit Transfer) card. Clients receiving [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program, [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance, and/or Senior Benefits can choose to have their benefits placed on either the Quest card or direct deposited into their personal bank account.
