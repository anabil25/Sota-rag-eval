122-16        RESTRICTIONS
FOR USING ATAP BENEFITS AT CERTAIN LOCATIONS

Quest cards may not be used to make purchases with
Temporary Assistance cash benefits, or to withdraw cash benefits from
ATMs located in bars, liquor stores, gambling or any adult entertainment
establishments. The Division will remove the capability to accept Quest
from all ATMs and card reading devices that are located in such venues.
