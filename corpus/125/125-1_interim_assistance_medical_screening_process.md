125-1          INTERIM
ASSISTANCE MEDICAL SCREENING PROCESS

Interim Assistance is a temporary cash grant available
to Adult Public Assistance (APAAdult Public Assistance)
applicants who are waiting for a decision on a Supplemental Security Income
(SSISupplemental Security Income) application. IAInterim Assistance was established as a special subcategory
of APAAdult Public Assistance in order to provide
for the basic needs of APAAdult Public Assistance
applicants who must concurrently apply for the SSI program.

Refer to Adult Public Assistance [MS
426](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/apa/426/426_interim_assistance.htm) for more information about APAAdult Public Assistance
and IAInterim Assistance programs.
