Work Services Procedure Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #2

MANUAL: Work Services Procedure Manual

DATE: December 3, 2018

We are making several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of suggestions from the Field and Work Services providers.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Work Services Policy Team at WSTA@alaska.gov or [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 1001-2](../1001/1001-2_employability_assessment.md)      EMPLOYABILITY ASSESSMENT

- Clarified that both new and returning clients need to complete the Employment Planning Interview, including the TA 5

[MS 1001-3](../1001/1001-3_conducting_the_employaility_assessment.md)      INITIAL EMPLOYMENT PLANNING INTERVIEW INSTRUCTIONS

- Changed title of subsection from "Initial Employment Planning Interview" to "Initial Employment Planning Interview Instructions" for clarity
- Removed previous information about the Initial Employment Planning Interview (EPI) and replaced with detailed instructions
- Renumbered section accordingly

[MS 1003-3 C (2)](../1003/1003-3_work_verification.md#1003-3_C._2._SSI_/_SSDI)      SSI / SSDI

- Clarified documentation requirements for activity hours related to SSI / SSDI activities

[MS 1003-6](../1003/1003-6_performance_confirmation.md)      PERFORMANCE CONFIRMATION

- Clarified when and how long a Work Services provider must follow performance confirmation protocols as a result of a Work Services Quality Review (WSQR)

[MS 1004-1](../1004/1004-1_when_to_provide_supportive_services.md)      WHEN TO PROVIDE SUPPORTIVE SERVICES

- Clarified that there are spending limits for certain categories (i.e., medical/dental/relocation)
- Revised NOTE to clarify when Supportive Services can be offered during the Post-TA period

[MS 1007-4](../1007/1007-4_good_cause_or_an_exemption_is_established.md)      GOOD CAUSE OR AN EXEMPTION IS ESTABLISHED

- Removed old guidance instructing the case manager to apply a baby exemption if the client qualifies rather than applying a penalty for non-compliance as this policy no longer applies

[MS 1013](../1013/1013_post_temporary_assistance_employment_services.md)      WORK SERVICES CASE MANAGEMENT IN POST-TA

- Added link to WSPM 1004-1 that clarifies Post-TA eligibility when a child ages out, makes a permanent move, custody change, or out-of-state move

[MS 1018-5](../1018/1018-5_management_of_case_transfers.md)      MANAGEMENT OF CASE TRANSFERS

- Clarified the process for change of address or community expectations as it relates to client relocation

[MS 1018-6](../1018/1018-6_reserved.md)      GRIEVANCE PROCEDURE

- Extended the deadline for submission of Grievances from 10 days to 15 days to give WS providers more time to evaluate potential grievances

ENTIRE MANUAL

- Removed all references to Program Service Aide's (PSA's) throughout the manual as this aspect of Job Development is not currently being used
