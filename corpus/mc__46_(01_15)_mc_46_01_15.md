Alaska Temporary Assistance Program Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #46

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: January 2, 2015

This year Social Security announced a [COLA](#)Cost of Living Allowance increase effective January 1, 2015. We have updated the Alaska Temporary Assistance income eligibility and shelter allowance standards to reflect the annual [COLA](#)Cost of Living Allowance adjustments in Social Security benefits. The new standards are effective beginning with the benefit month of January 2015. The [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program manual was also updated to reflect recent changes in federal legislation in response to the Cobell v. Salazar class-action lawsuit and same-sex marriage court decisions.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 701-1 D](../701/701-1_what_is_the_60-month_time_limit_.md#701-1_D._MONTHS_THAT_DO_NOT_COUNT_TOWARD_THE_60-MONTH_TIME_LIMIT)      Months that Do Not Count Towards the 60-Month Time Limit

- Adds a bullet stating that months used as a minor teen parent do not count towards the client's 60-month lifetime limit.

[MS 712-3](../712/712-3_parental_relationship.md)      Parental Relationship

- Adds a definition of a stepparent.
- Adds a note regarding same-sex married couples.

[MS 723-1 B](../723/723-1_good_cause.md#723-1_B._GOOD_CAUSE_FOR_NOT_COOPERATING_WITH_CSSD)      Good Cause for Not Cooperating with [CSSD](#)Child Support Services Division

- Adds the [cssdgoodcause@alaska.gov](mailto:cssdgoodcause@alaska.gov) email address for notifying the [CSSD](#)Child Support Services Division of a good cause decision.
- Removed language that stated a good cause notice should be mailed to the [CSSD](#)Child Support Services Division.

[MS 753-6](../753/753-6_other_exclusions.md)      Other Exclusions

- Adds a bullet to exempt retained settlement payments from the Cobell v. Salazar class-action lawsuit for up to one year from receipt.

[MS 757-15](../757/757-15_atap_payments.md)      Restitution Payments

- Adds income received from the settlement of the Cobell v. Salazar class-action lawsuit as an exempt restitution payment.

[MS 780-1 G](../780/780-1_definitions.md#780-1_G._RATABLE_REDUCTION)      Ratable Reduction

- Updates the percentage of need payable and ratable reduction amounts for 2015.

[MS 780-3](../780/780-3_calculating_the_payment_amount.md)      Calculating the Payment Amount

- Updates the examples of payment calculations to reflect the 2014 cost of living adjustment.

[Addendum 2](../addenda/addendum_2_-_program_standards.md)      Temporary Assistance Income and Payment Standards

- Updates the income eligibility standards and maximum shelter allowances for 2015.
