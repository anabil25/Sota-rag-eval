ADLTC - Alaska Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #42

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  December 01, 2016

This year Social Security announced a COLA (Cost of Living Allowance) increase effective 2017.  We have updated [Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum_1.md) and the examples throughout the manual with the following items to reflect the 0.3%  increases for 2017:

1. - An increase to the Long Term Care community spouse resource and income allowance and household member standards;
   - An increase to the Excess Home Equity resources maximum; and
   - An increase to the Special Long Term Care Income Standard
   - Medicare Part A and B premiums
   - Medicare Part D premium and coverage information, including changes to the Low-Income Subsidy program

In addition to the COLA change, this manual change consists of several small technical changes or clarifications as a result of suggestions from the field.  Also, we have replaced all reference to Family Medicaid with MAGI Medicaid and updated the links to the new MAGI Medicaid manual.

Highlights of these revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at e-mail [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).
