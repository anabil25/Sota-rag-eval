719-2          EMPLOYABILITY ASSESSMENT

An initial assessment of the employability, strengths, skills, prior work experience, training, and other characteristics affecting the ability to work is required no later than 30 calendar days after determination of Temporary Assistance eligibility for each parent or caretaker who is:

- Age 18 or older; or
- Under age 18, without a high school diploma or [GED](#)General Equivalency Diploma, and not attending secondary school.

Information needed to conduct the initial assessment is collected on the Employment Planning Information (EPI) form ([TA 5](#)Employability Assessment). See [WSPM](#)Work Services Procedure Manual [MS 1001-2](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/workservices1/1001/1001-2_employability_assessment.htm) for additional information.
