STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #5

MANUAL: Work Services Procedure Manual

DATE: April 1, 2020

We are making several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of suggestions from the Field and Work Services providers.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Work Services Policy Team at WSTA@alaska.gov or [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 1003](../1003/1003_performance_measures,_work_activities,_and_work_verification.md)      PERFORMANCE MEASURES, WORK ACTIVITIES, AND WORK VERIFICATION

- Fixed all incorrect hyperlinks throughout the chapter.

[MS 1003-1 D (2)](../1003/1003-1_performance_measures.md#1003-1_D._MEETING_THE_RATE)      MEETING THE RATE

- Clarified that if the parent is under 20 and graduates high school, they must participate in work activities.

[MS 1003-2](../1003/1003-2_work_activities.md)      WORK SERVICES ACTIVITY CODES CHART

- Added substance abuse activities after "mental health" under code B5 on chart.
- Under VE, clarified that only a total of 12 months of vocational education are allowed over the 60 month lifetime limit of the program.
