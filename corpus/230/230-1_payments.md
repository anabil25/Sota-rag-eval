230-1        PAYMENTS

A. LIMITS OF ASSISTANCE

Except for burial assistance, the amount of General Relief Assistance paid on behalf of eligible applicants is based on the extent of the specified need and the availability of program funds, but cannot exceed a total of $120 per person per month. Refer to [section 240-3](../240/240-3_payment.md), for an explanation of burial assistance payment limitations.

B. BUDGETING FOR NEED

Monthly need standards have been established to aid in the determination of eligibility for GRAGeneral Relief Assistance recipients. The amount of the monthly need standard is determined by the size of the recipient's household. (Refer to [section 220-1 G](../220/220-1_non-financial_eligibility.md#220-1_G._HOUSEHOLD_COMPOSITION) for a definition of a GRAGeneral Relief Assistance household).

After an applicant's net monthly countable income has been determined (refer to [section 220-3](../220/income.md)), the caseworker should consult the following table to determine the need standard.

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Number of Persons | Maximum Need Standards | Max Pay |
| 1 | $300 | $120 |
| 2 | 400 | $240 |
| 3 | 500 | $360 |
| 4 | 600 | $480 |
| 5 | 700 | $600 |

Add $100 for each additional household member.

The caseworker will compare the GRAGeneral Relief Assistance household's net monthly countable income to the applicable need standard.

If the income is equal to or higher than the need standard, the applicant is not eligible.

If the income is under the need standard and all other eligibility criteria are met, the applicant is eligible for assistance up to $120 per person (per month).

Example:

Need standard: $400 (one adult, one child)
Income               -250
Difference          $150 (Eligible for up to $240)

C. PAYMENT EXCLUSIONS

- GRA payments for shelter costs, food, fuel, clothing, utilities, and funeral and burial assistance must be made to a vendor.  They are never made to the recipients.
- Rent or utility deposits are not covered by GRA.  Payment for telephone charges of any kind will not be covered.
- No GRA vendor payments will be made for services or goods provided prior to the month of application.
- GRA can pay a reconnect fee (if necessary) and the current bill only.  When a client has been disconnected it's usually due to failure to pay their bill (past bills.)  GRA is only for services received the month of application.

D. RETROACTIVE BENEFITS

General Relief Assistance is not provided for the month(s) prior to the month of application.
