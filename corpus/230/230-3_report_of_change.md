230-3   REPORT OF CHANGE

The caseworker must advise all applicants to report any changes that might affect their eligibility. Changes that must be reported include, but are not limited to, changes in the nature of the specific need, living arrangements, income, and resources. The recipient or the applicant's authorized representative must report such changes to the nearest District Office orally or in writing within 10 days of their occurrence.

Reports of change on GRAGeneral Relief Assistance vendor payment cases must be recorded in the case record for use at the time of the next application.
