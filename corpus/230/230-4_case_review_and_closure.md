230-4         CASE REVIEW AND CLOSURE

Each case should be opened during the month of eligibility. GRAGeneral Relief Assistance cases are closed the following month. In the closure of GRAGeneral Relief Assistance vendor cases, no client notification is necessary, as the payment is understood to meet a one-time-only need.

If a recipient contacts DPADivision of Public Assistance and indicates that he or she wishes assistance to be continued, the case will be reviewed  when the individual submits a new application.
