117-7          POSTPONEMENTS

The client or client representative may request
a postponement of a fair hearing date and time by directly contacting
the Office of Administrative Hearings. Once the request is received by
the Office of Administrative Hearings, a decision of approval or denial
will be issued by the assigned administrative law judge. Once the decision
is rendered, the Office of Administrative Hearings will notify both the
client and the agency of its decision by written notice. If the request
is approved, the notice will identify the new hearing date and time.
