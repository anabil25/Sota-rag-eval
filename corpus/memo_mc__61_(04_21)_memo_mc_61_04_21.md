APA - Alaska Adult Public Assistance Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE
OF ALASKA

Department of Health
and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #61

MANUAL: Adult Public Assistance

DATE: April 1, 2021

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy, clarifying
existing policy, and align policies within our policy manuals. Other changes
are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the result of
policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have
any questions, contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

[410](../../410/410_application_process.md)
           Application
Process

- Added information regarding application
  signature requirements

[410-2(A)](../../410/410-2_the_application_form.md#410_2_A__WHAT_IS_AN_ACCEPTABLE_APPLICATION_FORM_)
   What is An Acceptable Application Form?

- Clarified that a NFAPNative Family Assistance Program application can be used to
  recertify APAAdult Public Assistance and
  related Medicaid

[410-2(D)](../../410/410-2_the_application_form.md#410_2_D__WHAT_IS_AN_IDENTIFIABLE_APPLICATION_)
   What is an Identifiable Application?

- Added
  a link to the application signature requirements, section 410

[410-2(E)](../../410/410-2_the_application_form.md#410_2_E__WHO_CAN_SIGN_THE_APPLICATION_FORM?)
    Who can sign the Application?

- Added a link to the application
  signature requirements, section 410

[410-5(E)](../../410/410-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#410_5_E__WITHDRAWING_AN_APPLICATION)
     Withdrawing
an Application

- Added a link to the application
  signature requirements, section 410

[420-2(B)](../../420/420-2_concurrent_participation_in_the_ssi_program.md#420_2_B__TIME_LIMIT_FOR_PURSUIT_OF_SSI_BENEFITS)
    Time Limit for Pursuit of SSISupplemental Security Income
Benefits

- Updated section that applicants
  and recipients who are eligible for SSI must apply and be in pay status
  within 30 days of an APAAdult Public Assistance
  application

[420-2(D)](../../420/420-2_concurrent_participation_in_the_ssi_program.md#420_2_D__BENEFITS_TO_APA_RECIPIENTS_PENDING_AN_SSI_DECISION)       Benefits
to APAAdult Public Assistance recipients Pending
an SSISupplemental Security Income Decision

- Updated section that an APAAdult Public Assistance recipient whose income or household
  type becomes eligible for SSISupplemental Security Income
  must apply for and be in pay status with SSISupplemental Security Income
  within 30 days of the change

[432-1(F)](../../432/432-1_real_property.md#432-1_F.___DINGELL_ACT_LANDS)
    Dingell Act Lands

- Added new subsection regarding
  Dingell Act lands

[433-4](../../433/433-4_able_accounts.md)
    ABLE Accounts

- Updated 2021 Max Allowable contribution
  for certain employed ABLE account holders

[452-1](../../452/452-1_payment_calculations.md)
    APA Payments

- Added examples for cases with earned
  income

[460-3](../../460/460-3_ssi_and_apa_deeming.md)
    SSISupplemental Security Income
and APAAdult Public Assistance Deeming

- Revised section for clarity

[470-1](../../470/470-1_ineligible_institutions.md)
    Ineligible Institutions

- Removed verbiage indicating applications
  for adults in DOCDepartment of Corrections
  or BOPFederal Bureau of Prisons custody will
  be processed by the Field Services Unit as this is no longer the case

[480-1](../../480/480-1_reviews.md)
    Reviews

- Clarified a NFAPNative Family Assistance Program
  application can be used to recertify APAAdult Public Assistance
  and related Medicaid
- Added a link to application signature
  requirements, section 410
