441           COUNTABLE INCOME

This chapter describes types of income that are counted when determining income eligibility for the APAAdult Public Assistance program. Certain income and resources of spouses and/or alien sponsors is considered available (deemed) to meet the needs of an individual or couple in determining eligibility for APAAdult Public Assistance. Refer to chapter [460](../460/460_financial_responsibility_and_deeming.md) for the policy on the deeming of income and resources of spouses and/or alien sponsors.
