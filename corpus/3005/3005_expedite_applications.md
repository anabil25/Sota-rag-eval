3005      EXPEDITED APPLICATIONS

Expedited service is provided to households in crisis, life-threatening crisis, or to households who meet the medically necessary criteria as defined in [HAP](#)Heating Assistance Program [MS 3000-2](../3000/3000-2_definitions.md). All households must also meet the eligibility criteria listed in [HAP](#)Heating Assistance Program [MS 3005-1](3005-1_expedited_applications.md).
