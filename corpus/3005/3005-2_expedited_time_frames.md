3005-2      EXPEDITED TIME FRAMES

Expedited eligibility service is provided to a household if they have submitted a [SRHUD](#)Subsidized Rental Housing Utility Deposit application or if the applicant meets crisis or life-threatening crisis criteria.

Households eligible for expedited service because of a [SRHUD](#)Subsidized Rental Housing Utility Deposit application submission will have their application processed within 10 days after receipt of the completed application in a [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance office. [SRHUD](#)Subsidized Rental Housing Utility Deposit applications are accepted year-round, October 1 through September 30.

Crisis applications are accepted October 1 through June 30 and should be processed within 18 hours after [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance receives a complete application for a life-threatening crisis and within 48 hours for non-life threatening crisis.
