790-2          CHANGES THAT MUST BE ACTED ON

The caseworker is required to take action and redetermine eligibility and benefit amount when:

- A change is reported by the family, including a request to close the case.
- A system interface provides information that may affect the family's eligibility or benefit amount.
- The family's case manager reports information gathered while working with a parent or caretaker to help the family become self-sufficient; or
- Information becomes known from other sources.
