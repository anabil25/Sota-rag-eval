790-7          EFFECT OF CHANGES ON OTHER PROGRAMS

Many changes reported on a [TA](#)Temporary Assistance case may affect [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program, [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance, Medicaid, [GRA](#)General Relief Assistance, or [CAMA](#)Chronic and Acute Medical Assistance cases, even if they require no [TA](#)Temporary Assistance casework. The caseworker must examine each reported change to determine if action is required for any related program.

The caseworker must also consider the effect of reported changes on a participant's current [FSSP](#)Family Self-Sufficiency Plan. The caseworker should notify the participant's Work Services case manager of any change that may affect the participant's efforts to move the family towards self-sufficiency, such as employment-related changes, illness, etc.
