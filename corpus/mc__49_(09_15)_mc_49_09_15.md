STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #49

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: September 1, 2015

The Alaska Temporary Assistance heating and non-heating utility standards change effective October 2015. The Eligibility Information System (EIS) will automatically apply these new standards when determining eligibility and benefit amounts beginning October 2015. We also made a change based on an inquiry from Contracted Services Quality Assurance.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team via email at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 720-5](../720/720-5_self-sufficiency_planning_requirement.md)      Self-Sufficiency Planning Requirement

- Corrected the language in this subsection to state that the father of an unborn child may develop an [FSSP](#)Family Self-Sufficiency Plan, rather than must develop an [FSSP](#)Family Self-Sufficiency Plan before the child is born.

[Addendum 2](../addenda/addendum_2_-_program_standards.md)      Temporary Assistance Income and Payment Standards

- Updates the heating and non-heating utility standards for FFY 2015.
