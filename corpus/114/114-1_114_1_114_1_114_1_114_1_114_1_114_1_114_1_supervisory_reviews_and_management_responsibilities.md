114-1          SUPERVISORY
REVIEWS AND MANAGEMENT RESPONSIBILITIES

Conducting regular and thorough Supervisor
Case Reviews is vital to ensuring work quality, assessing performance,
and developing skills of staff.

Team supervisors work with managers in ensuring
the effectiveness of case reviews in their respective areas. Staff selected
for this role must be highly knowledgeable, mature, and objective in reaching
decisions on case reviews and communicating their findings. Supervisors
and lead workers ensure that necessary corrections are completed and returned
within appropriate times and identifies additional support and training
needs.
