1001-7          SUBSTANCE ABUSE

Some features of a participant's personal and work history may indicate a problem with substance abuse. Indications of substance abuse include:

- A pattern of absences from work;
- Frequently getting fired from jobs;
- One or more arrests for Driving While Intoxicated; and,
- Unexplained gaps in employment.

If assessment indicates a possible substance abuse problems that may negatively impact the participant's ability to take part in work activities, the Case Manager should refer them to a qualified substance abuse counselor. If the results of the screening indicate a need for substance abuse treatment, then the Case Manager may assign the treatment as an [FSSP](#)Family Self Sufficiency Plan activity.
