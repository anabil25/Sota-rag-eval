1001-11          SUPPORTIVE SERVICES

Supportive Services are financial, social, or other services which help families get, keep, and advance on the job. Supportive Services are used to support client participation in appropriate work activities.

The need for Supportive Services will be proactively addressed as case managers identify participant's potential challenges to achieving full engagement, see[WSPM](#)Work Services Procedure Manual [MS 1002](../1002/1002_family_self-sufficiency_plan_(fssp).md) and [WSPM](#)Work Services Procedure Manual [MS 1004](../1004/1004_supportive_services.md).
