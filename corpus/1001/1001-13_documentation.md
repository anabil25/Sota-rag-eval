1001-13          dOCUMENTATION

Documentation of a client's participation in completing the [EA](#)Employability Assessment and of the Case Manager's attempts to engage the client is required to demonstrate due diligence.

A copy of the TA5 will be maintained in the client's hard file (if applicable) and all elements of the [EA](#)Employability Assessment are included in the [CLNO](#)Client Notes.
