1001-4          MINIMUM SCREENING REQUIREMENTS

Federal regulations requires that all clients are screened, at a minimum, for the following issues at the time of initial assessment:

- Domestic violence;
- Mental health;
- Employment history;
- Education; and,
- Substance abuse.

The [EPI](#)Employment Planning Interview should be completed before the development of the initial [FSSP](#)Family Self Sufficiency Plan.  Minimum screening requirements should only be used in cases of emergent need.  The full [EPI](#)Employment Planning Interview should be completed at the earliest opportunity.
