1001-3          CONDUCTING THE EMPLOYABILITY ASSESSMENT

The [EA](#)Employment Planning Interview will help determine:

1. If a client must participate in temporary Assistance work activities, and if so, the number of hours of participation that will be considered a "full schedule of activities" ([WSPM MS 1003-2](../1003/1003-2_work_activities.md)); or,
2. If the client is exempt or has good cause for not participating in work activities ([WSPM MS 1006](../1006/1006_good_cause.md)).

If the [EA](#)Employability Assessment determines that clients are excused from work activities of have Good Cause for not participating in work activities, they must still develop an [FSSP](#)Family Self Sufficiency Plan per [ATAP MS 723-1](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/ta.htm#t=723%2F723-1_good_cause.htm) and [ATAP MS 730-2.](http://http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/ta.htm#t=730%2F730-2_exemptions_from_work_activities.htm)

Do not allow the TA5 to drive the conversation.  Remain open and flexible to your client's responses and allow for deviation in order to explore topics.
