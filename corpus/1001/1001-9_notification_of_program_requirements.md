1001-9          NOTIFICATION OF PROGRAM REQUIREMENTS

[DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance requires that clients are provided with a thorough description of program and participation requirements that include:

1. [FSSP](#)Family Self Sufficiency Plan development, see [WSPM](#)Work Services Procedure Manual [MS 1002](../1002/1002_family_self-sufficiency_plan_(fssp).md);
2. Work activity participation requirements, see [WSPM](#)Work Services Procedure Manual [MS 1003-2](../1003/1003-2_work_activities.md); and,
3. Penalties, see [WSPM](#)Work Services Procedure Manual [MS 1007](../1007/1007_penalties_and_disqualifications.md).
