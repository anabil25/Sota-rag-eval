STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #71

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: April 1, 2022

The self-employment annualization table has been updated based on changes in the Federal Poverty Guidelines (FPG) for Alaska, effective April 1, 2022.

Highlights of all the policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 705-4 B](../705/705-4_verification.md#705-4_B._INFORMATION_FROM_DATA_SYSTEMS_AND_EIS_INTERFACES)      INFORMATION FROM DATA SYSTEMS AND INTERFACES

- Clarified that [SVES](#)State Verification Exchange System must always be checked at application recertification, and death match alerts that are generated during the certification period must be processed within 10 days.

[MS 759-6](../759/759-6_income_from_self-employment.md)      INCOME FROM SELF-EMPLOYMENT

- Updated Self-Employment Standards due to [FPL](#)Federal Poverty Limit changes effective April 2022.

[MS 770-8 C](../770/770-8_deeming_income_and_resources.md#770-8_C._MINOR_PARENTS)      MINOR PARENTS

- Removed contradictory sentence regarding the inclusion and exclusion of unearned income for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program applicants.
