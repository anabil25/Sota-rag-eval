STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #40

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: July 1, 2013

This manual change reflects the updated Fair Hearing regulations regarding how a hearing must be requested and includes a link to the Administrative Manual section 117 for this process. A new section was created since no section currently exists.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 705-5 E](../705/705-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#705-5_E._WITHDRAWING_AN_APPLICATION)      Withdrawing an Application

- Updated language to explain that withdrawals for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program applications must be made in writing.

[MS 794](../794/794_fair_hearings.md)      Fair Hearings

- Creates a new section for fair hearing policy. This section states that requests for hearings must be made in writing and refers all procedures to the Administrative Manual Section 117.
