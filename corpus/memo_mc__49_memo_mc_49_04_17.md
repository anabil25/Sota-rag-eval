STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health
and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   MC
#49

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  April 3, 2017

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy, clarify existing
policy, and align policies within our program policy manuals. Other changes
are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the result of
policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these policy revision are described below.  If you
have any questions, contact the Policy and Program Development Team at
[dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

[426-2 F](../../426/426-2_eligibility_requirements_for_interim_assistance.md).
      CONTACTING THE MEDICAL REVIEWER

- Added a note to write "terminal",
  when appropriate, on the Interim Assistance paperwork when sending
  to the Medical Reviewer

[433-3](../../433/433-3_permanent_fund_dividend.md)         PERMANENT
FUND DIVIDEND

- Deleted a reference to the PFD
  Hold Harmless manual because it no longer exists

[433-4](../../433/433-4_able_accounts.md)         ABLE
ACCOUNTS

- Clarified
  that housing distribution from an ABLE account only is counted as
  a resource the month after the month of receipt
