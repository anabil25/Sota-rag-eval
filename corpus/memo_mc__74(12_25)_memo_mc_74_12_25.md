STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   [MC](previous_transmittals/memo_mc__62_(09_21).md#)Manual Change #74

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  December 1, 2025

This year Social Security announced a 2.8% Cost of Living Allowance (COLACost of Living Allowance) increase effective January 2026. We have incorporated the 2026 standards into the examples and Addendum 1. The COLACost of Living Allowance adjustment also causes:

- An increase in the income limit for Substantial Gainful Activity; and
- An increase in the student earned income exclusion.

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy and clarify existing policy.

Highlights of these policy revision are described below.  If you have any questions, contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[410-4(A)](../410/410-4_verification.md)          Interfaces that must be checked

- Changed WinStar to Alaska Child Support Enforcement Services System (ACSESS).

[410-7(C)](../410/410-7_initial_month_benefits.md#410_7_C__ELIGIBILITY_FOR_MEDICAID)          Eligibility for Medicaid

- Revised section and example to align with current [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance 30 day pend and reviewing for [MAGI](#)Modified Adjusted Gross Income expansion eligibility.

[432-4(L)6](../432/432-4_retained_cash_payments_and_in-kind_payments.md#432_4_L_6__Cash_distributions)          Cash Distributions

- Due to a new bill HR 42 revised section to include all [ANCSA](#)Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act corporation distributions, including distributions from [ANCSA](#)Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act Settlement trusts, received from July 7, 2025 - July 6, 2030 are excluded as income in the month of receipt and as a resource if retained for aged 65 or older, blind, or disabled individuals.

[433-1](../433/433-1_transfer_of_resources.md)          Transfer of Resources

- Updated all examples within the chapter to reflect the new 2026 standards.

[433-2(A)](../433/433-2_conditional_benefits.md#433_2_A_ELIGIBILITY_FOR_CONDITIONAL_BENEFITS)          Eligibility for Conditional Benefits

- Updated the [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income payment to the new 2026 amount in order to accurately calculate if an applicant is eligible for conditional benefits.

[433-4](../433/433-4_able_accounts.md)          [ABLE](#)Achieving a Better Life Experience Accounts

- [ABLE](#)Achieving a Better Life Experience Regulations change from age 26 to 46 effective 1/1/26.

[433-4](../433/433-4_able_accounts.md)          [ABLE](#)Achieving a Better Life Experience Accounts

- Updated 2026 Max Allowable contribution for certain [ABLE](#)Achieving a Better Life Experience account holders.

[441-1(E)(2)B](../441/441-1_earned_income.md#441_1_E_NET_EARNINGS_FROM_SELF_EMPLOYMENT)          Using Actuals as Cost of Doing Business

- Removed "Depreciation" from Examples of deductions not allowed.
- Added "Depreciation" to Examples of allowable deductions.

[442-3(A)8](../442/442-3_unearned_income_exclusions.md#442_3_A_8__Alaska_Native_Claims_Settlement_Act__ANCSA__payments)          Unearned Income Exclusions

- Due to a new bill HR 42 revised section to include all [ANCSA](#)Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act corporation distributions, including distributions from [ANCSA](#)Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act Settlement trusts, received from July 7, 2025 - July 6, 2030 are excluded as income in the month of receipt and as a resource if retained for aged 65 or older, for blind or disabled individuals.

[452-1(A)](../452/452-1_payment_calculations.md#452-1_A.____APA_PAYMENTS_)          [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance Payments

- Updated all examples in subsection to reflect the new 2026 standards.

[452-5(D)](../452/452-5_computation_of_benefits.md#452_5_D_ASSISTED_LIVING_HOME_PAYMENTS)          Assisted Living Home Payments

- Updated Assisted Living Home example to reflect the new 2026 standards.

[452-6](../452/452-6_issuance_of_apa_payments.md)          Issuance of [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance Payments

- Revised benefit delivery options for [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance to [EBT](#)Electronic Benefit Transfer and Direct Deposit.

[452-10(B)](../452/452-10_temporary_assistance_and_apa.md#452_10_B__TEMPORARY_ASSISTANCE_TO_APA_CONVERSIONS)          Temporary Assistance to [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance Conversions

- Updated examples in subsection to reflect the new 2026 standards.

[452-12](../452/452-12_retroactive_apa_benefit_calculations.md)          Retroactive [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance Benefit Calculations

- Updated example to reflect the new 2026 standards.

[460-5](../460/460-5_income_allocations.md)          Income Allocations

- Updated all examples within section to reflect the new 2026 standards.
