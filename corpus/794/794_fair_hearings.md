794            FAIR HEARINGS

Any [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program applicant or recipient whose application is not acted on within 30 days after its receipt, or who is affected by any adverse action taken on his or her case, may request a fair hearing. The fair hearing request must be made in writing to any employee of the Division.

For fair hearing availability, policies, and procedures, refer to Administrative Procedures [MS 117](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/admin/117/117_right_to_a_fair_hearing.htm).
