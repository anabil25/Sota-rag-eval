**MANUAL:**

**DATE:**

This
update added information regarding interfaces that can be used to verify
residency. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications
that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below.
If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development
team at d[papolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).
