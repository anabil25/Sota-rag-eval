**STATE
OF ALASKA**

**Department
of Health and Social Services**

**Division of Public
Assistance**

**TRANSMITTAL
NUMBER:**   2018-02

**MANUAL:**
  Alaska Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program

**DATE:**
  July 2, 2018

This
update clarified caseworker responsibilities when there is a work required
SNAP household member and replaced the term "semi-annual" with
"simplified reporting" throughout the manual. Other changes
are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results
of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below.
If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development
team at d[papolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

**OVERVIEW
OF CHANGES**

**[MS 602-1(H)](../../602/602-1_h.md)**CASEWORKER RESPONSIBILITIES - WORK REQUIREMENTS

- Clarified
  that the F001 must be sent at application and recertification
  when there is a work required SNAP household member, anytime a
  previously exempt individual becomes subject to work requirements,
  and anytime a work required household member moves into the SNAP
  household

**[MS 602-2(A)](../../602/602-2_a.md)**DEFINITION AND TYPES OF RESOURCES

- Clarified
  that loans, other than deferred repayment educational loans, are
  counted as a liquid resources the month after receipt if the money
  is retained as cash or in a bank account

**[MS 602-2(B)24](../../602/602-2_b.md)**EXEMPT RESOURCES

- Clarified
  that in order for resources held by recipients of TANF, SSI, or
  APA to be exempt, they must be solely owned by the TANF, SSI,
  or APA recipient

**[MS
602-3(B)6&7](../../602/602-3_b.md#602_3_B_6__Countable_Vendor_Payments)**UNEARNED INCOME

- Clarified
  that BIA GA payments made to the household or to a vendor for
  the household are considered countable unearned income unless
  received after the month of intended use

**[MS 602-3(D)30](../../602/602-3_d._exempt_income.md)**EXEMPT INCOME

- Added
  note to clarify that BIA GA payments made to the household or
  to a vendor for the household are considered countable unearned
  income unless received after the month of intended use. Only BIA
  Burial Assistance payments made to a vendor are exempt.

**[MS 602-4(D)](../../602/602-4_d.md)**MEDICAL DEDUCTION

- Replaced
  link to IRS Standard Mileage Rates in 602-4(D)1(h) for the current
  information

**[MS 604-3](../../604/604-3.md)**REPORTING AND ACTING ON CHANGES DURING
THE CERTIFICATION PERIOD

- Clarified
  that an EBT transaction report showing out of state usage may
  be an indicator that a household moved out of state but should
  not be used as the only basis for closure. We must request proof
  of residency.

**[MS 605-1(B)](../../605/605-1_b.md)            STUDENTS**

- Revised
  student eligibility section to match federal regulations for clarity
