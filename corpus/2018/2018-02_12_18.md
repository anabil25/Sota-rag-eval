**STATE OF ALASKA**

**Department of Health
and Social Services**

**Division of Public
Assistance**

**TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:**  2018-02

**MANUAL:**  Senior Benefit Program

**DATE:**  December 3, 2018

We are making changes to incorporate new policy,
clarify existing policy, and align policies within our program policy
manuals. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications
that are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these revisions are described below.
 If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program
Development Team by e-mail at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov)

[330-2G](../../330__income/330-2_countable_income.md#330-2G._____TITLE_V_WAGES_AND_SALARIES)
    TITLE V WAGES AND SALARIES

- Clarified that wages and salaries provided
  under Title V are countable income

[340-4](../../340__payment/340-4_death_of_recipient.md)
    DEATH OF A RECIPIENT

- Added a new section to clarify when a payment
  can be reissued after the death of a recipient
