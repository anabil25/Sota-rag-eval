123-1          DOLDepartment of Labor DATA INQUIRY SCREENS

Effective October 2014, DOLDepartment of Labor launched their new system called Secure Access Manger (SAMSecure Access Manager). The system is available to DPADivision of Public Assistance staff for accessing confidential State of Alaska Unemployment Insurance (UI) information such as wage history, potential unemployment insurance eligibility, and unemployment insurance benefit payments of public assistance applicants and recipients.

123-1 A.      CONFIDENTIALITY

Information needed for the administration of public assistance or for the Unemployment Insurance benefit program may be exchanged between DPADivision of Public Assistance and DOLDepartment of Labor offices without a specific authorization from the client to release the information. The same confidentiality rules which apply to public assistance case information apply to information obtained from SAMSecure Access Manager and directly from DOLDepartment of Labor offices.

123-1 B.      SECURITY ACCESS TO DOL DATA SYSTEM

DPADivision of Public Assistance staff must complete a DOLDepartment of Labor LAN/Mainframe Work Order (07-2015) and a DOLDepartment of Labor Confidentiality Acknowledgement (07-2016) to get access to the Department of Labor data inquiry screens.

1. Staff must contact their supervisors to obtain the [DOL](#)Department of Labor forms for completion. These forms can be obtained from an Administrative Assistant.
2. The supervisor or other designated person with authority must approve the completed forms to Systems Support at [hss.dpa.systems.support@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.systems.support@alaska.gov).
3. Systems Support will create a ticket for the DOLDepartment of Labor access request and send the completed forms to [DOL](#)Department of Labor for approval.
4. DPADivision of Public Assistance staff will be notified via email by [DOL](#)Department of Labor staff with a username and password when access is granted.

Refer to the [SAM User Guide](https://go.dhss.ak.local/dpa/ETResources/Shared Documents/Forms/AllItems.aspx) on [DPA SharePoint](https://sp.dhss.alaska.gov/dpa/ETResources/SitePages/Home.aspx) for information accessing and navigating the DOLDepartment of Labor Data System, SAMSecure Access Manager.
