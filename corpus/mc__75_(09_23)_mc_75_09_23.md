STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #75

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: September 1, 2023

The Alaska Temporary Assistance Program (ATAP) heating and non-heating utility standards change effective October 2023. The Eligibility Information System (EIS) will automatically apply these new standards when determining eligibility and benefit amounts beginning October 2023.

Highlights of all the policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 758-6 E](../758/758-6_income_of_ssi___apa_recipients.md#758-6_E._ATAP_APPLICANTS_AND_IA)      [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program APPLICANTS WITH [IA](#)Interim Assistance

- Added section that mirrors [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance manual section [426-9](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/apa/426/426-9_temporary_assistance_and_interim_assistance.htm) that explains how [IA](#)Interim Assistance income is counted for an [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program applicant and what happens to the [IA](#)Interim Assistance benefit once [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program is approved.

[MS 780-19 G](../780/780-19_shelter_allowance.md#780-19_G._HOMELESS_SHELTER_DEDUCTION)      HOMELESS SHELTER DEDUCTION

- Updated the homeless shelter deduction to $180 for [FFY](#)Federal Fiscal Year 2024.

[Addendum 1](../addenda/addendum_1_-_exempt_alaska_native_villages.md)      EXEMPT ALASKA NATIVE VILLAGES

- Updated the exempt Alaska Native villages based on the 2023 [DOL](#)Department of Labor and Workforce Development employment survey results.

[Addendum 2](../addenda/addendum_2_-_program_standards.md)      [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program INCOME AND PAYMENT STANDARDS

- Updated the heating and non-heating utility standards for [FFY](#)Federal Fiscal Year 2024.
