Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #7

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: July 2, 2018

The MAGI manual has been updated with a current image of the State of Alaska paystub from the Integrated Resource Information System (IRIS), as well as clarified other allowable income tax deductions, and updated the 2018 tax filing threshold for tax dependents.

If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[818](../../818_magi_medicaid_income/818_magi_medicaid_income.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Income

- Updated the Note for the 2018 tax filing threshold for tax dependents.

[Addendum 4](../../addenda/addendum-4_magi_medicaid_income_deductions.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Income Deductions

- Updated the State of Alaska paystub to the current version from the SOA Department of Administration Integrated Resource Information System.
- Clarified allowable moving expenses and the time line of allowable alimony deductions.
- Removed the "Tuition and Fees" deduction, which expired 12/31/2017.
