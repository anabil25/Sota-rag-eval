323            SOCIAL SECURITY NUMBER

A Social Security Number is an eligibility factor for Senior Benefits.  To meet this requirement, an applicant must provide a Social Security Number (SSN(Social Security Number)) or show that he or she has applied for one. If an individual does not provide a Social Security Number or show that he or she has applied for one, eligibility for Senior Benefits does not exist.
