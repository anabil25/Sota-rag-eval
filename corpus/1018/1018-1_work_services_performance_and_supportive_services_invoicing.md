1018-1          WORK SERVICES PERFORMANCE AND SUPPORTIVE SERVICE INVOICING

Work Services is Pay-for-Performance contract. Monthly performance and supportive services invoices are required. Contractors will adhere to the following invoicing procedures:

The invoice must:

- Reference the Contractor's name, address and phone number
- Reference the contract number
- Include an invoice number
- Reference the Alaska Division of Public Assistance
- Itemize the contractual services provided during the period invoiced

The Contractor shall submit invoices to the email specified below within 10 days of [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance's Research and Analysis posting of performance outcomes.  Failure to include the required information on the invoice may cause an unavoidable delay to the payment process.  The State will pay all invoices within thirty (30) days of invoice approval by the DPA Project Director.

Email invoices to:

[doh.dpa.admin.finance@alaska.gov](mailto:doh.dpa.admin.finance@alaska.gov)

Please reference the contract name and number in the email subject line.

Notwithstanding any other provision of the contract, it is understood and agreed that the State shall withhold payment any time the Contractor fails to perform work as required under Appendix C or D of their contract.

A detailed list in Excel format of supportive services spent for the month that documents the expenditure must be submitted to [dpa.policy@hss.soa.directak.net](mailto:dpapolicy@hss.soa.directak.net).  Supportive Services issued to Post-TA clients must be identified with the letter "P" preceding the supportive service code.  The following must be included:
