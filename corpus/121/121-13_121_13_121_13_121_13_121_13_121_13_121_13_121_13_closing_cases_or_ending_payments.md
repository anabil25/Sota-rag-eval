121-13        CLOSING
CASES OR ENDING PAYMENTS

A payment shall be terminated in the month following
a determination that a recipient is no longer eligible, providing that
the evidence notice requirements are met.

Certain cases may contain no one who is eligible
for cash payments but may still contain persons eligible for benefits
related to associated Medicaid; the case payment must be stopped but the
case left open. See the [Aged, Disabled and Long Term
Care Medicaid manual](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/adltc/adltc.htm) for additional information.
