121-2          WHO MAY AUTHORIZE PAYMENTS

Persons assigned to positions classified as Eligibility Technicians, Eligibility Supervisors, and specified Support Staff are the only persons allowed to determine eligibility, establish the amount of a grant, or authorize payments through [EIS](#)Eligibility Information System.
