Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2022-02

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: September 1, 2022

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have questions, please contact Division Operations Support Team at [hss.dpadost@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpadost@alaska.gov) or the Policy and Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 100-4](../100/100-4_civil_rights.md)      CIVIL RIGHTS

- Added the FNS process requirement to collect ethnic and racial data on SNAP applicants who do not self-identify

[MS 100-5 A](../100/100-5_availability_of_program_information.md#100-5_A._PROGRAM_POLICY_MANUALS)      PROGRAM POLICY MANUALS

- Updated the weblink for the program and policy manuals on the Public Assistance website because the old weblink had expired

[MS 100-5 D](../100/100-5_availability_of_program_information.md#100-5_D._LIMITED_ENGLISH_PROFICIENCY_(LEP))      LIMITED ENGLISH PROFICIENCY (LEP)

- Added information if DPA receives any emails, correspondence, forms, or documents written in a language other than English for translation, it should be sent to [hss.dpadost@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpadost@alaska.gov)

[MS 100-8 G](../100/100-8_health_insurance_portability_and_accountability_act_(hipaa).md#100-8_G._ASSISTING_AN_INDIVIDUAL_IN_FILING_A_HIPAA_COMPLAINT)      ASSISTING AN INDIVIDUAL IN FILING A HIPAA COMPLAINT

- Revised the steps for filing a HIPAA complaint

[MS 100-8 H](../100/100-8_health_insurance_portability_and_accountability_act_(hipaa).md#100-8_H._BREACH_OR_SUSPECTED_BREACH_OF_CONFIDENTIAL_INFORMATION)      BREACH OR SUSPECTED BREACH OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

- Revised the reporting process for potential HIPAA breaches

[MS 100-8 J](../100/100-8_health_insurance_portability_and_accountability_act_(hipaa).md#100-8_J._HIPAA_PRIVACY_OFFICERS)      HIPAA PRIVACY OFFICERS

- Updated the HIPAA privacy officer information

[MS 103-2 B](../103/103-2_identifiable_applications.md#103-2_B._NOTICE_AND_INTERVIEW_REQUIREMENT)      NOTICE AND INTERVIEW REQUIREMENT

- Added note explaining when to send an N000 or N004

[MS 105-16](../105/105-16_verification_of_virtual_currency_cryptocurrency.md)      VERIFICATION OF VIRTUAL CURRENCY/CRYPTOCURRENCY

- Added subsection regarding the procedure to calculate fair market value for virtual currency/cryptocurrency (i.e. Bitcoin)

[MS 106-2 B](../106/106-2_irs_unearned_income_match.md#106-2_B._CONFIDENTIALITY_AND_DISCLOSURE_OF_INFORMATION)     CONFIDENTIALITY AND DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION

- Added language that requires staff to wear identification badges when near federal tax information

[MS 106-5](../106/106-5_instant_eligibility_verification_system_(ievs).md)      INSTANT ELIGIBILITY VERIFICATION SYSTEM (IEVS)

- Clarified that the three parts of PARIS are: the Veterans Administration Match; Department of Defense/Office of Personnel Management Match (regarding active or retired military and Federal employees); the Interstate Match (duplicate payments made to the same client in more than one State)
- Clarified that Appriss felony data is considered verified upon receipt
- Revised by adding note stating that the only time minors are entered into IEVS is to receive SAVE verification. This is done at application and when an alien is being added to a case
- Added Alaska Health Analytics & Vital Records to the Data Source table

[MS 108-2 B](../108/108-2_content_of_a_notice.md#108-2_B._REQUIRED_NOTICE_CONTENT)      REQUIRED NOTICE CONTENT

- Clarified that the conversion factor should be included in the gross income calculation whenever possible and added an example

[MS 108-3 B](../108/108-3_guidelines_for_creating_the_free_form_section.md#108-3_B._INCLUDE_IMPORTANT_INFORMATION_SPECIFIC_TO_THE_ACTION_TAKEN_ON_THE_CASE_OR_ELIGIBILITY_DECISION)      INCLUDE IMPORTANT INFORMATION SPECIFIC TO THE ACTION TAKEN ON THE CASE OR ELIGIBILITY DECISION

- Revised subsection, due to CAPS errors, to clarify that when denying or closing a case for failure to provide, the caseworker should only list information in the denial/closure notice that is required to determine eligibility (not deductions)

[MS 108-5](../108/108-5_eis_notice_listing.md)      EIS NOTICE LISTING

- Added L100 and removed L018 and L704 based on 4/1/22 broadcast
- Added N004 (Notice of Flexible Interview) to the list of EIS notices
- Amended W419 to read "Case Closed - Client Deceased"

[MS 121-3 D](../121/121-3_to_whom_payments_will_be_made.md#121-3_D._CARE_OF__A_DPA_OFFICE)      "CARE OF" A DPA OFFICE AND MAILING BENEFITS

- Revised title to include Mailing Benefits
- Clarified that DPA must ensure all Medicaid cards are available and issued to eligible individuals and methods of receiving those cards

[MS 121-3 E](../121/121-3_to_whom_payments_will_be_made.md#121-3_E._PROHIBITION_AGAINST_DPA_EMPLOYEES)      PROHIBITION AGAINST DPA EMPLOYEES

- Clarified that a DPA employee cannot act as an authorized representative on a specific case they are directly involved in processing, authorizing, or recertifying benefits and/or case

[MS 121-14](../121/121-14_stop_payment_and_forgery_procedures.md)      STOP PAYMENT AND FORGERY PROCEDURES

- Updated email address to [hss.dpa.systems.support@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.systems.support@alaska.gov) since [eishelp@alaska.gov](mailto:eishelp@alaska.gov) is no longer valid
- Revised that staff will send all Stop Payment requests (GEN 21) via email to [hss.dpa.systems.support@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.systems.support@alaska.gov) since submitting them on the Systems Support website is no longer available

[MS 128-4 B](../128/128-4_refugee_assistance.md#128-4_B._REFUGEE_CASH_AND_MEDICAL_ASSISTANCE)      REFUGEE CASH AND MEDICAL ASSISTANCE

- Updated the eligibility period for Refugee Cash and Medical Assistance from eight months to twelve months per a change in federal rules
