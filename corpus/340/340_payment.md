340-1        PAYMENT LEVEL

An individual who is eligible for the Senior Benefits Program will receive $125, $175 or $250 each month as determined by their gross income level as shown in the chart in [SBP manual section 330-1](../330__income/330-1_income_limits.md).  This benefit cannot be assigned or garnished to satisfy a debt.
