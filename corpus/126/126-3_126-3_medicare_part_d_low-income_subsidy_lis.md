126-3          MEDICARE PART D LOW-INCOME SUBSIDY (LISLow-Income Subsidy)

The Social Security Administration (SSASocial Security Administration) has primary responsibility for determining eligibility for the Medicare Part D LISLow-Income Subsidy. The majority of these applications will be process at the Wilkes Barre Data Operations Center in Wilkes Barre, Pennsylvania. Some LISLow-Income Subsidy application may be sent to DPADivision of Public Assistance for processing.

126-3 A.      DPADivision of Public Assistance LISLow-Income Subsidy DETERMINATIONS

In the event a LISLow-Income Subsidy applicant insists on DPADivision of Public Assistance making the LISLow-Income Subsidy determination, staff must:

1. First, ensure the inquiring applicant is not a Medicaid recipient. If the person is already a Medicaid recipient, there is no need for the applicant to complete the LISLow-Income Subsidy application, as Medicaid recipients are automatically deemed eligible for the LISLow-Income Subsidy.
2. If the person is not on Medicaid, provide the applicant with the SSASocial Security Administration -1020B application form.
3. After the applicant completes the SSASocial Security Administration -1020B application form:

- Register it in EISEligibility Information System as a General Assistance case to the full service office 030 9 99.
- Upload the application to ILINX for statewide processing (refer to [Admin MS 104-1 C](../104/104-1_first_contact_resolution.md#104-1_C._APPLICATIONS__ADD-TO-PROGRAM_AND_REAPPLICATIONS__GEN_72_) about when applications are received by the office).

126-3 B.      LISLow-Income Subsidy CASE NUMBERING

- Only one person per case number.
- Register the application as a General Assistance (GAGeneral Relief Assistance) case.
- Couples can apply on the same application; however, each individual must have a separate case number.
