1006-1          REASONS FOR GOOD CAUSE

There are three specific areas where good cause must be explored:

- Failure to attend school, see [ATAP MS 723-1 A](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/723/723-1_good_cause.htm#723_1_a__good_cause_for_failure_to_attend_school);
- Not cooperating with [CSSD](#)Child Support Services Division, see [ATAP MS 723-1 B](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/723/723-1_good_cause.htm#723_1_b__good_cause_for_not_cooperating_with_csed);
- Non-compliance with the [FSSP](#)Family Self Sufficiency Plan or Work Activity requirements, see [ATAP MS 723-1 C](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/723/723-1_good_cause.htm#723_1_c__good_cause_for_non_compliance_with_the_fssp_or_work_activities_requirements).
