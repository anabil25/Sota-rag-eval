1006-3          SUPPORTING A GOOD CAUSE CLAIM

When a client has discussed a situation that may be considered good cause, the Work Services case manager will support the client in collecting the documentation necessary for explaining good cause. The caseworker will confirm whether their situation meets the criteria and that there is adequate documentation.

Case manager actions:

1. Enter a Client Note (CLNO) and Case Note (CANO) documenting the good cause reason.
2. Send the request to and supporting documentation through the [DPA Sucure Document Portal](https://dpaforms.ilinx.com/ILINXFlex/public-eform?param=WEUwT0U5d0g5QTV2aFZiZlI2Z0VEdyUzRCUzRCAg).

1. Email details:

- Subject Line: "Work Services"
- Body of email: Client ID, request, and CANO reference (e.g., "0600444444 - type of good cause begins 01/05/21 - see CANO dated 1/25/21")
