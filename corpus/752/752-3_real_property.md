752-3          REAL PROPERTY

All real property which is not the home, for sale or unsalable, jointly owned with an absent or non-agreeing co-owner, or otherwise exempt, will be counted against the resource limitation. The amount to be considered will be the owner's equity, which is defined as fair market value less legal encumbrances.
