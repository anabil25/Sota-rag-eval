Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #24

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: April 1, 2024

We have updated the income standards for the MAGI Medicaid categories that are effected by the change in Federal Poverty Guidelines that is effective April 1, 2024. We have also made several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field. An overview of the changes are listed below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov.](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov)

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[806-1](../../806_application_and_renewal_process/806-1_the_application.md#806-1_The_Application)    Acceptable Applications

- Updated section to include [VCC](#)Virtual Contact Center and removed outdated information.

[810](../../810_social_security_enumeration/810_social_security_enumeration.md)   Social Security Enumeration

- Removed SharePoint reference.

[812(B)](../../812_medical_support_assignment/812_medical_support_assignment.md#812_B.______WHO_IS_NOT_REQUIRED_TO_COOPERATE)    Who is not required to cooperate

- Revised note due to the postpartum period change.

[813(D)](../../813_development_of_income/development_of_income.md#813_D._______EXEMPTIONS_FOR_APPLYING_FOR_UIB)     Exemptions for Apply to [UIB](#)Unemployment Insurance Benefit

- Revised due to the postpartum period change.

[816-1(B)](../../816_magi_medicaid_categories/816-1_magi_category_eligibility_factors.md#816-1_B._PREGNANT_WOMEN)    Pregnant Women

- Extended the postpartum period from 60 days to 12 months effective 2/1/24.
- Revised section to remove reference to the requirement that the pregnant woman must have applied for and receiving Medicaid coverage on or before the date of termination of pregnancy.
- Clarified that an individual is not eligible for PW Medicaid at the 201 - 225 % income level if they are eligible for Medicaid in another category or if their dependent children (as defined in [MAGI MS 816-1 D](../../816_magi_medicaid_categories/816-1_magi_category_eligibility_factors.md#816-1_d._pARENT_CARETAKER_RELATIVE)) are not receiving Medicaid benefits or enrolled in minimum essential coverage.

[816-1(D)](../../816_magi_medicaid_categories/816-1_magi_category_eligibility_factors.md#816-1_d._pARENT_CARETAKER_RELATIVE)     Parent / Caretaker Relative

- Clarified that being eligible for [IHS](#)Indian Health Service services alone does not meet the minimum essential coverage requirement.

[816-1(F)](../../816_magi_medicaid_categories/816-1_magi_category_eligibility_factors.md#816-1_f._fORMER_FOSTER_CARE__FFC__)     Former Foster Care

- Revised second criteria "individuals who received state or tribal foster care in any state and was enrolled in Medicaid upon reaching age 18" and removed note.

[818](../../818_magi_medicaid_income/818_magi_medicaid_income.md)     [MAGI](#)Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Income

- Updated dependent tax filing thresholds.

[828-1](../../828_transitional_medicaid/828-1_transitional_medicaid_households.md#828-1_Transitional_Medicaid_Households)     Transitional Medicaid Households

- Revised example due to the postpartum period change.

[833 (C)](../../833_hospital_presumptive_eligibility/833_hosptial_presumptive_eligibility_determination.md#833_C._Subtypes)    Subtypes

- Updated the income standards due to the Federal Poverty Guidelines change effective April 1, 2024.

[Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum-1_magi_medicaid_income_standards.md)     [MAGI](#)Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Income Standards

- Updated the income standards due to the Federal Poverty Guidelines change effective April 1, 2024.

[Addendum 2](../../addenda/addendum-2_magi_medicaid_subtypes.md)     [MAGI](#)Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Subtypes

- Added a new Medicaid category for pregnant women who have countable income between 201 - 225% [FPL](#)Federal Poverty Limit.
