ADLTC - Alaska Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #70

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  December 1, 2025

This year Social Security announced a Cost of Living Allowance (COLACost of Living Allowance) increase effective January 2026. We have updated Addendum 1 and the examples throughout the manual with the following items to reflect the 2.8% increase for 2026:

- An increase to the Long Term Care community spouse resource and income allowance and household member standards;
- An increase to the Excess Home Equity resource maximum;
- An increase to the Special Long Term Care Income Standard;
- Changes in Medicare Part A and B premiums; and
- Changes in Medicare Part D premium and coverage information, including changes to the Low-Income Subsidy program

We have also made several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field. An overview of the changes are listed below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[506(A)](../../506_medicare/506_medicare.md#506_A_MEDICARE_ENROLLMENT)       Medicare Enrollment

- Revised the note clarifying if an individual applies for Medicare and is denied Part A because they would only be eligible if they pay for part A, Medicaid continues, and the caseworker must make the [QMB](#)Qualified Medicare Beneficiaries determination.

[520(D)](../../520_eligibility_requirements_that_follow_apa_policy/520_elig_req_apa_policy.md#520_D_Alaska_Native_Claims_Settlement_Act_(ANCSA)_payments_and_Assets)    Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act (ANCSA) Payments and Assets

- Due to a new bill HR 42 revised section to include:
- All [ANCSA](#)Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act distributions, including distributions from [ANCSA](#)Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act Settlement trusts, received from 7/7/25 - 7/6/30 are excluded as income in the month of receipt and as a resource if retained for aged 65 or older, blind, or disabled individuals.
- These distributions are excluded as income in Cost of Care determinations for Special Long Term Care Waiver and institutional cases for the above listed individuals.

[523(B)](../../523_medicaid_exceptions_to_apa_income_policy/523_medicaid_except_to_income.md#523_B_______REASONABLE_COMPATIBILITY)       Reasonable Compatibility

- Added note clarifying that "interface" should be used as verification in [EIS](#)Eligibility Information System for reasonable compatibility as client statement is not allowed for earned income.

[523(K](../../523_medicaid_exceptions_to_apa_income_policy/523_medicaid_except_to_income.md#523_K_DEVELOPMENT_OF_INCOME_REQUIREMENTS_DO_NOT_APPLY_TO_MEDICAID))       Development of Income

- Added subsection to clarify development of income requirements no longer apply to Medicaid.

[525(E)](../../525_general_medicaid_trust_policy/525_general_medicaid_trust.md#525_E__HARDSHIP_EXCEPTIONS)       Hardship Exceptions

- Clarified that a transfer of asset is the only hardship exception that can be allowed.

[528(G)](../../528_processing_cases_with_trusts/528_processing_cases_with_trusts.md#528_G.________TRUST_CHANGES)       Trust Changes

- Clarified the process for trustee changes.
- Court paperwork assigning new trustee must be sent to policy for approval.
- The Med 20 with new trustee information must be sent to [DHCS](#)Division of Health Care Services.
- If the original trust is amended it must be sent to policy for review and approval.

[554(D)](../../554_transfer_of_assets/554_transfer_of_assets.md#554_D_Look_Back_Period)       Look Back Period

- Updated example due to [COLA](#)Cost of Living Allowance change.

[570(E)3](../../570_post_eligibility_treatment_of_income_(coca)/570_post_eligibility_and_coc.md#570_E_3__Family_Member_Allowance)       Allowable Deduction for Cost of Care Determinations

- Updated example of [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [ANI](#)Adult Not Included standard due to [COLA](#)Cost of Living Allowance change.

[570(G)](../../570_post_eligibility_treatment_of_income_(coca)/570_post_eligibility_and_coc.md#570_G__RETROACTIVE_COST_OF_CARE_ADJUSTMENTS)       Retroactive Cost of Care Adjustments

- Clarified [COCA](#)Cost of Care understated liabilities can be captured in more than one month, such as when lump sum or sums were reported late.
- You can increase the cost of care for multiple months when multiple lump sums were received in separate months.

[570(H)](../../570_post_eligibility_treatment_of_income_(coca)/570_post_eligibility_and_coc.md#570_J__HOME_AND_COMMUNITY_BASED_WAIVER_CASE_EXAMPLES)       Nursing Home Examples

- Updated example due to [COLA](#)Cost of Living Allowance change.

[570(J)](../../570_post_eligibility_treatment_of_income_(coca)/570_post_eligibility_and_coc.md#570_J__HOME_AND_COMMUNITY_BASED_WAIVER_CASE_EXAMPLES)      Home and Community Based Waiver Case Examples

- Updated example due to [COLA](#)Cost of Living Allowance change.

[580(D)](../../580_medicare_savings_programs/580_medicare_premium_assistance.md#580_D_3__Subsidy_Levels)       Low Income Subsidy (LIS) Program

- Updated example due to [COLA](#)Cost of Living Allowance change.

[Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum_1.md)       Program Standards

- Updated example due to [COLA](#)Cost of Living Allowance change.
