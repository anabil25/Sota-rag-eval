324             U.S. CITIZENSHIP AND ELIGIBLE ALIEN STATUS

 To receive Senior Benefits, a person must be:

- - A United States citizen or U.S.  National;
  - A qualified alien, as described in section [324-2](324-1_qualified_aliens.md).  Qualified aliens may be subject to limited eligibility as explained in [section 324-4.](324-4.md)
  - A Native American born in Canada or Mexico who has treaty rights to cross the U.S.United States/Canada and U.S.United States /Mexico borders, as described in [section 324-3](324-2.md).
