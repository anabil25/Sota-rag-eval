STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  2024-02

MANUAL:  Senior Benefit Program

DATE:  September 3, 2024

We have updated the program standards with the new payment levels.

Highlights of these revisions are described below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy & Program Development Team by e-mail at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[330-1](../../330__income/330-1_income_limits.md)     INCOME LIMITS

- Updated payment level effective July 1 to $125 for recipients whose income falls between 100% and 175% of [FPL](2022-01_(04_22).md#)Federal Poverty Limits.
