250-2        HEARING
POLICY

Any applicant or recipient whose application
is not acted on within 30 days after its receipt , or who is affected
by any adverse action taken on his or her case, may request a fair hearing.
 The fair hearing request must be made in writing to any employee
of the Division.  For fair hearing availability, policy and procedures,
refer to Administrative Procedure Manual section 117.

Applicants or recipients of GRAGeneral Relief Assistance with unresolved GRAGeneral Relief Assistance
hearings may apply for GRAGeneral Relief Assistance
in a subsequent month.
