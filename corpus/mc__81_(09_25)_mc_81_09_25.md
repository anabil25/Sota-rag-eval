STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #81

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: September 2, 2025

The Alaska Temporary Assistance Program (ATAP) heating and non-heating utility standards change effective October 2025. The Eligibility Information System (EIS) will automatically apply these new standards when determining eligibility and benefit amounts beginning October 2025.

Highlights of all the policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 740-3(A)](../740/740-3_other_public_assistance_programs.md#740-3_A._SNAP_CATEGORICAL_ELIGIBILITY)        SNAP CATEGORICAL ELIGIBILITY

- Revised period of categorical eligibility after an [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program case closes from six to twelve months to align with the SNAP certification period.

[MS 780-11](../780/780-11_delivery_of_payment.md)            DELIVERY OF PAYMENT

- Revised for clarity

[MS 780-12](../780/780-12_restricted_payments.md)            RESTRICTED PAYMENTS

- Removed subsection as this information was added to MS 780-11

[MS 780-19(G)](../780/780-19_shelter_allowance.md#780-19_G._HOMELESS_SHELTER_DEDUCTION)       HOMELESS SHELTER DEDUCTION

- Updated the homeless shelter deduction for [FFY](#)Federal Fiscal Year 26

[A](../addenda/addendum_1_-_exempt_alaska_native_villages.md)[DDENDUM 1](../addenda/addendum_1_-_exempt_alaska_native_villages.md)       EXEMPT ALASKA NATIVE VILLAGES

- Updated the exempt Alaska Native villages based on the 2025 [DOL](#)Department of Labor and Workforce Development employment survey results

[ADDENDUM 2](../addenda/addendum_2_-_program_standards.md)        ATAP INCOME AND PAYMENT STANDARDS

- Updated the heating and non-heating utility standards for [FFY](#)Federal Fiscal Year 26
