Alaska Temporary Assistance Program Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #26

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: January 4, 2010

We are clarifying that verbal verification of self-employment income and expenses is allowable, although written verification is preferred if available. Verbal verification can be received from a collateral contact or the self-employed individual.

Other highlights to policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaskagov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaskagov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 705](../705/705_application_process.md)      Application Process

- Replaces the word "attend" with "participate" to reflect that face to face interviews are no longer required.

[MS 705-4](../705/705-4_verification.md)      Verification

- In the Direct Data Systems table, replaces the Automated Status Verification System (ASVS) with Verification Information System (VIS). The [VIS](#)Verification Information System is the online electronic verification process used by the SAVE Program.

[MS 713-9](../713/713-9_foreign_born_children_adopted_by_u.s.md)      Systematic Alien Verification for Entitlements (SAVE) Program

- Clarifies that [VIS](#)Verification Information System is the online electronic verification process used by the [SAVE](#)Systematic Alien Verification for Entitlements program

[MS 717-4](../717/717-4_failure_to_cooperate_with_csed.md)    Failure to Cooperate with [CSSD](#)Child Support Services Division

- Changes email address workers use to notify [CSSD](#)Child Support Services Division of a good cause determination to [cssdgoodcause@alaska.gov](mailto:cssdgoodcause@alaska.gov).

[MS 759-6](../759/759-6_income_from_self-employment.md)      Expenses Allowed as Costs of Doing Business

- Updates the mileage rates with new amounts

[MS 759-6 C](../759/759-6_income_from_self-employment.md#759-6_C._VERIFICATION_OF_SELF-EMPLOYMENT_INCOME_AND_EXPENSES)      Verification of Self-Employment Income and Expenses

- Clarifies that verbal verification of self-employment income and expenses is allowed.
