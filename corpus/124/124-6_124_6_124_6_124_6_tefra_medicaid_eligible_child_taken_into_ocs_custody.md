124-6          TEFRATax Equity and Fiscal Responsibility Act MEDICAID ELIGIBLE CHILD TAKEN
INTO OCSOffice of Children's Services CUSTODY

In the rare instance that a child receiving
TEFRATax Equity and Fiscal Responsibility Act Medicaid is taken
into custody, the child's TEFRATax Equity and Fiscal Responsibility Act
Medicaid case should be changed to a Title IV-E case. It is very important
for OCSOffice of Children's Services to communicate
with DPADivision of Public Assistance by emailing
DOSTDivision Operations Support Team when it is anticipated
the child will return home. DOSTDivision Operations Support Team
will notify the TEFRATax Equity and Fiscal Responsibility Act
caseworker to take appropriate action on the case. OCSOffice of Children's Services
will provide DPADivision of Public Assistance as much
notice as possible so that the TEFRATax Equity and Fiscal Responsibility Act
caseworker can review the past Medicaid case and determine what is needed
to re-open the TEFRATax Equity and Fiscal Responsibility Act
Medicaid case, such as a level of care determination by Comagine Health
or a disability decision by Disability Determination Service.

Refer to Administrative Procedures Manual [section
124-1](124-1_child_or_youth_taken_into_ocs_custody.md) for processing a TEFRATax Equity and Fiscal Responsibility Act
child taken into custody.
