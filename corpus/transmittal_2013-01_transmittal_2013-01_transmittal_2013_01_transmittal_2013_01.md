Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL: 2013-01

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: May 15, 2013

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised. Highlights of the revisions are described below.

If you have any questions, please contact Field Services at [dpafield@alaska.gov](mailto:dpafield@alaska.gov) or Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 100-6A](../100/100-6_interpreter_services.md#100-6_A._LIMITED_ENGLISH_SPEAKING_INDIVIDUALS)      DPA CONTRACTED TELEPHONE INTERPRETER SERVICE

- Updates the name and contact information for DPA's contracted telephone interpreter service.

[MS 100-8H](../100/100-8_health_insurance_portability_and_accountability_act_(hipaa).md#100-8_H._BREACH_OR_SUSPECTED_BREACH_OF_CONFIDENTIAL_INFORMATION)      BREACH OR SUSPECTED BREACH OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

- Clarifies the "received date" for breach or suspected breach of confidentiality.

[MS 101-7](../101/101-7_timeliness_standards.md)      TIMELINESS STANDARDS

- Clarifies what date of receipt for applications submitted in DPA offices, to fee agents and NFAP, and applications placed in drop boxes.

[MS 101-8](../101/101-8_registering_household_members_listed_on_the_application.md)      REGISTERING HOUSEHOLD MEMBERS LISTED ON THE APPLICATION

- Adds an exception to clarify that clerical staff are not required to add household members when registering a recertification.

[MS 102-2](../102/102-2_screening.md)      SCREENING

- Adds a note allowing clerical staff to screen GEN 50Bs for expedited service by reviewing the three questions on page A.

[MS 103-3D](../103/103-3_case_numbering_on_eis_and_aries.md#103-3_D._CASE_NUMBERING_AT_A_GLANCE)      CASE NUMBERING ON EIS

- Adds a note under Working Disabled cases for couples in which both individuals are eligible and each person needs their own case number.

[MS 103-6C](../103/103-6_retention_of_case_records.md#103-6_C._RECORD_STORAGE_PROCEDURES)      RECORD STORAGE PROCEDURES

- Updates the link for obtaining a copy of the Records Transfer List.

[MS 104-4](../104/104-4_adverse_action_calendar.md)      ADVERSE ACTION CALENDAR

- Updates the information available in the Adverse Action Calendar – 30 day pend deadline.

[MS 105-1B](../105/105-1_verification_of_information.md#105-1_B._MANDATORY_VERIFICATION)      MANDATORY VERIFICATION

- Corrects the information under the FS column in regards to requiring verification of residency.

[MS 105-2](../105/105-2_responsibility_for_providing_verfication.md)      RESPONSIBILITY FOR PROVIDING VERIFICATION

- Clarifies what "deadline date" means for accepting verification.

[MS 109](../109/109_case_note_documentation.md)      CASE NOTE DOCUMENTATION

- CANO Format: Adds instruction under Work Requirements/Disability/Penalties to document which track (FF/WF) would be assigned based on answers provided in the ITT.
- CANO Format with Prompts: Includes the same information added to CANO Format; deletes language that instructs staff to skip documenting residency and intent to stay in Alaska for FS-only cases; corrects the manual citing under MED from 109-H to 109-G.
- Addendum F: Adds WF and FF as commonly used acronyms.

[MS 116-2](../116/116-2_application_processing.md)      NFAP AND DPA CASE PROCESSING PROCEDURE

- Clarifies the procedure for handling TA applications received in areas served by NFAP.
