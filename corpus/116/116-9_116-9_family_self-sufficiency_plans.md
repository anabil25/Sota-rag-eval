116-9          FAMILY
SELF-SUFFICIENCY PLANS

Family Self-Sufficiency Plans do not transfer between
DPADivision of Public Assistance and an NFAPNative Family Assistance Program. FSSPFamily Self-Sufficiency Plans
need to be re-established to reflect the family's current situation and
to comply with the requirements of Alaska Temporary Assistance. Refer
to Alaska Temporary Assistance [MS 719](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/719/719_the_family_self-sufficiency_plan.htm) for further
guidance on Family Self-Sufficiency Plan.
