116-13         60-MONTH
TIME LIMIT

Countable months of assistance received under an
NFAPNative Family Assistance Program also count as months
of assistance for Alaska Temporary Assistance's 60-month time limit. When
a family that was previously eligible for NFAPNative Family Assistance Program
benefits applies for Alaska Temporary Assistance, the eligibility worker
must review the number of countable months of assistance received under
the NFAPNative Family Assistance Program program and
update the information on EISEligibility Information System,
if needed.
