116-3          VERIFICATION

Applications received from an NFAPNative Family Assistance Program
should include copies of documentation and verification the NFAPNative Family Assistance Program worker obtained from the applicant.
If additional information or verification is needed to process the application,
the DPADivision of Public Assistance eligibility worker
will contact the applicant to get this information.

DPADivision of Public Assistance
may accept and use information provided on NFAPNative Family Assistance Program
forms such as employment and income information, landlord statements,
shelter deductions, household composition, and child support.
