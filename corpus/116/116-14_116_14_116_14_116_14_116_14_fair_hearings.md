116-14         FAIR
HEARINGS

An NFAPNative Family Assistance Program
will use its own internal processes to conduct fair hearings.

If a client requests a fair hearing for an NFAPNative Family Assistance Program and other programs, DPADivision of Public Assistance will forward the NFAPNative Family Assistance Program fair hearing request to the appropriate
NFAPNative Family Assistance Program organization and
pursue the fair hearing request for other programs. Refer to [MS
117](../117/117_right_to_a_fair_hearing.md) for further guidance on fair hearing requests.
