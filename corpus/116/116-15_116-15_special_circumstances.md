116-15         SPECIAL
CIRCUMSTANCES

A family who is eligible to be served by a Native
Family Assistance Program may request to be served by Alaska Temporary
Assistance, when the family demonstrates "special circumstances"
exist. "Special circumstances" may exist when an individual
chooses not to claim a tribal affiliation, and has not previously received
tribal services. Refer to Alaska Temporary Assistance [MS
710-4](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/710/710-4_native_family_assistance_programs_and_eligibility_for_alaska_temporary_assistance.htm) for further guidance regarding special circumstances.
