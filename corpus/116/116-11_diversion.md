116-11         DIVERSION

Diversion payments issued by an NFAPNative Family Assistance Program
are disregarded for Alaska Temporary Assistance purposes. They will not
count toward the criteria of once a year up to a maximum of four diversion
determinations in a lifetime. Diversion income will only count if it was
issued in the month of application.
