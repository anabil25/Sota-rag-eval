116-10         OVERPAYMENTS
AND RECOUPMENTS

Active Alaska Temporary Assistance claims will not
transfer to an NFAPNative Family Assistance Program.
The NFAPNative Family Assistance Program organization
will use its own internal system to recoup overpayments.

Note:

EIS-user NFAPNative Family Assistance Program
Agency:

The NFAPNative Family Assistance Program worker and the
respective DPADivision of Public Assistance worker
will work together and develop a method to account for the recouped portion
used to correctly determine the SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program
eligibility and benefit amount.

In addition, if a case has been transferred to an NFAPNative Family Assistance Program,
and an Alaska Temporary Assistance Program error from previous month(s)
is discovered, DPADivision of Public Assistance is
still responsible for initiating action to correct the error. The NFAPNative Family Assistance Program worker will inform the respective
DPADivision of Public Assistance staff of the error.
DPADivision of Public Assistance will follow Alaska
Temporary Assistance policy for correcting payments. Refer to Alaska Temporary
Assistance [MS 792](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/792/792_incorrect_payments.htm) for further guidelines.
