STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2023-01

MANUAL: Heating Assistance Program

DATE: April 3, 2023

Several sections of the Heating Assistance Program Policy Manual have been revised in order to incorporate new policy, clarify existing policy, and align policies within our division's policy manuals.

If you have questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov.](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov)

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 3001-4C](../3001/3001-4_verification.md#3001-4_C._HEAT_OR_RENT_COST)        HEAT OR RENT COST

- Added examples to assist caseworkers in identifying when a client is paying for heat indirectly through rent.

[MS 3002-1A](../3002/3002-1_households.md#3002-1_A._TYPES_OF_HOUSEHOLDS)       TYPES OF HOUSEHOLDS

- Replaced obsolete housing category codes with current [ECOS](#)Energy Community Online System codes.

[MS 3003-1B](../3003/3003-1_countable_income.md#3003-1_B._UNEARNED_INCOME)       UNEARNED INCOME

- Added language to clarify how Native dividends are counted.

[MS 3010](../3010/3010_reserved.md)            PERFORMANCE MEASURES

- Updated to include explanation of what the performance measures are and the importance of collecting all client data required by [LIHEAP](#)Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program funding source, Administration of Children and Families (ACF).

[MS 3011](../3011/3011_fraud.md)            FRAUD

- Created subsections for client and vendor fraud.
