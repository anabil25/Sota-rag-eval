Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #14

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: December 1, 2020

We have made several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field. An overview of the changes are listed below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[806-2](../../806_application_and_renewal_process/806-2_actions_taken_on_the_application.md) Actions Taken on the Application

- Removed section 806-2 G and information regarding the E-14 waiver as it has expired

[806-3](../../806_application_and_renewal_process/renewal_requirements.md) Renewal Requirements

- Removed section 806-3 A and information regarding the E-14 waiver as it has expired

[815 D](../../815_residents_of_institutions/815_residents_of_institutions.md#815_D._______PENAL_INSTITUTION) Penal Institution

- Added clarification that the Medicare Savings Program is not requested when a Medicaid recipient is incarcerated

[818](../../818_magi_medicaid_income/818_magi_medicaid_income.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Income

- Added clarification regarding the tax dependent threshold for non-tax filers

[823-1 B](../../823_verification_and_documentation/823-1_sources_of_electronic_verification.md#823-1_B._____OTHER_ELECTRONIC_DATA_SOURCES) Other Electronic Data Sources

- Updated references of NSTARNorthern Support Through Automated Resources to the new system WinStarState of Alaska Child Support Services Division System
- Added information regarding AVSAsset Verification System/EVSElectronic Verification System

[Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum-1_magi_medicaid_income_standards.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Income Standards

- Updated the Parent and Caretaker Relatives and Under 21 standards due to the 1.3% COLACost of Living Allowance increase.
