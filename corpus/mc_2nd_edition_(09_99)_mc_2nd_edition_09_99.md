MEMORANDUM

DATE: September 1, 1999

TO: Temporary Assistance Manual Holders

FROM: Policy and Program Development Team

SUBJECT: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program Manual, 2nd Edition

We are pleased to present you with the 2nd edition of the Temporary Assistance manual. Several changes have been made in this edition of the manual to make it clearer and more usable.

The most noticeable change is that the chapter and page numbering systems have been made consistent with other Division policy manuals. The chapters are now numbered in the 700 series, and the page numbers are in the letter-number format.

Additionally, there has been a major reorganization of information regarding penalties. All information about penalties, including information about good cause reasons, has been consolidated in a single chapter. This change has affected seven chapters as follows:

Chapter IV-7 (new Chapter 717) Child Support

Sections IV-7.5 (Good Cause) and IV-7.6 (Non-Cooperation Penalty Provisions) have been removed from this chapter. This material is now located in the [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 723-2](../723/723-2_beginning_a_penalty.md).

Chapter IV-12 (new Chapter 722) Job Refusal or Job Quit

Sections IV-12.4 through IV-12.7 (Penalty Period through Good Cause) have been removed from this chapter. This material is now located in [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 723-5](../723/723-5_other_penalty_information.md).

Chapter IV-13 (new Chapter 723) Penalties

This chapter now contains general penalty provisions and specific information about each type of [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program penalty.

Chapter V-8 (new chapter 737) Attendance and Performance

This chapter now contains information about non-compliance with work activities that was previously Section V-9.1 (Conciliation). The policy in this chapter has been reorganized and expanded.

Chapter V-9 (New chapter 738) Non-Compliance

This chapter has been reserved in the second edition of the Temporary Assistance Manual. Section V-9.1 (Conciliation) is now located in [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program MS 737-3. Sections V-9.2 (Good Cause Reasons), V-9.3 (Mandatory Penalty Periods) and V-9.4 (Lifting a Penalty) are now located in [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 723-4](../723/723-4_amount_of_reduction_in_assistance.md).

Chapter VII-1 (new chapter 770) Special Types of Case Processing

Section VII-1.3 H (School Attendance Good Cause Determination) has been removed from this chapter. This information is now located in [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 723-3](../723/723-3_ending_a_penalty.md).

Chapter XII-1 (new chapter 792) Incorrect Payments

Section XII-1.3 E (Disqualification Penalties) and Sections XII-1.3 G through XII-1.3 J (Disqualification Notice Requirements through Other Factors) have been removed from this chapter. This information is now located in [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 723-1](../723/723-1_good_cause.md) and [723-6](../723/723-6_reserved.md).

The new edition also contains a number of policy clarifications, which are detailed in the chart below.

We welcome your comments on the new edition of the manual and any suggestions you have for further policy clarification. Please send your comments, suggestions, and any questions you have to the Policy and Program Development Team at HPAPOLY or call us at 465-3347.
