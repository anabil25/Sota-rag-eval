715-3          UNBORN CHILDREN

For [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program eligibility purposes, a pregnant woman in her last trimester is considered to have a child who meets the age requirement. Unborn children are not eligible for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program in their own right until they are born.
