715-1          CHILDREN UNDER AGE 18

Except as provided in [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 715-2](715-2_children_age_18.md) below, a dependent child must be under the age of 18 to qualify for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program. Eligibility continues through the month in which the child reaches age 18.
