STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2025-01

MANUAL: Heating Assistance Program

DATE: April 1, 2025

Sections of the Heating Assistance Program Policy Manual have been revised in order to incorporate new policy and clarify existing policy.

If you have questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov.](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov)

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

THROUGHOUT THE MANUAL

- Changed Child Support Services Division (CSSD) to Child Support Enforcement Division (CSED) due to their name change

[3001-4(H)](../3001/3001-4_verification.md#3001-4_H_INFORMATION_FROM_DATA_SYSTEMS_AND_EIS_INTERFACES)         INFORMATION FROM DATA SOURCES AND EIS INTERFACES

- Revised section for clarity

[3002-1(B)](../3002/3002-1_households.md#3002-1_B._RESIDENCY)         RESIDENCY

- Clarified when caseworkers should consider Alaska residency questionable

[3002-1(D)](../3002/3002-1_households.md#3002-1_D._CITIZENSHIP_AND_ELIGIBLE_ALIEN_STATUS)         CITIZENSHIP AND ELIGIBLE ALIEN STATUS

- Revised to clarify that alien status documentation must be validated through [SAVE](#)Systematic Alien Verification for Entitlements in [IEVS](#)Instant Eligibility Verification System

[3004-5](../3004/3004-5_grant_computation.md)              GRANT COMPUTATION

- Clarified that [ECOS](#)Energy Community Online System calculates the points and that any point adjustments noted in the manual are only used for manual calculations

[3008-4(A)](../3008/3008-4_methods_of_payment.md#3008-4_A._VENDOR_PAYMENT)          VENDOR PAYMENT

- Clarified that clients are not allowed to change vendors after benefit issuance unless they have moved out of their current vendor's service area, their primary fuel type has changed due to a change in residence, or their primary heating system has become inoperable

[3008-6](../3008/3008-6_grant_transfers.md)               GRANT TRANSFERS AND REFUNDS

- Clarified that clients are not allowed to change vendors after benefit issuance unless they have moved out of their current vendor's service area, their primary fuel type has changed due to a change in residence, or their primary heating system has become inoperable

[Addendum B](../addendum/addendum_b.md)    TRIBAL PROVIDERS

- Updated contact information for Tlingit-Haida Regional Housing Authority (THRHA)
