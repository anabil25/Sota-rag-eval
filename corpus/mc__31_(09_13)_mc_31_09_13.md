ADLTC - Alaska Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE
OF ALASKA

Department of Health
and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #31

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled
and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  September 30, 2013

We
are making several changes to incorporate new policies, clarify existing
policies, and align policies with those in the Family Medicaid Manual.
Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are
the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.  This
manual change reflects the updated Medicaid policies, as well as corrects
some errors from past manual changes.

Highlights
of these revisions are described below.  If you have any questions
please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or
e-mail [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW
OF CHANGES

[MS 500](../../500/500.md)      OVERVIEW OF AGED, DISABLED AND LONG TERM CARE MEDICAID

- Correct
  the state regulation citations to eliminate references to repealed
  Medicaid regulations at Title 7 Chapter 43.

[MS 501 A](../../501/501_medicaid_recipient_id_card.md)  ISSUING
MEDICAID TO ELIGIBLE RECIPIENTS

- Adds
  information about new Denali and Denali KidCare ID Cards that will
  be issued to all Medicaid recipients.

[MS
513 A](../../513/513_alaska_residency.md#513_A_DEFINITION_OF_ALASKA_RESIDENCY)  DEFINITION
OF ALASKA RESIDENCY

- Changes
  the definition of Alaska residency.

[MS
520 B](../../520/520_elig_req_apa_policy.md#520_B__U_S__CITIZENSHIP_AND_ELIGIBLE_ALIEN_STATUS)  **PROOF
OF
UNITED STATES CITIZENSHIP AND QUALIFIED
ALIEN STATUS**

- Combines
  former manual sections 520-B and 520-C into single manual section
  at 520-B to coordinate with similar changes in the Family Medicaid
  manual.
- Allows
  copies of copies of original documents to be accepted as documentary
  proof.
- Presents streamlined
  documentary verification requirements for U.S. citizenship and identity.

[MS
520 C](../../520/520_elig_req_apa_policy.md#520_B__U_S__CITIZENSHIP_AND_ELIGIBLE_ALIEN_STATUS)  **REASONABLE
OPPORTUNITY PERIOD**

- Adds
  revised policy that Medicaid benefits are available to persons claiming
  to be U.S. citizens, U.S. nationals, or qualified aliens during a
  90-day reasonable opportunity period if their citizenship when status
  cannot be promptly verified through an electronic data match or documentary
  proof.

[MS 520 H](../../520/520_elig_req_apa_policy.md#520_H__NOTICES)
  NOTICES

- Changes
  the linked manual section from APA Manual section 481 to Family Medicaid
  manual section 5006.

[MS
560 E](../../560/560_hcb_waiver.md#560_E__HCB_WAIVER_REFERRALS_AND_INTERAGENCY_COMMUNICATIONS)    HCB WAIVER TYPES

- Add
  to note that before DPA can copy Care Coordinators on all notices,
  we must have a signed Release of Information.
