731            WORK: ASSESSMENT AND ASSIGNMENT

Assessment is the process of examining characteristics affecting the participant's employability and determining their need for services to achieve self-sufficiency.

After making an initial assessment of their employability, the case manager assigns the participant to work activities. Participants must be informed in writing of assigned work activities or changes to assigned work activities on the [FSSP](#)Family Self-Sufficiency Plan. Refer to [WSPM](#)Work Services Procedure Manual [MS 1001](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/workservices1/1001/1001_engagement_and_assessment.htm?rhtocid=_2_0) for additional information on assessments.
