731-1          INITIAL ASSESSMENT

The information needed to conduct the initial assessment is collected by the case manager. The Employment Planning Information (EPI) form ([TA 5](#)Employability Assessment) is used to screen for:

- Domestic Violence
- Substance Abuse
- Criminal Record
- Physical or Mental Illness
- Labor Market Information (LMI)
