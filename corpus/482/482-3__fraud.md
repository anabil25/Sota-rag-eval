482-3        FRAUD

If the case worker has reason to believe that a recipient has received APAAdult Public Assistance benefits that he or she is not entitled to because the recipient intentionally withheld, concealed, or misrepresented information, a fraud referral may be appropriate. When APAAdult Public Assistance fraud is suspected, refer to the Administrative Procedures Manual [section 112](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/admin/112/112_fraud_control.htm) for proper procedures for fraud referrals.
