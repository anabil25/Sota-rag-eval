482           CLAIMS

An APAAdult Public Assistance recipient may occasionally receive more or less assistance than he or she is entitled to. This chapter contains APAAdult Public Assistance policy regarding overpayment or underpayment situations.

Note:  

Unintentional household error and agency error claims for APAAdult Public Assistance cases do not have a limit to how far back a claim can be established. Claims should be established beginning the date the error originally occurred
