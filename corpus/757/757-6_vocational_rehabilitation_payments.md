757-6          VOCATIONAL REHABILITATION PAYMENTS

Payments made by the:

- U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), or
- Division of Vocational Rehabilitation (DVR), Alaska Department of Education

for vocational rehabilitation are considered complimentary program benefits and are disregarded as income.

Note:

Any [VA](#)Department of Veterans Affairs payment which is not for vocational rehabilitation is considered unearned income. [VA](#)Department of Veterans Affairs educational assistance is treated as educational income and certain educational costs may be allowed as deductions (see [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 758-3 B](../758/758-3_education_assistance.md#758-3_B._OTHER_EDUCATION_ASSISTANCE)).
