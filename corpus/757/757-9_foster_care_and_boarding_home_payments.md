757-9          FOSTER CARE AND BOARDING HOME PAYMENTS

Any foster care or adult foster care payment made by any division of the Department of Health (DOH) to a [TA](#)Temporary Assistance caretaker relative in order to care for a foster child or foster adult who is not receiving [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program benefits will not be counted as income in determining eligibility or payment amounts. A child cannot be included in the assistance unit and be receiving Title IV-E Foster Care or State-funded foster care benefits for the same period.

Any boarding home payment made by the state, local government, or school board to a [TA](#)Temporary Assistance caretaker relative in order to care for a non-[ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program eligible student while that student is attending school will not be counted as income.
