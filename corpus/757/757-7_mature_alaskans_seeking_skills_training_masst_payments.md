757-7          MATURE ALASKANS SEEKING SKILLS TRAINING (MASST) PAYMENTS

Allowances, earnings, and payments from an employer and funded by the [MASST](#)Mature Alaskans Seeking Skills Training program are exempt income. The [MASST](#)Mature Alaskans Seeking Skills Training program, federally known as the Senior Community Service Employment Program (SCSEP), is administered under Title V of the Older Americans Act, P.L. 100-175.
