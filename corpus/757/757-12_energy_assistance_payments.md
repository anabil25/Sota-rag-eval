757-12        ENERGY ASSISTANCE PAYMENTS

All payments made to or on behalf of an [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program applicant or recipient by the Division's Energy Assistance Program are completely disregarded as income, including annual direct cash payments to [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program recipients. Energy assistance monthly payments made by the Alaska Housing Finance Corporation (AFHC) to its tenants are disregarded as income.
