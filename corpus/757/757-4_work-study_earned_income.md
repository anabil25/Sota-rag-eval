757-4          WORK-STUDY EARNED INCOME

College or university students may be enrolled in a work-study program in which they attend school part-time and work part-time at a job. Any income an applicant or recipient earns from employment in a work-study program will not be counted as income for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program purposes.

This disregard applies to both TA adults and children. The caseworker must verify that part-time employment of an applicant or recipient who claims to be in a work-study program is in fact a work-study job and not individually acquired regular part-time job.
