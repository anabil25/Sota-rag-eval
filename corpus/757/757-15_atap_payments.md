757-15        [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program PAYMENTS

Any [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program retroactive corrective benefit payment is disregarded as income in the month it is received. Regular ongoing benefit payments are not counted as [TA](#)Temporary Assistance income.
