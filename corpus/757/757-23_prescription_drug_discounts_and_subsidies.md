757-23        PRESCRIPTION DRUG DISCOUNTS AND SUBSIDIES

Some individuals may qualify for discounts or subsidies to help them with the cost of prescription drugs. These include the Medicare-approved prescription drug discount card and annual credit to be used for drug purchases, the Medicare Part D Low-Income Subsidy, and the former Alaska SeniorCare Program prescription drug benefit. These are exempt income for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program.
