757-16        [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program SUPPORTIVE SERVICE PAYMENTS

Payments made for supportive services under the [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program are exempt regardless of whether they are paid to a vendor or directly to the client. This includes payments made for child care, transportation, work-related expenses, and any other [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program-related supportive services. Such payments do not need to be verified unless the source is questionable.
