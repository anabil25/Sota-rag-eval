757-11        EXPERIMENTAL HOUSING ALLOWANCE PAYMENTS

The Experimental Housing Allowance Program (EHAP) provides long-range housing assistance to low income families in 12 states (not Alaska). [EHAP](#)Experimental Housing Allowance Program payments to applicants and recipients are disregarded.
