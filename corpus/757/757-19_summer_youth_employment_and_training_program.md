757-19        SUMMER YOUTH EMPLOYMENT AND TRAINING PROGRAM

Any income earned from participation in the Summer Youth Employment and Training Program operated under the Workforce Innovation & Opportunity Act (WIOA) of 2014 is exempt as income.
