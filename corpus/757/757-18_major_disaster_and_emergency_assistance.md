757-18        MAJOR DISASTER AND EMERGENCY ASSISTANCE

Any federal major disaster emergency assistance or any comparable disaster assistance provided by states, local governments, or disaster assistance organizations are exempt. Verification is not required unless the source of the funds is questionable.
