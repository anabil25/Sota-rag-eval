ADLTC - Alaska Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #50

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  April 1, 2019

We have updated the Federal Poverty Level guidelines for income eligibility.  
We are making several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.
 

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at  [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:DPAPolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 506-C](../../506/506_medicare_buy_in.md#505_C_MEDICARE_CARD)  Medicare Card

- Updated section with the new Medicare Card that was revised in 2018

[MS 520-N](../../520/520_elig_req_apa_policy.md#520_N.______INELIGIBLE_SPOUSE_RETIREMENT_FUND)  Ineligible Spouse Retirement Fund

- Added new subsection explaining that an ineligible spouse's retirement fund is an exempt resource

[MS 528-A](../../528/528_processing_cases_with_trusts.md#528_A_INITIAL_CASE_PROCESSING)  Initial Case Processing

- Updated #2 to state Policy will email the specific caseworker who sent in the trust along with their Eligibility Office Manager and Regional Manager when approving or denying trusts

[MS 534-K](../../534/534_working_disabled_medicaid_buy-in.md#534_K__EXAMPLE_OF_AN_ELIGIBILITY_DETERMINATION)  Example of an Eligibility Determination

- Updated this section to reflect the 2019 Federal Poverty Level changes

[MS 534-M](../../534/534_working_disabled_medicaid_buy-in.md#534_M__EXAMPLE_OF_AN_ELIGIBILITY_DETERMINATION_WITH_A_MEDICAID)  Example of an Eligibility Determination with a Medicaid Qualifying Trust

- Updated this section to reflect the 2019 Federal Poverty Level changes

MS 561-F  APDD, IDD, or ALI Waivers with APA Cash Assistance

- Added eligibility codes 71 & 74 to the 21 and over in the table as the IDD waiver does not have an age limit

[MS 562-F](../../562/562_processing_hcb_waiver.md#562_F.______Processing_HCB_waiver_cases_for_Title_IV-E_Eligible_Individuals__)   HCB Waiver Cases for a Women Receiving Breast and Cervical Cancer Medicaid

- Added Ladies First in parenthesis after BCHC in the title of this section as Alaska changed the name of the program in 2018 (The Federal Government has not changed the name of the BCHC program)

[MS 570](../../570/570_post_eligibility_and_coc.md)       Post Eligibility and Cost of Care

- Clarified that cost of care is determined using total gross income minus allowable deductions and disregards

[MS 570-A](../../570/570_post_eligibility_and_coc.md#570_A__DEFINITIONS)    Definitions

- Added to Total Gross Income definition, including money deposited in a Medicaid Qualifying Trust

[MS 575](../../575/575_breast_and_cervical_cancer.md)       BCHC Screening

- Added Ladies First in parenthesis after BCHC in the title of this section as Alaska changed the name of the program in 2018 (The Federal Government has not changed the name of the BCHC program)
- Added that Alaska updated the BCHC program to be called "Ladies First" in 2018

[MS 575-B](../../575/575_breast_and_cervical_cancer.md#575_B.______APPLICATION)     Application

- Updated LTC office location from suite 410 to 120

[Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum_1.md)

- Updated with 2019 FPL Changes

[Addendum 2](../../addenda/addendum_2.md)

- Added Ladies First to BCHC (Breast and Cervical Health Check)
- Updated QM & SL resource limit

[Addendum 8](../../addenda/addendum_8.md)     III. 1619B Eligibility

- Updated where to find the 1619B list in Luminist due to a program change from Document Direct to Luminist
