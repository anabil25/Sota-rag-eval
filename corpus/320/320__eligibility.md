320                 FACTORS OF ELIGIBILITY

To be eligible for Senior Benefits, an individual must meet the following eligibility criteria:

- Age;
- Alaska Residency;
- US Citizenship or Qualified Alien status;
- Social Security Enumeration; and
- Financial Need.

Reminder:

Senior Benefits does not have a resource limit.
