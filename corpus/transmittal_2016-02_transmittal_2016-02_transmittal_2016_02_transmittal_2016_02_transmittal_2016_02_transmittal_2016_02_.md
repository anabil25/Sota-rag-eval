Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2016-02

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: June 1, 2016

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised. Highlights of the revisions are described below.

If you have any questions, please contact Field Services at [dpafield@alaska.gov](mailto:dpafield@alaska.gov) or the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

100      General Information

[100-3H](../100/100-3_confidentiality.md#100-3_H._REQUESTS_FOR_INFORMATION_FROM_OTHER_AGENCIES) Requests for Information from Other Agencies

- Replaces the name Maxium to Alaska Family Services as one of the agencies that has a grant agreement with the Division to access client information.

103      Casefiles

[103-6C](../103/103-6_retention_of_case_records.md#103-6_C._RECORD_STORAGE_PROCEDURES) Records Storage Procedures

- Clarifies sending records to AAA Archives to Juneau.

108      Notices

[108-6A](../108/108-6_aries_correspondences.md#108-6_A._LEGAL_CITATION_IN_GENERAL_CORRESPONDENCE) Legal Citation in General Correspondence

- Adds that legal citation must be included when sending General Correspondence in ARIES specific to each program.

117      Right to a Fair Hearing

[117-1D](../117/117-1_fair_hearing_requst.md#117-1_D._TIME_LIMITS_TO_REQUEST_A_FAIR_HEARING) Time Limits to Request a Fair Hearing

- Clarifies when the division must receive the request for fair hearings.
