Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #17

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: December 1, 2021

This year Social Security announced a 5.9% [COLA](#)Cost of Living Allowance increase that effects the Parent/Caretaker Relative and Under 21 Medicaid categories starting in January of 2022. We have also made several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field. An overview of the changes are listed below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[806](../../806_application_and_renewal_process/application_and_renewal_process.md)   Application Process

- Clarified that telephonically recorded application signatures are accepted through the Virtual Call Center (VCC)
- Revised note to clarify that for paper applications, the signature must either be telephonically recorded by a caseworker through the [VCC](#)Virtual Contact Center or a "wet signature"

[806-3](../../806_application_and_renewal_process/renewal_requirements.md)   Renewal Requirements

- Added link to MS 806 regarding accepting a telephonic application and telephonically recorded application signature

[808](../../808_us_citizenship_and_eligible_alien_status/808_us_citizenship_and_eligibile_alien_status.md)   US Citizenship and Eligible Alien Status

- Added [COFA](#)Compact of Free Association Migrants as a group eligible for Medicaid benefits

[808-5(B)](../../808_us_citizenship_and_eligible_alien_status/808-5_non_qualified_aliens.md#808-5_B.___NON-IMMIGRANTS)   Non-Immigrants

- Clarified that although a [COFA](#)Compact of Free Association migrant is exempt from the five-year waiting period, if their status changes to a qualified alien as outlined in MS 808-2, the five-year waiting period will apply unless they meet an exemption listed in MS 808-4(D)

[810](../../810_social_security_enumeration/810_social_security_enumeration.md)   Social Security Enumeration

- Added link to new addendum 7 for the notice template when they must request an [SSN](#)Social Security Number from the [SSA](#)Social Security Administration

[818](../../818_magi_medicaid_income/818_magi_medicaid_income.md)   MAGI Medicaid Income

- Revised note to clarify that when using non-filer rules, a child's income is excluded unless it is above the tax filing threshold

[823-1(B)](../../823_verification_and_documentation/823-1_sources_of_electronic_verification.md#823-1_B._____OTHER_ELECTRONIC_DATA_SOURCES)   Other Electronic Data Sources

- Added note referring caseworkers to Admin MS 106-5 for additional information regarding [IEVS](#)Instant Eligibility Verification System and which data sources are considered "verified upon receipt"

823-1(C)   Secondary Sources of Verification 

- Removed subsection as APPRISS and [PARIS](#)Public Assistance Reporting Information System are now available in [IEVS](#)Instant Eligibility Verification System

[833(C)](../../833_hospital_presumptive_eligibility/833_hosptial_presumptive_eligibility_determination.md#833_C._Subtypes)   Hospital Presumptive Eligibility Subtypes

- Added revised [MIBW](#)Medical Insurance Budget Worksheet Subtype Worksheet to include the Parent/Caretaker Relative and Under 21 standards as of January 2022

[Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum-1_magi_medicaid_income_standards.md)   MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Income Standards

- Updated the Parent and Caretaker Relatives and Under 21 standards due to the 5.9% COLACost of Living Allowance increase

[Addendum 2](../../addenda/addendum-2_magi_medicaid_subtypes.md)   MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Subtypes

- Removed Medicaid Subtype of "EO" (Eligible for FM if not Institutionalized) as this is no longer valid
- For the Medicaid Subtypes of AL and EI, revised terminology to "ineligible non-immigrant" due to [COFA](#)Compact of Free Association Migrants

Addendum 7   Social Security Enumeration Request Letter Template

- Added a new addendum for the social security enumeration request letter template
