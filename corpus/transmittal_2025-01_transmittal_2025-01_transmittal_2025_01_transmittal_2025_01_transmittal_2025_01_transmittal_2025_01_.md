Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2025-01

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: April 1, 2025

A few sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have questions, please contact Division of Operations Support team at [hss.dpadost@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpadost@alaska.gov) or the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

ENTIRE MANUAL

- Changed Child Support Services Division (CSSD) to Child Support Enforcement Division (CSED)

[MS 100-3 I](../100/100-3_confidentiality.md#100-3_I._GUARDIANSHIP_AND_CONSERVATORSHIP)      GUARDIANSHIP AND CONSERVATORSHIP

- Clarified that the payee fields on the ADDR screen must be completed for individuals that have full guardianship or conservatorship so that benefits cannot be paid to the individual

[MS 100-6 A](../100/100-6_interpreter_services.md#100-6_A._LIMITED_ENGLISH_PROFICIENCY_(LEP))      LIMITED ENGLISH PROFICIENCY ([LEP](#)Limited English Proficiency)

- Replaced TeleLanguage interpreter with Language Link (effective 8/1/24)

[MS 108-5](../108/108-5_eis_notice_listing.md)      EIS NOTICE LISTING

- Added new processing delay notices
- Added ESAP notices:

- F113 ‒ SNAP ESAP APPLICATION APPROVED
- F120 ‒ SNAP ESAP RECERTIFICATION APPROVED
- F760 ‒ SNAP ESAP HOUSEHOLD CONVERTED TO SNAP
- X760 ‒ SNAP ESAP HOUSEHOLD CONVERTED TO SNAP

[MS 121-14 A](../121/121-14_stop_payment_and_forgery_procedures.md#121-14_A._STOP_PAY_PROCEDURES)      STOP PAY PROCEDURES

- Clarified that the [GEN 21](#)Stop Payment Affidavit Form must be signed with a handwritten signature

[MS 122-10](../122/122-10_direct_deposit_for_cash_benefits.md)      DIRECT DEPOSIT FOR CASH BENEFITS

- Clarified that the [GEN 153](#)Direct Deposit Request must be signed with a handwritten signature
