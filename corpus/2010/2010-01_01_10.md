STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2010-01

MANUAL:   Alaska Food Stamp Program

DATE:   January 4, 2010

We are clarifying that verbal verification of self-employment income and expenses is allowable, although written verification is preferred if available.  Verbal verification can be received from collateral contacts or the self-employed individual.

Other highlights to policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or e-mail [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 601](../../601/601.md)  Application Process

- Replaces the word "attend" with "participate" to reflect that face to face interviews are no longer required.

[MS 601-4 A](../../601/601-4.md#601_4_A_VERIFICATION_REQUIREMENTS_FOR_INITIAL_APPLICATIONS)  Verification

- In the Direct Data Systems table, replaces the Automatic Status Verification System (ASVS  ) with Verification Information System (VISVerification Information System). The VISVerification Information System is the online electronic verification process used by the SAVESystematic Alien Verification for Entitlements Program.

[MS 602-1 A](../../602/602-1_a.md)  Excluded Household

- Clarifies that Alaska courts do not expunge drug felony convictions.

[MS 602-1 D](../../602/602-1_d.md)  Citizenship and Eligible Alien Status, Ineligible Alien

- Replaces obsolete INS Form G-641, Application for Verification of Information, with INS Form G-845, Document Verification Request.
- Corrects references to the Automated Status Verification System (ASVS  ) with Systematic Alien Verification for Entitlements (SAVE  ) Program.

[MS 602-2 B](../../602/602-2_b.md)  Exempt Resources

- Clarifies that Aleut reparation payments received from a trust established by the Secretary of the Interior are countable resources if retained.
- Adds the date Agent Orange settlement payments ended.

[MS 602-3 A](../../602/602-3_a.md)  Earned Income

- Adds a link to [605-2 D](../../605/605-2_d.md), Income from Self-Employment.

MS 602-3 D  Exempt Income

- Clarifies that Aleut reparation payments received from a trust established by the Secretary of the Interior are countable income.
- Adds the date Agent Orange settlement payments ended.   Also clarifies VA disability payments for diseases related to Agent Orange exposure are not exempt.

[MS 603-3](../../603/603-3.md)  Determining Monthly Allotment

- Replaces the word "attend" with "participate" to reflect that face to face interviews are no longer required.

[MS 604-2](../../604/604-2.md)  Recertification

- Replaces the word "attend" with "participate" to reflect that face to face interviews are no longer required.
- Removes the requirement for a face-to-face interview.
- Corrects references to the Automated Status Verification System (ASVS  ) with Systematic Alien Verification for Entitlements (SAVE  ) Program.

[MS 605-1 B](../../605/605-1_b.md)  Students

- Updates the mileage rates with new amounts.

[MS 605-1 D](../../605/605-1_d.md) Institutions

- Replaces the word "attend" with "participate" to reflect that face to face interviews are no longer required.

[MS 605-2 D](../../605/605-2_d.md)  Income from Self-Employment

- Updates the mileage rates with new amounts.
- Clarifies that verbal verification of self-employment income and expenses is allowed.

[Addendum 6](../../addenda/addendum_6.md)

- Corrects references to the Automated Status Verification System (ASVS  ) with Systematic Alien Verification for Entitlements (SAVE  ) Program.
- Replaces obsolete INS Form G-641, Application for Verification of Information, with INS Form G-845, Document Verification Request.
- Updates table showing amount of earnings needed to earn one quarter of coverage.
