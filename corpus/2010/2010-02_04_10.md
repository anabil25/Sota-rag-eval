STATE
OF ALASKA

Department of Health
and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2010-02

MANUAL:   Alaska Food
Stamp Program

DATE:   April 1, 2010

 We are making several changes to incorporate new policy, clarify
existing policy and align policies within our program policy manuals.
 This includes:

1. - An Eligibility Review Form (GEN 72Eligibility review application) may be used as an initial application
     form if it is received in the month following a case closure.
      The case closure may be for any reason.
   - Interface verification policies
     are changed to more clearly describe when an interface must be
     checked.  It is no longer necessary to check all interfaces
     at each application, recertification, or review.
   - Beginning December 19, 2009, Afghan
     and Iraqi special immigrants are treated the same as refugees
     and are not subject to a five-year bar.
   - A non-transferable Limited Entry
     Fishing Permit is an exempt resource.
   - Funds held in a dedicated account
     established to hold a minor's retroactive SSISupplemental Security Income
     benefits are an exempt resource.
   - Vehicle resource policies have
     been clarified in the Family Medicaid, Food Stamp, and Temporary
     Assistance program manuals.  Staff can now accept a client's
     statement for vehicle value, ownership, and use.  Additional
     verification will only be necessary if the information is questionable.
   - Income received from the lease
     of a Limited Entry Fishing Permit is treated as unearned rental
     income.

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that
are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Other highlights to policy revisions are described below.  If you
have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team
at 465-3382 or e-mail [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS
601-2 A](../../601/601-2_the_application_form.md#601-2_A.What_is_an_Acceptable_Application)  What Is An Acceptable
Application Form?

1. - Clarifies that the GEN 72Eligibility review application, Eligibility Review Form, can be accepted
     as an initial application in the month following case closure
     regardless of the reason for closure.

[MS 601-4
A](../../601/601-4.md#601_4_A_VERIFICATION_REQUIREMENTS_FOR_INITIAL_APPLICATIONS)  Verification Requirements
for Initial Application

1. - Clarifies which interfaces
     must be checked at every application and review, and which should
     be checked if the caseworker receives questionable information.

[MS
602-1 A](../../602/602-1_a.md)  Exceptions to Household
Definitions

1. - Clarifies that children who
     are not under parental control are not required to be added to
     the Food Stamp case.

[MS
602-1 D](../../602/602-1_d.md) Citizenship and Eligible
Alien Status

1. - Adds Afghan and Iraqi special
     immigrants to the list of qualified aliens.
   - Adds Afghan and Iraqi special
     immigrants to the list of alien eligibility requirements that
     must be met in addition to being a qualified alien.
   - Adds information about the
     type of proof that may be used to verify Afghan and Iraqi special
     immigrant status.

 [MS 602-2 B](../../602/602-2_b.md) Exempt Resources

- Clarifies that a Limited Entry Fishing
  Permit that Commercial Fisheries Entry Commission (CFECCommercial Fisheries Entry Commission) has classified as non-transferable has no value.
- Clarifies that funds held in a dedicated
  account are exempt.

[MS
602-2 C](../../602/602-2_c.md) Vehicles

1. - Clarifies that staff can now
     accept a client's statement for vehicle value, ownership, and
     use.  Additional verification will only be necessary if the
     information is questionable.

[MS 602-3
B](../../602/602-3_b.md) Unearned Income

1. - Clarifies that rental income
     received in a lump sum shall be averaged over the months of intended
     use.
   - Clarifies that income from
     the lease of a limited entry fishing permit is considered unearned
     rental income.

[MS 602-4
D](../../602/602-4_d.md)  Allowable Medical Expenses

1. - Clarifies expenses for service
     animals are not limited to dogs.

[MS 602-4
E](../../602/604-2_e.md)   Shelter Deduction

1. - Clarifies lease agreements
     and /or contact with the landlord are acceptable forms of verification
     of shelter and utility expenses.
   - Clarifies that shelter or utility
     expenses are allowed if in a non-household member’s name as long
     as the household can verify they are responsible for the expense.

[MS
604-2 E](../../604/604-2.md#604_2_E_VERIFICATION_REQUIREMENTS)  Verification Requirements:  Recertification

1. - Clarifies which interfaces
     must be checked at every application and review, and which should
     be checked if the caseworker receives questionable information.

MS 604-3
A Semi-Annual Reporting

1. - Adds note to clarify that reports
     of change which result from clients moving from one area of the
     state to another may result in a change to their benefit.

[MS 605-2
C](../../605/605-2_c.md) Non-household Members

1. - Clarifies that children are
     not generally considered visitors in their parent's home.

[MS 605-2
D](../../605/605-2_d.md)  Income From Self-Employment

1. - Clarifies that rental income
     received in a lump sum shall be averaged over the months of intended
     use.
   - Clarifies that income from
     the lease of a limited entry fishing permit is considered unearned
     rental income.
