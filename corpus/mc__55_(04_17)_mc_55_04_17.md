STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #55

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: April 3, 2017

Based on the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008, the Food Stamp Program was renamed the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP). All of the outdated Food Stamp language has been updated to reflect the current [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program term in this manual change. The Self-Employment Annualization Standards have been updated for 2017 based on the change in federal poverty level for Alaska.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).
