Alaska Temporary Assistance Program Manual

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #68

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: April 1, 2021

The self-employment annualization table has been updated based on changes in the Federal Poverty Guidelines (FPG) for Alaska, effective April 1, 2021.

Highlights of all the policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 700-4](../700/700-4_verification_and_documentation.md)      VERIFICATION AND DOCUMENTATION

- Clarified that verification of shelter costs are required prior to allowing the deductions, due to a policy change that went into effect December 2020.
- Added note clarifying that if obtaining verification of shelter costs would delay processing the initial application, the household may elect to have their application processed without the allowances.

[MS 705](../705/705_application_process.md)      APPLICATION PROCESS

- Added language regarding signature requirements.

[MS 705-2 D](../705/705-2_the_application_form.md#705-2_D._WHAT_IS_AN_IDENTIFIABLE_APPLICATION)      WHAT IS AN IDENTIFIABLE APPLICATION?

- Added link to application signature requirements (MS 705).

[MS 705-2 E](../705/705-2_the_application_form.md#705-2_E._WHO_CAN_SIGN_THE_APPLICATION_FORM)      WHO CAN SIGN THE APPLICATION FORM?

- Added link to application signature requirements (MS 705).

[MS 705-4](../705/705-4_verification.md)      VERIFICATION REQUIRED PRIOR TO ALLOWING DEDUCTIONS

- Added verification requirement of shelter expenses, due to a policy change that went into effect December 2020.
- Removed note about requirement to verify subsidized housing costs, since this requirement now pertains to ALL shelter costs.

[MS 705-5 E](../705/705-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#705-5_E._WITHDRAWING_AN_APPLICATION)      WITHDRAWING AN APPLICATION

- Rewrote section for clarity.
- Added link to application signature requirements (MS 705).

[MS 712-3](../712/712-3_parental_relationship.md)      PARENTAL RELATIONSHIPS

- Added definition for CSSD's "Affidavit of Paternity" form.

[MS 717-6](../717/717-6_procedures_for_exchanging_information.md)      PROCEDURES FOR EXCHANGING INFORMATION

- Updated Paternity Affidavit form number reference.

[MS 754-9](../754/754-9_land,_stock,_and_payments_made_to_native_americans.md)      LAND, STOCK, AND PAYMENTS MADE TO NATIVE AMERICANS

- Added new subsection regarding Dingle Act Lands.

[MS 757-8](../757/757-8_2020_census_income.md)      CENSUS INCOME

- Extended 2020 Census income exemption through October 31, 2021.

[MS 759-6](../759/759-6_income_from_self-employment.md)      INCOME FROM SELF-EMPLOYMENT

- Updated the Self-Employment Standards due to FPL changes effective April 2021.

[MS 780-19](../780/780-19_shelter_allowance.md)      SHELTER ALLOWANCE

- Removed option to verify shelter costs when questionable, due to a policy change that went into effect December 2020.

[MS 780-19 H](../780/780-19_shelter_allowance.md#780-19_H._VERIFICATION_OF_SHELTER_COSTS)      VERIFICATION OF SHELTER COSTS

- Added verification requirement of shelter expenses, due to a policy change that went into effect December 2020.

[MS 780-19 I](../780/780-19_shelter_allowance.md#780-19_I._ACCEPTABLE_VERIFICATION)      WHEN SHELTER COSTS MUST BE VERIFIED

- Subsection removed, content included in 780-19 H.

MS 780-19 K      VERIFICATION EXAMPLES

- Section removed since examples of questionable shelter cost situations are no longer applicable due to a policy change that went into effect December 2020.

[MS 790-1](../790/790-1_change_reporting_requirements.md)      CHANGE REPORTING REQUIREMENTS

- Removed option to verify shelter costs when questionable, due to a policy change that went into effect December 2020.

[MS 790-5 A](../790/790-5_verification_of_changes.md#790-5_A._REQUIRED_VERIFICATION)      REQUIRED VERIFICATION

- Removed option to verify shelter costs when questionable, due to a policy change that went into effect December 2020.

[MS 790-8](../790/790-8_periodic_reviews.md)      PERIODIC REVIEWS

- Added link to application signature requirements (MS 705).

[ADDENDUM 2](../addenda/addendum_2_-_program_standards.md)      TA INCOME AND PAYMENT STANDARDS

- Updated the South Central Utility Region.
