1000-3          ACHIEVING SELF-SUFFICIENCY

The goal of Work Services is to assist clients to increase self-sufficiency.

1000-3 A.      PROGRAM DEFINITION OF SELF-SUFFICIENCY

- An [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program household achieves a level of earnings that exceeds the [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program income and payment standards, see [ATAP Addendum 2](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/addenda/addendum_2.htm); or,
- An [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program household voluntarily closes their case with earnings; or,
- An [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program client is approved for Social Security benefits.

1000-3 B.      EXIT PLANNING STARTS AT INTAKE

- Every interaction with your client should be focused on achieving a permanent exit from [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program.
