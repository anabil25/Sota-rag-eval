STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #35

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  December 1, 2012

This year Social Security announced a Cost of Living Allowance (COLA) increase effective January 2013.  The Adult Public Assistance need and maximum payment standards are adjusted by the same percentage as the COLACost of Living Allowance increase in Social Security benefits.  This year the COLACost of Living Allowance increase is 1.7%.  We have incorporated the 2013 standards into the examples and [Addendum 1.](../../addendum/addendum_1.md)  These standards become effective with the benefit month of January 2013.  The COLACost of Living Allowance adjustment also causes:

1. - An increase in the income limit for Substantial Gainful Activity; and
   - An increase in the student earned income exclusion.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).
