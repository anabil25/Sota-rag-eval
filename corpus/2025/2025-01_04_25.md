STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  2025-01

MANUAL:  Senior Benefit Program

DATE:  April 1, 2025

We have updated the program standards with the new Federal Poverty Levels. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these revisions are described below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy & Program Development Team by e-mail at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[310-1(D)](../../310__application/310-1_the_application_form.md#310-1_D._What_is_an_Identifiable_Application)  WHAT IS AN IDENTIFIABLE APPLICATION

- Revised to section for clarity and to align with business processes.

[310-3(D)](../../310__application/310-3_verification.md#310-3_D._Verification)  VERIFICATION

- Changed Child Support Services Division (CSSD) to Child Support Enforcement Division (CSED).
- Revised section for clarity.

[322](../../322__residency/322_alaska_residency.md)  ALASKA RESIDENCY

- Clarified when caseworkers should consider Alaska residency questionable.

[330-1](../../330__income/330-1_income_limits.md)  INCOME LIMITS

- Updated the income limits due to the Federal Poverty Guidelines change effective April 1, 2025.

[ADDENDUM 1](../../addendum/addendum_1_-_eis_notices.md)

- Removed as an [EIS](#)(Eligibility Information System) notice list exists in the Admin Manual.
