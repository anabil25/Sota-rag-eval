753-5          [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program CORRECTIVE BENEFIT PAYMENTS

Any [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program corrective benefit payment is not considered as income or as a resource in the month received and the month after it is received.
