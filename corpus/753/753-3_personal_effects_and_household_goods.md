753-3          PERSONAL EFFECTS AND HOUSEHOLD GOODS

Basic items of personal effects and household goods, those which are essential to day-to-day living, are exempt. Luxury items which are not essential to daily life may count against the resource limit. (See [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 752-5](../752/752-5_luxury_items.md).)
