TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   [MC](memo_mc__62_(09_21).md#)Manual Change #68

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  September 1, 2023

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy and clarify existing policy.

Highlights of these policy revision are described below. If you have any questions, contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[410-3(B)](../../410/410-3_the_interview.md#410_3_B____WHO_MUST_PARTICIPATE_THE_INTERVIEW_)       Who Must Participate in the Interview

- Clarified that if legally married, only one spouse needs to participate in the interview

[426-7](../../426/426-7_converting_interim_assistance.md)           Converting Interim Assistance

- Added a link to [EIS](#)Eligibility Information System Procedure 2012-1

[Addendum 3](../../addendum/addendum_3_countable_and_excluded_income_for_adult_public_assistance.md)   Countable and Excluded Income for Adult Public Assistance

- Added Needs Based Income, such as Senior Benefits
- Added a link to [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance MS 442-3(B)
- Added [VA](#)Department of Veterans Affairs Disability Payments
