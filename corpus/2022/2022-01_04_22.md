STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  2022-01

MANUAL:  Senior Benefit Program

DATE:  April 1, 2022

We have updated the program standards with the new Federal Poverty Levels. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these revisions are described below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy & Program Development Team by e-mail at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[310-2A](../../310__application/310-2_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#310-2_A._Approving_the_Application)     APPROVING THE APPLICATION

- Clarified that a Senior Benefits application can be used for second month eligibility if first month is denied

[330-1](../../330__income/330-1_income_limits.md)     INCOME LIMITS

- Updated income limits based on the 2022 [FPL](#)Federal Poverty Limits changes that are effective 4/1/22
- Added a note to clarify the use of the couple standard even if only one is eligible

[330-2](../../330__income/330-2_countable_income.md)    COUNTABLE INCOME

- Clarified that the countable monthly income should be what is expected during the certification period

[330-2H](../../330__income/330-2_countable_income.md#330-2H_DEATH_BENEFITS)    DEATH BENEFITS

- Added new subsection regarding death benefit income
