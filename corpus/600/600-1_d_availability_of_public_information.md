600-1 D.     AVAILABILITY OF
PUBLIC INFORMATION

Regulations, Plans of Operations, federal procedures,
the Alaska SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Manual,
and supplemental instructions issued on policy and procedures shall be
maintained by the SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program
Policy Unit in Juneau for examination by the public. The Alaska SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Manual will also be available for
examination by the public in all DPADivision of Public Assistance
offices.
