600-1 H.     DOCUMENTATION

The goal of documentation is to establish a clear and complete record, so any person reading the case will understand what was done and why.  Clear and complete documentation is the foundation of a positive supervisory or QAQuality Assessment review, and an essential part of quality casework. Refer to [MS 601-4.](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/601/601-4.htm)

Documentation is a record of:

- Factors of eligibility verified and how they were verified.
- Information used to determine eligibility and benefit amount, including:

1. The source of the information;
2. Why this information was used.
3. The method used to convert income and expense information into monthly amounts; and
4. The verification that supports the information;

- Actions taken on a case and the reasons supporting those actions.
- Contacts with the client, partner agencies, contractors/grantees and third party sources (collateral contacts).
