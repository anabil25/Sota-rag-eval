STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #22

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  July 1, 2009

This manual change implements the new policy announced by broadcast on June 12 that all AmeriCorps payments are exempt income.  This change is effective July 1, 2009.

In addition to other technical changes, the section on allowable absences was revised to explain that an individual remains eligible when out of state for their own illness or that of a family member whether they became ill while out of state or left the state due to illness.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 423-2 B](../../423/423-2_absence_from_the_state.md#423_2_B____EXCEPTIONS_FOR_CERTAIN_ALLOWABLE_ABSENCES)  Exceptions For Certain Allowable Absences

- Clarifies that a recipient does not have to be out of state when they or a family member becomes ill to remain eligible. If an individual leaves the state due to their own illness or that of a family member and is not able to return within 30 days, they remain eligible for assistance.

[MS 426-2 C](../../426/426-2_eligibility_requirements_for_interim_assistance.md#426_2_C__4__ Authorization for Release of Protected Health Information (Gen 150))  Interim Blindness or Disability Decision

- This section was updated with information about the new GEN 150 IAInterim Assistance form.

[MS 426-2 E](../../426/426-2_eligibility_requirements_for_interim_assistance.md#426_2_E__IAR_AUTHORIZATION_REQUIREMENT)   IAR  Authorization Requirement

- Clarifies which fields must be completed by the caseworker.

[MS 442-3 A](../../442/442-3_unearned_income_exclusions.md#442_3_A_INCOME_EXCLUSIONS_AUTHORIZED_BY_FEDERAL_LAWS)  Volunteer Service Payments

- Clarifies that all AmeriCorps payments are exempt income.
