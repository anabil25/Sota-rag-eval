APA - Alaska Adult Public Assistance Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health
and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #58

MANUAL: Adult Public Assistance

DATE: April 1, 2020

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy, clarifying
existing policy, and align policies within our policy manuals. Other changes
are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the result of
policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have
any questions, contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

[410-2A](../../410/410-2_the_application_form.md#410_2_A__WHAT_IS_AN_ACCEPTABLE_APPLICATION_FORM_)
     WHAT IS AN ACCEPTABLE APPLICATION FORM

- Added that Senior Benefits will accept the GEN 72 to determine
  continued eligibility. This change is effective with the April 2020
  form revision.

[410-2C](../../410/410-2_the_application_form.md#410_2_C__WHEN_IS_AN_APPLICATION_NOT_REQUIRED_)
     WHEN IS AN APPLICATION NOT REQUIRED

- Revised section to remove automatic APAAdult Public Assistance
  eligibility without application for an SSISupplemental Security Income
  recipient turning 18 years of age. This change is being done to match
  regulations.
- Renumbered section

[410-3A](../../410/410-3_the_interview.md#410_3_A____WHEN_IS_AN_INTERVIEW_REQUIRED_)
     WHEN IS AN INTERVIEW REQUIRED

- Added that interviews are required
  during initial application and at reviews

[423-3](../../423/423-3_applicants_receiving_assistance_from_other_states.md)
     APPLICANTS RECEIVING ASSISTANCE FROM OTHER
STATES

- Added note to clarify when an application
  must be pended due to an out of state address showing with the Social
  Security Administration

[425-2A](../../425/425-2_who_determines_disability_blindness.md#425_2_A_TEMPORARY_DISABILITY_OR_BLINDNESS_DECISIONS)
     TEMPORARY DISABILITY OR BLINDNESS OR DISABILITY
DECISIONS

- Updated section to match federal
  regulations allowing a licensed advanced practice registered nurse
  or licensed physician assistant within their licensed scope of practice
  to determine disability

[425-2C](../../425/425-2_who_determines_disability_blindness.md#425_2_C_SSA_BLINDNESS_OR_DISABILITY_DECISIONS)
     SSA BLINDNESS OR DISABILITY DECISIONS

- Added note to clarity that APAAdult Public Assistance applications are not pended
  for proof of appeal if the SSASocial Security Administration
  denies due to no disability

[426-2](../../426/426-2_eligibility_requirements_for_interim_assistance.md)      ELIGIBILITY
REQUIREMENTS FOR INTERIM ASSISTANCE

- Added note to clarify when to authorize
  an IAInterim Assistance case

[426-2C(2)](../../426/426-2_eligibility_requirements_for_interim_assistance.md#426_2_C__2__Blind_APA_applicants_required_to_submit_an_AB__3_form)
     BLIND APA APPLICANTS REQUIRED TO SUBMIT
AN AB 3 FORM

- Updated section to match federal
  regulations allowing a licensed advanced practice registered nurse
  or licensed physician assistant within their licensed scope of practice
  to determine disability

[426-2C(3)](../../426/426-2_eligibility_requirements_for_interim_assistance.md#426_2_C__3__Disabled_APA_applicants_required_submit_and_AD__2_form)
     DISABLED APA APPLICANTS REQUIRED SUBMIT
AN AD 2 FORM

- Updated section to match federal
  regulations allowing a licensed advanced practice registered nurse
  or licensed physician assistant within their licensed scope of practice
  to determine disability

[430-1](../../430/430-1_resources_defined.md)
     RESOURCES

- Added note to clarify that client
  statement is allowed regarding resources unless questionable. Cases
  that involve a trust still require expenditure review.

[432-2C](../../432/432-2_personal_property.md#432-2_C.___BURIAL_SPACE_EXCLUSION)
     BURIAL SPACE EXCLUSION

- Revised section for clarity
- Renamed subsection to Burial Space
  Exclusion
- Added an example showing what expenses
  shown on a burial contract would be considered burial space and which
  would be considered burial funds

[432-2D](../../432/432-2_personal_property.md#432-2_D._BURIAL_FUND_EXCLUSION)
     BURIAL FUNDS EXCLUSION

- Revised section for clarity
- Renamed subsection title to Burial
  Funds Exclusion
- Renumbered list within the subsection

[433-4(1)](../../433/433-4_able_accounts.md)
     ABLE ACCOUNTS

- Added 2020 Max Allowable contribution
  amount of $15,000

[433-4(8)](../../433/433-4_able_accounts.md)
     CERTAIN EMPLOYED ABLE ACCOUNT BENEFICIARIES
MAY MAKE ADDITIONAL CONTRIBUTIONS

- Added a table for yearly additional
  savings for certain employed account holders

[442-2G](../../442/442-2_earned_income_exclusions.md#442_2_G_IMPAIRMENT_RELATED_WORK_EXPENSES_FOR_DISABLED)
     IMPAIRMENT RELATED WORK EXPENSES FOR DISABLED

- Added note to clarify when IRWEImpairment Related Work Expensess can be excluded from earned
  income and when a disability decision is needed

[442-2I](../../442/442-2_earned_income_exclusions.md#442_2_I_WORK_EXPENSES_FOR_THE_BLIND)
     WORK EXPENSES FOR THE BLIND

- Added note to clarify when blind
  work expenses can be excluded from earned income and when a blindness
  decision is needed
- Updated mileage link

[480-1D](../../480/480-1_reviews.md#480_1_D_INTERVIEW_REQUIREMENTS)
     INTERVIEW REQUIREMENTS

- Clarified that an interview must
  be done for APAAdult Public Assistance and
  APAAdult Public Assistance related Medicaid
  reviews
