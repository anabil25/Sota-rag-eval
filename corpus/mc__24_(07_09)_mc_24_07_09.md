STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #24

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: July 1, 2009

This manual change implements the new policy announced by broadcast on June 12, that all AmeriCorps payments are exempt income. These changes are effective July 1, 2009.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 757-5](../757/757-5_volunteer_service_payments.md)      Volunteer Service Payments

- Clarifies that all AmeriCorps and SeniorCorps payments are exempt income
