ADLTC - Alaska Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #57

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  September 1, 2021

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy, clarifying existing policy, and align policies within our policy manuals. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[520 A](../../520/520_elig_req_apa_policy.md#520_A__APPLICATION_AND_REVIEW_PROCESS)      APPLICATION AND REVIEW PROCESS

- Clarified that an [SSP](#)Self Service Portal/[FFM](#)Federally Facilitated Marketplace is the only acceptable application that doesn't need a "wet" signature

[550 B](../../550/550_eligibility_categories_for_an_individual_living_in_an_institution.md#550_B.__INDIVIDUAL_ELIGIBLE_FOR_SSI_OR_APA_CASH_ASSISTANCE_IF_NOT_IN_AN_INSTITUTION)      INDIVIDUAL ELIGIBLE FOR [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income OR [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance CASH ASSISTANCE IF NOT IN AN INSTITUTION

- Clarified that the Refused Cash category is also included.

[554 K](../../554/554_transfer_of_assets.md#554_K_Annuities)      ANNUITIES

- Clarified how a [TOA](#)Transfer of Assets should be calculated with an annuity that has caused a penalty.

[576 E](../../576/576_emergency_treatment_for_aliens.md#576_E.______COORDINATION_WITH_THE_DIVISION_OF_HEALTH_CARE_SERVICES___DHCS__)    COORDINATION WITH DIVISION OF HEALTH CARE SERVICES (DHCS)

- Added a note clarifying that the letter from [DHCS](#)Division of Health Care Services indicating an individual received services of an emergent nature and indicating the covered dates of services is not a determination of eligibility.

[580 D](../../580/580_medicare_premium_assistance.md#580_D___LOW_INCOME_SUBSIDY__LIS__PROGRAM)      LOW-INCOME SUBSIDY (LIS) PROGRAM

- Added a link to the LIS business process at [Administrative MS 126-3](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/admin/126/126-3.htm).
