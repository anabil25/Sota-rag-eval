759-5          WORKFORCE INNOVATION & OPPORTUNITY ACT (WIOA) PAYMENTS

Trainees receiving Intensive Services under the Workforce Innovation & Opportunity Act of 2014 (WIOA) may receive periodic income as part of their training and employment program. This income may be earned or unearned.

759-5 A.      UNEARNED [WIOA](#)Workforce Innovation & Opportunity Act INCOME

All unearned [WIOA](#)Workforce Innovation & Opportunity Act payments are exempt and do not count in the eligibility determination or payment computation. They are excluded because the [WIOA](#)Workforce Innovation & Opportunity Act program is complementary to [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program, and is not an income maintenance program.

759-5 B.      EARNED [WIOA](#)Workforce Innovation & Opportunity Act INCOME

Except for income earned from participation in the Summer Youth Employment and Training Program, earned [WIOA](#)Workforce Innovation & Opportunity Act payments count as income in the 185% test, the eligibility determination, and the payment computation if the [WIOA](#)Workforce Innovation & Opportunity Act participant is a minor parent, adult parent, or other caretaker relative included in the assistance unit.
