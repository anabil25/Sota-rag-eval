759            EARNED INCOME

Earned Income consists of money received, in cash or in-kind, from wages, salary or commissions, or by paycheck advance in exchange for the performance of services by the employee.

Self-employment earned income consists of money received, in cash or in-kind, for producing or selling goods or services, after all allowable expenses of earning the income are subtracted.
