759-3          JURY DUTY PAYMENTS AND WITNESS FEES

Jury duty payments and witness fees count as earned income in the month of receipt and are subject to the work incentive deductions. Any portion of a jury duty payment or witness fee that is designated as per diem or as a reimbursement for such items as food, lodging, parking, or transportation is disregarded.
