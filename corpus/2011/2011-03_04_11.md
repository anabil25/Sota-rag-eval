#

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health
and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2011-03

MANUAL:   Alaska Food
Stamp Program

DATE:   April 1, 2011

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy, clarify existing
policy, and align policies within our program manuals.  These changes
include:

1. - New policy excluding federal income tax returns as income in
     the month of receipt and as a resource for 12 months from the
     date of receipt.

1. - Clarification that verification of child support and child
     care expenses may be postponed for expedite food stamp cases.

1. - Clarification that expedite cases must be processed, and benefits
     authorized by the 4th day. Previous policy stated that benefits
     were to be authorized by the 7th day.
   - A change in policy making subsidized adoption payments countable
     unearned income.  This change is a result of a recent clarification
     from the Food and Nutrition Service.

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that
are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below.
 If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program
Development Team at 465-3382 or e-mail [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS
601-5 F](../../601/601-5.md#601_5_F_WHEN_THE_APPLICATION_IS_DELAYED)  When the Application is Delayed

1. - Clarifies that if expedited
     FSFood Stamp benefits that are not authorized
     by the 4th day following the application filing date, the application
     will be considered delayed.  Previous policy stated that
     expedited benefits must be authorized by the 7th day.

[MS 602-2
B](../../602/602-2_b.md)  Excluded Resources

1. - Adds
     new policy in subsection 31 to explain that federal income tax
     returns received after December 31, 2009 are an exempt resource
     for 12 months from the date of receipt.
   - Clarifies that the value of federal income tax returns received
     within the last 12 months should be deducted from countable liquid
     resources.

[MS 602-2
D](../../602/602-2_d.md)  Other Household Resources

1. - Removes income tax refunds from
     Non-recurring Lump Sum Payment section.

[MS 602-3
B](../../602/602-3_b.md)  Unearned Income

1. - Adds subsidized adoption payments to list of countable unearned
     income.

[MS 602-3 D](../../602/602-3_d._exempt_income.md)
 Exempt Income

1. - Removes
     income tax refunds as a type of non-recurring lump sum payment
     in sub-section 13.
   - Removes adoption subsidies
     as a source of exempt income in section 35.
   - Adds new policy in sub-section
     35 to explain that federal income tax returns received after December
     31, 2009 are excluded from income.

[MS
602-4 E (7)](../../602/604-2_e.md#602_4_E_7.__Verification_of_Shelter_Costs)  Verification
of Shelter Costs

1. - Changes
     the word “or” to “and” in the sentence about recertifications
     so the wording is consistent with application and certification
     sentences.

[MS
604-2 B (3)](../../604/604-2.md#604_2_B_RECERTIFICATION:_APPLICATION_FILING_DEADLINES)  Application
Filed During the Month Following the Expiration of the Certification Period

1. - Revises
     section referencing time frame for processing an application to
     clarify that the expedited FSFood Stamp
     benefits must be authorized by the 4th day.  Previous policy
     stated that expedited benefits must be authorized by the 7th day.

MS
604-3A(3)(d)  Changes
That Must Be Acted on for Semi-Annual Reporting

1. - Removes
     FSFood Stamp  E&T
     non-compliance from disqualification penalty section.

[MS
605-1 C](../../605/605-1_c.md)  Boarders/Foster Care Children and Adults

1. - Changes policy to count
     subsidized adoption payments as countable income.

[MS
605-2 D](../../605/605-2_d.md)  Income From Self-Employment

1. - Updates the Self-Employment
     Annualization Standards

[MS 605-3(b)(1)](../../605/605-3.md)   Expedited
Service Entitlement

1. - Adds a note and example to clarify that
     federal income tax returns are disregarded when determining a
     household's eligibility for expedited service.
   - Clarifies
     that the amount claimed by the household is acceptable verification.

[MS
605-3B(2)](../../605/605-3_b.md#605_3_B_2__Verification_for_Expedited_Service_Applications)  Verification
for Expedited Service Applications

1. - Clarifies
     that verification of child support and child care expenses may
     be postponed for expedite food stamp cases.

[MS
605-4B(2)](../../605/605-4_b.md#605_4_B_2__Subsistence_Hunting_and_Fishing_Statement)  Subsistence
Hunting and Fishing Statement

1. - Eliminates
     the requirement for a "signed" Subsistence Hunting and
     Fishing Statement from the head of household.

[MS
605-4B(3)](../../605/605-4_b.md#605_4_B_3__Special_ID_Card_for_Subsistence_Hunting_and_Fishing)  Special
ID Card for Subsistence Hunting and Fishing

1. - Eliminates
     the instructions to enter a Y in the ID CARD REQ field on
     the FSADFood Stamp Allotment Determintion screen.

[MS
606-1A(2)(b)](../../606/606-1.md#606_1_A__MONTHLY_FOOD_STAMP_ISSUANCE)  Monthly
Food Stamp Issuance

1. - Clarifies approval of the EBTElectronic Benefit Transfer unit is required when the EBTElectronic Benefit Transfer card is to be delivered to the office.

[MS 606-2](../../606/606-2.md)   Food
Stamp Use at Retailers and Wholesalers

1. - Updates
     telephone contact information for retailers.
