#

     STATE
OF ALASKA

Department of Health
and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2011-04

MANUAL:   Alaska Food
Stamp Program

DATE:   August 1,
2011

We are making several changes to clarify existing policy as a result
of feedback from the field.  These changes include:

1. - Clarification of the requirement to verify shelter deductions
     for individuals who live in subsidized housing;
   - Instruction on registering a recertification received the month
     after it's due; and
   - Clarification explaining when stolen benefits can be replaced.

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that
are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below.
 If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program
Development Team at 465-3382 or e-mail [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 602-4](../../602/602-4.md)
 Deductions

1. - Adds an exception to clarify
     that an expense must be verified if it results in a deduction
     and is questionable.

[MS 602-4
E](../../602/604-2_e.md)  Shelter Deduction

1. - Adds a note to section 7 stating
     that rent for individuals in subsidized housing must be verified.

[MS
604-2 B](../../604/604-2.md#604_2_B_RECERTIFICATION:_APPLICATION_FILING_DEADLINES)  Recertification:  Application Filing Deadlines

1. - Adds
     instruction on registering a recertification received the month
     after it's due.

MS 604-3 B  Status Reporting

1. - Clarifies that new shelter
     costs must be verified if questionable when a status reporting
     household reports new shelter expenses.

[MS 605-4 B](../../605/605-4_b.md#605_4_B_2__Subsistence_Hunting_and_Fishing_Statement)  Subsistence
Hunting and Fishing

1. - Changes field name from SUBSIST
     HUNT/FISH to FS SUBSISTENCE ID CARD in second paragraph.

[MS
606-1 A](../../606/606-1.md#606_1_A__MONTHLY_FOOD_STAMP_ISSUANCE)  Monthly Food Stamp Issuance

1. - Updates
     EIS Procedure from 2000-10 to 2010-1
   - Adds a link to  EIS Procedure
     2010-1

[MS 606-3](../../606/606-3.md)
 Replacing Food Stamp Benefits

1. - Changes
     the title of the 2nd section from “Replacing Benefits Already
     Spent” to “Replacing Benefits Lost in a Disaster” to more correctly
     describe this section.
   - Adds a new paragraph to explain
     that if an Alaska Quest card is reported stolen by a client and
     benefits were used, only the benefits that were used after the
     report of loss are eligible for replacement.
   - Adds an example.
