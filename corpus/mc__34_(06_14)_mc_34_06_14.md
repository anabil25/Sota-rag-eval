ADLTC - Alaska Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE
OF ALASKA

Department of Health
and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #34

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled
and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  June 2, 2014

We have updated the ADLTC manual to include several small changes.  Highlights
of these revisions are described below.  If you have any questions
please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or
e-mail [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW
OF CHANGES

[520-D](../../520/520_elig_req_apa_policy.md#520_D__DEVELOPMENT_OF_INCOME)         DEVELOPMENT
OF INCOME

- Adds a note to explain that if a client is potentially eligible
  for income from UIB, early retirement or retirement they must pursue
  it.

[554-B](../../554/554_transfer_of_assets.md#554_B_Verification_and_Documentation)        VERIFICATION AND DOCUMENTATION

- Adds that we can accept client statement for bank accounts unless
  questionable.

[562-A](../../562/562_processing_hcb_waiver.md#562_A.______MAGI_WAIVER_CASES)
        **PROCESSING
HCB HOME AND COMMUNITY BASED WAIVER CASES FOR A CHILD RECEIVING ANY MAGI
MEDICAID**

- Deleted
  the reference to DKC, Denali Kidcare, Family Medicaid or Under 21
  Medicaid.

[562-B](../../562/562_processing_hcb_waiver.md#562_B.______APA_RELATED_MEDICAID_OR_LTC_MEDICAID_WAIVER_CASES)
        **PROCESSING
HCB WAIVER CASES WHEN A DDS DETERMINATION IS PENDING**

- Adds
  a step to send notice N011 requesting a MED3 form.
