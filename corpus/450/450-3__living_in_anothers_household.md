450-3        LIVING IN ANOTHER'S HOUSEHOLD (SSI  "B" LIVING ARRANGEMENT)

An individual or couple is In another's household and on the lower need and payment standards only if the individual or couple receives shelter and all of their meals from others living in the household unless any of the situations in section [450-2](450-2_living_independently_(ssi__a__living_arrangement).md) apply.
