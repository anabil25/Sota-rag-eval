450           LIVING ARRANGEMENTS

An individual's or couple's living arrangement determines the APAAdult Public Assistance need and payment standards against which countable income is compared when determining APAAdult Public Assistance eligibility and calculating APAAdult Public Assistance benefit amounts. This section describes these different living arrangements and provides guidance in determining the correct living arrangement for APAAdult Public Assistance purposes.
