113-9          Q-FORM
PROCESS

The Quality Assessment feedback form (Q-Form) is
used to report QAQuality Assessment error
findings to DPADivision of Public Assistance Leadership,
DOSTDivision Operations Support Team, Program Policy,
Staff Development and Training, and Program Integrity staff.

The Q-Form is completed by the QAQuality Assessment
reviewer to document the circumstances of an error finding during the
review process. The narrative will include the specific error element
and an explanation of the error determination.

The QAQuality Assessment
Supervisor will upload the Q-Form and associated review documents to the
QAQuality Assessment SharePoint site in
the monthly CAPSCorrective Action Planning Spreadsheets folder.

The Q-Form is also used to notify the responsible
eligibility team by email of a household's program disqualification for
refusing to cooperate with the QAQuality Assessment
review process and will attach the Q-Form to the email.

Note:

This process requires forwarding of the email and attachments.
