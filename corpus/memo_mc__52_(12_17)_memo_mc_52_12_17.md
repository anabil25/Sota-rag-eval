STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   MC #52

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE: December 1, 2017

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy, clarify existing policy, and align policies within our program policy manuals. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these policy revision are described below.  If you have any questions, contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

[433-1](../../433/433-1_transfer_of_resources.md)      TRANSFER OF RESOURCES

- Updated all examples within the chapter to reflect the new 2018 standards

[433-2A](../../433/433-2_conditional_benefits.md#433_2_A_ELIGIBILITY_FOR_CONDITIONAL_BENEFITS)    ELIGIBILITY FOR CONDITIONAL BENEFITS

- Updated the SSI payment standard to the new 2018 amount in order to accurately calculate if an applicant is eligible for conditional benefits

[433-4](../../433/433-4_able_accounts.md)      ABLE ACCOUNTS

- Added a table showing the maximum yearly contribution

[452-1](../../452/452-1_payment_calculations.md)       PAYMENT CALCULATIONS

- Updated all examples within the chapter to reflect the new 2018 standards

[452-2](../../452/452-2_budgeting_exceptions_for_ssi_recipients.md)       BUDGETING EXCEPTIONS FOR SSI CASES

- Updated all examples within the chapter to reflect the new 2018 standards

[452-5](../../452/452-5_computation_of_benefits.md)       COMPUTATION OF BENEFITS

- Updated all examples within the chapter to reflect the new 2018 standards

[460-5](../../460/460-5_income_allocations.md)       INCOME ALLOCATIONS

- Updated all examples within the chapter to reflect the new 2018 standards

[470-3A](../../470/470-3_temporary_absence_from_institution.md#470_3_A_TEMPORARY_ABSENCE)    TEMPORARY ABSENCE

- Changed Xerox to Conduent State Healthcare LLC. as the business name has changed

[480-1A](../../480/480-1_reviews.md#480_1_A_REVIEW_S_FOR_NON_SSI_RECIPIENTS)    REVIEWS FOR NON-SSI RECIPIENTS

- Added a note explaining that a review should be done when we receive a new GEN 50C or GEN 72 for other programs
