Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2015-01

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: June 1, 2015

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised. Highlights of the revisions are described below.

If you have any questions, please contact Field Services at [dpafield@alaska.gov](mailto:dpafield@alaska.gov) or the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

101      The Application Process

- [101-1](../101/101-1_definitions.md) (Definitions) Adds definition for "assistance unit."
- [101-6](../101/101-6_incomplete_applications.md) (Assisting Applicants with Incomplete Applications) Adds new subsection C to this section to clarify that division staff can provide assistance in completing applications when needed.

103      Case Files

- 103-1A (General Rules for Creating Case File Folder) Changes "DKC" to MAGI Medicaid only cases.
- 103-1C (The Organization of Two-Part File Folder) Changes "DKC" to MAGI Medicaid only for two-part file folders and removes GEN 132 for DKC applications.
- [103-6B](../103/103-6_retention_of_case_records.md#103-6_B._RECORD_STORAGE_OVERVIEW) (Records Storage Overview) Change the word from "contractors" to "providers" when cases management files are received from Work Services.

104      Statewide Process Management

- [104-1A](../104/104-1_first_contact_resolution.md#104-1_A._OFFICES_PARTICIPATING_IN_FIRST_CONTACT_RESOLUTION) (Offices Participating in First Contact Resolution) An update and removes of some exceptions that were previously granted to other offices.
- [104-1B](../104/104-1_first_contact_resolution.md#104-1_B._RECEIVING_CALLS_AND_PAPERWORK) (Receiving Calls and Paperwork) An update to the way each office will handle incoming paperwork and/or phone calls and to the way each office will handle incoming paperwork and how it's received, i.e. mail in/drop off, in person, scanned, faxed, emailed.
- [104-1C](../104/104-1_first_contact_resolution.md#104-1_C._APPLICATIONS__ADD-TO-PROGRAM_AND_REAPPLICATIONS__GEN_72_) (Applications, Add-to-Programs and Reapplications (GEN 72)) Updates the instruction for handling applications, add to programs and re-applications for each office, identifying the procedures depending on how the paperwork, applications and GEN 72s are received.
- [104-1D](../104/104-1_first_contact_resolution.md#104-1_D._PENDED_APPLICATIONS) (Pended Applications) Allows for sending out of pended applications to the "home" offices.
- [104-1E](../104/104-1_first_contact_resolution.md#104-1_E._DENYING_FOR_FAILURE_TO_PROVIDE) (Denying for Failure to Provide) Updates the procedure to identify which office is responsible for failure to provide the information.
- [104-1F](../104/104-1_first_contact_resolution.md#104-1_F._INTERVIEWS_AND_NO-SHOWS) (No Show) Updates information on when to send the paperwork out to the "home" offices.
- [104-1H](../104/104-1_first_contact_resolution.md#104-1_H._RECERTIFICATION_AND_REVIEWS) (Recertification and Reviews) Updates processing and handling information for each office depending on how the paperwork is received.
- [104-1I](../104/104-1_first_contact_resolution.md#104-1_I._REPORTS_OF_CHANGE) (Reports of Change) Updates processing information depending on how the paperwork is received.
- [104-1J](../104/104-1_first_contact_resolution.md#104-1_J._PENDED_INFORMATION) (Pended Information) Identifies the steps/procedures for each office and also allows for sending off the pended information to the "home" offices depending on how the paperwork is received.
- [104-1K](../104/104-1_first_contact_resolution.md#104-1_K._OTHER_REMINDERS) (Other Reminders) Updates procedures.

105      Verification of Information

- [105-1](../105/105-1_verification_of_information.md) (Verification of Information) Clarifies that out-of-state benefits must be verified when Alaska residency is questionable or when an individual moves to Alaska and receives benefits in another state.
