STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health
and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  2019-02

MANUAL:  General Relief
Assistance

DATE:  September 3, 2019

This manual change updated GRA policies to be in line with state regulations
for both GRA and GRA-Burial Assistance.  Other
changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the
results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these policy revisions are explained below. If you have
any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at
[dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGE

[210-1](../../210/210-2_interview_requirements.md)
 Interview Requirements

- Clarified
  the interview requirement for GRA non-burial.  Exempting the
  interview for GRA non-burial is to be used only as a last resort

[220-2](../../220/220-2_resources.md#220-2_C._PERSONAL_RESOURCES)
 Resources

- Clarified
  that resources are measured at the time of interview for GRA burial.
   All money in a bank account at the time of interview is counted
  as a resource regardless of whether the money was income that was
  deposited in the same month

[220-3](../../220/income.md)
 Income

- Corrected
  terminology from Food Stamps to SNAP for consistency
- Clarified
  that the GRA program does not have a development of income requirement,
  but a GRA applicant must apply for prior resources (linked to GRA
  MS 220-2D)

[230-1](../../230/230-1_payments.md#230-1_C._PAYMENT_EXCLUSIONS)
 Payments

- Clarified
  that the reconnection fee is an allowable utility expense that GRA
  can potentially cover

[240-1](../../240/240-1_non-financial_eligibility.md#240-1_B._RESPONSIBLE_RELATIVES_NOT_LIVING_WITH_THE_DECEASED_AT_THE_TIME_OF_DEATH)
 Responsible Relatives and GRA Burial Services

- Clarified
  that attempts must be made to obtain legally responsible relative
  contact information and attempt to contact each legally responsible
  relative to ask about their ability to pay for or contribute to burial
  expenses

[240-3](../../240/240-3_payment.md#240-3_F._CONTRIBUTIONS)
 Payment

- Clarified
  that contributions made by BIA GA, and other related or non-related
  individuals will be deducted from the GRA burial approval amount

[240-4](../../240/240-4_prior_resources.md)
 Prior Resources

- Clarified
  that GRA burial applications must be pended for application of prior
  resources even if it will take time for determination of eligibility
  for the prior resource
