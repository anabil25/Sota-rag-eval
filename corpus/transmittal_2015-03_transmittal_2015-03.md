Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2015-03

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: December 1, 2015

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised. Highlights of the revisions are described below.

If you have any questions, please contact Field Services at [dpafield@alaska.gov](mailto:dpafield@alaska.gov) or the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

100      General Information

[100-6A](../100/100-6_interpreter_services.md#100-6_A._LIMITED_ENGLISH_SPEAKING_INDIVIDUALS) Interpreter Services

- Update DPA Contracted Telephone Interpreter Service from Language Line Services to CTS.

101      The Application Process

[101-2E](../101/101-2_forms.md#101-2_E._ARIES_SSP__SELF_SERVICE_PORTAL__FORM) Application Forms

- Remove GEN 75 – review application for Denali Kidcare.
- Update section "G" for MED 4 Form to section "E."

101-2F Application Forms

- Remove GEN 132 – initial application for Denali Kidcare
- Add FFM as an electronic MAGI based Medicaid application for any Medicaid category for adults and children submitted through Federally Facilitated Marketplace to the list of DPA Application Forms.

101-2G Application Forms

- Add SSP as an electronic application for any Medicaid category for adults and children through Self Service Portal on My Alaska to the list of DPA Application Forms.

105      Verification Procedures

[105-14A](../105/105-14_systematic_alien_verification_for_entitlements_-_electronic_verification_process.md#105-14_A._SYSTEMATIC_ALIEN_VERIFICATION_FOR_ENTITLEMENT__SAVE_) Verification Information System (VIS)

- Refer to DPA Interface User Guide for information on accessing SAVE through Verification Information System (VIS)

108      Notices

[108-5](../108/108-5_eis_notice_listing.md) EIS Notice Listing

- Update the list of EIS notices, removing inactive notices as well as adding new notices.

[108-6](../108/108-6_aries_correspondences.md) ARIES Correspondence Listing

- Add new section for ARIES Correspondences, listing auto and manual generated correspondences

117      Right to a Fair Hearing

[117-1B](../117/117-1_fair_hearing_requst.md#117-1_B._HOW_TO_REQUEST_A_FAIR_HEARING) How to Request a Fair Hearing

- Add [dpa.csqa@alaska.gov](mailto:dpa.csqa@alaska.gov) email when sending fair hearing requests to Contracted Service Quality Assurance Unit.

[117-4B](../117/117-4_pre_hearing_conference.md#117-4_B._RESPONSIBILITIES_OF_THE_SUPERVISOR__SITE_MANAGER__OR_REGIONAL_MANAGER) Responsibilities of the Supervisor, Site Manager, or Regional Manager

- Add [dpa.csqa@alaska.gov](mailto:dpa.csqa@alaska.gov) email when forwarding fair hearing requests to DPA hearing representatives in Contracted Service Quality Assurance Unit.

Addendum

[109-A](../addenda/addendum_109_a_-_reserved.md) Addendum CANO Format

- Update Work Requirements/Disability/Penalty to include ABAWD participation in the household, document any countable ABAWD months used, and identify any exemptions from ABAWD participation
