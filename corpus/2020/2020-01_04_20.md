STATE
OF ALASKA

Department
of Health and Social Services

Division
of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2020-01

MANUAL:
Senior Benefit Program

DATE:
April 1, 2020

We have revised the
Senior Benefits Program Manual with updated Federal Poverty Levels for
Alaska. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications
that are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these
revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact
the Policy and Program Development Team by e-mail at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[310-1](../../310__application/310-1_the_application_form.md)
     THE APPLICATION FORM

- Added that
  Senior Benefits will accept the GEN 72 to determine continued eligibility.
  This change is effective with the April 2020 form revision.

[324-6](../../324_citizenship/324-6_documentation_of_u.s._citizenship_and_eligible_alien_status.md)
     DOCUMENTATION OF U.S. CITIZENSHIP AND ELIGIBLE
ALIEN STATUS

- Added GEN 72
  as an acceptable recertification application for Senior Benefits effective
  April 1, 2020

[330-1](../../330__income/330-1_income_limits.md)
     INCOME LIMITS

- Updated income
  limits based on the 2020 FPLFederal Poverty Limits
  changes that are effective April 1, 2020

[360-1](../../360__case_maintenance/360-1_reviews.md)
     REVIEWS

- Clarified that
  a GEN 72 can be used to recertify Senior Benefits
