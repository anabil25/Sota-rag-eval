**STATE OF ALASKA**

**Department of Health and Social Services**

**Division of Public Assistance**

**TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:**   2020-02

**MANUAL:**   Alaska Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program

**DATE:**   September 1, 2020

This update includes yearly changes to the income and utility standards that take effect October 1, 2020. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](../dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

**OVERVIEW OF CHANGES**

**[MS 601-2(D)](../../601/601-2_the_application_form.md#D._WHAT_IS_AN_IDENTIFIABLE_APPLICATION_)           What is an Identifiable Application?**

- - Clarified that a post office deliverable address (residential or mailing) is required for an application to be considered identifiable

**[MS 601-3(C)](../../601/601-3.md#601_3_C_WHO_CAN_CONDUCT_THE_INTERVIEW_)       What Can Conduct the Interview?**

- - Clarified that a NFAPNative Family Assistance Program agency not allowed to conduct a SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program interview

**[MS 601-3(E)](../../601/601-3.md#601_3_E_SCHEDULING_INTERVIEWS)       Scheduling Interviews**

- - Clarified that sending an EISEligibility Information System notice informing the applicant of their interview date and time is only required if the applicant has not agreed to a scheduled interview during a phone conversation or been provided with a printout from appointment plus or the "What do I need to bring to my interview?" sheet in the GEN 50CApplication for Services

**[MS 602-1(A)](../../602/602-1_a.md#602_1_A_5__Excluded_Household_Members)       Households**

- - Clarified what verifications can be used to verify that a conviction falls under U.S. Code Title 18 Chapter 10

**[MS 602-2(B)](../../602/602-2_b.md)       Exempt Resources**

- - Clarified that money held in an approved Medicaid Qualifying Trust does not count as a resource to the individual

**[MS 602-2(D)](../../602/602-2_d.md#602_2_D_6_Significant_Lottery_or_Gaming_Winnings)       Other Household Resources**

- - Added substantial lottery and game winnings policy as required by the Farm Bill

**[MS 602-3(B)](../../602/602-3_b.md#602_3_B_7__Other_Sources_of_Countable_Unearned_Income)       Unearned Income**

- - Added substantial lottery and gaming winnings policy as required by the Farm Bill
  - Clarified that income deposited directly into an approved Medicaid Qualifying Trust does not count as income to the individual

**[MS 602-4(E)](../../602/604-2_e.md#602_4_E_6._Homeless_Shelter_Deduction)       Shelter Deduction**

- - Updated the homeless shelter deduction from $152 to $157

**[MS 604-2](../../604/604-2.md)       Recertification**

- - Clarified that a GEN 50CApplication for Services or GEN 72Eligibility review application is received, it must be used to establish a new certification period for all open programs

**[MS 604-3](../../604/604-3.md)       Reporting and Acting on Changes During the Certification Period**

- - Added substantial lottery and gaming winnings as a change that must be reported and acted on as required by the Farm Bill

**[MS 605-3(B)](../../605/605-3_b.md)       Expedited Service**

- - Removed information regarding the Postpone Interview Waiver as this is no longer allowed by FNSFood and Nutrition Service

**[MS 607-1](../../607/607-1.md)** **Calculating the Correct Benefit**

- - Updated Example 1 to reflect 10-10-10 and adverse action rules that still apply when processing changes

**[MS 608-1](../../608/608-1.md)       Fair Hearings**

- - Clarified if the household requests for a fair hearing before the effective date of action, we should be continuing benefits automatically unless the household indicates that they do not want continued benefits

**[Addendum 2](../../addenda/addendum_2.md)       ABAWD Work Requirement Exempt and Non-Exempt Areas**

- - Added Marshall under Kusilvak Census Area

**[Addendum 4](../../addenda/addendum_4.md)       SNAP Standards and Maximum Allotments**

- - Updated the FFYFederal Fiscal Year 2021 maximum allotments and utility standards released by FNSFood and Nutrition Service

**[Addendum 9](../../addenda/addendum_9.md)       Approved Substance Abuse Programs**

- - Updated the phone number for the Salvation Army Adult Rehabilitation Program
  - Changed the name of Haven House to Sitka Counseling and updated their address

**Addendum 10       Alaska Permanent Fund Dividend EIS Notice for PFD Hold Harmless**

- - Removed postponed interview notices
