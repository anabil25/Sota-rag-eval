Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #47

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  April 2, 2018

We have updated the Program Standards and Pickle Amendment Chart for 2018.  We have also updated other sections that use Federal Poverty Level guidelines for income eligibility.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at  [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:DPAPolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 520-M](../../520/520_elig_req_apa_policy.md#520_M._________CROWD_SOURCING)  Eligibility Requirements That Follow APA

[MS 528-A](../../528/528_processing_cases_with_trusts.md#528_A_INITIAL_CASE_PROCESSING)  Initial Case Processing

- Added trust approval forms are now located on e-forms
- Added that only Medicaid Qualifying Trust documents need to be sent to Policy

[MS 533](../../533/533_tefra.md)  Disabled Children at Home (TEFRATax Equity and Fiscal Responsibility Act, P.L. 97-248)

- Changed Long Term Care Coordinator to TEFRATax Equity and Fiscal Responsibility Act, P.L. 97-248/Specialized Medicaid PAA throughout the chapter

[MS 534-K](../../534/534_working_disabled_medicaid_buy-in.md#534_K__EXAMPLE_OF_AN_ELIGIBILITY_DETERMINATION)  Example of an Eligibility Determination

- Updated this section to reflect the 2018 Federal Poverty Level changes
