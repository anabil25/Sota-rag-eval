Administrative Procedures Manual

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2023-03

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: December 1, 2023

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have questions, please contact Division of Operations Support Team at [hss.dpadost@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpadost@alaska.gov) or the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 100-3 H](../100/100-3_confidentiality.md#100-3_H._REQUESTS_FOR_INFORMATION_FROM_OTHER_AGENCIES)      REQUESTS FOR INFORMATION FROM OTHER AGENCIES

- Removed the Child Care Program Office (CCPO) from the list of other agencies that exchange information with [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance without a written release of information from the client since they are part of [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance.

[MS 101-2 B](../101/101-2_forms.md#101-2_B._GEN_72_FORM)      GEN 72 FORM

- Removed statement that the [GEN 72](#)Eligibility Review Form is used by the Office of Children's Services (OCS) due to a Medicaid renewal process change.

[MS 108-5](../108/108-5_eis_notice_listing.md)      EIS NOTICE LISTING

- Removed X007 as it is no longer in use due to an [OCS](#)Office of Children's Services Medicaid renewal process change.

[MS 122-3](../122/122-3_excessive_card_replacements.md)      EXCESSIVE CARD REPLACEMENTS

- Revised excessive card replacements as being four or more cards issued within the past twelve (12) months to align with federal rules.

[ADDENDUM 109 F](../addenda/addendum_109_f_-_dpa_acronyms.md)      DPA ACRONYMS

- Added acronyms related to fair hearings: Office of Administrative Hearings (OAH), Administrative Law Judge (ALJ), Alaska Legal Services Corporation (ALSC), and Office of Public Advocacy (OPA).
