Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #9

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: April 1, 2019

We have updated the income standards for the MAGI Medicaid categories that are effected by the change in Federal Poverty Guidelines effective April 1, 2019. We are also making several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field. An overview of the changes are listed below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[818](../../818_magi_medicaid_income/818_magi_medicaid_income.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Income

- Updated tax filing threshold for tax dependents to 2019 limit
- Corrected section title to match table of contents

[833](../../833_hospital_presumptive_eligibility/833_hosptial_presumptive_eligibility_determination.md) Hospital Presumptive Eligibility

- Added "Ladies First" to the BCHCBreast and Cervical Health Check program title as Alaska has updated the [BCHC](#)Breast and Cervical Health Check program to be called "Ladies First". Continued to use BCHCBreast and Cervical Health Check in the title as the federal government has not changed the name of the [BCHC](#)Breast and Cervical Health Check program to Ladies First.
- Updated income standards due to the Federal Poverty Guidelines change effective April 1, 2019
- Corrected table to contents to match section title

[Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum-1_magi_medicaid_income_standards.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Income Standards

- Updated income standards due to the Federal Poverty Guidelines change effective April 1, 2019

[Addendum 2](../../addenda/addendum-2_magi_medicaid_subtypes.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Subtypes

- Added (Ladies First) in parenthesis after BCHCBreast and Cervical Health Check as Alaska has updated the BCHC program to be called "Ladies First". The Federal Government has not changed the name of the BCHCBreast and Cervical Health Check program.

[Addendum 3](../../addenda/addendum-3_countable_and_excluded_income_for_magi_medicaid.md) Countable and Excluded Income for MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

- Added information about TCOLATerritorial Cost of Living Allowance
