Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #13

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: September 1, 2020

We have made several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field. An overview of the changes are listed below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[806-1](../../806_application_and_renewal_process/806-1_the_application.md) The Application

- Added a note clarifying that boarding schools or hospitals applying for Medicaid benefits on behalf of a child (when the stay is less than 30 days) must provide a signed statement from the child’s parent(s) or caretaker relative allowing them to complete and submit the Medicaid application

[808-6](../../808_us_citizenship_and_eligible_alien_status/808-6_proof_of_united_states_citizenship_and_qualified_alien_status.md) Proof of United States Citizenship and Qualified Alien Status

- Revised section to clarify that documentary evidence issued by a federally recognized tribe verifies US citizenship as long as the document identifies the Federally recognized Indian Tribe that issued the document, identifies the individual by name; and confirms the individual’s membership, enrollment, or affiliation with the Tribe.

[815-1](../../815_residents_of_institutions/815-1_children_living_in_residential_treatment_centers_over_30_days.md) Hospitalized Children Receiving Treatment Over 30 Days

- Added link to the clarification in 806-1 regarding applications from a hospital when the child’s stay is less than 30 days

[816-1 F](../../816_magi_medicaid_categories/816-1_magi_category_eligibility_factors.md#816-1_f._fORMER_FOSTER_CARE__FFC__) Former Foster Care

- Added a note clarifying that if the FFCFormer Foster Care child is potentially eligible for a Medicaid category higher on the hierarchy list, they must be asked to provide all of the information needed to make an eligibility decision in that category prior to having Medicaid authorized in the FFCFormer Foster Care category

[819 C](../../819_types_of_income/819_types_of_income.md#819_C._OTHER_INCOME) Rental Income

- Revised section to remove reference to “owner” as rental income is countable even if the person receiving the rent does not own the property

[820-6](../../820_budgeting_income/820-6_specialized_budgeting.md) Specialized Budgeting

- Added clarification for PFDPermanent Fund Dividend based on addition of the Specialized Budgeting section added in [MC #12](mc__12_(04_20).md)

[Addendum 3](../../addenda/addendum-3_countable_and_excluded_income_for_magi_medicaid.md) Countable and Excluded Income for MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

- Added clarification for Missionary Income
- Added link the MAGI MS 820-6 B for PFDPermanent Fund Dividend budgeting
