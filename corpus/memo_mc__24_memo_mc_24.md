STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #24

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  January 4, 2010

Previously policy required applicants attend an interview, meaning they had to appear in person. Since face-to-face interviews are no longer required, the word “attend” has been changed to “participate”.

Written verification such as an individual’s tax returns or business records is the preferred method to verify self-employment income. We revised the self-employment chapter to clarify that verbal verification of self-employment income and expenses is acceptable when written verification is not available.

We updated the Interim Assistance chapter to clarify when a new medical review is required. If an Interim Assistance case closes and a new application is submitted at a later date, it is not necessary to request a new medical review (including requesting a new AD 2, IAR and GEN 151a’s) at reapplication if there was no final SSI decision during the time the Interim Assistance case was closed. A new disability assessment by the Medical Reviewer is only required if a new SSI application was submitted during the individual’s period of ineligibility.

Other highlights to policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or e-mail dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

- Replaces the word "attend" with "participate" to reflect that face to face interviews are no longer required.

[MS 410-2C](../../410/410-2_the_application_form.md#410_2_C__WHEN_IS_AN_APPLICATION_NOT_REQUIRED_)   When is an Application Not Required?

- Adds a note to clarify how APA is registered when an SSISupplemental Security Income child on Medicaid turns 18.
- Adds link to Administrative Manual Section 103-3 & 124-3.

[MS 410-4](../../410/410-4_verification.md)   Verification

- In the Direct Data Systems table, replaces the Automatic Status Verification System (ASVS ) with Verification Information System (VIS ). The VISVerification Information System is the online electronic verification system used by the SAVESystematic Alien Verification for Entitlements Program.

[MS 425-2 A](../../425/425-2_who_determines_disability_blindness.md#425_2_A_TEMPORARY_DISABILITY_OR_BLINDNESS_DECISIONS)  Who Determines Disability/Blindness

- Adds licensed clinical psychologist with a PhD as a physician who can complete the AD #2  form.

[MS 426-2C](../../426/426-2_eligibility_requirements_for_interim_assistance.md#426_2_C__1__APA_applicants_who_are_eligible_to_receive_Social_Security) Interim Blindness or Disability Determination

- Clarifies that a new medical review is not needed for IAInterim Assistance applicants who have not received a final SSISupplemental Security Income decision while their IAInterim Assistance case was closed.
- Adds licensed clinical psychologist with a PhD as a physician who can complete the AD #2  form.

[MS 432-4 N](../../432/432-4_retained_cash_payments_and_in-kind_payments.md#432_4_N_WORLD_WAR_II_RESTITUTION_FUNDS) World War II Restitution Payments

- Clarifies that Aleut reparation payments received from a trust established by the Secretary of the Interior are countable resources if retained

[MS 432-4O](../../432/432-4_retained_cash_payments_and_in-kind_payments.md#432_4_O_AGENT_ORANGE_SETTLEMENT_FUNDS) Agent Orange Settlement Funds

- Adds the date Agent Orange settlement payments ended.

[MS  441-1 D](../../441/441-1_earned_income.md#441_1_E_NET_EARNINGS_FROM_SELF_EMPLOYMENT) Net Earnings from Self-Employment

- Clarifies that verbal verification of self-employment income and expenses is allowed.

[MS 442-3A(10)](../../442/442-3_unearned_income_exclusions.md#442_3_A_10__World_War_II_restitution_payments) World War II Restitution Payments

[MS 442-3A(11)](../../442/442-3_unearned_income_exclusions.md#442_3_A_11__Agent_Orange_settlement_payments) Agent Orange Settlement Payments

- Adds the date Agent Orange settlement payments ended. Also clarifies VADepartment of Veterans Affairs disability payments for diseases related to Agent Orange exposure are not exempt.
