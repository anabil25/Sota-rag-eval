1003-5          ENTERING WORK ACTIVITIES

Once verification activities has been received and the case manager has confirmed that the documentation meets the standards described above and in the Work Verification Plan, see [WSPM](#)Work Services Procedure Manual [MS 1003-3](1003-3_work_verification.md), the hard-copy verification must be placed in the client's file. The case manager enters a [CLNO](#)Client Notes describing the completed activities and the activity hours are then entered onto the Work Activity screen in [CMS](#)Case Management System.

Total monthly hours for each code may only be rounded up once, after all calculations have been completed.

Example:

3.5 hours of work search a day for 15 days = (52.5 rounded to 53) NOT 60 (3.5 rounded to 4 hours x 15).

If the [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program case closes, the case manager is responsible for the follow-up with the client to confirm and enter any work activity hours or employment hours before closing the Work Services case and the Work Activity screen in [CMS](#)Case Management System.

Detailed instruction on entering work activities into [CMS](#)Case Management System can be found in section eight of the [CMS](#)Case Management System User Guide.
