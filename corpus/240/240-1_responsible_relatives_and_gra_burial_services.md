240-1         RESPONSIBLE RELATIVES AND GRAGeneral Relief Assistance BURIAL SERVICES

Certain relatives are considered legally responsible for the funeral and burial expenses of a deceased person.  (Refer to section [220-1F](../220/220-1_non-financial_eligibility.md#220-1_F._LEGALLY_RESPONSIBLE_RELATIVES)) Relative responsibility applies to funeral and burial assistance as follows:

A.  RESPONSIBLE RELATIVES LIVING WITH THE DECEASED AT THE TIME OF DEATH

Responsible relatives living with the deceased at the time of death include all persons who are related to the deceased person by blood, marriage, or adoption, who are age 18 or older. The caseworker must consider the income and resources of all relatives living with the deceased at the time of death when determining eligibility for funeral and burial assistance.

**Note:**

Burial applications will include the income of the deceased and their responsible relatives living with the deceased at the time of death.  This countable income applies to reapplications in the month after death or after a previous application has been denied.

B. RESPONSIBLE RELATIVES NOT LIVING WITH THE DECEASED AT THE TIME OF DEATH

The caseworker shall attempt to contact any legally responsible relatives not living with the deceased person at the time of death and explain to them their responsibility to either provide for the funeral and burial expenses, or to reimburse the state for any burial assistance provided to the deceased under the GRAGeneral Relief Assistance program.

If a legally responsible relative indicates inability to pay for or contribute to funeral and burial expenses, the reason why must be documented.

If the responsible relative agrees to provide for the funeral and burial expenses of the deceased, eligibility for assistance does not exist.  However, funeral and burial assistance shall not be delayed or denied if a legally responsible relative cannot be located, or if the relative refuses or claims to be unable to provide for the funeral and burial expenses of the deceased.

Note:

We cannot take client statement from the person participating in the interview that other legally responsible relatives cannot pay for or contribute to funeral and burial expenses.  An attempt must be made to contact each legally responsible relative to ask about their ability to pay for or contribute to funeral and burial expenses.  These contacts must be documented.
