420           DEVELOPMENT OF INCOME

To be eligible for Adult Public Assistance, an applicant or recipient must apply for and diligently pursue any other benefits for which the Division believes he or she may be eligible. This chapter explains the development of income requirements of the APAAdult Public Assistance program.
