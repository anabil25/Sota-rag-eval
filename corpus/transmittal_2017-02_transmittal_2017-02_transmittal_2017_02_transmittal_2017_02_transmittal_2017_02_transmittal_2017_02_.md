Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2017-02

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: June 15, 2017

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised. Highlights of the revisions are described below.

If you have any questions, please contact Field Services at [dpafield@alaska.gov](mailto:dpafield@alaska.gov) or the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

- Changed Food Stamp to SNAP throughout the manual as the program name has changed
- Changed QARC to CAPS throughout the manual as the quality assurance review process has changed
- Removed DKC reference and/or replaces DKC and Family Medicaid with MAGI Medicaid throughout the manual as the Family Medicaid program ended December 31, 2013.

100      General Information

[100-3H](../100/100-3_confidentiality.md#100-3_H._REQUESTS_FOR_INFORMATION_FROM_OTHER_AGENCIES) Requests for Information from Other Agencies

- Added Yukon Kuskokwim Health Corporation (YKHC) to the list of agencies that have a signed MOA with DPA
- Clarified what information DPA is not allowed to release to other agencies, even with a signed MOA
- Added Child Support Services Division (CSSD) to the list of agencies that DPA can release public assistance information without a written consent from the client

[100-6A](../100/100-6_interpreter_services.md#100-6_A._LIMITED_ENGLISH_SPEAKING_INDIVIDUALS) Limited English Speaking Individuals

- Changed DPA contracted telephone interpreter services to Telelanguage as this changed May 1, 2017

101      The Application Process

[101-5A](../101/101-5_where_to_submit_an_application.md#101-5_A._DPA_OFFICES) DPA Offices

- Removed office listing in this section and replaced with a link to the official list of DPA office
- Added link to the online application

[101-5D](../101/101-5_where_to_submit_an_application.md#101-5_D._LOCATION_OF_APPLICATIONS) Location of Applicants

- Updated section explaining which office processes an application and case assignment as the process has changed

113      Quality Assessment

[113-2](../113/113-2_method_of_quality_assessment_review.md) Method of Quality Assessment Review

- Clarified that all QA reviews are assigned to the office that the case is CARC'd to when sample is pulled for review

[113-3](../113/113-3_quality_assessment_feedback.md) Quality Assessment Feedback

- Revised this entire section to explain the new CAPS process and clarify how the QA Reviewer reviews and documents errors or findings, and uploads to the CAPS folder

[113-4](../113/113-4_corrective_action_planning_spreadsheet_(caps).md) Corrective Action Planning Spreadsheet (CAPS)

- Removed section explaining the Quality Assessment Review Committee (QARC) and replaced it with a new section describing the Corrective Action Planning Spreadsheet (CAPS) as the quality review process has changed
- Explained the CAPS process and what corrective action must be taken on case errors

[113-8](../113/113-8_fraud_referrals.md) Fraud Referrals

- Updated that field staff will consider any QA fraud referral recommendation during the discussion of the case

[113-9](../113/113-9_q-form_process.md) Q-Forms

- Clarified that the QA Supervisor will upload the Q-Forms to the QA SharePoint site in the monthly CAPS folder
