1010-1          BENEFITS OF RECEIVING [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income / [SSDI](#)Social Security Disability Insurance

1. Although every case is different, [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program recipients who are approved for [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income typically receive:

- Increased income;
- Access to vocational retraining programs and supported work programs; and,
- Non time-limited eligibility for cash and medical assistance.

1. Households receiving [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program as well as [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income often have a higher total income because [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income payments are not counted as income to the rest of the household.
2. [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance benefits through an [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program caseload reduction.
