1010          SOCIAL SECURITY DISABILITY SCREENING AND ADVOCACY

Supplemental Security Income (SSI) and Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) are disability benefit programs administered by the Social Security Administration (SSA). Screening for a client's potential referral for these programs is a required part of a Work Services provider's due diligence. If medical documentation or assessments support the likelihood that a client may qualify, [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income / [SSDI](#)Social Security Disability Insurance may be used as the employment goal on the [FSSP](#)Family Self Sufficiency Plan, see [MS 1002-2](../1002/1002-2_components_of_the_fssp.md).

[SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income and [SSDI](#)Social Security Disability Insurance approval counts in the performance "Cases That Closed with Earnings" and "Case That Don't Return," see [MS 1003-1](../1003/1003-1_performance_measures.md#1003-1_C._PERFORMANCE_MEASURES_DEFINITIONS).
