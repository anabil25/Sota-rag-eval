102-6          INTERVIEWS
IN REMOTE AREAS

Those household residing in remote areas of Alaska
where a caseworker or Fee Agent is not available to meet the program's
interview requirement may meet the requirement with a telephone interview.
The caseworker must document the circumstances and the eligibility decision
in the case record. Such documentation and resulting eligibility decisions
should be re-examined if a DPADivision of Public Assistance
staff member visits the community in which the household resides.
