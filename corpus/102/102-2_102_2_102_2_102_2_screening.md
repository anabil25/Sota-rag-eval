102-2          SCREENING

Prior to the interview or processing a mail-in application, a knowledgeable DPADivision of Public Assistance staff member may screen the application to:

- Provide the client with a basic understanding of what type of verification materials to bring to the eligibility interview;
- Refer the applicant to other community resources; and
- Schedule an eligibility interview, if required.

Note:

Each SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program application must be screened for entitlement to expedited service the same day the application is received in the District Office.

Clerical staff may screen for entitlement to expedited service by reviewing the three questions on page 7 of the GEN 50CApplication for Services.
