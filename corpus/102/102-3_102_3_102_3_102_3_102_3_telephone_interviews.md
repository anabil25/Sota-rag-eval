102-3          TELEPHONE
INTERVIEWS

Interviews can be conducted face-to-face, by phone
or, in rare circumstances when a face-to-face or phone interview is not
possible, by correspondence. The caseworker may require a face-to-face
interview if they determine it is appropriate to the situation unless
attending in person is a hardship to the household. A face-to-face interview
must be requested by the household.

When conducting telephone interviews, staff must
verify the identity of the person over the phone to ensure client confidentiality.
Staff must ask the person to verify the last four digits of their Social
Security Number and date of birth, and ensure that it matches with the
information the applicant provided on their application. This information
must also be documented in EISEligibility Information System
CANOCase Notes.

Refer to the [CANO Desk
Guide](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/node/37) posted on the DPAweb under eForms for additional information
on documenting this information.
