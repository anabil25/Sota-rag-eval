102-5          CONSEQUENCES
OF NO INTERVIEW

If the household refuses to participate in an interview
and is unable to provide good cause for such action, the application must
be denied according to the program's
requirements. "Refuses to participate" means that there
is clear evidence the household is able to complete an interview, but
demonstrates that it will not take the actions required to complete the
process. Refer to the appropriate policy
manual for the program's interview requirements.
