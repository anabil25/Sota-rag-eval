PERSONAL ALLOWANCE DEDUCTIONS FOR DEEMING INCOME

The personal allowance deduction for a stepparent, sponsor of an alien, or parent of a minor parent whose income is deemed available to the assistance unit is:

1. $1,192 if the deemor does not have any other dependents in the home for whom he or she receives an allowance; or
2. The basic adult-included need standard if the deemor is entitled to an allowance for themselves and at least one other dependent in the home. Use the one caretaker relative column in the Adult-Included Need Standards table to determine the appropriate allowance amount.
