STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #38

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  December 2, 2013

This year Social Security announced a Cost of Living Allowance (COLA) increase effective January 2014. The Adult Public Assistance need and maximum payment standards are adjusted by the same percentage as the COLACost of Living Allowance increase in Social Security benefits. This year the COLACost of Living Allowance increase is 1.5%. We have incorporated the 2014 standards into the examples and [Addendum 1.](../../addendum/addendum_1.md)  These standards become effective with the benefit month of January 2014. The COLACost of Living Allowance adjustment also causes:

1. - An increase in the income limit for Substantial Gainful Activity; and
   - An increase in the student earned income exclusion.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 433-1](../../433/433-1_transfer_of_resources.md)  Transfer of Resources

- Updates examples to reflect the 2014 need and maximum payment standards.

[MS 433-2 A](../../433/433-2_conditional_benefits.md) Eligibility for Conditional Benefits

- Updates the SSISupplemental Security Income payment standard to the new 2014 amount in order to accurately calculate if an applicant is eligible for conditional benefits.

[MS 452](../../452/452_payments.md)  Payments

- Updates examples to reflect the 2014 need and maximum payment standards.

[MS 460-5](../../460/460-5_income_allocations.md)  Income Allocations

- Updates examples and standards to reflect the 2014 need and maximum payment standards.

[Addendum 1](../../addendum/addendum_1.md)  Program Standards

- Updates program standards to reflect the COLACost of Living Allowance changes for 2014.
