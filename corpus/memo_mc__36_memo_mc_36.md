STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   MC #36

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  March 8, 2013

This manual change updates program policy in conjunction with changes to the Medicaid, Alaska Temporary Assistance, and Food Stamp manuals regarding good faith efforts to sell property.

Highlights to policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 426 2 (F)](../../426/426-2_eligibility_requirements_for_interim_assistance.md#426_2_F_CONTACTING_THE_MEDICAL_REVIEWER) CONTACTING THE MEDICAL REVIEWER

- Updates the Medical Reviewer's address from Suite 814 to Suite 460.

[MS 433-2 G](../../433/433-2_conditional_benefits.md#433_2_G_DEFINITION_OF_REASONABLE_EFFORTS) DEFINITION OF REASONABLE EFFORTS

- Adds a note clarifying good faith efforts to sell property, gives examples to include examples of "good faith effort" sale listings, i.e. Craigslist, online realtor, newspaper ads, etc
