STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #39

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  December 1, 2015

Included in this manual change is the recent clarification from CMSCenters for Medicare & Medicaid Services that Alaska Native and American Indian Real Property is excluded as a resource for Medicaid.

This year Social Security announced that there will be no COLA (Cost of Living Allowance) increase for 2016.  Social Security did update the Substantial Gainful Activity (SGA) limit which is added to Addendum 1.

Highlights of these revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team by e-mail [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[524-L](../../524/524_medicaid_except_to_resource.md#524_L.______ALASKA_NATIVE_AND_AMERICAN_INDIAN_REAL_PROPERTY_RESOURCE_EXCLUDED)       ALASKA NATIVE AND AMERICAN INDIAN REAL PROPERTY RESOURCE EXCLUDED

- Adds clarification that all real property including land and any structures built on the land, owned by an Alaska Native and American Indian is excluded as a resource.

[554-C](../../554/554_transfer_of_assets.md#554_C_Transfer_of_Asset_Rules_Apply)      TRANSFERS OF ASSET RULES APPLY

- Adds clarification that all real property owned by an Alaska Native or American Indian is excluded from being a countable resource or as a potential transfer of asset penalty.

- Updated 2016 standards.

- Updated 2016 standards.
