SNAP - Alaska SNAP Manual

**STATE OF ALASKA**

**Department of Health**

**Division of Public Assistance**

**TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:**   2023-02

**MANUAL:**   Alaska Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program

**DATE:**   September 1, 2023

This update includes yearly changes to the income and utility standards that take effect October 1, 2023. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

**OVERVIEW OF CHANGES**

**[MS 601-4(A)](../../601/601-4.md#601_4_A_VERIFICATION_REQUIREMENTS_FOR_INITIAL_APPLICATIONS)           Verification Requirements for Initial Applications**

- Clarified IPV information is now checked in [IEVS](#)Instant Eligibility Verification System, not [eDRS](#)Electronic Disqualified Recipient System

**[MS 602-1(A)(5)](../../602/602-1_a.md#602_1_A_5__Excluded_Household_Members)       Excluded Household Members**

- Removed mention of HowWeGuide.  The Division no longer uses this guide
- Clarified an individual is potentially ineligible for [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program if they are convicted of a drug related felony that occurred on or before 2/7/14.  We need to first look at the date the crime occurred.

**[MS 602-1(L)](../../602/602-1_l.md)       Able-Bodied Adults Without Dependents**

- Updated [ABAWD](#)Able-bodied Adult Without Dependents time limits based on the Fiscal Responsibility Act of 2023
- Updated ABAWD exceptions based on the Fiscal Responsibility Act of 2023
- Updated ABAWD 36-month period to match current 36-month period
- Updated note that explains Alaska is ABAWD exempt through October 31, 2023

**[MS 602-2(B)](../../602/602-2_b.md#602_2_B_4__Retirement_and_Pension_Plans)[(4)](../../602/602-2_b.md#602_2_B_4__Retirement_and_Pension_Plans)       Exempt Resources**

- Clarified retirement accounts that are excluded from resources regardless of employment status

**[MS 602-4](../../602/604-2_e.md)       Deductions**

- Updated the FFY 2024 homeless shelter deduction released by [FNS](#)Food and Nutrition Service

**[MS 604-1(A)](../../604/604-1.md#604_1_A_LENGTH_OF_CERTIFICATION_PERIOD)      Certification Period**

- Amended section "When Assigning a 24-month Certification Period" to align with Interim Report requirements at mid-certification
- Added information regarding the division's change to authorize 12 and 24 month certification periods with the requirement to submit an interim report at the mid-point of the certification period
- Removed bullet "to align with other programs" for circumstances where the caseworker can provide a shorter certification period.  Statement does not align with federal SNAP certification length regulations

**[MS 604-2](../../604/604-2_interim_report.md)       Interim Report**

- Added information regarding the division's change to authorize 12 and 24 month certification periods with the requirement to submit an interim report at the mid-point of the certification period

**[MS 605-7(A)(2)](../../605/605-7_permanent_fund_processing.md#605-7_A._Verification_of_PFD_Income)      Verification of PFD Income**

- Clarified note section to reflect we should be using IEVS and not [INME](#)Inquiry Menu 9 to verify [PFD](#)Permanent Fund Dividend information

**[MS 606-3](../../606/606-3.md)       Replacing SNAP benefits**

- Clarified SNAP benefits that are lost due to a household misfortune are limited to household misfortunes such as a fire, flood, loss of electricity, equipment malfunction, or other disaster

**[607-3](../../607/607-3.md#607_3_E_ESTABLISHING_AN_OVERPAYMENT_CLAIM)       Claims**

- Added information regarding the requirement to establish claims before the last day of the quarter following the quarter in which the overpayment or trafficking incident was discovered

**[Addendum 4](../../addenda/addendum_4.md)      Program Standards and Maximum Allotments**

- Updated the FFY 2024 maximum allotments and utility standards released by FNS
