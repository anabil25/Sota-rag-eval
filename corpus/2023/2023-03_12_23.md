SNAP - Alaska SNAP Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

**STATE OF ALASKA**

**Department of Health**

**Division of Public Assistance**

**TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:**   2023-03

**MANUAL:**   Alaska Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program

**DATE:**   December 1, 2023

This year the Social Security Administration announced a [COLA](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/transmittals/2023-03_(12_23).md#)Cost of Living Allowance increase effective January 2024, which increased the payee fee that can be excluded as unearned income.

Highlights of this and other policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

**OVERVIEW OF CHANGES**

**[MS 600-2](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/600/600-2_definitions.md)           Definitions**

- Clarified work definition regarding [ABAWD](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/transmittals/2023-03_(12_23).md#)Able-bodied Adult Without Dependents

**[MS 601-3(A)(2)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/601/601-3.md#601_3_A_WHEN_IS_AN_INTERVIEW_REQUIRED_)       The Interview**

- Added link to [MS 604-3(C)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/604/604-2.md#604_3_C_RECERTIFICATION_) for clarity that households that may have the recertification interview waived are households in which all adult members are elderly and/or disabled and have no earned income.

**[MS 601-5(D)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/601/601-5.md#601_5_D_DENYING_THE_APPLICATION)       Denying the Application**

- Clarified that we cannot deny a [SNAP](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/transmittals/2023-03_(12_23).md#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program application when applicants verbally indicate they are over the income or resource limit during the interview

**[MS 602-1(B)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/602/602-1_b.md)       Residency**

- Clarified households that leave the state for purposes of residing in another state will have their cases closed for not meeting the residency requirement

**[MS 602-1(E)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/602/602-1_e.md)       Social Security Numbers**

- Removed reference to Addendum 12 as it is being removed so it's not available to the public

**[MS 602-1(F)(3)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/602/602-1_f.md)      ABAWD**

- Updated [ABAWD](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/transmittals/2023-03_(12_23).md#)Able-bodied Adult Without Dependents age from 18 through 49 to 18 through 52

**[MS 602-1(G)(5)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/602/602-1_g.md#5._ABAWDs)       ABAWDs**

- Updated [ABAWD](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/transmittals/2023-03_(12_23).md#)Able-bodied Adult Without Dependents exemptions to include discretionary exemptions, and homeless individuals, veterans, and individuals aging out of foster care as required by the Fiscal Responsibility Act of 2023.
- Updated link to read SNAP [MS 602-1G(2)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/602/602-1_g.md#602_1_G_2__Individuals_Exempt_from_Work_Requirements)

**[MS 602-1(L)(2) and (6)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/602/602-1_l.md)      Able Bodied Adults Without Dependents**

- Revised note to align with the new [ABAWD](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/transmittals/2023-03_(12_23).md#)Able-bodied Adult Without Dependents waiver time limit criteria beginning 11/01/2023 through 10/31/2024
- Added discretionary exemption policy that will be used during the current ABAWD waiver

**[MS 602-3(B)(2)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/602/602-3_b.md#602_3_B_2__SSI_SSA_VA_Income)       Unearned Income**

- Updated payee fees to reflect [COLA](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/transmittals/2023-03_(12_23).md#)Cost of Living Allowance change

**[602-4(E)(7)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/602/604-2_e.md#602_4_E_7.__Verification_of_Shelter_Costs)       Shelter Costs**

- Clarified verification of shelter costs must be verified if the information is more than 60 days old, even changes that have not changed by more than $25, to align with federal regulations

**[604-1](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/604/604-1.md)      Certification Period**

- Clarified households that are eligible for 24 month certification periods are households in which all adult household members are elderly or disabled and have no earned income

**[604-2(C)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/604/604-2_interim_report.md)      Interim Report: Household Fails To Act Or Requested Verifications Is Not Received**

- Revised to clarify the only time we close an individual with adequate notice is if the [IR](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/transmittals/2023-03_(12_23).md#)Interim Report
   is not received
- Clarified when households submit the IR untimely, but prior to the last day of the month in which it is due

**[604-3](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/604/604-2.md)      Recertification**

- Clarified that a new review/recertification period cannot be established for [SNAP](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/transmittals/2023-03_(12_23).md#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program/Medicaid when processing applications or recertifications for other programs
- Fixed link from 604-2B to 604-3B. This was changed due to the new Interim Report section being added to the manual

**[604-3(C)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/604/604-2.md#604_3_C_RECERTIFICATION_)      Recertification: Caseworker Actions**

- Clarified households that may have the recertification interview waived are households in which all adult members are elderly and/or disabled and have no earned income, to align with the approved waiver language

**[604-3(E)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/604/604-2.md#604_3_E_VERIFICATION_REQUIREMENTS)      Verification Requirements: Recertification**

- Clarified all income reported at recertification needs to be verified if the information is more than 60 days old, even changes that have not changed by more than $50, to align with federal regulations

**[605-7(A)(2)](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/605/605-7_permanent_fund_processing.md#605-7_A._Verification_of_PFD_Income)      PFD Interface**

- Clarified caseworkers must check [IEVS](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/transmittals/2023-03_(12_23).md#)Instant Eligibility Verification System, not [INME](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/transmittals/2023-03_(12_23).md#)Inquiry Menu 9, when verifying [PFD](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/transmittals/2023-03_(12_23).md#)Permanent Fund Dividend eligibility

**[Addendum 2](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/addenda/addendum_2.md)**

- Revised to align with the new [ABAWD](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/transmittals/2023-03_(12_23).md#)Able-bodied Adult Without Dependents waiver, effective 11/01/2023 - 10/31/2024

**[Addendum 11](../../../../../previous transmittals/transmittal_2023-3_1223/robohelp_rh2020/contents/addenda/addendum_11.md)**

- Clarified foster care income is exempt, dependent on nature of foster care payments, and household living circumstances

**Addendum 12**

- Removed Addendum 12 so the information is not available to the public
