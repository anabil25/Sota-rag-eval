361-4         SITUATIONS THAT REQUIRE TIMELY NOTICE

The Senior Benefits worker must provide timely notice in the following situations:

- - Action to terminate benefits.
  - Action to reduce benefits.
  - Action to suspend benefits.
  - Adverse action taken on a review application.
