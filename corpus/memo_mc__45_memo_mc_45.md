APA - Alaska Adult Public Assistance Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   MC #45

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  December 1, 2015

We are making changes to clarify the recent Medicaid exclusion of Alaska Native and American Indian real property exclusion does not apply to the Adult Public Assistance program.

Since there is no COLA increase for 2016 the figures remain the same as 2015.  Updated Addendum 1 with new SGA limit.

Added the PFD hold harmless section back to the manual.

Highlights to policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

[430](../../430/430_resources.md)          RESOURCES

- Adds a note to clarify that the change to Medicaid policy regarding Alaska Native and American Indian property does not apply to APA resources.

[431-1](../../431/431-1_real_property_and_property_rights.md)       REAL PROPERTY AND PROPERTY RIGHTS

- Adds a note to clarify that the APA excluded property is different from Medicaid policy regarding Alaska Native and American Indian real property.

[432-1E](../../432/432-1_real_property.md#432-1_E__NATIVE RESTRICTED LAND)      NATIVE RESTRICTED LAND

- Added a hyperlink to ADLTC MS 524-L for exempt Alaska Native and American Indian resources for Medicaid

[433-3](../../433/433-3_permanent_fund_dividend.md)        PERMANENT FUND DIVIDEND

- Added the PFD hold harmless section back into the APA manual.

[450-4](../../450/450-4_living_in_an_assisted_living_home.md)      LIVING IN AN ASSISTED LIVING HOME

- Updated the link to ET Resources where the Assisted Living Home and Nursing Home contact list is now house.

[Addendum 1](../../addendum/addendum_1.md)

- No COLA increase for 2016, updated Addendum 1 to reflect that.
