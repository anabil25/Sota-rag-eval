720-4          TWO-PARENT EXTENSION CRITERIA

Two-parent families reach the time limit if either one of the parents has received 60 months of assistance. In order for two-parent families to receive an extension, both parents must meet one of the extension criteria described [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 701-4](../701/701-4_extension_criteria.md). If a two-parent family includes a parent who does not meet any of the extension reasons, the family is not eligible for an extension.
