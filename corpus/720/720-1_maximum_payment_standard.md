720-1          MAXIMUM PAYMENT STANDARD

The maximum payment for a two-parent family is the same as for a family with one caretaker relative. The only exception to this policy is if one of the parents is incapacitated. In that situation, a higher payment standard is allowed. For more information on payment standards, see [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 780](../780/780_payments.md).
