STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #23

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  September 1, 2009

As a result of the Food and Nutrition Service approving the waiver of  face-to-face interviews for Alaska, changes have been made to the interview chapter to remove the requirement to document why the face-to-face interview was waived. This change was announced by broadcast on August 4 and is retroactive to June 1, 2009.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 410-3 A](../../410/410-3_the_interview.md#410_3_A____WHEN_IS_AN_INTERVIEW_REQUIRED_)  When Is An Interview Required

- Removes the requirement to conduct a face-to-face interview.

[MS 410-3 D](../../410/410-3_the_interview.md#410-3 D. HOW TO CONDUCT AN INTERVIEW)  When Can the Face-to-Face Interview Be Waived

- Changes title from "When Can the Face-to-Face Interview Be Waived" to "How To Conduct An Interview"
- Removes reasons a face-to-face interview can be waived.
- Clarifies interviews can be face-to-face, by phone or by correspondence.
- Clarifies caseworker must conduct a face-to-face interview if requested by the client.

[MS 410-3 E](../../410/410-3_the_interview.md#410-3_E__SCHEDULING_INTERVIEWS)  Scheduling Interviews

- Changes title from "Conducting Interviews" to "Scheduling Interviews"
