714-2          APPLICANTS RECEIVING ASSISTANCE FROM OTHER STATES

Applicants (including children) who have recently arrived in Alaska and who indicate they are receiving assistance from another state shall be eligible for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program in Alaska when they satisfy the residency requirement and are otherwise eligible. However, [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program benefits cannot be authorized until eligibility in the other state is terminated. Verification of out-of-state case closure is required.
