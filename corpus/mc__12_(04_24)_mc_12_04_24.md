TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #12

MANUAL: Work Services Procedure Manual

DATE: April 1, 2024

We have made made technical corrections and incorporated the changes outlined below:

If you have any questions, please contact the Work Services Policy team at WSTA@alaska.gov or the Policy & Program Development team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 1003-3 A. 6](../1003/1003-3_work_verification.md#1003-3_A._6._Subsistence) SUBSISTENCE

- Added a note describing communities in which subsistence is intended to be used as a work participation activity.

[MS 1003-3 A. 12](../1003/1003-3_work_verification.md#1003-3_A._12._Substance_Abuse,_Domestic_Violence_Counseling,_Mental_Health_and_Medical) SUBSTANCE ABUSE, DOMESTIC VIOLENCE, MENTAL HEALTH AND MEDICAL 

- Removed the reference to "six weeks or less" of treatment.

[MS 1005-3 A.](../1005/1005-3_community_(cwe)_and_business_work_experience_(bwe).md#1005-3_A._1._CWE_Site_Placement_and_Activities_Requirements) SITE PLACEMENT SPECIFICS; SUBSISTENCE

- Removed reference to subsistence under community work experience.

[MS 1018-5 A. 1.](../1018/1018-5_management_of_case_transfers.md#1018-5_A._CHANGE_OF_COMMUNITY) CHANGE OF COMMUNITY

- Allowed the [WSP](#)Work Services Provider to change the [CARC](#)Case Assignment Record Control upon confirmation that the client has reported the change to [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance.

[MS 1018-6](../1018/1018-6_reserved.md) GRIEVANCE PROCEDURE

- Deleted the Grievance procedure and reserved the section.
