STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #52

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  December 2, 2019

This year Social Security announced a Cost of Living Allowance (COLACost of Living Allowance) increase effective January 2020.  We have updated [Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum_1.md) and the examples throughout the manual with the following items to reflect the 1.6% increase for 2020:

- An increase to the Long Term Care community spouse resource and income allowance and household member standards;
- An increase to the Excess Home Equity resources maximum;
- An increase to the Special Long Term Care Income Standard;
- Changes in Medicare Part A and B premiums; and
- Changes in Medicare Part D premium and coverage information, including changes to the Low-Income Subsidy program

In addition to the COLACost of Living Allowance change, we have expanded the use of the Refused Cash (RCRefused Cash) Medicaid subtype effective with the benefit month of January 2020 to include individuals who have countable income above the appropriate APAAdult Public Assistance need/payment standard but below the Expanded Refused Cash monthly income limit listed in [ADLTC MS 530(C)](../../530/530_ssi_and_apa_related_categories.md#530_C._USE_OF_THE_REFUSED_CASH_MEDICAID_SUBTYPE).

This manual change also consists of several small technical changes or clarifications as a result of suggestions from the field. Highlights of these revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at e-mail [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).
