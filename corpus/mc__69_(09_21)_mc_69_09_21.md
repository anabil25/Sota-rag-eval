Alaska Temporary Assistance Program Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #69

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: September 1, 2021

The Alaska Temporary Assistance Program (ATAP) heating and non-heating utility standards change effective October 2021. The Eligibility Information System (EIS) will automatically apply these new standards when determining eligibility and benefit amounts beginning October 2021.

Highlights of all the policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 705-4 B](../705/705-4_verification.md#705-4_B._INFORMATION_FROM_DATA_SYSTEMS_AND_EIS_INTERFACES)      INFORMATION FROM DATA SYSTEMS AND [EIS](#)Eligibility Information System INTERFACES

- Removed [INGENS](#)Public Information Database as this is no longer a valid interface now that [IEVS](#)Instant Eligibility Verification System is available.
- Clarified that [IEVS](#)Instant Eligibility Verification System must be checked at application, recertification, and when an adult is being added to a case.

[MS 710-3](../710/710-3_disqualifying_conditions.md)      DISQUALIFYING CONDITIONS

- Clarified that an individual must meet one of the qualifying conditions for each drug felony conviction.
- Added examples of acceptable verification, including the Felony Verification form (GEN 157).

[MS 712-3 C](../712/712-3_parental_relationship.md#712-3_C._VERIFYING_PARENTAL_RELATIONSHIP_FOR_CHILDREN_BORN_IN_ANOTHER_STATE)      VERIFYING PARENTAL RELATIONSHIP FOR CHILDREN BORN IN ANOTHER STATE

- Added [HAVR](#)Health Analytics and Vital Records Paternity Specialist contact information since [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance is unable to provide clients with [HAVR](#)Health Analytics and Vital Records's Affidavit of Paternity form and the form is not readily available online.

[MS 712-3 D](../712/712-3_parental_relationship.md#712-3_D._THE_HEALTH_ANALYTICS_AND_VITAL_RECORDS_AFFIDAVIT_OF_PATERNITY)      THE HEALTH AND ANALYTICS AND VITAL RECORDS "AFFIDAVIT OF PATERNITY"

- Added [HAVR](#)Health Analytics and Vital Records Paternity Specialist contact information since [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance is unable to provide clients with [HAVR](#)Health Analytics and Vital Records's Affidavit of Paternity form and the form is not readily available online.
- Updated how to distribute original copies of the affidavit of paternity form to align with field processes.

[MS 717-6 A](../717/717-6_procedures_for_exchanging_information.md#717-6_A._APPLICATION_MATERIALS_TO_CSSD)      APPLICATION MATERIALS TO [CSSD](#)Child Support Services Division

- Added [HAVR](#)Health Analytics and Vital Records Paternity Specialist contact information since [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance is unable to provide clients with [HAVR](#)Health Analytics and Vital Records's Affidavit of Paternity form and the form is not readily available online.

[MS 752-10 B](../752/752-10_vehicles.md#752-10_B._COUNTABLE_VEHICLES)      COUNTABLE VEHICLES

- Revised example #2 to reflect the change from [INGENS](#)Public Information Database to [IEVS](#)Instant Eligibility Verification System.

[MS 770-7](../770/770-7_ineligible_drug_felons.md)      INELIGIBLE DRUG FELONS

- Clarified that an individual must meet one of the qualifying conditions for each drug felony conviction.
- Added examples of acceptable verification, including the Felony Verification form (GEN 157).

[MS 780-19 G](../780/780-19_shelter_allowance.md#780-19_G._HOMELESS_SHELTER_DEDUCTION)      HOMELESS SHELTER DEDUCTION

- Revised the homeless shelter deduction from $157 to $160.

[ADDENDUM 1](../addenda/addendum_1_-_exempt_alaska_native_villages.md)      EXEMPT ALASKA NATIVE VILLAGES

- Updated the "Exempt Alaska Native Villages" list based on the 2021 [DOL](#)Department of Labor and Workforce Development survey results.

[ADDENDUM 2](../addenda/addendum_2_-_program_standards.md)      [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program INCOME AND PAYMENT STANDARDS

- Updated the heating and non-heating utility standards for [FFY](#)Federal Fiscal Year 2022.
