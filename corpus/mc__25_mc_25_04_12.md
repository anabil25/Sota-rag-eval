TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #25

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled
and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  April 2, 2012

Due to
Federal review, the Department must restructure the current Older Alaskans
(coded AG in EISEligibility Information System) and
Adults with Physical Disabilities (coded DA in EISEligibility Information System)
waivers.  In EISEligibility Information System,
these Medicaid waiver codes are entered on the MEREMedical Reference
screen.  To eliminate the need for new waiver codes, the Department
has decided to keep the current four waiver codes (AG, DA, MC, and MD).
 We have updated [Section
515](../../515/515_cont_elig_and_child.md), [Section 533](../../533/533_tefra.md), [Section
534](../../534/534_working_disabled_medicaid_buy-in.md), [Section 560](../../560/560_hcb_waiver.md), [Section
561](../../561/561__eis_hcb_waiver_codes.md), [Section 562](../../562/562_processing_hcb_waiver.md),
and [Addendum 5](../../addenda/addendum_5.md) with the changes
outlined below.  

If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development
Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:DPAPolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

- **AG
  Code:**Most of the waiver cases previously coded as DA have
  been changed to the AG waiver code.  The AG code now covers
  adults who meet a nursing level of care.  The new name for
  this waiver is Older and Disabled Adults (ODA).  The AG
  code was previously used for Older Alaskans (OA) and was limited to
  adults age 65 or older.
- **DA Code:**The DA code now covers
  Adults with Physical and Developmental Disabilities (APDD waiver).  This
  waiver was previously called the Adults with Physical Disabilities
  (APD) waiver.
- **MC Code:**  No change.  This
  code continues to cover Children with Complex Medical Conditions (CCMC
  waiver).
- **MD
  Code:**  A name change only.  This code continues
  to cover adults and children with Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities
  (IDD waiver).  This waiver was previously called the Mental
  Retardation and Developmental Disabilities (MRDD) waiver.
