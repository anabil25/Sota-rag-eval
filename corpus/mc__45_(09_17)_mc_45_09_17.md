ADLTC - Alaska Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health
and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #45

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled
and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  September 1, 2017

We are making
changes to clarify existing policies and align policies with those in
the MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Manual
whenever possible. Other changes are either technical in nature, clarifications
that are the result of policy questions, or suggestions from the field.  This manual change reflects
the updated Medicaid policies, as well as corrects some errors from past
manual changes.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have
any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at
 [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:DPAPolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS
514-E](../../514/514_residents_of_institutions.md#514_E_TREATMENT_FACILITIES_AND_HALFWAY_HOUSES)  SUBSTANCE ABUSE TREATMENT FACILITIES AND COMMUNITY RESIDENTIAL
CENTERS (HALFWAY HOUSES)

- Added
  a new section regarding Medicaid eligibility at Treatment Facilities
  and Halfway Houses based on a CMSCenters for Medicare & Medicaid Services
  clarification.

[MS
560-E](../../560/560_hcb_waiver.md#560_E__HCB_WAIVER_REFERRALS_AND_INTERAGENCY_COMMUNICATIONS)  HCB WAIVER REFERRALS AND INTERAGENCY COMMUNICATIONS

- Revised
  screening coupon section to explain when a screening coupon needs
  to be issued.

[MS
570-F](../../570/570_post_eligibility_and_coc.md#570_F__WHEN_A_COST_OF_CARE_LIABILITY_BEGINS_AND_ENDS)  WHEN A COST-OF-CARE LIABILITY BEGINS AND ENDS

- Added an
  example to explain that COCACost of Care
  is applied when a late review is received and there is no lapse in
  Medicaid coverage and that COCACost of Care
  isn't applied when a late review is received and there is a lapse
  in Medicaid coverage.
