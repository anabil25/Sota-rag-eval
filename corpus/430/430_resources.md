430           RESOURCES

To be eligible for assistance, an individual or couple must have countable resources that do not exceed the resource limits of the APAAdult Public Assistance program. This chapter explains the resource requirements of the APAAdult Public Assistance program. Certain resources of ineligible spouses and/or alien sponsors are considered available (deemed) to meet the needs of an individual or couple in determining eligibility for APAAdult Public Assistance. Refer to chapter [460](../460/460_financial_responsibility_and_deeming.md) for the policy on the deeming of resources of ineligible spouses and/or alien sponsors.

Note:

Real property (including any structures built on the land) owned by Alaskan Native and American Indian is still a countable resource to the Adult Public Assistance program. Please see [ADLTC MS 524-L.](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/adltc/524/524_medicaid_except_to_resource.htm) for more information on Medicaid Exceptions to APAAdult Public Assistance Resource Policy.
