430-3        WHEN RESOURCE VALUES ARE MEASURED

Resources are measured as of the first moment of the month. Anyone whose countable resources exceed the resource limit as of the first moment of the month is ineligible for the entire month, even if some of the assets are disposed of later in the month. Similarly, anyone whose resources do not exceed the resource limit as of the first moment of the month is resource-eligible for the entire month, even if the assets exceed the resource limit later in the month.
