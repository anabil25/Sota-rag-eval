812-2        ASSIGNMENT OF MEDICAL SUPPORT RIGHTS

A Medicaid applicant or recipient signs over to the State of Alaska the right to medical support from all sources for any child included in the Medicaid household as a condition of eligibility.

Assignment of Rights is automatic upon application. When assistance ends, so does the assignment. If a former recipient reapplies, assignment is automatic upon re-opening of the Medicaid case.

812-2 A.   EFFECTIVE DATE OF ASSIGNMENT

Assignment takes effect the date the caseworker opens the Medicaid case and authorizes the initial benefit.

812-2 B.   WHAT CHILD SUPPORT ASSIGNMENT COVERS

The assignment covers obtaining and continuing medical support during the period of Medicaid eligibility.

812-2 C.   WHAT CHILD SUPPORT ASSIGNMENT DOES NOT COVER

Assignment does not cover medical support for children who are not receiving Medicaid.
