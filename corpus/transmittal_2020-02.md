STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2020-02

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: September 1, 2020

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have any questions, please contact Division Operations Support Team at [dpadost@alaska.gov](mailto:dpadost@alaska.gov) or the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 100-4 F](../100/100-4_civil_rights.md#100-4_F._WHERE_A_CLIENT_MAY_FILE_A_CIVIL_RIGHTS_COMPLAINT)      CIVIL RIGHTS

- Updated USDA contact information

[MS 103-1](../103/103-1_electronic_document_management.md)      THE CASE FILE FOLDER

- Added a section and hyperlinks to resources that address the implementation of the ILINX Electronic Document Management system
- Revised underissuances references to underpayments

[MS 108-5](../108/108-5_eis_notice_listing.md)      EIS NOTICE LISTING

- Updated EIS notice listing to remove notices that are no longer being used and add new notices

[MS 111](../111/111_program_losses_and_claims_collection.md)      PROGRAM LOSSES AND CLAIMS COLLECTIONS

- Added guidance for staff to submit both GEN 95 and APA 5 to Claim Unit for APA claims

[MS 111-1](../111/111-1_determining_the_amount_of_a_program_loss.md)      DETERMINING THE AMOUNT OF A PROGRAM LOSS

- Added guidance to ensure staff understand that the APA 5 is required in addition to the GEN 95 when processing an APA claim

[MS 113-8](../113/113-8_fraud_referrals.md)      FRAUD REFERRALS

- Revised fraud referral information to match the current process

[MS 124-3](../124/124-3_ssi_children_and_youth.md)      SSI CHILDREN AND YOUTH

- Removed references to automatic APA eligibility without application as this policy changed April 1, 2020

ENTIRE MANUAL

- Replaced all Field Services Support Unit (FSSU) and Field Services references with Division Operations Support Team (DOST) since the unit was renamed
- Replaced all [dpafield@alaska.gov](mailto:dpafield@alaska.gov) email address references to [dpadost@alaska.gov](mailto:dpadost@alaska.gov) since it was changed
- Updated all references of the Statewide Process Management Guide to the HowWe Guide since the HowWe Guide replaced it
- Updated all HowWe Guide links
