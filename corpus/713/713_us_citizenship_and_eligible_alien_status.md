713            U.S. CITIZENSHIP AND ELIGIBLE ALIEN STATUS

To receive Temporary Assistance, a person must be:

- A United States citizen or U.S. National;
- A qualified alien, as described in [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 713-2](713-2_qualified_aliens.md). Qualified aliens may be subject to limited eligibility as described in [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 713-4](713-4_five_year_waiting_period.md).
- A Native American born in Canada or Mexico who has treaty rights to cross the U.S. / Canada or U.S. / Mexico borders, as described in [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 713-3](713-3_native_americans_born_outside_the_united_states.md).
- A Compact of Free Association migrant, as described in ATAP [MS 713-5 B](713-5_non-qualified_aliens.md#713-5_B._NON-IMMIGRANTS).

Refer to [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 770-8](../770/770-8_deeming_income_and_resources.md) for policy related to sponsored aliens.
