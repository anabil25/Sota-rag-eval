451-1        INCOME ELIGIBILITY

To be found income eligible for APAAdult Public Assistance, an applicant or recipient must have a total monthly countable income (including countable income of a spouse or alien sponsor) that, after rounding, is equal to or less than the appropriate APAAdult Public Assistance need standard. Income is rounded up to the nearest whole dollar from $ .50 and rounded down to the nearest whole dollar from $ .49.

The appropriate APA need standard is determined by the applicant's or recipient's living arrangement. (Refer to section [450](../450/450_living_arrangements.md) for the policy on living arrangements. Refer to [Addendum 1](../addendum/addendum_1.md) for APA need standards.)

Countable income is the amount of income remaining after all possible income allocations and exclusions have been applied to the applicant's or recipient's income, and to the income of the applicant's or recipient's spouse or alien sponsor, if applicable. (Refer to sections [440](../440/440_income.md), [441](../441/441_countable_income.md), [442](../442/442_income_exclusions.md), and [443](../443/443_special_income_provisions.md) for APAAdult Public Assistance income-counting policy. Refer to section [460](../460/460_financial_responsibility_and_deeming.md) for APAAdult Public Assistance financial deeming policy.)
