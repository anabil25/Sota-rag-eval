451-2        INTERIM ASSISTANCE ELIGIBILITY DETERMINATIONS

The same income eligibility procedure that applies to an APAAdult Public Assistance applicant or recipient also applies to individuals who are otherwise eligible to receive Interim Assistance. Once an individual is determined eligible for Interim Assistance, he or she remains income-eligible as long as his or her countable income, after rounding, is equal to or less than the appropriate APAAdult Public Assistance need standard.
