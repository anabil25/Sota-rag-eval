740-1          POST TEMPORARY ASSISTANCE CASE MANAGEMENT AND SUPPORTIVE SERVICES

If an adult family member included in the assistance unit has earnings from employment at the time the [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program case closes, the household may be eligible for up to 12 months of post Temporary Assistance (post-[TA](#)Temporary Assistance) case management, including supportive services.

See [WSPM](#)Work Services Procedure Manual [MS 1013](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/workservices1/1013/1013_post_temporary_assistance_employment_services.htm?rhtocid=_14_0) for additional information.

Note:

A family is not eligible for post-[TA](#)Temporary Assistance if their case closes because:

- They no longer reside in Alaska; or
- They no longer have an eligible child in the home.
