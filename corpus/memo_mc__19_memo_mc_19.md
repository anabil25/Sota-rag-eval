APA - Alaska Adult Public Assistance Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #19

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  October 15, 2008

In response to questions from field staff, we are clarifying that fleeing felons who are not eligible for SSISupplemental Security Income or SSA benefits and individuals who are not eligible for SSISupplemental Security Income benefits due to the SSISupplemental Security Income program’s stricter citizenship requirements may be eligible for APA if they meet all other eligibility requirements.

In addition, the IRS mileage allowance table has been removed from Addendum 1.  In manual sections where the mileage rate is referenced, a link to the IRS website showing the most current mileage rate has been supplied.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 420-2 A](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/APA_Manual/Working Copy/Robohelp/420/420-2_CONCURRENT_PARTICIPATION_IN_THE_SSI_PROGRAM.md#420_2_A____RELATIONSHIP_BETWEEN_THE_SSI_AND_APA_PROGRAMS)  Relationship Between The SSISupplemental Security Income and APA Programs

- Clarifies that applicants who are not eligible for SSISupplemental Security Income due to fleeing felon status or citizenship requirements may still be eligible for APA if they meet other eligibility requirements.

[MS 425-2 D](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/APA_Manual/Working Copy/Robohelp/425/425-2_WHO_DETERMINES_DISABILITY_BLINDNESS.md#425_2_D____STATE_ONLY_BLINDNESS_OR_DISABILITY_DECISIONS)  State-Only Blindness or Disability Decisions

- Adds fleeing felons and more stringent citizenship requirements to the list of reasons applicants may not qualify for SSISupplemental Security Income .

[MS 430-2](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/APA_Manual/Working Copy/Robohelp/430/430-2_RESOURCE_LIMITS.md)  Resource Limits

- Clarifies the resource limit for a couple is $3000 as long as at least one member is eligible for assistance.

[MS 442-2 I (1)](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/APA_Manual/Working Copy/Robohelp/442/442-2_EARNED_INCOME_EXCLUSIONS.md#442_2_I_WORK_EXPENSES_FOR_THE_BLIND)  Work Expenses For The Blind

- Adds reference to use the business mileage rate
- Adds IRS website to get the current business mileage rate

[MS 442-3 A (7)(c)(5)](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/APA_Manual/Working Copy/Robohelp/442/442-3_UNEARNED_INCOME_EXCLUSIONS.md#442_3_A_7__Educational_Assistance)  Income Exclusions Authorized By Federal Laws

- Adds reference to use the business mileage rate
- Adds IRS website to get the current business mileage rate

[MS 452-5 A (1)](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/APA_Manual/Working Copy/Robohelp/452/452-5_COMPUTATION_OF_BENEFITS.md#452_5_A_1__Payment_Limit)  Regular APA Benefit Payments

- Adds a second example to show how to budget a fleeing felon’s income.

[Addendum 1](file:////hssjnuaobf2/dpa/Policy/Online_Manuals/APA_Manual/Working Copy/Robohelp/Addendum/Addendum_1.md)  Program Standards

- Removes the IRS mileage allowance table.
