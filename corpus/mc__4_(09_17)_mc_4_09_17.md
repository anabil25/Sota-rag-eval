Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #4

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: September 1, 2017

We are making several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field. One major change is potential Medicaid eligibility for individuals living in a Community Residential Center (also known as a Halfway House) and a new Addendum that lists the Community Residential Centers (CRC's) in Alaska.

Highlights of the further policy changes are described below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[808-4](../../808_us_citizenship_and_eligible_alien_status/808-4_five_year_waiting_period.md) Aliens Exempt From the Five Year Waiting Period

- Added note clarifying that an Amerasian immigrant must have been born between 12/31/1950 and 10/23/1982 to be classified as Amerasian by USCISU.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services.

[815 E.](../../815_residents_of_institutions/815_residents_of_institutions.md#815_E._______SUBSTANCE_ABUSE_TREATMENT_FACILITIES_AND_COMMUNITY_RESIDENTIAL_CENTERS__HALFWAY_HOUSES_) Substance Abuse Treatment Facilities and Community Residential Centers (Halfway Houses)

- Added new Medicaid eligibility criteria for individuals living at a Community Residential Center (Halfway House).
- Describes how to verify with Department of Corrections if the incarcerated individual is considered confined or has freedom of movement in a furlough placement.

[823-2](../../823_verification_and_documentation/823-2_reasonable_compatibility.md) Reasonable Compatibility

- Clarified that reasonable compatibility is based off the client stated income not the income electronically reported by DOLDepartment of Labor and Workforce Development. The income electronically reported by DOLDepartment of Labor and Workforce Development must be within 10% of the client stated gross monthly income.

[826 G.](../../826_home_and_community_based_waiver_services/826_home_and_community_based_waiver_services.md#826_G._____PROCESSING_HCB_WAIVERS_AND_EIS_CODES) Processing HCBHome and Community Based Waivers and EISEligibility Information System Codes

- Added a link to [ADLTC Manual section 560](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/adltc/560/560_hcb_waiver.htm) which gives a better understanding of the Medicaid screening coupon process.

[Addendum 6](../../addenda/addendum-6_community_residential_treatment.md) Community Residential Centers (CRC's)

- Added Addendum 6 that lists the Community Residential Centers in Alaska. If a Medicaid applicant or recipient indicates they are staying at one of these addresses, the Eligibility Technician must follow the instructions outlined in [815(E)](../../815_residents_of_institutions/815_residents_of_institutions.md#815_E._______SUBSTANCE_ABUSE_TREATMENT_FACILITIES_AND_COMMUNITY_RESIDENTIAL_CENTERS__HALFWAY_HOUSES_) to determine if the individual is eligible for full Medicaid benefits or limited inmate Medicaid coverage.
