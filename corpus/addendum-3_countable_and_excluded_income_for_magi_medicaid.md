Addendum 3

Countable and Excluded income for MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

\*If an individual has a Medicaid Income Qualifying Trust (Miller Trust) or Special Needs Trust, please follow [ADLTC manual section 528](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/adltc/528/528_processing_cases_with_trusts.htm).

\*A tax dependent's countable income under the tax filing threshold is not countable. See [section 818](../818_magi_medicaid_income/818_magi_medicaid_income.md).
