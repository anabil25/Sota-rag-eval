STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #13

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

Date:  July 1, 2009

Tribal documents such as  a tribal enrollment card or a Certificate of Degree of Indian blood may be used to verify both citizenship and identity.  This manual change adds this information to the citizenship chapter and includes two other minor changes.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:DPApolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 520 B](../../520/520_elig_req_apa_policy.md)  US Citizenship and Eligible Alien Status

- Adds tribal documents to the list of documents which can verify both citizenship and identity
- Clarifies instructions regarding the BV code and when a screen print is placed in the case file

[MS 554 L](../../554/554_transfer_of_assets.md#554_L_6_Notification_When_a_Transfer_of_Assets_Penalty_is_Imposed)  When a transfer of assets penalty is imposed, immediately notify:

- Replaces Natalia Manfredi's name with that of Douglas Myers in bullet #1
