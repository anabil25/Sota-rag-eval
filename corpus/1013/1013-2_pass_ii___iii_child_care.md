1013-2          PASS II / III CHILD CARE

When an [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program case closes with earnings, the family with children eligible for childcare is referred to the local [PASS](#)Parents Achieving Self-Sufficiency II administrator for childcare assistance services, see [ATAP MS 740-2](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/740/740-2_pass_ii_child_care.htm). All families with potentially eligible children are informed of the resource through the [PASS](#)Parents Achieving Self-Sufficiency II referral notice (W150). Additional information about [PASS](#)Parents Achieving Self-Sufficiency II / III can be found at: [https://health.alaska.gov/dpa/Documents/dpa/programs/ccare/Documents/Manuals-Brochures/Child-Care-Assistance-Program-Policies-and-Procedures.pdf](http://health.alaska.gov/dpa/Documents/dpa/programs/ccare/Documents/Manuals-Brochures/Child-Care-Assistance-Program-Policies-and-Procedures.pdf) section 4050.

All requests for case closure with income should include a request that the [PASS](#)Parents Achieving Self-Sufficiency II notice is distributed according to [ATAP 740-2.](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/ta.htm#t=740%2F740-2_pass_ii_child_care.htm&rhsearch=child%20care&rhhlterm=child%20care)
