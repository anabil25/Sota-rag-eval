1013          POST TEMPORARY ASSISTANCE EMPLOYMENT SERVICES

When a client closes their [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program case with earnings from employment, there are several resources still available to them from [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance. Post Temporary Assistance Employment Services (Post-[TA](#)Temporary Assistance) are available to clients for 12 months after closing their [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program case with earnings. They do not have to meet any financial threshold to qualify for Post-[TA](#)Temporary Assistance. If a client loses their job while they are eligible for Post-[TA](#)Temporary Assistance, provide services to assist with re-employment, especially if those services will prevent the client from returning to the [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program caseload, see [MS 1004-1](../1004/1004-1_when_to_provide_supportive_services.md).
