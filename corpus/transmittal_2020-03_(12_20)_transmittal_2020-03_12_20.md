STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2020-03

MANUAL: Heating Assistance Program

DATE: December 1, 2020

Several sections of the Heating Assistance Program Policy Manual have been revised in order to incorporate new policy, clarify existing policy, and align policies within our policy manuals.

If you have questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 3001-4 (A)](../3001/3001-4_verification.md)             VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS FOR INITIAL APPLICATIONS

- Removed the requirement for a client to provide an electric vendor account number and billing statement, when 0% of the grant will be used to pay the electric bill, as this action is no longer required.
- Replaced lettered list with bulleted list, to better align with the SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program manual.

[MS 3001-4 (F)](../3001/3001-4_verification.md#3001-4_F._INCOME)             INCOME

- Replaced "signature" month with "submission" month to better align with the SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program manual.
- Added "note" to clarify that DOLDepartment of Labor records should only be used to verify Unemployment Insurance Benefits.

[MS 3002-1 (E)](../3002/3002-1_households.md#3002-1_E._SOCIAL_SECURITY_NUMBERS)           SOCIAL SECURITY NUMBERS

- Aligned section with SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program MSManual Section [602-1E](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/602/602-1_e.htm)

[MS 3003-4 (A)](../3003/3003-4_income_from_self-employment.md#3003-4_A._DEFINITIONS_RELATING_TO_SELF-EMPLOYMENT)            DEFINITIONS RELATING TO SELF-EMPLOYMENT

- Corrected subsections by replacing subsection lettering with subsection numbering.

[MS 3003-4 (A)(3)](../3003/3003-4_income_from_self-employment.md#3003-4_A._DEFINITIONS_RELATING_TO_SELF-EMPLOYMENT)       RENTAL INCOME

- Aligned section with SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program MS [605-2D(1c)](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/605/605-2_d.htm)

[MS 3003-4 (A)(8)](../3003/3003-4_income_from_self-employment.md#3003-4_A._DEFINITIONS_RELATING_TO_SELF-EMPLOYMENT)      SELF-EMPLOYMENT COSTS OF DOING BUSINESS

- Aligned section with SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program MSManual Section [605-2D(1h)](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/605/605-2_d.htm)

[MS 3003-4 (B)](../3003/3003-4_income_from_self-employment.md#3003-4_B._COSTS_OF_DOING_BUSINESS)           COSTS OF DOING BUSINESS

- Corrected subsections by replacing subsection lettering with subsection numbering.

[MS 3003-4 (B)(2a)](../3003/3003-4_income_from_self-employment.md#3003-4_B._COSTS_OF_DOING_BUSINESS)    EXPENSES ALLOWED AS COSTS OF DOING BUSINESS

- Aligned section with SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program MSManual Section [605-2D(3b1)](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/605/605-2_d.htm)

[MS 3003-4E](../3003/3003-4_income_from_self-employment.md#3003-4_E._SELF-EMPLOYMENT_ANNUALIZATION_STANDARD)              SELF-EMPLOYMENT ANNUALIZATION STANDARD

- Aligned section with SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program MSManual Section [605--2D(7)](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/605/605-2_d.htm)
- Added Annualization Standards Table.

[MS 3004-1](../3004/3004-1_eligibility_determination_timeframes.md)                 ELIGIBILITY DETERMINATION TIME FRAMES

- Replaced reference to "HAPHeating Assistance Program Juneau office" with "DPADivision of Public Assistance offices," due to HAPHeating Assistance Program now processing statewide.

[MS 3006](../3006/3006_srhud_(subsidized_rental_housing_utility_deposit).md)                    SRHUD (SUBSIDIZED RENTAL HOUSING UTILITY DEPOSIT)

- Removed dated reference to HAP not being available year-round.
- Replaced numbered list with bullets.
