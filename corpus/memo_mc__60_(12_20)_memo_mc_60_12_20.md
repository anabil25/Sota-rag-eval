STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health
and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   MC
#60

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE: December 1, 2020

This year Social Security announced a 1.3% Cost of Living Allowance
(COLACost of Living Allowance) increase effective
January 2021. We have incorporated the 2021 standards into the examples
and [Addendum 1](../../addendum/addendum_1.md).  These
standards become effective with the benefit month of January 2021. The
COLACost of Living Allowance adjustment also
causes:

- An increase in the income limit for Substantial Gainful Activity;
  and
- An increase in the student earned income exclusion.

We also made several changes to incorporate new policy, clarify existing
policy, and align policies within our program policy manuals. Other changes
are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the result of
policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these policy revision are described below.  If you
have any questions, contact the Policy and Program Development Team at
[dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[410-2(E)](../../410/410-2_the_application_form.md#410_2_E__WHO_CAN_SIGN_THE_APPLICATION_FORM?)
    Who Can Sign the Application Form

- Clarified
  that both spouses have to sign a couple case application or review
  form.

[410-3(A)](../../410/410-3_the_interview.md#410_3_A____WHEN_IS_AN_INTERVIEW_REQUIRED_)
    When an Interview is Required

- Clarified that an SSISupplemental Security Income only APAAdult Public Assistance/MEMedicaid case who doesn't receive a GEN 72Eligibility Review Form does not have to do a review
  interview.

[410-4](../../410/410-4_verification.md)
          Verification

- Added IEVS to interfaces that must
  be checked
- Changed Child Support interface
  NSTAR to WINSTAR

[432-4L](../../432/432-4_retained_cash_payments_and_in-kind_payments.md#432_4_L_ALASKA_NATIVE_CLAIMS_SETTLEMENT_ACT__ANCSA__ASSETS)          Alaska
Native Claims Settlement Act (ANSCA)

- Added a note that a dividend direct
  deposited to a trust is still countable income for SSI purposes.  This
  income cannot be legally assigned to a trust.

[433-1](../../433/433-1_transfer_of_resources.md)
     Transfer of Resources

- Updated all examples within the chapter to reflect the new 2021
  standards.

[433-2A](../../433/433-2_conditional_benefits.md#433_2_A_ELIGIBILITY_FOR_CONDITIONAL_BENEFITS)
    Eligibility for Conditional Benefits

- Updated the SSISupplemental Security Income
  payment standard to the new 2021 amount in order to accurately calculate
  if an applicant is eligible for conditional benefits.

[440-2](../../440/440-2_when_income_is_counted.md)
    When Income is Counted

- Clarified that for public assistance benefits, we consider the
  month of intended use to be the month of normal receipt.

[452-1](../../452/452-1_payment_calculations.md)
     Payment Calculations

- Updated all examples within the section to reflect the new 2021
  standards.

[452-5](../../452/452-5_computation_of_benefits.md)
    Computation of Benefits

- Updated Assisted Living Home example to reflect the new 2021 standards.

[452-10B](../../452/452-10_temporary_assistance_and_apa.md)
   Temporary Assistance to APAAdult Public Assistance
conversions

- Revised examples to reflect the new 2021 standards.

[452-12](../../452/452-12_retroactive_apa_benefit_calculations.md)
     Retroactive APAAdult Public Assistance
Benefit Calculations

- Revised example to reflect the new 2021 standards.

[460-5](../../460/460-5_income_allocations.md)
    Income Allocations

- Updated all examples within the chapter to reflect the new 2021
  standards.

[480-1D](../../480/480-1_reviews.md#480_1_D_INTERVIEW_REQUIREMENTS)
    Interview Requirements

- Clarified that an SSISupplemental Security Income only APA/ME recipient who doesn't
  receive a GEN 72Eligibility Review Form
  does not have to do a review interview.
