STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #77

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: April 1, 2024

The self-employment annualization table has been updated based on changes in the Federal Poverty Guidelines (FPG) for Alaska, effective April 1, 2024.

Highlights of all the policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 700-4](../700/700-4_verification_and_documentation.md)      VERIFICATION AND DOCUMENTATION

- Clarified that ineligibility cannot be determined based solely on the information listed on a client's application or recertification. The client must be given the opportunity to verbally confirm or provide documentation.

[MS 718-1](../718/718-1_social_security_enumeration_requirements.md)      SOCIAL SECURITY ENUMERATION REQUIREMENTS

- Removed SharePoint reference to [SSN](#)Social Security Number Enumeration Letter as staff have received new instruction on how to proceed with this scenario via broadcast.

[MS 759-6 E](../759/759-6_income_from_self-employment.md#759-6_E._BUDGETING_SELF-EMPLOYMENT_INCOME)      INCOME FROM SELF-EMPLOYMENT

- Updated Self-Employment Standards due to [FPL](#)Federal Poverty Limit changes effective April 2024.
