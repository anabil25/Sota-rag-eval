**STATE OF ALASKA**

**Department of Health and Social Services**

**Division of Public Assistance**

**TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:**  2021-03

**MANUAL:**  Senior Benefit Program

**DATE:**  December 1, 2021

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy, clarify existing policy, and align policies within our program policy manuals. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy & Program Development Team by e-mail at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

**OVERVIEW OF CHANGES**

[310](../../310__application/310_application_process.md)       APPLICATION PROCESS

- Revised section for clarity
- Added the ability to accept a telephonic application and telephonically recorded application signature
- Revised note to clarify that for paper applications, the signature must either be telephonically recorded by a caseworker through the [VCC](#)Virtual Call Center or a "wet signature"

[340-4](../../340__payment/340-4_death_of_recipient.md)    DEATH OF RECIPIENT

- Revised subsection to include cancellations of outstanding payments
- Added link to Admin MS 121-15

[360-1](../../360__case_maintenance/360-1_reviews.md)   REVIEWS

- Clarified that when a GEN 50C, GEN 152, or GEN 72 is received it must be used to establish a new review/recertification period
- Linked to MS 310 for the application process and signature requirements
