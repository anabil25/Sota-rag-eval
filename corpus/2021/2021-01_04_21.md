Division

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2021-01

MANUAL:
Senior Benefit Program

DATE:
April 1, 2021

We have revised the
Senior Benefits Program Manual with updated Federal Poverty Levels for
Alaska. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications
that are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these
revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact
the Policy and Program Development Team by e-mail at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).
