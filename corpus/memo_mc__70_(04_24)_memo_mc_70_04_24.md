APA - Alaska Adult Public Assistance Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   [MC](memo_mc__62_(09_21).md#)Manual Change #70

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  April 1, 2024

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy and clarify existing policy.

Highlights of these policy revision are described below. If you have any questions, contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[410](../../410/410_application_process.md)                    Application Process

- Clarified we can only add on a program to an existing application if the application is less than 30 days old.

[420-2(B)1](../../420/420-2_concurrent_participation_in_the_ssi_program.md#420__2_B_1__APA_applicants)         [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance Applicants

- Clarified an application who is receiving [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income, must be receiving the maximum payment of [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income they are entitled to, or the application should be pended for pursuit of [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income maximum payment.
- Added an example

[420-2(B)2](../../420/420-2_concurrent_participation_in_the_ssi_program.md#420_2_B_2__APA_recipients)         [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance Recipients

- Clarified that a recipient of [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance must be receiving the maximum [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income payment they are entitled to continue to receiving [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance.

[433-4](../../433/433-4_able_accounts.md)              [ABLE](#)Achieving a Better Life Experience Accounts

- Updated 2024 Max Allowable Additional Contributions for certain employed [ABLE](#)Achieving a Better Life Experience account beneficiaries.
