Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2025-02

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: September 2, 2025

A few sections in the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have have questions, please contact Division of Operations Support team at [hss.dpadost@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpadost@alaska.gov) or the Policy & Program Development team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 100-3 E](../100/100-3_confidentiality.md#100-3_E._SUBPOENAS_AND_COURT_ORDERS)      SUBPOENAS AND COURT ORDER

- Updated Assistance Attorney General from Alexander Hildebrand to Justin Nelson

[MS 100-3 H](../100/100-3_confidentiality.md#100-3_H._REQUESTS_FOR_INFORMATION_FROM_OTHER_AGENCIES)      REQUESTS FOR INFORMATION FROM OTHER AGENCIES

- Added [WIC](#)Women, Infants, and Children to the list of partner agencies, contractors, and grantees that do not require a release, along with the specific information that can be released

[MS 101-4 B](../101/101-4_receipt_of_an_identifiable_application.md#101-4_B._IDENTIFIABLE_APPLICATION_-_STATEMENT_OF_TRUTH_NOT_SIGNED)      IDENTIFIABLE APPLICATION - STATEMENT OF TRUTH NOT SIGNED

- Removed the requirement to have the applicant initial each of the acknowledgements in the Acknowledgement of Understanding of Truth section of the application and recertification as these were removed from the forms
- Clarified that if [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance receives an identifiable application (contains a name, address, and signature) that does not have a signature on the Acknowledgement of Understanding and Statement of Truth page, it should be registered as a request for service for the selected programs ([MAGI](#)Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid, [HAP](#)Heating Assistance Program, and [SB](#)Senior Benefits must be registered as normal) and a copy of the application returned to the household for signature. An eligibility determination cannot be made without a completed, signed application

[MS 101-4 C](../101/101-4_receipt_of_an_identifiable_application.md#101-4_C._IDENTIFIABLE_APPLICATION_WITH_NO_PROGRAM_SELECTED)      IDENTIFIABLE APPLICATION WITH NO PROGRAM

- Clarified that an identifiable application with no program selected must be registered as a request for [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (RF)

[MS 101-7](../101/101-7_timeliness_standards.md)      TIMELINESS STANDARDS

- Clarified that for all programs, the date of application is the date the application is received by [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance. If the application is received outside normal business hours, [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance will consider the date of application the next business day. Exception: for Medicaid applications received from the [FFM](#)Federally Facilitated Marketplace, the date of application is the date the application was received by the [FFM](#)Federally Facilitated Marketplace

[MS 105-1 B](../105/105-1_verification_of_information.md#105-1_B._MANDATORY_VERIFICATION)      MANDATORY VERIFICATION

- Revised section for clarity and updated SharePoint link to <https://sp.dhss.alaska.gov/dpa/staffresources/SitePages/Home.aspx>

[MS 111-5 E](../111/111-5_recoupments_repayments_and_allotment_reductions.md#111-5_E._EXPUNGEMENT_OF_BENEFITS)      EXPUNGEMENT OF BENEFITS

- Revised to include [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program
- Added information regarding the system generated notices of [EBT](#)Electronic Benefit Transfer inactivity
- Revised to include [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance and [SB](#)Senior Benefits

[MS 121-5](../121/121-5_delivery_of_payments.md)      DELIVERY OF PAYMENTS

- Revised benefit delivery options for [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance and Senior Benefits to [EBT](#)Electronic Benefit Transfer and Direct Deposit

[MS 122](../122/122_electronic_benefit_transfer_(ebt).md)      ELECTRONIC BENEFIT TRANSFER (EBT)

- Revised benefit delivery options for [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance and Senior Benefits to [EBT](#)Electronic Benefit Transfer and Direct Deposit

[MS 122-12](../122/122-12_submitting_direct_deposit_enrollment_forms.md)      FAXING DIRECT DEPOSIT ENROLLMENT FORMS

- Updated heading title to "Submitting Direct Enrollment Forms"
- Revised the process when staff uploads the [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance Direct Deposit Enrollment form is ILINX to mark it "worked/completed" prior to submitting an email request to [EBT](#)Electronic Benefit Transfer Help Desk

[MS 122-13](../122/122-13_rejected_direct_deposit_benefits.md)      REJECTED DIRECT DEPOSIT BENEFITS

- Updated [EBT](#)Electronic Benefit Transfer contact phone number to 1-888-620-1111

[MS 123-2](../123/123-2_potential_unemployment_insurance_benefit_eligibility.md)      POTENTIAL UNEMPLOYMENT INSURANCE BENEFITS ELIGIBILITY

- Clarified that development of income requirements only apply to the [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program and [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance programs

[MS 128-4 B](../128/128-4_refugee_assistance.md#128-4_B._REFUGEE_CASH_AND_MEDICAL_ASSISTANCE)      REFUGEE CASH AND MEDICAL ASSISTANCE

- Changed program limit from 12 months to 4 months due to a federal rule change
