STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #11

MANUAL: Work Services Procedure Manual

DATE: December 1, 2023

We have made multiple technical corrections and incorporated the change outlined below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Work Services Policy Team at WSTA@alaska.gov or the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 1000](../1000/1000_work_services_overview.md)  WORK SERVICES OVERVIEW

- Corrected typos, hyperlinks, and aligned language from the [DOH](#)Department of Health public facing website for the [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program.

[MS 1000-2](../1000/1000-2_principles.md)  PRINCIPLES OF WORK SERVICES

- Clarified collaboration language to include the client(s)' role and to acknowledge the many differences in communities throughout Alaska.

[MS 1000-3](../1000/1000-3_achieving_self-sufficiency.md)  ACHIEVING SELF-SUFFICIENCY

- Added "self-sufficiency" as a goal of [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program.

[MS 1001](../1001/1001_engagement_and_assessment.md)  ENGAGEMENT AND ASSESSMENT

- Corrected typos, hyperlinks, and made other technical corrections as recommended from the field and Training.

[MS 1001-1](../1001/1001_engagement_and_assessment.md)  RAPID ENGAGEMENT

- Added program status in which clients may be referred to [WSP](#)Work Services Provider.
- Added language about clients reapplying beyond 60 months.
- Replaced "referral" with "engagement" and added "including Supportive Services" at the end of the first sentence.
- Clarified when the 30 day "rapid engagement" timeframe begins.
- Added language for the variety of ways the client becomes known as the [WSP](#)Work Services Provider.

- Replace "employment" with "employability" for consistency.
- Made technical corrections as recommended by the field.

- Added language describing parameters for minimum screening.

[MS 1001-6](../1001/1001-6_domestic_violence.md)  DOMESTIC VIOLENCE

- Added link to CDVSA home page.
