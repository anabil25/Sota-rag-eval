755-3          BENEFIT PROGRAMS FOR WHICH APPLICATION IS NOT REQUIRED

Application is not required for:

- Supplemental Security Income (SSI).
- Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA) General Assistance.
- Any other [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance programs.
