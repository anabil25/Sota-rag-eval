STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #36

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  March 2, 2015

We have updated the Program Standards and Pickle Amendment Chart for 2015.  We have also updated other sections that use Federal Poverty Level guidelines for income eligibility.

Highlights of these revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team by e-mail [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[520-C](../../520/520_elig_req_apa_policy.md#520_C._REASONABLE_OPPORTUNITY_PERIOD)        REASONABLE OPPORTUNITY PERIOD

- Updated policy, per CMS, to state that an individual can have more than one reasonable opportunity period.
- Advised when a fraud referral is appropriate.
- Deleted section 3 to remove language indicating only adequate notice is needed. Per CMS clarification adverse action is required.

[534-K](../../534/534_working_disabled_medicaid_buy-in.md#534_K__EXAMPLE_OF_AN_ELIGIBILITY_DETERMINATION)        EXAMPLE OF AN ELIGIBILITY DETERMINATION
