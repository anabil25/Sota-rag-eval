470-4        PARTIAL MONTH INSTITUTIONAL RESIDENCE

If otherwise eligible, an individual may receive APAAdult Public Assistance benefits for the month of admission or discharge from an institution listed in [470-1](470-1_ineligible_institutions.md), above, if he or she lives outside the institution for at least one day of the month.

An individual who expects release from an institution may apply for APAAdult Public Assistance before release. The application may be approved upon proof of the applicant's actual discharge from the institution. Benefits will begin with the month the applicant is discharged from the institution or the month in which the applicant meets all eligibility factors for the APAAdult Public Assistance program, whichever is later.
