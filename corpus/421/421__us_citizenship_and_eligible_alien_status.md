421           U.S. CITIZENSHIP AND ELIGIBLE ALIEN STATUS

To receive [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance, a person must be:

- A United States citizen or U.S. National;
- A qualified alien, as described in section [421-2](421-2_qualified_aliens.md).  Qualified aliens may be subject to limited eligibility as explained in section [421-4;](421-4_five_year_waiting_period.md)
- A Native American born in Canada or Mexico who has treaty rights to cross the U.S./Canada and U.S./Mexico borders, as described in section [421-3](421-3_native_americans_born_outside_the_united_states.md).

Refer to MS [460-2B](../460/460-2_financial_responsibility.md#460_2_B____INCOME_AND_RESOURCES_OF_ALIEN_SPONSORS) for policy related to sponsored aliens.
