Administrative Procedures Manual

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2023-01

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: April 3, 2023

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have questions, please contact Division Operations Support Team at [hss.dpadost@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpadost@alaska.gov) or the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

ENTIRE MANUAL

- Removed all references related to HowWe Guide as it is no longer used
- Removed references to SYSOPS website since it is no longer available

[MS 100-3 (H)](../100/100-3_confidentiality.md#100-3_H._REQUESTS_FOR_INFORMATION_FROM_OTHER_AGENCIES)      REQUESTS FOR INFORMATION FROM OTHER AGENCIES

- Added Health Management Systems (HMS) next to DHCS as they are the new fiscal agent as of April 1, 2023

[MS 100-6 (B)](../100/100-6_interpreter_services.md#100-6_B._SPEECH_AND_HEARING_IMPAIRED_INDIVIDUALS)      SPEECH AND HEARING IMPAIRED INDIVIDUALS

- Added toll-free number to access services for speech and hearing-impaired individuals

[MS 103-3 (C)](../103/103-3_case_numbering_on_eis_and_aries.md#103-3_C._CASE_NUMBERING_RULES_FOR_DPA_PROGRAMS)      CASE NUMBERING RULES FOR DPA PROGRAMS

- Added an example under #4 for when there are two parents in the household who are married wanting benefits for their own children, and there are no common children between them, that the same app can be used if both signed it and that we would register two different [TA](#)Temporary Assistance cases with each as the respective [PI](#)Primary Information Person to their children's [TANF](#)Temporary Assistance for Needy Families case
- Added a situation under #6 in which a [HH](#)House Hold is applying for both [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance and [TANF](#)Temporary Assistance for Needy Families and separate case numbers are assigned

[MS 103-7](../103/103-7_case_file_transfer.md)      CASE TRANSFER

- Updated information about where to send the case file transfer requests
- Added control clerk email

[MS 104](../104/104_statewide_process_management.md)      STATEWIDE PROCESS MANAGEMENT

- Revised section to remove PathOS and replaced with current™

[MS 104-1](../104/104-1_first_contact_resolution.md)      FIRST CONTACT RESOLUTION

- Updated where to locate list of office representatives for scanning documents, which is found on SharePoint

[MS 105-1 (C)](../105/105-1_verification_of_information.md#105-1_C._QUESTIONABLE_INFORMATION)      QUESTIONABLE INFORMATION

- Added client statement is acceptable for race, ethnicity, and gender if not questionable

[MS 117-4](../117/117-4_pre_hearing_conference.md)      PRE-HEARING CONFERENCE

- Clarified when a client requests a hearing and submits a signed form authorizing someone to represent them, the agency must conduct the pre-hearing with the attorney directly and not the client

[MS 123-1 (B)](../123/123-1_dol_data_inquiry_screens.md#123-1_B._SECURITY_ACCESS_TO_DOL_DATA_SYSTEM)      SECURITY ACCESS TO [DOL](#)Department of Labor DATA SYSTEM

- Updated procedure when submitting [DOL](#)Department of Labor access request forms
