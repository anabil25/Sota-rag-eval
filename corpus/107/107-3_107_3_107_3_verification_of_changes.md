107-3          VERIFICATION
OF CHANGES

Generally, the caseworker must verify any change
that could affect eligibility or benefit amount. Certain types of changes
that require action but do not affect eligibility or the benefit amount
may not require verification. Refer to the appropriate program policy
manual to determine when verification is required.
