107-4          PENALTY
FOR NOT REPORTING

Failure to report changes in a timely manner may
result in the wrong amount of benefits being issued. The client will be
asked to repay any overpayment resulting from not reporting. The client
may also be referred to the Fraud Control Unit if an intentional program
violation is suspected.

Refer to [chapter
112](../112/112_fraud_control.md) for more information on fraud control and referral.
