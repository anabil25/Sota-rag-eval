777-3         CRISIS OR EMERGENCY ASSISTANCE

Crisis, or emergency assistance paid to, or on behalf of a low-income adult(s) with dependent child(ren) under the age of 19. [NRST](#)Non-Recurrent Short-Term benefits for crisis, or emergency assistance are intended not to exceed the household's maximum payment over three months. Crisis or emergency payments are most often made as direct vendor payments to landlords, childcare providers, automotive repair businesses, etc. For additional information, please see [WSPM Section 1004-4](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/workservices1/1004/1004-4_supportive_service_codes_and_definitions.htm) regarding Auto Repair, Emergency Shelter, etc.
