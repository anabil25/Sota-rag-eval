777-2         SKIMMED [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program BENEFITS REPLACEMENT

The replacement of stolen [TANF](#)Temporary Assistance to Needy Families benefits that were skimmed by electronic equipment or otherwise fraudulently obtained by accessing an electronic benefits transfer (EBT) card. [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program benefits will be restored in a lump sum payment to replace the amount of [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program stolen benefits if the theft is reported to [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance within 90-calendar days from the date of the electronic theft transaction. Please see form [GEN172](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/e-forms/pdf/GEN-172.pdf) on DPAweb for the reissuance of skimmed [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program benefits.
