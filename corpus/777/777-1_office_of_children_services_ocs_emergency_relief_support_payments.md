777-1         OFFICE OF CHILDREN SERVICES (OCS) EMERGENCY RELIEF SUPPORT PAYMENTS

The Emergency Relief Support program through [OCS](#)Office of Children's Services issues cash assistance payments to eligible households seeking federal foster care licensure, [TANF](#)Temporary Assistance to Needy Families, or Tribal [TANF](#)Temporary Assistance to Needy Families. These payments are issued to the household on behalf of the child(ren) for up to two-months. For additional information, please see the [OCS](#)Office of Children's Services' [Program Instruction 16-02](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/training/OCS/cps/Program_Instructions/16-02_Emergency_Relief_Support_Program.htm) in the Child Protective Services Manual.
