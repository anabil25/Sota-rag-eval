777            NON-RECURRENT SHORT-TERM (nRST) BENEFITS

Division of Public Assistance uses federal [TANF](#)Temporary Assistance to Needy Families funding, state general funds (GF), and Permanent Fund Dividend Hold Harmless (PFD HH) funding for a variety of [NRST](#)Non-Recurrent Short-Term benefits that are intended to deal with a specific crisis situation, or episode of need, but will not extend beyond four months.
