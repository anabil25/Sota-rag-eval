ADLTC - Alaska Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #68

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  April 1, 2025

We have updated the 2025 Federal Poverty Level guidelines for income eligibility effective April 1, 2025. We have also updated other sections that use Federal Poverty Level guidelines for income eligibility.

This manual change also consists of several technical changes or clarifications as a result of suggestions from the field. Highlights of these revisions are described below. 

If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[505](../../505_third_party_liability_(tpl)_and_recovery/505_third_party_liability_and_recovery.md)     Third Party Liability (TPL) and Recovery 

- Changed Child Support Services Division (CSSD) to Child Support Enforcement Division (CSED).

[506(B)](../../506_medicare/506_medicare.md#505_B_MEDICARE_ENROLLMENT)     Medicare Enrollment

- Removed instructions to refer an individual who is found to be ineligible for Medicare Savings Plan to the Social Security Office to apply for Medicare as [DHCS](#)Division of Health Care Services confirmed that this is an unnecessary step.

[506(J)](../../506_medicare/506_medicare.md#506_J_SOCIAL_SECURITY_OFFICES)     Social Security Offices

- Removed the Ketchikan office as it is closed and updated fax and phone numbers for Anchorage, Juneau, and Fairbanks offices.

[511](../../511_child_support_cooperation/511_child_support_cooperation.md)     Child Support Cooperation

- Changed Child Support Services Division (CSSD) to Child Support Enforcement Division (CSED).

[513](../../513_alaska_residency/513_alaska_residency.md)     Alaska Residency

- Added note clarifying when caseworkers should consider Alaska residency questionable.

[516](../../516_retroactive_medicaid/516_retroactive_medicaid.md)     Retroactive Medicaid

- Clarified that retroactive Medicaid uses actual income.

[520(D)](../../520_eligibility_requirements_that_follow_apa_policy/520_elig_req_apa_policy.md#520_D._RESERVED)    Reserved

- Due to a federal rule change, removed the requirement to apply for other benefits as a condition of Medicaid eligibility.

[522(B)5](../../522_medicaid_exceptions_to_apa_eligibility_policy/522_medicaid_exception_to_apa_citizenship_requirements.md#522_B._______ACTIONS_TAKEN_ON_THE_APPLICATION1)    Actions Taken on the Application

- Clarified that Medicaid application withdrawal requests can be done with a recorded signature the [VCC](#)Virtual Call Center.

[530(D)](../../530_ssi_and_apa_related_categories/530_ssi_and_apa_related_categories.md#530_D_SSI__DISABLED_ADULT_CHILDREN_)    [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income Disabled Adult Children

- Clarified how to verify [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income Disabled Adult Children eligibility.

[534(K)](../../534_working_disabled_medicaid_buy-in/534_working_disabled_medicaid_buy-in.md#534_K__EXAMPLE_OF_AN_ELIGIBILITY_DETERMINATION)   Example of an Eligibility Determination

- Updated examples with 2025 Federal Poverty Level Changes.

[534(M)](../../534_working_disabled_medicaid_buy-in/534_working_disabled_medicaid_buy-in.md#534_M__EXAMPLE_OF_AN_ELIGIBILITY_DETERMINATION_WITH_A_MEDICAID)   Example of an Eligibility Determination with a Medicaid Qualifying trust

- Updated examples with 2025 Federal Poverty Level Changes.

[580](../../580_medicare_savings_programs/580_medicare_premium_assistance.md)   Medicare Savings Programs

- Clarified that [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income recipients who are eligible for Medicare are automatically eligible for [QMB](#)Qualified Medicare Beneficiaries and must be enrolled the first of the month after Medicaid eligibility is determined.
- Clarified that [SLMB](#)Specified Low Income Medicare Beneficiaries can be authorized for up to 3 months prior to the [QMB](#)Qualified Medicare Beneficiaries start date.

[Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum_1.md)   

- Updated with 2025 Federal Poverty Level Changes
- Updated Pickle chart

[Addendum 5](../../addenda/addendum_5.md)
