STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2008-01

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: June 30, 2008

We have updated several chapters of the Administrative Procedures Manual. The General Information chapter now includes new sections on ethical conduct and reporting abuse and neglect and additional instruction on the disclosure of client information. Several sections on processes are now in the manual, including fee agent verification of citizenship and identity documents, a pre-hearing process for Interim Assistance cases, the State On-line Query (SOLQ), and the voter registration requirements. We updated the EIS notice list in the Notice chapter. Numerous revisions are made in the Referrals chapter. Several revisions were made in the Title IV-E / DPA Transfer Protocols chapter. We updated the Senior Benefits and Medicare Part D Low-Income Subsidy (LIS) Case Processing chapter.

Highlights of these revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3347 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).
