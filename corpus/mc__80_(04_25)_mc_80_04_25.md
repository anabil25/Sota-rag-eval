STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #80

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: April 1, 2025

The self-employment annualization table has been updated based on the changes in the Federal Poverty Guidelines (FPG) for Alaska, effective April 1, 2025.

Highlights of all the policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 705-4 B](../705/705-4_verification.md#705-4_B._INFORMATION_FROM_DATA_SYSTEMS_AND_EIS_INTERFACES)      INFORMATION FROM DATA SYSTEMS AND [EIS](#)Eligibility Information System INTERFACES

- Revised section for clarity

[MS 705-5 E](../705/705-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#705-5_E._WITHDRAWING_AN_APPLICATION)      WITHDRAWING AN APPLICATION

- Clarified that [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program application withdrawal requests can be done with a recorded signature through the [VCC](#)Virtual Contact Center

[MS 714-1 C](../714/714-1_alaska_residency.md#714-1_C._VERIFICATION_OF_RESIDENCY)      VERIFICATION OF RESIDENCY

- Revised section to remove duplicate information and clarify when caseworkers should consider Alaska residency questionable

[MS 740-2 B](../740/740-2_pass_ii_child_care.md#740-2_B._PERIOD_OF_ELIGIBILITY)      PERIOD OF ELIGIBILITY

- Revised note to clarify the [PASS](#)Parents Achieving Self-Sufficiency II process

[MS 759-6 E](../759/759-6_income_from_self-employment.md#759-6_E._BUDGETING_SELF-EMPLOYMENT_INCOME)      BUDGETING SELF EMPLOYMENT INCOME

- Updated Self-Employment Standards due to [FPL](#)Federal Poverty Limit changes effect April 2025

[MS 790-3 C](../790/790-3_acting_on_reported_changes.md#790-3_C._CHANGE_RESULTS_IN_INELIGIBILITY)      CHANGE RESULTS IN INELIGIBILITY

- Revised note to clarify the [PASS](#)Parents Achieving Self-Sufficiency II process
