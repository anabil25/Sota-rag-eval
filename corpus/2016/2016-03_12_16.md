STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  2016-03

MANUAL:  General Relief Assistance

DATE:  December 1, 2016

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy and clarify existing policy.

Highlights of these policy revisions are explained below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGE

[MS 240-2 A](../../240/240-2_specialized_burial_provisions.md#240-2_A._PUBLIC_ASSISTANCE_RECIPIENT_BURIALS).  Public Assistance Recipient Burials

- Deleted policy in this section that states responsible relatives living with the deceased at the time of death who claim not to have available resources for the burial of their relative will not have their needs, income, or resources considered in the eligibility determination, as this is old policy.
- Deleted the sentence stating,  "BIA-GABureau of Indian Affairs General Assistance is not a prior resource for these persons."
- Clarified that certain public assistance recipients are income eligible for burial assistance, not necessarily resource eligible.
- Added a note clarifying that although certain public assistance recipients are income eligible for burial assistance, we must still determine resource eligibility.

[MS 240-2 B](../../240/240-2_specialized_burial_provisions.md#240-2_B._COURT_ORDERED_BURIALS).  Court Ordered Burials

- Added a note clarifying that the caseworker must case note that it was a court ordered burial and send the itemized billing to [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov) for approval prior to payment being authorized.

[MS 240-3 C](../../240/240-3_payment.md#240-3_C._OTHER_SERVICES).  Other Services

- Added a note clarifying that if a family is requesting that DPA pay to transport the deceased for burial rather than burying them in the city of residence because it is less expensive, we must find out the final burial plans. If another funeral home will also be requesting GRA funds to complete the burial, these estimated expenses must be considered when determining if it is less costly to bury the deceased somewhere other than the city of residence.

[MS 240-4 A](../../240/240-4_prior_resources.md#240-4_A._BUREAU_OF_INDIAN_AFFAIRS_BURIAL_ASSISTANCE).  Prior Resources

- Deleted note that BIA-GA burial assistance is not a prior resource for Public Assistance recipients listed in section 240-2A as this is old policy.

[Addendum 3](../../addendum/addendum_3_judicial_districts_of_alaska.md)

- Changed Barrow to Utqiagvik due to a town name change.
