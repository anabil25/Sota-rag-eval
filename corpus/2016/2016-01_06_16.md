STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  2016-01

MANUAL:  General Relief Assistance

DATE:  June 1, 2016

This manual change updates whose income is countable for the GRA Burial Program. It is based on recent guidance from the Department of Law and a reduction in available resources allocated to the Division of Public Assistance for the GRA Burial Program.

Highlights of these policy revisions are explained below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGE

[MS 240-1](../../240/240-1_non-financial_eligibility.md#240-1_A._RESPONSIBLE_RELATIVES_LIVING_WITH_THE_DECEASED_AT_THE_TIME_OF_DEATH)  Responsible Relatives and GRA Burial Services

- Adds a note clarifying that we are now counting the income of the deceased and the responsible relatives who were living with the deceased at the time of death.
- Deletes a note stating that the income and resources of responsible relatives is not considered in the month following the death.
