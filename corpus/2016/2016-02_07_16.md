STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  2016-02

MANUAL:  Senior Benefit Program

DATE:  July 1, 2016

We have updated the Senior Benefits Program manual to include a citizenship requirement in response to a change in State Statute.  We also updated the income section to include guidance about how to prospect ANCSAAlaska Native Claim Settlement Act Dividend payments.

Highlights of these revisions are described below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team by e-mail at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[320](../../320__eligibility/320_factors_of_eligibility.md)        FACTORS OF ELIGIBILITY

- Added citizenship to the factors of eligibility.

[324](../../324_citizenship/u.s._citizenship_and_residency.md)        CITIZENSHIP

- Added a chapter for the new citizenship requirements that are effective July 1, 2016 due to a change in State Statute.

[330-2 E.](../../330__income/330-2_countable_income.md#330-2_E._ANCSA_DISTRIBUTIONS)  ANCSAAlaska Native Claim Settlement Act DISTRIBUTIONS

- Added a new subsection which clarifies that ANCSAAlaska Native Claim Settlement Act distributions are countable income for the Senior Benefits Program and how to prospect the income.

[360-3 A.](../../360__case_maintenance/360-3_suspension_and_termination.md#360-3_A.__Suspension)   SUSPENSION

- Deleted a repetitive note for clarity.

[360-3 B.](../../360__case_maintenance/360-3_suspension_and_termination.md#360-3_B._Termination)   TERMINATION

- Deleted a sentence in the note that refers to a uncompleted work request that is no longer planned.
