**STATE
OF ALASKA**

**Department
of Health and Social Services**

**Division
of Public Assistance** 

**TRANSMITTAL
NUMBER:**   2016-5

**MANUAL:**
  Alaska
Food Stamp Program

**DATE:**
  December
1, 2016

This
year Social Security announced a 0.3% Cost of Living Allowance (COLA)
increase effective
January 1, 2017.  Other changes are either technical in nature or
clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions
from the field.

Highlights
of these and other policy revisions are described below.  If you
have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development
Team at (907)465-3328 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

**OVERVIEW
OF CHANGES**

[**MS
602-1C**](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/602/602-1_c.htm) **IDENTITY**

- Removed SVES (State Verification
  Exchange System) as a verification source for identity.
- Added information about accepting
  a Medicaid determination as acceptable verification for identity.  Change based on several
  inquiries and a FNS Policy announcement from 3/18/2014 titled, “SNAP
  and ACA Parts I and II Memo.”

·         Added a
hyperlink to MAGI MS 808-6 F. – Proof of Identity Only.

[**MS
602-2 B**](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/602/602-2_b.htm)**EXEMPT
RESOURCES**

·         Added
ABLE accounts to list of excluded resources for SNAP.

[**MS
604-3(A)**](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/604/604-3_a.htm) **SEMI-ANNUAL
REPORTING**

·         Added clarification
to semi-annual reporting section that all changes reported on any case
should be worked for SNAP cases.

[**MS
605-2(D)**](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/605/605-2_d.htm) **CASES
REQUIRING SPECIALIZED PROCEDURES**

·         Removed
language from the first example in section 605-2(D.)(6.)(b.) that implies
income can be prospected based on draws or advances.  Change
made based on a policy clarification previously sent and manual not updated
– emails dated 10/20/16 and 12/29/15.

[**MS
605-7(F)**](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/605/605-7_permanent_fund_dividend_special_processing.htm#605-7_F__PFD_At_Application_) **PFD
AT APPLICATION**

·         Removed
the term Family Medicaid and replaced it with MAGI Medicaid.

[**ADDENDUM
1**](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/addenda/addendum_1.htm) **CITY
AND VILLAGE CLASSIFICATION**

- Updated the city of Barrow
  with their new name, “Utqiagvik.”

[**ADDENDUM 2**](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/addenda/addendum_2.htm)
**ABAWD WORK REQUIREMENT
EXEMPT & NON-EXEMPT**

·         Updated the city of Barrow
with their new name, “Utqiagvik.”

[**ADDENDUM
5**](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/addenda/addendum_5.htm) **LIST
OF INCOME AND RESOURCES EXCLUDED BY OTHER FEDERAL LAWS**

- Updated list
  of Native funds that are excluded by Federal Law.
- Re-Organized
  addendum so that list of income and resources excluded by
  other federal laws now reads from most recent to oldest.

[**ADDENDUM 8**](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/addenda/addendum_8.htm)
**LIST
OF COMMUNITIES BY CENSUS AREAS**

·         Updated the city of Barrow
with their new name, “Utqiagvik.”
