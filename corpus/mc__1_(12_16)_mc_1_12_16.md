Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #1

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: December 1, 2016

Beginning January 1, 2014, due to the Affordable Care Act, MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid replaced Family Medicaid. This manual change encompasses the new policy that is used for MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid eligibility determinations. Historical Family Medicaid policy and procedures have been removed.

Highlights of the further policy changes are described below.

If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[806-1 B.](../../806_application_and_renewal_process/806-1_the_application.md#806-1_B.___AN_APPLICATION_iS_REQUIRED)  An Application is Required

- Corrected policy by removing the 90-day timeframe to reinstate a withdrawn application. This no longer applies since Alaska became a determination state.

[806-2 F.](../../806_application_and_renewal_process/806-2_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#806-2_F.___DELAYED_APPLICATION)  Delayed Application

- Added clarification for actions taken on applications more than 30 days old.

[806-3](../../806_application_and_renewal_process/renewal_requirements.md)  Renewal Requirements

- Clarified that renewals must be processed within 30 days of receipt by DPADivision of Public Assistance.
- Clarified that if a renewal form is not received, DPADivision of Public Assistance must attempt to renew the case within 30 days of the previously authorized certification period.

[808-6](../../808_us_citizenship_and_eligible_alien_status/808-6_proof_of_united_states_citizenship_and_qualified_alien_status.md) Proof of United States Citizenship and Qualified Alien Status

- Updated section to match federal regulations.
- 808-6 A. Clarified that individuals entitled to or receiving any Medicare benefits are exempt from providing proof of their U.S.United States citizenship.
- 808-6 E. Clarified that BVSBureau of Vital Statistics documents are not sufficient to verify citizenship on their own. An approved identification document must also be provided.

[808-7 B.](../../808_us_citizenship_and_eligible_alien_status/808-7_reasonable_opportunity_period.md) Medicaid Eligibility During the Reasonable Opportunity Period

- Clarified in the Note section that Pregnant Women Medicaid coverage will end if U.S.United States citizenship or qualified alien status is not verified by the end of the reasonable opportunity period.

[809 F.](../../809_alaska_residency/809_alaska_residency.md#809_F._______OUT-OF-STATE_INDIVIDUAL_MOVES_TO_ALASKA) Out of State Individual Moves to Alaska

- Clarified that for individuals moving to Alaska to receive Alaska Medicaid in a month that they already received Medicaid benefits in another state, as long as case closure in the other state has been verified, client statement is sufficient verification that benefits were not used in the other state.

[812 B.](../../812_medical_support_assignment/812_medical_support_assignment.md#812_B.______WHO_IS_NOT_REQUIRED_TO_COOPERATE)  Who is not Required to Cooperate

- Clarified a non-parent/caretaker relative is not required to cooperate with child support requirements.
- Removed exemption that applicants under the age of 21 are not required to cooperate with CSSDChild Support Services Division as this is old Family Medicaid policy.

[812-1](../../812_medical_support_assignment/812-1_informing_about_medical_support_orders.md) Informing Applicants and Recipients about Medical Support Orders and Requirements

- Added that if all other eligibility requirements are met except for statement of cooperation, non-cooperation, or good cause, the child(ren)’s Medicaid can be authorized and the parent/caretaker must be sent notification of the requirements. Also, if otherwise eligible, the child(ren)’s benefits should be authorized while waiting for the parent/caretaker to cooperate with CSSDChild Support Services Division.
- Clarified that the Child Support Information form is no longer sent to CSSDChild Support Services Division. This form is used by DPADivision of Public Assistance to collect whether a client agrees to cooperate, is requesting good cause, and information about the non-custodial parent. The individual will work directly with CSSDChild Support Services Division for choosing to opt out of cash enforcement support, keeping information confidential, etc.

[812-4 E.](../../812_medical_support_assignment/812-4_failure_to_cooperate_with_cssd.md#812-4_E.___PENALTY_FOR_FAILURE_TO_COOPERATE) Penalty for Failure to Cooperate

- Removed situation that an adult under 21 is not penalized for failure to cooperate with CSSDChild Support Services Division. A parent or guardian (over 18 years old), unless exempt, must cooperate with CSSDChild Support Services Division.

[813](../../813_development_of_income/development_of_income.md) Development of Income

- Clarified that if a person is part of the tax filing unit with household members requesting Medicaid, the individual needs to develop their income if applicable.

[813 D.](../../813_development_of_income/development_of_income.md#813_D._______EXEMPTIONS_FOR_APPLYING_FOR_UIB) Exemptions for Applying for UIBUnemployment Insurance Benefit

- Corrected terminology from post-graduate to post-secondary in exemption.

[813 G.](../../813_development_of_income/development_of_income.md#813_G._______PENALTY_FOR_FAILURE_TO_APPLY)  Penalty for Failure to Apply

- Clarified that if a household member fails to develop income the other household members will not be penalized.

[814 E. (1)](../../814_tpl_and_recovery/814_tpl_and_recovery.md#814_E._______ET_ACTIONS_RELATED_TO_TPL___TPR) ETEligibility Technician Actions Related to TPLThird Party Liability/TPRThird Party Resource

- Corrected TPLThird Party Liability policy to match Child Support Policy per clarification from CMSCenters for Medicaid and Medicare Services. The Medicaid benefit must be authorized while we are waiting for TPLThird Party Liability details.
- Clarified that termination of TPLThird Party Liability must be verified.

[814 G.](../../814_tpl_and_recovery/814_tpl_and_recovery.md#814_G._______COOPERATION_WITH_TPL___TPR_AND_INDIVIDUALS_UNDER_18) Cooperation with TPLThird Party Liability/TPRThird Party Resource and Individuals Under 18

- Changed the age to 18 from 21 years old to match MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income policy requirements.

[815-1](../../815_residents_of_institutions/815-1_children_living_in_residential_treatment_centers_over_30_days.md) Children in Residential Treatment Centers over 30 Days

- Removed information for children in residential treatment centers eligible in the Non-MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income category and added a link to the Residential Treatment Center section of the ADLTCAdult Disabled and Long Term Care manual.

[816-1 B.](../../816_magi_medicaid_categories/816-1_magi_category_eligibility_factors.md#816-1_B._PREGNANT_WOMEN)  Pregnant Women

- Per federal regulations, removed verification requirement for multiple expected births.  Client statement is acceptable.

[816-1 C.](../../816_magi_medicaid_categories/816-1_magi_category_eligibility_factors.md#816-1_C._CHILDREN_UNDER_THE_AGE_OF_19) Continuous Eligibility for Children

- Clarified that in a renewal situation, the new CEPContinuous Eligibility Period begins the first of the month after a renewal is worked, not the first of the month after the previously approved CEPContinuous Eligibility Period ended.

[816-1 D.](../../816_magi_medicaid_categories/816-1_magi_category_eligibility_factors.md#816-1_d._pARENT_CARETAKER_RELATIVE)  Parent/Caretaker Relative

- Corrected the policy that a parent who has had their legal and financial ties to a child severed by adoption cannot be considered parent/caretaker relative for that child. Per CMSCenters for Medicaid and Medicare Services, the parent/caretaker relative must have legal rights over the child to be considered a parent/caretaker relative for Medicaid purposes.

[817-1](../../817_magi_medicaid_household_composition/817-1_construct_a_magi_household_for_each_applicant.md) Construct a MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Household for Each Individual

- Corrected tax dependent household size as the tax dependent’s spouse’s income is countable if they are filing taxes jointly or living together.

[820 B.](../../820_budgeting_income/820_budgeting_income.md#820_B.__PROSPECTIVE_DETERMINATION) Prospective Determination

- Removed Family Medicaid policy “However, if it appears that the household will again be prospectively eligible in the following month, the ongoing case and benefit may be suspended for a month and then reinstated without a new application.”

[821-1](../../821_budgeting_self-employment_income/821_budgeting_self-employment_income.md) Budgeting Self Employment Income

- Rewrote section and updated policy. Self-employment will be annualized, or divided by 12 months, regardless of whether or not the employment is seasonal.

[823 A.](../../823_verification_and_documentation/823_verification_and_documentation.md#823_a.___THE_FOLLOWING_FACTORS_OF_ELIGIBILITY_REQUIRE_PROOF:) The Following Factors of Eligibility Require Proof:

- Added clarification that TPLThird Party Liability termination requires verification.

[824](../../824_magi_medicaid_change_reporting_requirements/824_magi_medicaid_change_reporting_requirements.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Change Reporting Requirements

- Updated required reports of change that affect eligibility. Removed change of address as this does not affect eligibility. Added changes of tax filing status, marriage, and divorce.

[824-1](../../824_magi_medicaid_change_reporting_requirements/824-1_acting_on_reported_changes.md) Acting on Reported Changes

- Added exception to clarify that information received on an HPEHospital Presumptive Eligibility application is not considered a report of change.

[826](../../826_home_and_community_based_waiver_services/826_home_and_community_based_waiver_services.md)  Home and community Based Waiver Services

- Added waiver service information as it is applicable to MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid
- Adding Aging and Disability Resource Center as another entity involved with administering waivers.

[828](../../828_transitional_medicaid/828_transitional_medicaid.md) Transitional Medicaid

- Rewrote section for clarity
- Removed adverse action to correct policy.
- Corrected example to clarify that a married couple living together are considered joint tax filers.

[829](../../829_title_iv-e_foster_care_and_adoption_assistance/829_title_iv-e_foster_care_and_adoption_assistance.md) Title IV-E Foster Care and Adoption Assistance

- Added Tlingit and Haida as an approved tribal entity that can approve Title IV-E eligibility.

[831](../../831_emergency_treatment_for_aliens/831_emergency_treatment_for_aliens.md) Emergency Treatment for Aliens

- Clarified that applicants can apply for retroactive Medicaid per CFR 435.117 c.
- Added clarification when a new application is needed and when it is not.

[Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum-1_magi_medicaid_income_standards.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Income Standards

- Removed the annualization standards as they are no longer applicable under the Affordable Care Act regulations.

[Addendum 3](../../addenda/addendum-3_countable_and_excluded_income_for_magi_medicaid.md) Countable and Excluded Income for MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

- Excluded AmeriCorps Award if paid to an educational institution.
- Added as countable income:  state tax refunds, contractual income, Veteran Survivor’s benefits, AmeriCorps awards and stipends if paid in cash.
- Added example about earned and unearned in-kind income for clarification.
- Added income of a tax dependent if the amount is under the tax filing threshold.

[Addendum 4](../../addenda/addendum-4_magi_medicaid_income_deductions.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Income Deductions

- Removed the sentence “The person must have earnings in the year to qualify for the deduction” to avoid confusion.

[Addendum 5](../../addenda/addendum-5_military_leave_and_earnings_statement.md) Military Leave and Earnings Statement

- Updated to match the Affordable Care Act regulations

Breast and Cervical Cancer Medicaid

- Removed section as this type of coverage is explained in the ADLTCAdult Disabled and Long Term Care manual.
