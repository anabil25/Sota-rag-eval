APA - Alaska Adult Public Assistance Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   [MC](memo_mc__62_(09_21).md#)Manual Change #67

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  April 3, 2023

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy and clarify existing policy.

Highlights of these policy revision are described below. If you have any questions, contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[420-2(B)](../../420/420-2_concurrent_participation_in_the_ssi_program.md#420_2_B__TIME_LIMIT_FOR_PURSUIT_OF_SSI_BENEFITS)       Time Limit for Pursuit of [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income Benefits

- Clarified that [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance applicants have 30 days from the interview date to be in pay status with [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income.
- Clarified that for [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance recipients, benefits continue until the Social Security Administration makes a decision, if they provide proof of an [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income application.

[433-4](../../433/433-4_able_accounts.md)           [ABLE](#)Achieving a Better Life Experience Accounts 

- Updated 2023 Max Allowable Additional Contributions for certain employed [ABLE](#)Achieving a Better Life Experience account beneficiaries.

[441-2L](../../441/441-2_unearned_income.md#441_2_L_SUPPORT_AND_ALIMONY_PAYMENTS)        Support and Alimony Payments

- Clarified that child support is only countable to an [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance recipient if the child no longer lives with the [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance recipient.

[452-12](../../452/452-12_retroactive_apa_benefit_calculations.md)      Retroactive [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance Benefit Calculations

- Added information regarding [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance underpayments when [IA](#)Interim Assistance is converted to [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance.
