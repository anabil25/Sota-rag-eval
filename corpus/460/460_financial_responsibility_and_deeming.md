460           FINANCIAL RESPONSIBILITY AND DEEMING

In determining an individual's eligibility for Adult Public Assistance, certain other individuals are considered financially responsible for the APAAdult Public Assistance applicant or recipient. This chapter explains how the income and resources of financially responsible individuals are treated when determining eligibility and benefit levels for APAAdult Public Assistance.
