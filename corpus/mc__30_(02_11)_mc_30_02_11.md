STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #30

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: February 1, 2011

As a result of feedback from the field, we have made a few changes to the Temporary Assistance manual to further clarify when staff should verify shelter deductions. As always, we appreciate your input and strive to improve our manuals and policies to help those in the field.

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 700-4 A](../700/700-4_verification_and_documentation.md#700-4_A._VERIFICATION_REQUIREMENTS)      Verification Requirements

- Clarifies that shelter costs are only verified if questionable.
- Reorganizes into three sections: items that must be verified to determine eligibility for [TA](#)Temporary Assistance, items that must be verified to determine the benefit amount, and items that must be verified if questionable.

[MS 780-19 D](../780/780-19_shelter_allowance.md#780-19_D._SHARED_SHELTER_COSTS)      Shared Shelter Costs

- Clarifies that shared shelter costs only need to be verified if questionable.

[MS 790-1](../790/790-1_change_reporting_requirements.md)      Change Reporting Requirements

- Clarifies that when a household reports a change in shelter it only needs to be verified if questionable.
