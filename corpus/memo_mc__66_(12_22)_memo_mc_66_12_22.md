APA - Alaska Adult Public Assistance Manual

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   MC #66

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE: December 1, 2022

This year Social Security announced a 8.7% Cost of Living Allowance (COLACost of Living Allowance) increase effective January 2023. We have incorporated the 2023 standards into the examples and Addendum 1. These standards become effective with the benefit month of January 2023. The COLACost of Living Allowance adjustment also causes:

- An increase in the income limit for Substantial Gainful Activity; and
- An increase in the student earned income exclusion.

We also made several changes to incorporate new policy, clarify existing policy, and align policies within our program policy manuals. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the result of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these policy revision are described below.  If you have any questions, contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

ENTIRE MANUAL

- Changed all references from [DHSS](#)Department of Health and Social Services (State) to [DOH](#)Department of Health.

[400-4(C)](../../400/400-4_verification.md#400_4_C__Failure_to_provide_necessary_verification)        FAILURE TO PROVIDE NECESSARY VERIFICATION

- Clarified that if a client provides verification before the effective date of the case closure, the verification must be accepted and the case processed accordingly.

[410-3(A)](../../410/410-3_the_interview.md#410_3_A____WHEN_IS_AN_INTERVIEW_REQUIRED_)         WHEN IS AN INTERVIEW REQUIRED

- Clarified that individuals who only receive [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance/MED benefits that also receive any amount of [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income are not required to complete an annual interview. If they stop receiving [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income or they are the PI or SP on a [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Progam case, an annual interview is required for [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance/MED.

[410-3(E)](../../410/410-3_the_interview.md#410-3_E__SCHEDULING_INTERVIEWS)       SCHEDULING INTERVIEWS

- Revised verbiage for clarity.
- Removed reference to request for service.

- Clarified [IEVS](#)Instant Eligiblity Verification System is used to check Quarterly Wages.

[421-5(B)](../../421/421-5_non-qualified_aliens.md#421_5_B____NON_IMMIGRANTS)       NON-IMMIGRANTS
