| DW | Working Disabled Medicaid Buy-In |
| GF | Individuals eligible for State APAAdult Public Assistance cash program(s) in December 1973 |

| Disabled Adult Children; or |

| QM | Qualified Medicare Beneficiaries (QMBQualified Medicare Beneficiaries) |
| RC | Eligible for but refusing APAAdult Public Assistance cash payments or has countable income above the APA need/payment standard but below the Expanded Refused Cash Income Limits outlined in [ADLTC MS 530(C)](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/adltc/530/530_ssi_and_apa_related_categories.htm#530_C_SSI_OR_APA_ELIGIBLE__BUT_NOT_RECEIVING_CASH_PAYMENTS) |
| SA | Ineligible for SSISupplemental Security Income /APAAdult Public Assistance because of alien sponsor deeming |
| SI | Receiving SSISupplemental Security Income |
| SL | Specified Low Income Medicare Beneficiary.  (SLMBSpecified Low Income Medicare Beneficiaries) |

| ST | Not receiving SSISupplemental Security Income |
| UL | Emergency Alien (AD, AB, or OA) – undocumented illegal alien only |
| WD | Waiver applicant who does not require a disability determination, but does need a waiver determination.  Eligibility is for the cost of a Waiver Agency's assessment for Waiver eligibility.  Non-standard coupon. |
