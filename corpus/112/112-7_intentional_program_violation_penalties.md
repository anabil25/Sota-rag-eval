112-7          INTENTIONAL
PROGRAM VIOLATION PENALTIES

The Intentional Program Violation penalties apply
whether a client signs a disqualification-hearing waiver, is found guilty
of an IPVIntentional Program Violation in a hearing,
or is found guilty in a criminal prosecution by court of law.

The IPVIntentional Program Violation
provision for Administrative Disqualifications applies only to the SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program and Alaska Temporary Assistance
programs. Individuals disqualified from the SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program
or Alaska Temporary Assistance programs will
not be disqualified from the Medicaid program.

Note:

See the program's policy manual for specific rules on fraud disqualifications
and/or penalties, and to determine if it applies to the individual or
to the household.
