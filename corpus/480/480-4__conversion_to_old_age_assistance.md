480-4        CONVERSION TO OLD AGE ASSISTANCE

When a blind or disabled APAAdult Public Assistance recipient reaches age 65, the case worker will convert the individual's APAAdult Public Assistance case to the Old Age Assistance (OAA ) category. The case shall be converted to OAAOld Age Assistance for the benefit month in which the recipient reaches age 65.
