480           CASE MAINTENANCE

This chapter provides information on situations and changes that the case worker must act on to determine ongoing eligibility and benefit amounts for APAAdult Public Assistance recipients. All of the general eligibility factors discussed in the preceding sections of this manual also apply to ongoing APAAdult Public Assistance cases.
