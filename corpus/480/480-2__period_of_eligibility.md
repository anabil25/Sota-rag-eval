480-2        PERIOD OF ELIGIBILITY

Once APAAdult Public Assistance eligibility is established, it continues until DPADivision of Public Assistance determines that it no longer exists, or the recipient notifies DPADivision of Public Assistance that he or she no longer desires assistance.

Example:

An APAAdult Public Assistance recipient submits his or her annual review application on June 15. The review period ends on June 30. On June 25, the case worker determines that the recipient's bank account balance exceeds the resource limit for APAAdult Public Assistance.

July benefits must be authorized, since the case worker was not able to meet timely adverse action notice requirements. Benefits are terminated effective July 31.
