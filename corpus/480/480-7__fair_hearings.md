480-7        FAIR HEARINGS

Any APAAdult Public Assistance applicant or recipient whose application is not acted on within 30 days after its receipt, or who is affected by any adverse action taken on his or her case, may request a fair hearing. The fair hearing request must be made in writing to any employee of the Division.   

Hearing procedures are detailed in the [Administrative Procedures Manual at section 117-2](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/admin/117/117-2.htm).
