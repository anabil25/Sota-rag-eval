Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #25

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: September 3, 2024

We have made several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field. An overview of the changes are listed below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov.](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov)

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[805(A)](../../805_medicaid_recipient_id_card/805_medicaid_recipient_id_card.md#805_A._________ISSUING_MEDICAID_TO_ELIGIBLE_RECIPIENTS)    Issuing Medicaid to Eligible Recipients

- Clarified that identification cards are issued to individuals eligible for retroactive Medicaid.
- Removed reference to [CAMA](#)Chronic and Acute Medical Assistance as the program ended June 30, 2024.

[824](../../824_magi_medicaid_change_reporting_requirements/824_magi_medicaid_change_reporting_requirements.md#824_MAGI_Medicaid_Change_Reporting_Requirements)   [MAGI](#)Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Change Reporting Requirements

- Clarified that if an early renewal is completed based on a report of change, interfaces must be checked.

[831(E)](../../831_emergency_treatment_for_aliens/831_emergency_treatment_for_aliens.md#831_E._______COORDINATION_WITH_THE_DIVISION_OF_HEALTH_CARE_SERVICES__DHCS_)    Coordination with the Division of Health Care Services

- Updated Emergency Treatment for Aliens contact information for [DHCS](#)Division of Health Care Services.
