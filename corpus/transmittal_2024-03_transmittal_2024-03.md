STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health
Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2024-03

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: December 2, 2024

A few sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have questions, please contact Division of Operations Support Team at [hss.dpadost@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpadost@alaska.gov) or the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 100-4 C](../100/100-4_civil_rights.md#100-4_C._RECEIVING_CIVIL_RIGHTS_COMPLAINTS)      RECEIVING CIVIL RIGHTS COMPLAINTS

- Revised section to include information regarding completing the [GEN 102](#)Review - Disposition of Civil Rights Complaint form for Civil Rights complaints

[MS 100-10 C](../100/100-10_ethical_conduct.md#100-10_C._POTENTIAL_CONFLICT_OF_INTEREST)      POTENTIAL CONFLICT OF INTEREST

- Revised criteria for handling confidential cases

[MS 101-2](../101/101-2_forms.md)      FORMS

- Re-titled section from Application Forms to Forms
- Referred to the program policy manuals for when a [GEN 72](#)Eligibility Review Form may be used as an initial application

[MS 101-5](../101/101-5_where_to_submit_an_application.md)      WHERE TO SUBMIT AN APPLICATION

- Clarified the different ways an individual may submit an application for Public Assistance benefits

[MS 101-7](../101/101-7_timeliness_standards.md)      TIMELINESS STANDARDS

- Clarified the different modes in which an individual may submit an application, online, etc.

[MS 105-14](../105/105-14_systematic_alien_verification_for_entitlements_-_electronic_verification_process.md)      SYSTEMIC ALIEN VERIFICATION FOR ENTITLEMENTS - ELECTRONIC VERIFICATION PROCESS

- Revised section as caseworkers now access [SAVE](#)Systematic Alien Verification for Entitlements through [IEVS](#)Instant Eligibility Verification System

[MS 117-1 D](../117/117-1_fair_hearing_requst.md#117-1_D._TIME_LIMITS_TO_REQUEST_A_FAIR_HEARING)      TIME LIMITS TO REQUEST A FAIR HEARING

- Clarified when a fair hearing request is untimely, a non-referral notice is sent to the client
