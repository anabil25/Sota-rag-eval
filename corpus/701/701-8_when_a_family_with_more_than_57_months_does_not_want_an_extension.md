701-8          WHEN A FAMILY WITH MORE THAN 57 MONTHS DOES NOT WANT AN EXTENSION

In this situation, the Work Services case manager attempts to work with the family on a plan for their exit from the program. The exit plan describes referrals to resources or services available to the family after they leave assistance. A copy of the exit plan and any applicable forms are put in the case management file, and a [CANO](#)Case Notes notation is made indicating why the family did not request an extension (if known). See Administrative Procedures [MS 109-10](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/admin/109/109-10_case_managers_use_of_cano.htm) for additional [CANO](#)Case Notes requirement guidance.

The caseworker is informed of the extension results and acts to close the case with timely adverse notice, see [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 701-7](701-7_extension_procedures.md).
