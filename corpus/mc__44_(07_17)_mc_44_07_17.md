STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #44

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  July 3, 2017

We are making changes to clarify existing policies and align policies with those in the MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Manual. Other changes are either technical in nature, clarifications that are the result of policy questions, or suggestions from the field.  This manual change reflects the updated Medicaid policies, as well as corrects some errors from past manual changes.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at  [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:DPAPolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 505](../../505/505_third_party_liability_and_recovery.md)  Third Party Liability and Recovery

- Added information regarding FMAPFederal Medicaid Assistance Percentage and the important of using the correct Medicaid category and subtype, and indicating IHSIndian Health Service eligibility

[MS 505-C](../../505/505_third_party_liability_and_recovery.md#505_C.______APPLICANT___RECIPIENT_RESPONSIBILITY)  Applicant/Recipient Responsibility

- Clarified that verification of TPR/TPL termination is required

[MS 528-A](../../528/528_processing_cases_with_trusts.md#528_A_INITIAL_CASE_PROCESSING)  Initial Case Processing

- Clarified that the Policy & Program Development Unit will enter a case note on the existing Medicaid case number when approving a Medicaid Qualified Trust
- Updated instructions regarding how to find a trust in CourtView

[MS 554-L 6](../../554/554_transfer_of_assets.md#554_L_Transfer_Penalty_and_Partial_Months)  Transfer Penalty and Partial Months

- Updated DHCS email by replacing Doug Myers to [DHCS.Systems@alaska.gov](mailto:DHCS.Systems@alaska.gov)

[MS 560-F](../../560/560_hcb_waiver.md#560_F__HCB_WAIVER_SERVICES_AND_INSTITUTIONAL_ELIGIBILITY)  HCB Waiver Services and Institutional Eligibility

- Removed the note referencing the FASD waiver as this is no longer a valid waiver type

[ADDENDUM 2](../../addenda/addendum_2.md)

- Removed institutionalized from SI description as this is not correct
