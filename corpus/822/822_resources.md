822         RESOURCES

Resources are excluded for MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid. However, if an individual has substantial home equity that exceeds the amount listed in [Addendum 1](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/adltc/adltc.htm) of the ADLTCAdult Disabled and Long Term Care Manual, the individual will be ineligible for long-term care or home and community based waiver services, regardless of the individual's eligibility category. See [ADLTC MS 553-C](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/adltc/553/553_preventing_spousal_impoverish.htm#553_C.______SUBSTANTIAL_HOME_EQUITY) for policy on substantial home equity.
