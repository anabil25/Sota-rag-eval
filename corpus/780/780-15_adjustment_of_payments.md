780-15        ADJUSTMENT OF PAYMENTS

When funds are not available to meet full authorized benefit payments in the Temporary Assistance program, reductions will be made by [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance Central Office. The district offices, unless otherwise instructed, will continue to authorize the full benefit payment for which a family is eligible. Central Office will make necessary reductions with the funds that are available.
