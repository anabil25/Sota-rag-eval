780-4          ROUNDING DOWN

Cents are carried through the eligibility and grant determination computations. The grant is calculated carrying the cents, then rounded down from 99¢ to the next lower dollar. The rounded amount is the amount of the [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program benefit payment.
