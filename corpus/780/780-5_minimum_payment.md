780-5          MINIMUM PAYMENT

780-5 A.      PAYMENTS LESS THAN $10.00

No [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program benefit payment is made to an assistance unit if, before any recoupment, the rounded amount of the benefit payment is determined to be $9.00 or less for the payment month.

[TA](#)Temporary Assistance cases which are open for a zero grant because of the $10.00 minimum payment limitation are considered to be recipients of [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program and entitled to all [TA](#)Temporary Assistance-related benefits. However, they must continue to meet all [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program factors of eligibility including participation in assigned work activities, cooperation with [CSED](#)Child Support Enforcement Division, and residency.

780-5 B.      CORRECTIVE PAYMENTS

Corrective underpayments of less than $10 must be made, if the total entitlement for the payment month is at least $10.00.

780-5 C.      [CSED](#)Child Support Enforcement Division PASS-THROUGH PAYMENTS

[CSED](#)Child Support Enforcement Division "pass-through" child support payments are not considered [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program benefits and are not subject to the $10 payment limitation.
