780-10        CONCURRENT PAYMENTS

Except for specific exceptions allowed elsewhere in this manual, no person is entitled to receive cash assistance concurrently from more than one [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance assistance program, or concurrently from more than one federal cash assistance program. That is, no recipient can be provided benefits in the same calendar month in [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program and the [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income or [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance cash programs. However, a recipient can be a payee for more than one program as long as his needs are being met in only one program.
