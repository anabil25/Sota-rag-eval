780-7          WHO MAY AUTHORIZE PAYMENTS

In Alaska, [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance is the only agency that has the authority to authorize [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program cash payments. All [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program benefit payments will be authorized by a Regional Assistance Payments Manager or a staff member authorized or designated to act on the manager's behalf.
