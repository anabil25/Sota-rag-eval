780-14        CONTINUING PAYMENTS

Once eligibility has been established and payment authorized, the recipient is entitled to receive a benefit payment on a regular or continuing basis as long as eligibility exists. Except for retroactive corrective benefit payments, the full benefit for a month will normally be paid to the recipient in a single payment on the first day of each month.
