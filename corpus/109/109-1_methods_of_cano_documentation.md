109-1          METHODS OF CANOCase Notes DOCUMENTATION

Staff must refer to their supervisor regarding the approved methods of CANOCase Notes documentation used in their office. Currently, the methods available are:

- Free-form CANOCase Notes

Note:

This is the preferred method for new staff.

- Macro

Macros are typically used for certain tasks that are performed repeatedly on EISEligibility Information System such as writing notices, case note templates, and alerts. Caseworkers can automate these tasks by using macros. Refer to [MS 104-3](../104/104-3_tools_for_effective_workload_management.md) for more information on using macros.

- Cutting and Pasting

When cutting and pasting information from another document or program such as Word, staff must ensure the information is accurate and complete. Staff must also avoid cutting and pasting from email or Outlook. The characters or format is not always compatible with EISEligibility Information System which causes the system to freeze.

Some methods can be good shortcuts and time savers. However, staff must remember that regardless of the selected method of documentation, they own the CANOCase Notes they write and any errors the case may contain.
