1008-6          ENDING AN EXTENSION

An extension may end for the following reasons:

1. The household no longer meets eligibility for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program per [ATAP MS 710](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/ta.htm#t=710%2F710_eligibility__general.htm).
2. The situation that meets extension criteria is resolved.
3. The household member who qualified the household for  the extension leaves the household.
4. The client(s) fail to develop or comply with an [FSSP](#)Family Self Sufficiency Plan while on a Hardship extension.
5. There is no request for continued extension when the approved extension period ends.
