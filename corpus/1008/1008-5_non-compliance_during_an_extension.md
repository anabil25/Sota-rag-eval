1008-5          NON-COMPLIANCE DURING AN EXTENSION

Clients must develop and comply with a [FSSP](#)Family Self Sufficiency Plan per [WSPM 1002](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/workservices1/wspm.htm#t=1002%2F1002_family_self-sufficiency_plan_(fssp).htm) while receiving an extension.  The extension criteria identified in [ATAP MS 701-4](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/ta.htm#t=701%2F701-4_extension_criteria.htm) determines the penalty for non-compliance.

See [ATAP 701-5](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/ta.htm#t=701%2F701-5_compliance_with_family_self-sufficiency_plan_while_on_extension.htm) for guidance regarding a household's non-compliance of their [FSSP](#)Family Self Sufficiency Plan during the extension of benefits.
