754-8          [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance AND [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income TEMPORARY ASSISTANCE HOUSEHOLDS

754-8 A.      RESOURCES OF [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance / [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income RECIPIENTS ARE DISREGARDED

All resources of any person who is actually receiving Adult Public Assistance (APA), and/or Supplemental Security Income (SSI) benefits are disregarded in determining [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program eligibility for other family members.

Note:

For Temporary Assistance purposes, Interim Assistance payments are not considered an [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance benefit.

754-8 B.      [TA](#)Temporary Assistance HOUSEHOLDS WITH POTENTIAL [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance / [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income ELIGIBILITY

If [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance or [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income eligibility is expected or has been established but no payments are being received by a Temporary Assistance household member, eligibility regarding resources is determined using normal policies and procedures. The resources of the potential [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance / [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income member will be determined as available to the [TA](#)Temporary Assistance household under normal policies.

754-8 C.      [TA](#)Temporary Assistance HOUSEHOLDS WHICH LATER COME TO HAVE AN [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance / [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income RECIPIENT

When the caseworker gets notification that [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income or [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance (not Interim Assistance) payments have begun for a Temporary Assistance household member, eligibility regarding resources must be immediately redetermined. Exclude all the [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance / [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income recipient's resources from the assistance unit's resources. It may be necessary to contact the [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income District Office to discover what resources they consider available to the [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income applicant.

In most cases, the only resources that will be of concern in determining [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program eligibility will be those that are available, not exempt, and jointly owned by the [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program applicant or recipient and the [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance / [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income recipient. In such cases, the equity value of the resource is prorated.

754-8 D.      [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance / [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income HOUSEHOLDS WITH POTENTIAL TEMPORARY ASSISTANCE ELIGIBILITY

If [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance or [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income has already determined a household member to be eligible, the caseworker must exclude from the [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program determination all resources [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance (not Interim Assistance) or [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income included in determining [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance / [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income eligibility, but prorate all jointly held resources as detailed above.
