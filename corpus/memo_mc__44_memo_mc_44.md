STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   MC #44

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  September 1, 2015

We are making changes to clarify existing policy and adding a section on incarcerated adults.

Highlights to policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[425-2D](../../425/425-2_who_determines_disability_blindness.md#425_2_D____STATE_ONLY_BLINDNESS_OR_DISABILITY_DECISIONS)               STATE-ONLY BLINDNESS OR DISABILITY DECISIONS

- Clarifies that a SSA disability decision trumps a DDS disability decision.

[441-1C](../../441/441-1_earned_income.md#441_1_C_SICK_PAYMENTS)               SICK LEAVE

- Changed the title from sick payment to sick leave.

[460-1F](../../460/460-1_definitions.md#460_1_F____SPOUSE)               SPOUSE

- Clarifies definition of Spouse

[470-1](../../470/470-1_ineligible_institutions.md)               INELIGIBLE INSTITUTIONS

- Added clarification that an incarcerated individual may be eligible for Medicaid for hospital stays over 24 hours.

[470-3](../../470/470-3_temporary_absence_from_institution.md#470_3_A_TEMPORARY_ABSENCE)               TEMPORARY ABSENCE

- Replace Affiliated Computer Systems with Xerox, the State's fiscal agent.
