STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2025-03

MANUAL: Heating Assistance Program

DATE: December 1, 2025

Several sections of the Heating Assistance Program Policy Manual have been revised in order to incorporate new policy, clarify existing policy, and align policies within our division's policy manuals.

If you have questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov.](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov)

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 3000-2](../3000/3000-2_definitions.md)            DEFINITIONS

- Removed Adult Public Assistance (APA) from the "mixed household" definition, as receipt of APA does not qualify a household as categorically eligible. This change prevents misinterpretation and aligns the manual with federal [LIHEAP](#)Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program guidance.
- Removed "Public Assistance Household" definition as it is an obsolete term, which was specific to the previous eligibility system and is not applicable within [ECOS](#)Energy Community Online System.

[3001-4](../3001/3001-4_verification.md#3001-4_H_INFORMATION_FROM_DATA_SYSTEMS_AND_EIS_INTERFACES)                   VERIFICATIONS

- Updated the name of the child support payment system from WinStar to [ACSESS](#)Alaska Child Support Enforcement Services System.

[ADDENDUM G](../addendum/addendum_g.md)     COUNTABLE AND EXCLUDED INCOME FOR HAP

- Added Census income to align with DPA manuals.
- Added clarity to Educational Income rows (Title IV and Non-Title IV.)
