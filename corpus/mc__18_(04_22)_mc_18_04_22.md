Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #18

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: April 1, 2022

We have updated the income standards for the MAGI Medicaid categories that are effected by the change in Federal Poverty Guidelines that is effective April 1, 2022. We have also made several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field. An overview of the changes are listed below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov.](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov)

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[815-1](../../815_residents_of_institutions/815-1_children_living_in_residential_treatment_centers_over_30_days.md)     Hospitalized Children Receiving Treatment over 30 Days

- Changed terminology from "institutional deeming rules" to "Special [LTC](#)Long Term Care Category" for clarity

[818](../../818_magi_medicaid_income/818_magi_medicaid_income.md)        MAGI Medicaid Income

- Updated dependent tax filing threshold

[820-6](../../820_budgeting_income/820-6_specialized_budgeting.md)     Specialized Budgeting

- Clarified caseworker should use the last [PFD](#)Permanent Fund Dividend amount officially announced by the [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance policy unit when determining MAGI Medicaid eligibility. This amount is used until the policy announces a new [PFD](#)Permanent Fund Dividend amount to use.

[823-1(B)](../../823_verification_and_documentation/823-1_sources_of_electronic_verification.md#823-1_B._Other_Electronic_Data_Sources)   Other Electronic Data Sources

- Clarified that [SVES](#)State Verification Exchange System/HUB must always be checked at application and recertification, and death match alerts that are generated during the certification period must be processed within 10 days

[825 A](../../825_coordination_with_the_federally_facilitated_marketplace/825_coordination_with_the_federally_facilitated_marketplace.md#825__A.____Applications_Submitted_through_the_Federally_Facilitated_marketplace)     Application Submitted Through The Federally Facilitated Marketplace

- Revised section for clarity

[826(F)](../../826_home_and_community_based_waiver_services/826_home_and_community_based_waiver_services.md#826_F.______HCB_WAIVER_SERVICES_AND_INSTITUTIONAL_ELIGIBILITY)     [HCB](#)Home and Community Based Waiver Services and Institutional Eligibility

- Clarified that even though there isn't a resource test for [MAGI](#)Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid eligibility, if a MAGI Medicaid recipient needs institutionalization or [HCB](#)Home and Community Based waiver services, [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance must complete the 5-year look back as outlined in [ADLTC](#)Adult Disabled and Long Term Care MS 554(D) to ensure that a non-allowable transfer has not occurred. If it has, a [TOA](#)Transfer of Assets penalty must be applied.

[827](../../827_retroactive_medicaid/825_retroactive_medicaid.md)      Retroactive Medicaid Eligibility

- Added a note clarifying when verification of actual income for each month is not needed for retroactive Medicaid

[833 (C)](../../833_hospital_presumptive_eligibility/833_hosptial_presumptive_eligibility_determination.md#833_C._Subtypes)    Subtypes

- Updated the income standards due to the Federal Poverty Guidelines change effective April 1, 2022

[Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum-1_magi_medicaid_income_standards.md)     MAGI Medicaid Income Standards

- Updated the income standards due to the Federal Poverty Guidelines change effective April 1, 2022
