APA - Alaska Adult Public Assistance Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   MC #34

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  August 31, 2012

As part of the project to combine the PFD HH manual with existing manuals, the explanation of how to replace SSI lost due to receipt of PFD has been added to the APA manual. A few other technical changes have also been made.

Highlights to policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 430-4(A)(1)](../../430/430-4_assets_that_are_not_resources.md#430_4_A_SOME_ASSETS_ARE_NOT_RESOURCES)  Some Assets are not Resources

- Corrects the paragraph to say "Assets are not resources if the individual does not have: Any ownership interest; **or** the legal right, authority, or power to liquidate them."

[MS 433-3](../../433/433-3_permanent_fund_dividend.md) Permanent Fund Dividend

- Adds sub chapters referencing hold harmless procedures for SSI applicants and recipients.

[MS 452-7(C)](../../452/452-7_to_whom_payment_is_made.md#452_7_C____PAYMENT_TO_A_GUARDIAN) Payment to A Third Party

- Adds a note stating court appointed guardians or conservators do not need to sign the APA 14.

[MS 482](../../482/482_claims.md) Claims

- Adds a note stating the agency does not have a limit to how far back a claim can be established.
