Alaska Temporary Assistance Program Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #44

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: June 2, 2014

We are making several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 711-1](../711/711-1_the_temporary_assistance_unit.md)      The Temporary Assistance Unit

- Adds information defining types of [ANI](#)Adult Not Included cases.
- Clarifies when income, resources, and needs are included in the [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program grant and when work services referrals are appropriate for [ANI](#)Adult Not Included cases.
- Adds examples.

[MS 715-2](../715/715-2_children_age_18.md)      Children Age 18

- Replaces the term high school with secondary school to adhere to the definition of a dependent child per the [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program state statute.
- Adds a note to define secondary schools.

[MS 717-4 C](../717/717-4_failure_to_cooperate_with_csed.md#717-4_C._FAILURE_TO_COMPLETE_A_CHILD_SUPPORT_INFORMATION_FORM_GEN_53)      Failures to Complete a Child Support Information Form

- Clarifies when a new Child Support Information Form (Appendix D or 1603) is needed.

[MS 720-2 A](../720/720-2_two-parent_seasonal_benefit_reduction.md#720-2_A._SEASONAL_BENEFIT_REDUCTION_POLICY)      Seasonal Benefit Reduction Policy

- Updates the note in this subsection to provide guidance on how to process [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program cases that are not subject to the seasonal reduction for two-parent households.

[MS 723-1](../723/723-1_good_cause.md)      Good Cause

- Clarifies that a client needs to make a good cause claim before it is applied by the [ET](#)Eligibility Technician.
- Adds a cross reference to the [WSPM](#)Work Services Procedure Manual section 1014-1.

[MS 740-2](../740/740-2_pass_ii_child_care.md)      PASS II Child Care

- Removes all references to the Department of Education and Early Development and replaces them with the Child Care Program Office.
- Adds a link to the [PASS](#)Parents Achieving Self-Sufficiency II and [PASS](#)Parents Achieving Self-Sufficiency III child care providers.

[MS 754-10 A](../754/754-10_transfer_of_resources.md#754-10_A._DEFINITIONS)      Equity Value

- Removes the cross reference to [TA](#)Temporary Assistance MS 752-11 because that manual section no longer exists.
