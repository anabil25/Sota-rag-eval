Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2026-01

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: April 1, 2026

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have questions, please contact Division of Operations Support team at [hss.dpadost@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpadost@alaska.gov) or the Policy & Program Development team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 102-4](../102/102-4_fee_agent_interviews.md)      FEE AGENT INTERVIEWS

- Revised section to include [HAP](#)Heating Assistance Program

[MS 103-1 A](../103/103-1_electronic_document_management.md#103-1_A._ILINX_DOCUMENT_MANAGEMENT_SYSTEM)      ILINX DOCUMENT

- Removed Family Progress Review as no longer used

[MS 103-6](../103/103-6_retention_of_case_records.md)      RETENTION OF CASE RECORDS

- Updated contact information for Division's Records Officers

[MS 105-4](../105/105-4_fee_agent_verification.md)      VIEWING AND VERIFYING CITIZENSHIP AND IDENTITY DOCUMENTS FOR MEDICAID

- Revised section to align with current program policy and procedures
- Remove subsection A as Medicaid verification is obtained through electronic verification

[MS 105-14](../105/105-14_systematic_alien_verification_for_entitlements_-_electronic_verification_process.md)      SYSTEMATIC ALIEN VERIFICATION FOR ENTITLEMENTS - ELECTRONIC VERIFICATION PROCESS

- Clarified that due to confidentiality, [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance is not allowed to disclose client information to law enforcement agencies, including Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE), unless permissible under Section 100-3 of the Admin Manual.

[MS 106-5](../106/106-5_instant_eligibility_verification_system_(ievs).md)      INSTANT ELIGIBILITY VERIFICATIONS SYSTEM

- Added new Third Party Liability (TPL) data source
- Clarified that if an individual has a bank account at a financial institution that is not listed as an "early warning bank" in [IEVS](#)Instant Eligibility Verification System, they must use the "add bank" function when running an [IEVS](#)Instant Eligibility Verification System report for someone applying for or receiving [ADLTC](#)Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Manual Medicaid.

[MS 108-3 A](../108/108-3_guidelines_for_creating_the_free_form_section.md#108-3_A._MAKE_SURE_YOUR_WRITING_IS_CLEAR_AND_EASY_TO_READ)      MAKE SURE YOUR WRITING IS CLEAR AND EASY TO READ

- Clarified that staff used dollar sign in the notices when detailing income or amount used in the determination

[MS 108-5](../108/108-5_eis_notice_listing.md)      EIS NOTICE LISTING

- Added information regarding the new match notices (NDNH, death, and prisoner)
- Added M315 Application for [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income

[MS 108-6](../108/108-6_aries_correspondences.md)      ARIES CORRESPONDENCES

- Revised section to include new system generated notice (automatic renewal not received closure notice)
- Revised section to include new manually generated notices (Notice of Match Result and Notice of Prisoner Match)

[MS 114-2](../114/114-2_procedures.md)      PROCEDURES

- Updated missing links to case review guidelines/desk guide and process

[MS 122-6](../122/122-6_alternate_payee.md)      ALTERNATE PAYEE

- Updated contact information to [FIS](#)Fidelity National Information Services Customer Service when alternate payee reaches out to them
- Clarified the difference between clients requesting alternate payee card and alternate payee card with mandatory payee

[MS 122-11](../122/122-11_mandatory_payees.md)      MANDATORY PAYEES

- Revised section to clarify who can sign the direct deposit form in mandatory payee situations
- Added link to Admin MS 100-3(I)

[ADDENDUM 109-E](../addenda/addendum_109_e_-_fraud_referral_and_findings_cano_template.md)      FRAUD REFERRAL AND FINDINGS CANO TEMPLATE

- Updated to remove GEN 97 and GEN 40 forms since no longer used for fraud referrals
