Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2018-02

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: October 1, 2018

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have any questions, please contact Field Services at [dpafield@alaska.gov](mailto:dpafield@alaska.gov) or the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 101-2](../101/101-2_forms.md)      APPLICATION FORMS

- Removed reference to the GEN 33 / GEN 35A and replaced with the GEN 72 as the Offices of Children's Services (OCS) changed what forms they use to review their Medicaid cases
- Renumbered section

[MS 101-4](../101/101-4_receipt_of_an_identifiable_application.md)      RECEIPT OF AN IDENTIFIABLE APPLICATION

- Clarified that staff should register an identifiable application as a request for service and send a copy of the application to the applicant for signature if it is not signed
- Added paragraph explaining that if an identifiable application is received and no program is checked, staff should register it as a request for SNAP

[MS 103-6(C)](../103/103-6_retention_of_case_records.md#103-6_C._RECORD_STORAGE_PROCEDURES)      RECORD STORAGE PROCEDURES

- Updated link to the Records Transfer List (RTL) forms
- Removed instructions for completion of records transfer list in section "D" as a link to the step by step instructions was added to section "C"
- Renumbered section

[MS 105-14](../105/105-14_systematic_alien_verification_for_entitlements_-_electronic_verification_process.md)      SYSTEMATIC ALIEN VERIFICATION FOR ENTITLEMENTS - ELECTRONIC VERIFICATION PROCESS

- Clarified that SAVE is a fee based service and charges are assessed monthly based on the number of agency transactions

[MS 105-14(B)](../105/105-14_systematic_alien_verification_for_entitlements_-_electronic_verification_process.md#105-14_B._LOGGING_INTO_SYSTEMATIC_ALIEN_VERIFICATION_FOR_ENTITLEMENT__SAVE_)      LOGGING INTO VERIFICATION INFORMATION SYSTEM (VIS)

- Changed the date the Verification Information System (VIS) main page was last updated to March 2017
- Removed reference to mailing the G-845 form as the Department of Homeland Security no longer uses the paper form as of May 1, 2018

[MS 105-14(G)](../105/105-14_systematic_alien_verification_for_entitlements_-_electronic_verification_process.md#105-14_G._THIRD_STEP_VERIFICATION)      THIRD STEP VERIFICATION - USING THE G-845 FORM

- Removed references to the paper form G-845 as the Department of Homeland Security no longer uses the paper form as of May 1, 2018
- Removed steps regarding sending photocopy documentation with the paper G-845 form as these documents are now submitted electronically for the third step verification
- Renumbered section

[MS 108-5](../108/108-5_eis_notice_listing.md)      EIS NOTICE LISTING

- Replaced Document Direct with Luminist as Document Direct is no longer available

[MS 111-3](../111/111-3_completing_the_report_of_claim_determination_form_(gen_95).md)      COMPLETING THE REPORT OF CLAIM DETERMINATION FORM (GEN 95)

- Added that staff are required to list the relevant manual section(s) that support the agency's action to recoup overpayments on the GEN 95
