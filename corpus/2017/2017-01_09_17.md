STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health
and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  2017-01

MANUAL:  General Relief
Assistance

DATE:  September 1, 2017

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy and clarify
existing policy.

Highlights of these policy revisions are explained below. If you have
any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at
[dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGE

[220-1F](../../220/220-1_non-financial_eligibility.md#220-1_F._LEGALLY_RESPONSIBLE_RELATIVES)
 LEGALLY RESPONSIBLE RELATIVES

- Added
  grandchildren, who are over 18, to the definition of legally responsible
  relatives to match the Alaska Statutes

[240-2A](../../240/240-2_specialized_burial_provisions.md#240-2_A._PUBLIC_ASSISTANCE_RECIPIENT_BURIALS)
 PUBLIC ASSISTANCE RECIPIENTS BURIALS

- Removed "relatives who are
  able to provide" and replaced with "legally responsible
  relatives" for clarity.
- Revised note to clarify that using
  a Medicaid Qualifying trust for Medicaid eligibility does not protect
  a deceased's income and resources from counting for burial assistance.

[240-5](../../240/240-5_claims_against_the_estate.md)
 CLAIMS AGAINST THE ESTATE

- Removed links to Addendum 1 and
  Addendum 2 and replaced with a link to the current [claims
  against the estate](http://www.courtrecords.alaska.gov/webdocs/forms/p-310.pdf) and [demand for notice](http://www.courtrecords.alaska.gov/webdocs/forms/p-305.pdf)
  forms.

[250-1](../../250/250-1_notice_of_finding.md)
  NOTICE OF FINDINGS

- Added
  subsection A and B  and renamed Notice of Findings for Rent &
  Utilities
- Added
  subsection B Notice of Finding for Burial Assistance
- Clarified
  when approving burial assistance, ET's must indicate the approval
  amount in the notice and send a copy to the funeral home.

Addendum 3 renamed to [Addendum
1](../../addendum/addendum_3_judicial_districts_of_alaska.md)
