**STATE
OF ALASKA**

**Department
of Health and Social Services**

**Division of Public
Assistance**

**TRANSMITTAL
NUMBER:**   2017-01

**MANUAL:**
  Alaska
SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program

**DATE:**
  April
3, 2017

We renamed this manual to reflect the federal
name change from The Food Stamp Program to the Supplemental Nutrition
Assistance Program with the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008. We have made changes
to the self-employment annualization table based on changes in the Federal
Poverty Guidelines ([*FPG*](javascript:TextPopup(this))*Federal
Poverty Guidelines*)
for Alaska. We also updated the effective dates of Alaska's statewide
waiver from the ABAWDAble-bodied Adult Without Dependents
work requirements and time limits.  Other
changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the
results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights
of these and other policy revisions are described below.  If you
have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development
Team via email at: [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

**OVERVIEW
OF CHANGES**
