**STATE

**Department
of Health and Social Services**

**Division
of Public Assistance** 

**TRANSMITTAL
NUMBER:**   2017-03

**MANUAL:**
  Alaska Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program

**DATE:**
  December 1, 2017

This update expanded and
clarified information regarding identity verification and aligned the
SNAP qualified alien policy with other manuals. Other
changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the
results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below.
If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development
team at d[papolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

**OVERVIEW
OF CHANGES**

**[MS 600-2](../../600/600-2_definitions.md)
        DEFINITIONS**

- **Corrected link to Federal
  minimum wage.**

[MS 602-1 C.](../../602/602-1_c.md)
   IDENTITY

- Expanded and clarified information
  regarding identity verification.

**[MS 602-1 D.](../../602/602-1_d.md)    US
CITIZENSHIP AND ELIGIBLE ALIEN STATUS**

- Aligned qualified alien
  policy and people exempt from “5-Year” waiting period with other
  manuals

[MS
605-3B](../../605/605-3_b.md)    EXPEDITED SERVICE

- Clarified
  that postponed interviews are treated the same as other postponed
  verifications. If the client's interview is postponed and they
  "no-show" for the scheduled interview, they cannot have
  their interview postponed again.
