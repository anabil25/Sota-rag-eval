STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2014-01 (6/14)

MANUAL:  General Relief Assistance

DATE:  June 2, 2014

We are making changes to the manual to clarify verification of vendor acceptance of payment.

Highlights to policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 210-2](../../210/210-3_verification.md)  VERIFICATION

- Clarifies the vendor needs to accept the GRA payment in order to meet emergent need eligibility requirement.

[MS 220-1(B)](../../220/220-1_non-financial_eligibility.md#220-1_B._SPECIFIC_NEED) SPECIFIC NEED

- Clarifies the payment must meet the emergent need or there is no GRA eligibility.
