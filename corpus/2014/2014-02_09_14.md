TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  2014-02

MANUAL:  General Relief Assistance

DATE:  September 2, 2014

This manual change updates the definition of PRUCOLPermanently Residing Under Color of Law.  

Highlights of these policy revisions are explained below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGE

[MS 220-1C](../../220/220-1_non-financial_eligibility.md#220-1_C._U.S._CITIZEN_OR_LEGAL_ALIEN)  U.S. Citizen or Legal Alien

- Clarifies that a PRUCOLPermanently Residing Under Color of Law alien must have documentation from USCISUnited States Citizenship and Immigration Services stating they are PRUCOLPermanently Residing Under Color of Law.
