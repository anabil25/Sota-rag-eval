STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  2014-01

MANUAL:  Senior Benefit Program

DATE:  September 2, 2014

This manual change reflects the updated Fair Hearing regulations regarding how a hearing must be requested.  Also updates the process for underpayments and overpayments.

Highlights of these policy revisions are explained below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGE

[MS 362](../../362__fair_hearings/362_fair_hearings.md)     FAIR HEARINGS

- Clarifies that a fair hearing request must be in writing.

[MS 363-1](../../363__claims/363_claims.md#363-1_Underpayments)  UNDERPAYMENTS

- Updates section as there is now a process in place to issue underpayments

[MS 363-2](../../363__claims/363_claims.md#363-2_Overpayments)  OVERPAYMENTS

- Updates definition of an overpayment and that the Division is processing overpayments.

[MS 363-2 (A)](../../363__claims/363_claims.md#363-2_Overpayments)  AGENCY CAUSED

- Adds a new section on how to process agency caused overpayments

[MS 363-2 (B)](../../363__claims/363_claims.md#363-2_Overpayments)   TREATMENT OF A SENIOR BENEFIT OVERPAYMENT

- Adds a new section defining agency caused and client caused overpayments

[MS 363-2 (C)](../../363__claims/363_claims.md#363-2_Overpayments)   ESTABLISHING A CLAIM

- Adds a new section detailing how to establish a claim

[MS 363-2 (D)](../../363__claims/363_claims.md#363-2_Overpayments)   NOTICE REQUIREMENT

- Adds a new section on notice requirement regarding claims

[MS 363-2 (E)](../../363__claims/363_claims.md#363-2_Overpayments)   REPAYMENT OPTIONS

- Adds a new section on repayment options

[MS 363-2 (F)](../../363__claims/363_claims.md#363-2_Overpayments)   HARDSHIP PROVISION

- Adds a new section on Hardship Provision
