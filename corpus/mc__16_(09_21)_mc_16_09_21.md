Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #16

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: September 1, 2021

We have made several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field. An overview of the changes are listed below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[816-1(B)](../../816_magi_medicaid_categories/816-1_magi_category_eligibility_factors.md#816-1_B._PREGNANT_WOMEN)   Pregnant Women

- Clarified that verification of pregnancy is only required if the mother is expecting multiple births or if the caseworker finds the situation questionable

[819](../../819_types_of_income/819_types_of_income.md)  Types of Income

- Revised section for clarity by adding new subsections regarding earned income, unearned income, and exempt income
- Clarified that bonuses are treated like lump sum payments
- Clarified when private disability payments are countable and when they are exempt

[823-1(B)](../../823_verification_and_documentation/823-1_sources_of_electronic_verification.md#823-1_B._____OTHER_ELECTRONIC_DATA_SOURCES)   Other Electronic Data Sources

- Clarified that IEVSInstant Eligibility Verification System must be checked at application, renewal, and when an adult is being added to a case
- Removed INGENSPublic Information Database as this is no longer a valid interface now that IEVSInstant Eligibility Verification System is available

[823-2](../../823_verification_and_documentation/823-2_reasonable_compatibility.md)   Reasonable Compatibility

- Revised section for clarity

[828-2(F)](../../828_transitional_medicaid/828-2_transitional_medicaid_eligiblity.md#828-2_f.___ADDING_HOUSEHOLD_MEMBERS_TO_THE_TRANSITIONAL_MEDICAID_CASE)  Adding Household Members to the Transitional Medicaid Case

- For clarity, added another example with "adding a child" to the household

[831(E)](../../831_emergency_treatment_for_aliens/831_emergency_treatment_for_aliens.md#831_E._______COORDINATION_WITH_THE_DIVISION_OF_HEALTH_CARE_SERVICES__DHCS_)     Coordination with Division of Health Care Services (DHCS)

- Added note clarifying that the letter from DHCSDivision of Health Care Services indicating an individual received services of an emergent nature and indicating the covered dates of service is not a determination of eligibility. DPADivision of Public Assistance caseworkers must determine eligibility for Emergency Treatment for Aliens based on the criteria outlined in [831(B)](../../831_emergency_treatment_for_aliens/831_emergency_treatment_for_aliens.md#831_B.______ELIGIBILITY_REQUIREMENTS).

[Addendum 3](../../addenda/addendum-3_countable_and_excluded_income_for_magi_medicaid.md)   Countable and Excluded Income for MAGI Medicaid

- Revised table by removing detailed information and moved it into new subsections of [MS 819](../../819_types_of_income/819_types_of_income.md)
