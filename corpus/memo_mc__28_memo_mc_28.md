STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #28

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  January 3, 2011

This manual change updates program policy in conjunction with changes to the Medicaid, Alaska Temporary Assistance, and Food Stamp manuals.

Also included in this manual change is an update to the program standards for 2011. Each year the Adult Public Assistance need and maximum payment standards are adjusted to reflect the COLACost of Living Allowance increase in Social Security benefits. There will be no COLACost of Living Allowance increase for 2011.This manual change updates the 2011 standards, which become effective with the benefit month of January 2011.

Highlights to policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).
