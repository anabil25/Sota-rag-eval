1012-3          ADDITIONAL RESOURCES WHILE REFUSING CASH

While in refused cash status, the household still remains eligible for the following resources:

- The full amount of Child Support paid by the parent(s), see [ATAP MS 717-7](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/ta/717/717-7_how_cssd_collections_affect_case.htm);
- [PASS I Childcare](https://health.alaska.gov/media/igiccwuf/child-care-assistance-program-policies-and-procedures.pdf);see section 4040
- Supportive Services, see [MS 1004](../1004/1004_supportive_services.md).
