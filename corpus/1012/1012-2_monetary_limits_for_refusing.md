1012-2          MONETARY LIMITS FOR REFUSING

There is no monetary limit to the amount of cash that may be refused. Case managers may consider supplementing the family's needs with supportive services funds. All supportive services must be in alignment with requirements, see [MS 1004](../1004/1004_supportive_services.md).
