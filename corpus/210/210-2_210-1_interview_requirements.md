210-1         INTERVIEW REQUIREMENTS

The caseworker must interview the applicant prior to the determination of eligibility unless we are aware of extenuating circumstances that make the interview impossible.

If the applicant does not attend the scheduled interview and we are not aware of a reason that the applicant cannot attend an interview, the application should be denied.

Note:

An interview with the applicant is required and must be completed before an eligibility determination is made for burial assistance application.
