APA - Alaska Adult Public Assistance Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   [MC](memo_mc__62_(09_21).md#)Manual Change #72

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  April 1, 2025

We are making several changes to incorporate new policy and clarify existing policy.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions, contact the Policy & Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[410(4)](../../410/410-4_verification.md)     Verification

- Changed Child Support Services Division (CSSD) to Child Support Enforcement Division (CSED).
- Revised section for clarity

[410(5)E](../../410/410-5_actions_taken_on_the_application.md#410_5_E__WITHDRAWING_AN_APPLICATION)     Withdrawing an Application

- Clarified that [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance application withdrawal requests can be done with a recorded signature through the [VCC](#)Virtual Contact Center.

[423](../../423/423_alaska_residency.md)     Alaska Residency

- Clarified when caseworkers should consider Alaska residency questionable.

[433(4)8](../../433/433-4_able_accounts.md)     Certain Employed [ABLE](#)Achieving a Better Life Experience Account Beneficiaries May Make Additional Contributions

- Clarified the amount of additional contributions can be made for certain employed [ABLE](#)Achieving a Better Life Experience account beneficiaries.

[460(6)A(2)](../../460/460-6_income_exclusions_of_ineligible_spouse.md#460_6_A_2__SUPPORT_PAYMENTS)     Support Payments          

- Changed Child Support Services Division (CSSD) to Child Support Enforcement Division (CSED).

[Addendum 2](../../addendum/addendum_2_the_leave_and_earnings_statement.md)     The Leave and Earnings Statement

- Changed Child Support Services Division (CSSD) to Child Support Enforcement Division (CSED).
