STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #67

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: December 1, 2020

Social Security announced a 1.3% [COLA](#)Cost of Living Allowance increase effective January 1, 2021. We have updated the Alaska Temporary Assistance Program (ATAP) income eligibility and shelter allowance standards to reflect the annual [COLA](#)Cost of Living Allowance adjustments in Social Security benefits. The new standards are effective beginning with the benefit month of January 2021. The Eligibility Information System (EIS) will automatically apply these new standards when determining eligibility and benefit amounts beginning January 2021.

Highlights of all the policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 705-4 B](../705/705-4_verification.md#705-4_B._INFORMATION_FROM_DATA_SYSTEMS_AND_EIS_INTERFACES)      INFORMATION FROM DATA SYSTEMS AND [EIS](#)Eligibility Information System INTERFACES

- Replaced reference to [CSSD](#)Child Support Services Division's child support verification database [NSTAR](#)Northern Support through Automated Resources with the name of their new database WinStar.
- Added [IEVS](#)Instant Eligibility Verification System as an interface that must be checked during the application process to comply with federal requirements.

[MS 711-7](../711/711-7_temporary_absence_from_home.md)      TEMPORARY ABSENCE FROM HOME

- Added the reference "parent" in conjunction with "caretaker relative" to keep phrasing throughout the manual consistent.
- Added conditions and updated allowable circumstances for an allowable absence exceeding 30 days to align with the [TANF](#)Temporary Assistance to Needy Families State Plan.

[MS 712-1](../712/712-1_definition_of_a_caretaker_relative.md)      DEFINITION OF A CARETAKER RELATIVE

- Replaced reference to [CSSD](#)Child Support Services Division's child support verification database [NSTAR](#)Northern Support through Automated Resources with the name of their database WinStar.

[MS 712-3 B](../712/712-3_parental_relationship.md#712-3_B._VERIFYING_PARENTAL_RELATIONSHIP_FOR_CHILDREN_BORN_IN_ALASKA)      VERIFYING PARENTAL RELATIONSHIP FOR CHILDREN BORN IN ALASKA

- Replace reference to [CSSD](#)Child Support Services Division's child support verification database [NSTAR](#)Northern Support through Automated Resources with the name of their new database WinStar.

[MS 780-1 G](../780/780-1_definitions.md#780-1_G._RATABLE_REDUCTION)      RATABLE REDUCTION

- Updated the ratable reduction to reflect the 2021 Social Security [COLA](#)Cost of Living Allowance increase.

[MS 780-3](../780/780-3_calculating_the_payment_amount.md)      CALCULATING THE PAYMENT AMOUNT

- Updated the standards to reflect the 2021 Social Security [COLA](#)Cost of Living Allowance increase, along with examples A, B, C to reflect the updated Need Standards.

[ADDENDUM 2](../addenda/addendum_2_-_program_standards.md)      ALASKA TEMPORARY ASSISTANCE INCOME AND PROGRAM STANDARDS

- Updated the income eligibility standards and maximum shelter allowances for 2021.
- Updated the monetary amount referenced when determining deemed income of the stepparents to the current pregnant woman need standard.
