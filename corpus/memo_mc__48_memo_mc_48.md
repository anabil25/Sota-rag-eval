TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   MC
#48

MANUAL:  Adult Public Assistance

DATE:  December 1, 2016

This year Social Security announced a Cost of Living Allowance (COLACost of Living Allowance) increase effective January 2017.
 The Adult Public Assistance need and maximum payment standards are
adjusted by the same percentage as the COLACost of Living Allowance
increase in Social Security benefits.  This year the COLACost of Living Allowance increase is 0.3%.  We have
incorporated the 2017 standards into the examples and [Addendum
1](../../addendum/addendum_1.md).  These standards become effective with the benefit month of
January 2017.  The COLACost of Living Allowance
adjustment also causes:

- An increase in the income limit for Substantial Gainful Activity;
  and
- An increase in the student earned income exclusion.

Highlights of these policy revision are described below.  If you
have any questions contact the Policy and Program Development Team at
email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).
