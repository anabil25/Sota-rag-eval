Alaska Temporary Assistance Program Manual

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #48

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: June 1, 2015

We are making several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 711-4](../711/711-4_who_may_not_be_included_in_the_assistance_unit.md)      Who May Not Be Included in the Assistance Unit

- Updated the second note in this subsection to remove language about children receiving subsidized adoption payments being optional household members.

[MS 717-7](../717/717-7_how_csed_collections_affect_the_temporary_assistance_case.md)      How [CSSD](#)Child Support Services Division Collections Affect the Temporary Assistance Case

- Removed the note in the subsection B for families who lose eligibility for Family Medicaid due to an increase in child support income.

[MS 730-2](../730/730-2_exemptions_from_work_activities.md)      Exemptions from Work Activities

- Removed the language regarding work exemptions being applied in conjunction with the FSSP.

[MS 740-2](../740/740-2_pass_ii_child_care.md)      [PASS](#)Parents Achieving Self-Sufficiency II Child Care

- Removed the words "due to" earnings and replaced them with "with" for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program households closing their case with earned income and needing to be referred to a [PASS](#)Parents Achieving Self-Sufficiency II child care provider.

[MS 740-2 C](../740/740-2_pass_ii_child_care.md#740-2_C._REFERRAL_FOR_PASS_II)      Referral for [PASS](#)Parents Achieving Self-Sufficiency II

- Removed the words "due to" earnings and replaced them with "with" for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program households closing their case with earned income and needing to be referred to a [PASS](#)Parents Achieving Self-Sufficiency II child care provider.

[MS 756-4 A](../756/756-4_types_of_income.md#756-4_A._EARNED_INCOME)      Earned Income

- Removed the definitions of earned income and self-employment earned income. Information moved to MS 759.
- Added a cross reference to MS 759 (Earned Income).

[MS 756-4 B](../756/756-4_types_of_income.md#756-4_B._UNEARNED_INCOME)      Unearned Income

- Removed the definition of unearned income and the bulleted list of unearned income types. Information moved to MS 758.
- Added a cross reference to MS 758 (Unearned Income).

[MS 758](../758/758_unearned_income.md)      Unearned Income

- Added information that was previously located in MS 756-4 B to chapter 758.

[MS 759](../759/759_earned_income.md)      Earned Income

- Added information that was previously located in MS 759-4 A to chapter 759.
