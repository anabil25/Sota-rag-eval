200-1         CONFIDENTIALITY

There are very specific laws and regulations that protect information about public assistance clients.  See [Administrative Procedures manual section 100-3](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/admin/100/100-3.htm) for more information about confidentiality rules.
