200-2         CIVIL RIGHTS

The GRAGeneral Relief Assistance program is administered without regard to age, race, color, sex, handicap, religious creed, national origin, or political beliefs. If a client complains of discrimination, the first and only action of the caseworker is to take the matter directly to the supervisor.  See [Administrative Procedures Manual section 100-4](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/admin/100/100-4.htm) for more information about civil rights procedures.
