442           INCOME EXCLUSIONS

Certain income is excluded in determining the countable monthly income of an APAAdult Public Assistance applicant or recipient, together with the income of his or her spouse. This chapter explains the income exclusions allowed to APAAdult Public Assistance applicants and recipients. Refer to chapter [460](../460/460_financial_responsibility_and_deeming.md) for policy on income exclusions allowed to ineligible spouses and alien sponsors.
