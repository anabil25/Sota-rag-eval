ADLTC - Alaska Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC  #49

MANUAL:  Aged, Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid Eligibility Manual

DATE:  December 03, 2018

This year Social Security announced a COLA (Cost of Living Allowance) increase effective January 2019.  We have updated [Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum_1.md) and the examples throughout the manual with the following items to reflect the 2.8% increase for 2019:

- An increase to the Long Term Care community spouse resource and income allowance and household member standards;
- An increase to the Excess Home Equity resources maximum;
- An increase to the Special Long Term Care Income Standard;
- Changes in Medicare Part A and B premiums; and
- Changes in Medicare Part D premium and coverage information, including changes to the Low-Income Subsidy program

In addition to the COLACost of Living Allowance change, this manual change consists of several small technical changes or clarifications as a result of suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at e-mail [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 505 B](../../505/505_third_party_liability_and_recovery.md#505_B.______WHAT_IS_A_THIRD_PARTY_RESOURCE___TPR__)  What is a Third Party Resource

- Added long term care insurance to the list of third party resources and a note explaining how to notify TPLThird Party Liability if a long term care insurance policy exists.

[MS 554 D](../../554/554_transfer_of_assets.md#554_D_Look_Back_Period)  Look Back Period

- Updated example due to COLACost of Living Allowance increase

[MS 562 A](../../562/562_processing_hcb_waiver.md#562_A.______MAGI_WAIVER_CASES)  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Waiver Cases

- Removed the statement that MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income waiver cases do not impose a transfer of asset penalty.  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income waiver cases do impose a transfer of asset penalty.

[MS 570 E](../../570/570_post_eligibility_and_coc.md#570_E__ALLOWABLE_DEDUCTIONS_FOR_COST_OF_CARE_DETERMINATIONS)  Allowable Deductions for Cost-Of-Care

- Added a note to clarify that deductions must be verified before they are allowed to be used as a deduction.

[MS 570 E(3)](../../570/570_post_eligibility_and_coc.md#570_E_3__Family_Member_Allowance) Family Member Allowance

- Updated section to clarify how a Family Member Allowance should be calculated and corrected reference from the MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid standards to the ATAPAlaska Temporary Assistance Program ANIAdult Not Included standards.

[MS 570 H](../../570/570_post_eligibility_and_coc.md#570_H__NURSING_HOME_EXAMPLES)  Nursing Home Examples

- Updated example due to [COLA](#)Cost of Living Allowance increase

[MS 570 J](../../570/570_post_eligibility_and_coc.md#570_J__HOME_AND_COMMUNITY_BASED_WAIVER_CASE_EXAMPLES)  Home and Community Based Waiver Case Examples

- Updated example due to COLACost of Living Allowance increase

[Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum_1.md)

- Updated standards due to COLA change
- Updated Medicare Part A, Part B and Part D premiums for 2019
- Updated 2019 Spousal Impoverishment Standards

[Addendum 2](../../addenda/addendum_2.md)

- Removed MAGI Medicaid subtypes to eliminate confusion
