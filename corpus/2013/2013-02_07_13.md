**STATE OF ALASKA**

**Department of Health and Social Services**

**Division of Public Assistance**

**TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:**   2013-02

**MANUAL:**   Alaska Food Stamp Program

**DATE:**   July 1, 2013

This manual change reflects the updated Fair Hearing regulations regarding how a hearing must be requested and includes a link to the Administrative Manual section 117 for this process.

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at (907)465-3328 or email: [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

**[MS 602-1 H](../../602/602-1_h.md)****Nonfinancial Eligibility**

- Updates the manual to clarify that the F001 notice is to be sent to all eligible applicants and recertified households who have individuals required to meet work requirements.

**[MS 602-4 D](../../602/602-4_d.md)****Medical Deductions**

- Updates the link to the IRS Internal Revenue Service website for current mileage rates.

**[MS 605-1 B](../../605/605-1_b.md)****Students**

- **Updates the link to the IRS Internal Revenue Service website for current mileage rates.**

**[MS 605-2 D](../../605/605-2_d.md)****Income from Self-Employment**

- **Updates the link to the IRS Internal Revenue Service website for current mileage rates.**

**[MS 608-1 A&B](../../608/608-1.md)** **Fair Hearings**

- Updates fair hearing section to read that written and verbal requests for a hearing are acceptable for the Food Stamp program only. Other programs must have a written request.
- Removes section "B" detailing "continuation of benefits, " and now refers to Administrative Manual section 117.
