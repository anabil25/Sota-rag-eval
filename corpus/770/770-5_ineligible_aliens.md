770-5          INELIGIBLE ALIENS

An alien parent who is not a "qualified alien" is not eligible for [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program and is not referred to Work Services for case management. The resources and income of an ineligible alien parent/caretaker are considered wholly available to their dependent child, and a portion of their income and resources are included in calculating the payment determination.

If the ineligible alien is a dependent child, they are not included in the assistance unit; their resources and income are excluded.

Note:

In [EIS](#)Eligibility Information System, an ineligible alien parent/caretaker is coded 'DI'. If the ineligible alien is not a mandatory household member, they are excluded from the assistance unit entirely and coded 'OU'.

In [EIS](#)Eligibility Information System, an ineligible alien child is coded 'OU'.
