452-12          RETROACTIVE [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance BENEFIT CALCULATIONS

[IA](#)Interim Assistance recipients qualify for an [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance payment when they are approved for [SSA](#)Social Security Administration or [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income and their case is converted to [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance. Refer to manual section [410-7](../410/410-7_initial_month_benefits.md) for the policy regarding when APA payments begin.

Calculate a retroactive [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance payment for an [IA](#)Interim Assistance case conversion by subtracting the countable income from the [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance maximum payment standard. Then subtract the [IA](#)Interim Assistance payment.

Example:  [IA](#)Interim Assistance Recipient (based on 2026 payment standards)

Applicant applies for both [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance and [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income benefits on July 15, 2026. [IA](#)Interim Assistance benefits are approved beginning with the benefit month of July.  On October 28, 2026, the Social Security Administration notifies the applicant that [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income payments are approved back to the benefit month of August.

Because it is too late to stop the [IA](#)Interim Assistance payment for November, the [IA](#)Interim Assistance case is closed effective November 30, 2026, and an [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance case is opened beginning December 1. Since the applicant was notified of [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income eligibility in October, the applicant is eligible for [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance benefits beginning November 1. The retroactive [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance payment for November is calculated as follows:

$1,356    [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance Maximum Payment Standard
-    994    [SSI](#)Supplemental Security Income Income Standard
$  362     [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance payment

$362     [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance Payment
-280      Monthly [IA](#)Interim Assistance Payment
$ 82      Retroactive [APA](#)Adult Public Assistance Payment for November
