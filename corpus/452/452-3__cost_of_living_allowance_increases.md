452-3          COST OF LIVING ALLOWANCE (COLA ) INCREASES

Whenever SSISupplemental Security Income benefits are increased because of an increase in the cost of living, APAAdult Public Assistance need/payment standards are also increased. These cost-of-living increases occur each year on January 1.
