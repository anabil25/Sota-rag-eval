452           PAYMENTS

This section explains the methods used to calculate benefit amounts for the APAAdult Public Assistance program. It also explains how and to whom assistance payments are made. Refer to chapter [460](../460/460_financial_responsibility_and_deeming.md) for related policy on the deeming of income and resources of ineligible spouses and/or alien sponsors.
