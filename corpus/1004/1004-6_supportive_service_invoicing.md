1004-6           SUPPORTIVE SERVICE INVOICING

Supportive Service invoicing must be submitted by non-[DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance Work Services providers in accordance with the guidance provided in WSMP section [1018-1](../1018/1018-1_work_services_performance_and_supportive_services_invoicing.md).

[DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance Work Services will process Supportive Services through [JAS](#)Jobs Automated Payments System.
