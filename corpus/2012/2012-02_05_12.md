MANUAL:   Alaska Food
Stamp Program

DATE:   May 18, 2012

The Farm Bill of 2008
(which governs Food Stamp rules) mandated the resource limits for the
Food Stamp program increase each year based on the Consumer Price Index.  The
resource limit for households who are SPECAT and have at least one household
member who is disabled or age 60 or older increased to $3,250 effective
October 1, 2011.  There is no change to
the $2,000 resource limit.

Highlights
of these and other policy revisions are described below.  If
you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development
