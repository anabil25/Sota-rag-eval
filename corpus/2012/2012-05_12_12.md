**Department
of Health and Social Services**

**Division
of Public Assistance**

**TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:**
  2012-05

**MANUAL:**   Alaska
Food Stamp Program

**DATE:**   December
1, 2012

This
year Social Security announced a 1.7% Cost of Living Allowance (COLA)
increase effective January 1, 2013.  This manual change updates program
policy in conjunction with the COLACost of Living Allowance
increase, which affect the dollar fee limits charged for services performed
as a representative payee.  These fees are related to recipients
of SSISupplemental Security Income and are subject
to increase by the cost-of-living adjustment.

Other changes
are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results
of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of
these and other policy revisions are described below.  If
you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development
Team at (907)465-3328 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

**OVERVIEW OF CHANGES**

[MS 602-1
D](../../602/602-1_d.md) Citizenship and Eligible Alien Status

- Adds subsections
  8 and 9 to clarify policy involving citizenship for children born
  in another country to US citizens and for children who are being adopted
  as mandated by the "Child Citizenship Act of 2000."

602-3 B(2)](../../602/602-3_b.md#602_3_B_2__SSI_SSA_VA_Income) Unearned Income - SSI/SSA/VA Income

- Updates
  COLA budget for SSI/SSA/VA income.

[MS
604-4 C](../../604/604-4.md) Notice Requirements During the Certification Period

- Adds
  a number 7 to include adverse action regarding incarcerated household
  members.

[MS
605-1](../../605/605-1_d.md#605_1_D_1_Definition_and_Eligibility) D Institutions

1. - Updates
     the contact information for Behavioral Health.

[MS
605-2 D](../../605/605-2_d.md) Income from Self-Employment

[MS
605-7 G](../../605/605-7_permanent_fund_processing.md#605-7_G._PFD_at_Recertification_) PFD at Recertification
