STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #33

MANUAL: Alaska Temporary Assistance Program

DATE: August 1, 2011

As a result of requests from the field we have clarified when subsidized housing expenses must be verified.

Highlights to policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or email [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 705-4 A](../705/705-4_verification.md#705-4_A._VERIFICATION_REQUIRED_PRIOR_TO_ALLOWING_DEDUCTIONS)      Verification Required Prior to Allowing Deductions

- Adds a note stating that rent for individuals in subsidized housing must be verified.

[MS 780-19 I](../780/780-19_shelter_allowance.md#780-19_I._ACCEPTABLE_VERIFICATION)      When Shelter Costs Must Be Verified

- Adds a note stating that rent for individuals in subsidized housing must be verified at initial application, during the review period or at review if the amount has changed more than $25.00 or if the client has moved to a new location.
