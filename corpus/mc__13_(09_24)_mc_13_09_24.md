STATE OF ALASKA

DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: MC #13

MANUAL: Work Services Procedure Manual

DATE: September 3, 2024

We have made technical corrections and incorporated the changes outlined below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Work Services Policy team at WSTA@alaska.gov or the Policy & Program Development team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 1001-3 A.](../1001/1001-3_conducting_the_employaility_assessment.md#1001-3_A._CASE_MANAGER_RESPONSIBILITIES)      CASE MANAGER RESPONSIBILITIES

- Clarified that a copy of the ATAP Work Services Employment Planning Interview (TA 5) may be maintained in the client's hard copy or electronic file.

[MS 1004-3 (d)](../1004/1004-3_what_supportive_services_cannot_be_used_for.md)      WHAT SUPPORTIVE SERVICES CANNOT BE USED FOR

- Clarified supportive services limitations for subsistence activities.

[MS 1004-4](../1004/1004-4_supportive_service_codes_and_definitions.md)      SUPPORTIVE SERVICES CODES AND DEFINITIONS

- Revised note regarding Youth Enrichment Activities for clarity.

[MS 1004-6](../1004/1004-6_supportive_service_invoicing.md)      SUPPORTIVE SERVICES INVOICING

- Changed reference to [WSPM](#)Work Services Procedure Manual 1018-1.

[MS 1007-2](../1007/1007-2_requesting_a_penalty.md)      REQUESTING A PENALTY

- Added note to discourage the use of penalties while an ATAP application is in received or pended status.

[MS 1015-8](../1015/1015-8_performance_confirmation_report.md)      PERFORMANCE CONFIRMATION

- Revised section for clarity and added an example.

[MS 1018-1](../1018/1018-1_work_services_performance_and_supportive_services_invoicing.md)      WORK SERVICES PERFORMANCE AND SUPPORTIVE SERVICE INVOICING 

- Clarified that Supportive Services invoices must identify expenditures for post-TA clients (i.e. P-GV).
