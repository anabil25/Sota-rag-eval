Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #5

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: December 1, 2017

We are making several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field. The Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services provided clarification regarding the SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program waiver and individuals receiving Expansion Medicaid in the month they turn 65 years old.

Highlights of the further policy changes are described below.

If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[800](../../800_introduction_to_medicaid/800_introduction_to_medicaid.md) Introduction to Medicaid

- Replaced "Xerox Corporation" with "Conduent State Healthcare, LLC."

[801](../../801_general_medicaid_provisions/801_general_medicaid_information.md) General Medicaid Provisions

- Replaced "Xerox Corporation" with "Conduent State Healthcare, LLC."

[805 A](../../805_medicaid_recipient_id_card/805_medicaid_recipient_id_card.md#805_A._________ISSUING_MEDICAID_TO_ELIGIBLE_RECIPIENTS) Issuing Medicaid to Eligible Recipients

- Replaced "Xerox Corporation" with "Conduent State Healthcare, LLC."

806-2 G Approvals Based on SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Eligibility

- Added a new section based on the amended E-14 waiver approval from CMSCenters for Medicaid and Medicare Services allowing Medicaid for current SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program recipients back to the original MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid application date.

[809 B](../../809_alaska_residency/809_alaska_residency.md#809_B.____TEMPORARY_ABSENCE_AND_RESIDENTS_RESIDING_OUT_OF_THE_STATE) Temporary Absence and Residents Residing Out Of The State

- Replaced "Xerox Corporation" with "Conduent State Healthcare, LLC."

[816-1 G](../../816_magi_medicaid_categories/816-1_magi_category_eligibility_factors.md#816-1_G._EXPANSION_GROUP_) Expansion Group

- Coordinated with ADLTCAdult Disabled and Long Term Care Medicaid when a 64 year old Expansion Group recipient turns 65 as clarified by CMSCenters for Medicaid and Medicare Services.

[824-1](../../824_magi_medicaid_change_reporting_requirements/824-1_acting_on_reported_changes.md) Acting on Reported Changes

- Added paragraph clarifying client may close an already open MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid case verbally or in writing.
