MEMORANDUM

DATE: December 7, 1999

TO: All Temporary Assistance Manual Holders

FROM: Policy and Program Development Team

SUBJECT: Temporary Assistance Manual Change #1

This manual change updates the information in the Temporary Assistance manual with the increases in the 185% and need standards that take effect January 1, 2000. It also clarifies "date of entry" of qualified aliens, adds discrimination to the list of good cause reasons that excuse an individual from penalties, clarifies the policy on providing [PASS](#)Parents Achieving Self-Sufficiency I child care assistance after a Temporary Assistance case is closed, and makes other technical corrections. An outline of these changes is shown below.

All changes associated with the increase in the need standards are effective January 1, 2000. The other changes are effective with receipt of this manual change.

Please address any questions or comments to the Policy and Program Development Team by email at dpapolicy@health.state.ak.us, by SYSM to HPAPOLY or call us at 465-3347.
