108-4          HOW EISEligibility Information System NOTICE WORK

EISEligibility Information System automatically generates several informational notices to the client when an application is approved and in other specific situations (such as notification of school meal eligibility, which is sent to eligible households just before the beginning of each school year). EISEligibility Information System also generates other notices automatically in the following circumstances:

- When a SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program certification period ends,
- When a program review is due, and
- When [EIS](#)Eligibility Information System denies a request for services.

Other notices must be requested by the caseworker. Each notice has a specific message about eligibility or work requirements. There is also a general correspondence notice available that is an entirely free-form document.
