108-6          ARIES CORRESPONDENCES

ARIESAlaska's Resource for Integrated Eligibility Services Correspondences are either system or manually generated, depending on the actions performed on the case such as when a caseworker approves, denies, or pends a case. ARIESAlaska's Resource for Integrated Eligibility Services allows the caseworker to generate certain correspondences manually and add notes when additional information is required. The caseworker can search a list of manually generated documents in ARIESAlaska's Resource for Integrated Eligibility Services by selecting "Correspondences" from the top level navigation bar then select "Generate Manual" from the second level navigation bar, and then click the Search button. The most current ARIESAlaska's Resource for Integrated Eligibility Services Correspondences are listed below.

Manual Generated Correspondence

- Care Management Program Coupon
- Case Summary
- Eligibility Renewal Form
- General Information
- Medical Insurance Manual Issuance
- Request For Child Support Information
- Request For Medical Insurance Information

System Generated Correspondence

- Application Registration Notice/Medical Assistance Application Received
- Request For Verification
- Eligibility Notice
- Renewal Packet Form
- Application For Other Benefits

108-6 A.      LEGAL CITATION IN GENERAL CORRESPONDENCE

Correspondence sent to clients must contain federal or state statutes and regulations specific to each program that supports the Division's action on cases. System generated correspondence automatically contain specific regulations or statues when an action is taken on the case. When sending generated manual correspondence in ARIESAlaska's Resource for Integrated Eligibility Services, the caseworker is required to enter specific state or federal statues and regulations in the "Free Form Text" section of the correspondence that supports the Division's action.

The following federal and state regulations and statues must be included in the General Correspondence specific to each program:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| ATAPAlaska Temporary Assistance Program | AS 47.27.010 and 7 AAC 45.210 |
| SBSenior Benefits | AS 47.45.302 and 7 AAC 47.547 |
| APAAdult Public Assistance | AS 47.25.430 and 7 AAC 40.090 |
| SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program | CFR 273 and 7 AAC 40.090 |
| MEMedicaid | 42 CFR 435.952 and 7 AAC 100.016 |
| GRAGeneral Relief Assistance | AS 47.25.120 and 7 AAC 47.140 |
| CAMAChronic and Acute Medical Assistance | AS 47.08.150 and 7 AAC 48.52 |
