Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #3

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: July 3, 2017

We are making several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field. These changes include further clarification for IHSIndian Health Service eligible individuals, children in residential treatment centers, pregnant women, MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income household composition, appropriate usage of BVSBureau of Vital Statistics, FPLFederal Poverty Limit percentages, countable and excluded income for MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income, and deductions.

Highlights of the further policy changes are described below.

If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[814](../../814_tpl_and_recovery/814_tpl_and_recovery.md) Third Party Liability (TPL), Resource, (TPR) and Recovery

- Added information regarding FMAPFederal Medical Assistance Percentage and the importance of using the correct Medicaid category and subtype, and indicating IHSIndian Health Service eligibility

[815-1](../../815_residents_of_institutions/815-1_children_living_in_residential_treatment_centers_over_30_days.md) Children in Residential Treatment Centers Over 30 Days

- Clarified when we can use non-MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Methodology for children in Residential Treatment Programs

[816-1 B.](../../816_magi_medicaid_categories/816-1_magi_category_eligibility_factors.md#816-1_B._PREGNANT_WOMEN) Pregnant Woman

- Clarified that if a woman is eligible for retroactive PWPregnant Woman Medicaid, she is eligible for PW Medicaid for all months of her pregnancy
- Clarified that verification is required when multiple births are expected

[817-1](../../817_magi_medicaid_household_composition/817-1_construct_a_magi_household_for_each_applicant.md) Construct a MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Household for Each Applicant

- Re-wrote second set of bullets under section C to match non-filer chart

[823 A.](../../823_verification_and_documentation/823_verification_and_documentation.md#823_a.___THE_FOLLOWING_FACTORS_OF_ELIGIBILITY_REQUIRE_PROOF:) The Following Factors of Eligibility Require Proof

- Added verification of pregnancy when multiple births are expected

[823-1 C.](../../823_verification_and_documentation/823-1_sources_of_electronic_verification.md#823-1_C._____SECONDARY_SOURCES_OF_VERIFICATION) Other Electronic Data Sources

- Added note explaining that the division is charged for each BVSBureau of Vital Statistics request so other verification sources should be used whenever possible

[Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum-1_magi_medicaid_income_standards.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Income Standards

- Labeled the FPLFederal Poverty Limit% for each MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid category

[Addendum 2](../../addenda/addendum-2_magi_medicaid_subtypes.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Subtypes

- Removed PD Medicaid subtype as this is no longer valid

[Addendum 3](../../addenda/addendum-3_countable_and_excluded_income_for_magi_medicaid.md) Countable and Excluded Income for MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

- Clarified that the self-employment fishing income exemption only applies to Metlakatla Indian Community tribal members, living in Metlakatla, and fishing within the reservation boundaries
- Added scholarships to awards and grants
- Clarified educational expenses versus living expenses
- Clarified GI Bill income is exempt

[Addendum 4](../../addenda/addendum-4_magi_medicaid_income_deductions.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Income Deductions

- Clarified how to budget annual deductions
- Clarified tuition based deductions
