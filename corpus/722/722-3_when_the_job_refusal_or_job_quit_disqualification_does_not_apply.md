722-3          WHEN THE JOB REFUSAL OR JOB QUIT DISQUALIFICATION DOES NOT APPLY

The disqualification for refusal of or separation from suitable employment does not apply:

- If the job refusal or job quit occurs 60 days prior to the date of application;
- To the termination of self-employment or a subsistence activity;
- If the individual is exempt from participating in work activities (see [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program [MS 730-2](../730/730-2_exemptions_from_work_activities.md));
- If the family rebuts the presumption that their need for assistance is due to a job refusal or job quit; or
- If the individual has good cause.
