700-2          PURPOSE OF THE MANUAL

The [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program policy manual provides the caseworker with federal and state policies used to determine [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program eligibility, and program procedures to implement state and federal laws. This manual will assist [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance staff, partner agencies, contractors, and grantees with guidance needed to determine eligibility, assess employability, and appropriately assign or approve work activities intended to quickly lead families toward self-sufficiency and promote independence from the welfare system.

Manual organization loosely reflects the [ATAP](#)Alaska Temporary Assistance Program service delivery model. It begins with the application process, moves through eligibility determination, work and self-sufficiency requirements, payments, and ends with transitional services. A topical index is provided.
