Modified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:  MC #2

MANUAL:  MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid program

DATE: April 3, 2017

We are making several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of policy questions or suggestions from the field. We have also updated the income standards for the MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid categories that have income standards based on the Federal Poverty Guidelines (FPG).

Highlights of the further policy changes are described below.

If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

806-3 A. Renewals Based on SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Eligibility

- Added FMFamily Medicaid section 5005-7 D Renewals Based on SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Eligibility as this policy still applies.

[808-4 D.](../../808_us_citizenship_and_eligible_alien_status/808-4_five_year_waiting_period.md#808-4_D.___ALIENS_EXEMPT_FROM_THE_FIVE_YEAR_WAITING_PERIOD) Aliens Exempt from the Five Year Waiting Period

- Clarified that if an individual entered the U.S.United States as a qualified alien, the individual is exempt from the 5 year waiting period, even if the alien status changes.  For example, if a refugee becomes a permanent resident, the individual does not have wait 5 years.

[813-D.](../../813_development_of_income/development_of_income.md#813_D._______EXEMPTIONS_FOR_APPLYING_FOR_UIB) Exemptions for Applying for UIBUnemployment Insurance Benefit

- Removed the minimum wage amount.  The minimum wage will now be changing yearly with the cost of living.  To make sure the most current amount is used, the link to the DOLDepartment of Labor and Workforce Development website will be used.

[815 D.](../../815_residents_of_institutions/815_residents_of_institutions.md#815_D._______PENAL_INSTITUTION) Penal Institution

- Added examples for clarification
- Clarified verbiage regarding ‘furlough’

[817-1](../../817_magi_medicaid_household_composition/817-1_construct_a_magi_household_for_each_applicant.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Household Composition

- Clarified definition of custody for exception 3 for tax filing households
- Updated the tax filing threshold year from 2015 to 2016.  The tax filing threshold standards do not change.

[818](../../818_magi_medicaid_income/818_magi_medicaid_income.md) Income for MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

- Updated the tax filing threshold year from 2015 to 2016.  The tax filing threshold standards do not change.

[819](../../819_types_of_income/819_types_of_income.md) Types of Income

- Clarified that this section includes types of income that may or may not be countable for MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

[819 C.](../../819_types_of_income/819_types_of_income.md#819_C._OTHER_INCOME) Rental Income

- Clarified how to count income from a leased fishing permit

[833 A.](../../833_hospital_presumptive_eligibility/833_hosptial_presumptive_eligibility_determination.md#833_a.__Eligibility) Eligibility

- Added an example of when an individual is eligible for another HPEHospital Presumptive Eligibility period to avoid confusion
- Corrected the timeline when an individual can have a second HPEHospital Presumptive Eligibility approval

[Addendum 1](../../addenda/addendum-1_magi_medicaid_income_standards.md) MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid Income Standards

- Updated Federal Poverty Limit (FPLFederal Poverty Limit) income limits
- Corrected the date the categories of Parent Caretaker Relatives and Under 21 categories are updated to April 1 to correspond with the Cost of Living Allowance (COLACost of Living Allowance) income limit changes on January 1

[Addendum 3](../../addenda/addendum-3_countable_and_excluded_income_for_magi_medicaid.md) Countable and Excluded Income for MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid

- Added subsidized guardianship payments to exempt income
- Fixed the manual section reference for self-employment income budgeting
- Added Special Needs Trust as these trusts can be used to qualify for income eligibility for MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid
- Added clarification regarding self-employment fishing for tribal members from and living in Metlakatla
