608-1          FAIR
HEARINGS

Households may request a fair hearing on any action
taken by the agency that affects the household's participation in SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program.

If the household
requests a fair hearing before the effective date of the action (the date
the benefits are to be reduced or stopped) and its certification period
has not expired, we would continue their SNAP benefits at the previously
authorized amount pending a fair hearing decision, unless otherwise declined
by the household.

Hearings procedures are detailed in the [Administrative
Procedures Manual at section 117-2](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/admin/117/117-2.htm).

608-1 A.
    TIME FRAMES

Hearings must be requested within 90 days of the
date of action, either verbally  or in writing to any employee of
the Division.

A hearing requested to dispute a denial for restored
benefits (lost more than 90 days but less than one year from the date
of the request) is granted provided the hearing is requested within 90
days of the date the agency denied the request for the restored benefits.

Households may request a hearing at any time within
a certification period to dispute its current level of benefits.

Hearings must be completed and the household informed
of the decision within 60 days of the date the hearing request was received
in the DPADivision of Public Assistance office.
