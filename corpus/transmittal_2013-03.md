Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2013-03

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: November 15, 2013

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised. Highlights of the revisions are described below.

If you have any questions, please contact Field Services at [dpafield@alaska.gov](mailto:dpafield@alaska.gov) or Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[100-8](../100/100-8_health_insurance_portability_and_accountability_act_(hipaa).md)      Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act

- 100-8 Provides new information about HIPAA and the Privacy Rule.
- 100-8A Provides information about Protected Health Information and identifiers, and possible sources of PHI.
- 100-8G Clarifies the instruction for DPA staff in handling HIPAA complaints.
- 100-8H Clarifies the instruction for staff in handling breach or suspected breach of confidential information.
- 100-8I New section that provides instruction for sending electronic information to ensure that PHI is transferred securely.
- 100-8J Renumbered – HIPAA Privacy Officers

[101](../101/101_the_application_process.md)      The Application Process

- 101-2C Updates the form number from GEN 50B to GEN 50C and includes the programs for which the form can be used as an application
- 101-5B Adds reference to MS 120 for additional information about fee agents in DPA.
- 101-5B Includes exceptions and examples for handling applications received in Central Region and Specialized offices.

[103-1](../103/103-1_electronic_document_management.md)      Case File Folder

- 103-1A Includes instruction that DKC cases may be filed in a six part folder if the household has other open programs, and the PI for each program and case numbers are the same.
- 103-1B Clarifies that for part V and VI of the six-part folder, information about major and non-major programs may be filed in either section.

[103-2](../103/103-2_identifiable_applications.md)      Request for Service

- 103-2 Clarifies that a GEN 72 received after the month following the end of a certification period is considered a request for service, and a GEN 72 received in the month following case closure for any reason may be used as an initial application.
- 103-2A Updates the form number from GEN 50B to GEN 50C; updates the page number in the application which may be used as a request for service form.

[103-6](../103/103-6_retention_of_case_records.md)      Record Storage Procedures

- 103-6C Updates the procedure for preparing cases for archiving.
- 103-6D Updates the procedure for completing the Records Transfer List.
- 103-6E Updates the procedure for requesting stored records in Archives.

[103-7](../103/103-7_case_file_transfer.md)      Transferring an Active Case File

- 103-7D Adds the caseload number 9-99 to clarify that cases are assigned to this number when in transit.

[105-14](../105/105-14_systematic_alien_verification_for_entitlements_-_electronic_verification_process.md)      SAVE - Submitting an Initial Verification

- 105-14C Includes additional tips for entering information in SAVE.
- 105-14E Adds the number for technical support.
- 105-14F Adds additional guidelines for submitting the G-845 for USCIS.

[111](../111/111_program_losses_and_claims_collection.md)      Program Losses and Claims Collections

- 111 Adds instructions that a supervisor or lead must review and sign the GEN 95 prior to submission to the Claims Unit.
- 111-3 Clarifies that a supervisor or lead worker signature is required on the G-845.

[116-6](../116/116-6_change_reporting.md)      Change Reporting

- 116-6B Changes the procedure in field offices for handling reports of change for NFAP agencies. This aligns with the current procedure in the Statewide Process Management Guide.

[117](../117/117_right_to_a_fair_hearing.md)      Fair Hearings

- 117-2G Clarifies that one of the reasons for ineligibility for continued benefits is if the sole issue is due to a mass change.

[122](../122/122_electronic_benefit_transfer_(ebt).md)      Electronic Benefit Transfer

- 122-15 Adds information about restricting the use of ATM benefits in bars, liquors, gambling, or adult entertainment establishments.

[127](../127/127_reserved.md)      Medicaid Case Processing

- 127-1 and 2 Clarifies that if a DKC household has other programs, the case will remain carc'd with the open programs. If it is a stand-alone, it is assigned to DKC.
- 127-3 Clarifies that only stand-alone DKC cases will be transferred to the DKC office.

[128-4](../128/128-4_refugee_assistance.md)      Refugee Assistance

- 128-4A Updates the amount of one-time refugee settlement payment.

[Addendum 109-F](../addenda/addendum_109_f_-_dpa_acronyms.md)

- Adds acronyms related to MAGI Medicaid and ARIES
