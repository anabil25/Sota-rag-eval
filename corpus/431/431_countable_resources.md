431           COUNTABLE RESOURCES

This section describes assets that are countable resources in determining eligibility for the APAAdult Public Assistance program.

Unless specified otherwise, the value of a resource is its equity value. Equity value is the fair market value of the resource less any amounts owed or other legal encumbrances.
