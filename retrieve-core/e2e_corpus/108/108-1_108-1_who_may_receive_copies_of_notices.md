108-1          WHO MAY RECEIVE COPIES OF NOTICES

Copies of notices may be sent to:

- Applicants or recipients,
- Authorized representatives,
- Legal guardians,
- Anyone to whom the client has, in writing, authorized a release of information.

Copies of notices may be sent to agencies or individuals directly involved in the administration of a public assistance program. For example, cost-of-care notices may be sent to care providers, and copies of PASS II Child Care notices may be sent to Day Care Local Administrators. Refer to manual [section 100-3](../100/100-3_confidentiality.md) for information on confidentiality requirements.
