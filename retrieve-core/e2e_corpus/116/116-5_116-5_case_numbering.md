116-5          CASE
NUMBERING

EISEligibility Information System-user
NFAPNative Family Assistance Programs are assigned their
own FSOField Service Office or full service
office number. DPADivision of Public Assistance staff
must not register a program to a case number assigned to this FSOField Service Office.

Note:

Depending on the agreement with DPADivision of Public Assistance,
EISEligibility Information System-user NFAPNative Family Assistance Program employees may register the application
for DPADivision of Public Assistance when a client
applies for NFAPNative Family Assistance Program benefits
and other DPADivision of Public Assistance programs.
