Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2016-03

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: September 1, 2016

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised. Highlights of the revisions are described below.

If you have any questions, please contact Field Services at [dpafield@alaska.gov](mailto:dpafield@alaska.gov) or the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

100      General Information

[100-3H](../100/100-3_confidentiality.md#100-3_H._REQUESTS_FOR_INFORMATION_FROM_OTHER_AGENCIES)      Request for Information from Other Agencies

- Adds Department of Corrections (DOC) to list of partner agencies that DPA can release and receive requested information without written release of information from clients.

104      Statewide Process Management

[104-4B](../104/104-4_adverse_action_calendar.md#104-4_B._REQUEST_FOR_INFORMATION_DEADLINE_DATES)      Requests for Information Deadline Dates

- Updates section due to statewide workload that all offices can allow 15 days for households residing in rural areas to accommodate delays in mail delivery.

105      Verification Procedures

[105-14G](../105/105-14_systematic_alien_verification_for_entitlements_-_electronic_verification_process.md#105-14_G._THIRD_STEP_VERIFICATION)      Third Step Verification - Using G-845 Form

- Updates mailing address when sending Form G-845 and G-845s (Supplement), including for Cuban-Haitian Entrant Verification.

113      Quality Assessment (QA)

[113-7](../113/113-7_quality_assessment_reviewer_contact_with_the_caseworker.md)      QA Reviewer Contact with the Caseworker

- Removes the Food Stamp Program.

124      Title IV-E / DPA Transfer Protocols

[124-5](../124/124-5_child_returns_to_home.md)      Child Returns Home

- Updates Family Medicaid to MAGI Medicaid.
