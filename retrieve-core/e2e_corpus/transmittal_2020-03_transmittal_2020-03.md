Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2020-03

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: December 1, 2020

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have any questions, please contact Division Operations Support Team at [dpadost@alaska.gov](mailto:dpadost@alaska.gov) or the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

ENTIRE MANUAL

- Revised terminology from "field staff" to "caseworkers" for consistency
- Changed Qualis Health to Comagine Health due to the company's name change

[MS 100-8 J](../100/100-8_health_insurance_portability_and_accountability_act_(hipaa).md#100-8_J._HIPAA_PRIVACY_OFFICERS)      HIPAA PRIVACY OFFICERS

- Updated Privacy Officers contact information

[MS 101-4](../101/101-4_receipt_of_an_identifiable_application.md)       RECEIPT OF AN IDENTIFIABLE APPLICATION

- Clarified that the Acknowledgement of Understanding is considered part of the Statement of Truth

[MS 101-5](../101/101-5_where_to_submit_an_application.md)      WHERE TO SUBMIT AN APPLICATION

- Revised subsection D as Heating Assistance Program applications are processed statewide

[MS 102-2](../102/102-2_screening.md)      SCREENING

- Replaced outdated GEN 50B application references with GEN 50C
- Updated the page number on the GEN 50C used for screening expedited SNAP benefits

[MS 104-1](../104/104-1_first_contact_resolution.md)      FIRST CONTACT RESOLUTION

- Revised subsection C as Heating Assistance Program applications are processed statewide
- Revised section for offices handling clients in person, phone calls, mail and drop off documents
- Removed Coastal email since this is no longer valid
- Revised section on processing applications, reviews, reports of change, and pended cases
- Referred staff to HowWe Guide for handling and processing cases
- Changed PathOS to current™ as it was upgraded on October 6, 2020

[MS 104-3](../104/104-3_tools_for_effective_workload_management.md)      TOOLS FOR EFFECTIVE CASELOAD MANAGEMENT

- Removed note under #6 as ABAWD requirements are explained in the SNAP manual

[MS 105-13](../105/105-13_partial_list_of_verification_sources.md)      PARTIAL LIST OF VERIFICATION SOURCE

- Changed name of the child support system from NSTAR to WINSTAR in subsection B and D

[MS 106-5](../106/106-5_instant_eligibility_verification_system_(ievs).md)      INSTANT ELIGIBILITY VERIFICATION SYSTEM (IEVS)

- Added section to provide information on the Instant Eligibility Verification System (IEVS)

[MS 111-2](../111/111-2_underpayments_discovered_during_an_overpayment_determination.md)      UNDERPAYMENTS DISCOVERED DURING AN OVERPAYMENT DETERMINATION

- Corrected terminology from underissuance to underpayment for consistency

[MS 111-3](../111/111-3_completing_the_report_of_claim_determination_form_(gen_95).md)      COMPLETING THE REPORT OF CLAIM DETERMINATION FORM (GEN 95)

- Corrected terminology from underissuance to underpayment for consistency

[MS 112-4 A](../112/112-4_referral_of_suspected_fraud_cases.md#112-4_A._EARLY_FRAUD__EFD__AND_POST_CERTIFICATION_INVESTIGATION__PCI__REFERRALS)      EARLY FRAUD (EFD) AND POST CERTIFICATION INVESTIGATION (PCI) REFERRALS

- Retitled subsection A to include both early and post certification referrals
- Deleted information from subsection B
- Removed reference to GEN 97b for submitting a fraud referral as this is no longer the process
- Referred staff to Quicklinks (via I-Sight) section of DPAweb titled "Fraud Allegation Report Form" for fraud referral submission
- Renumbered section

[MS 117-1 B](../117/117-1_fair_hearing_requst.md#117-1_B._HOW_TO_REQUEST_A_FAIR_HEARING)      HOW TO REQUEST A FAIR HEARING

- Revised section to match federal regulations that allow a verbal fair hearing request for Medicaid

[MS 118](../118/118_voter_registration.md)      VOTER REGISTRATION

- Revised section for clarity

[MS 123-1 B](../123/123-1_dol_data_inquiry_screens.md#123-1_B._SECURITY_ACCESS_TO_DOL_DATA_SYSTEM)      SECURITY ACCESS TO DOL DATA SYSTEM

- Updated process for requesting DOL access since this is no longer handled through SYSOPS
- Updated SAM User Guide hyperlink to the SharePoint site

[MS 123-2 B](../123/123-2_potential_unemployment_insurance_benefit_eligibility.md#123-2_B._REFERRAL_TO_UIB)      REFERRAL TO UIB

- Updated where individuals can apply for unemployment benefits to myalaska.gov

[MS 123-3 A](../123/123-3_how_to_anticipate_monthly_uib_income.md#123-3_A._TALK_TO_THE_CLIENT)       TALK TO THE CLIENT

- Updated Unemployment Claimant handbook link throughout the section

[MS 123-3 B](../123/123-3_how_to_anticipate_monthly_uib_income.md#123-3_B._CHECK_DOL_SAM_SCREENS)      CHECK DOL SAM SCREEN

- Updated SAM User Guide hyperlink to the SharePoint site

[MS 126-2](../126/126-2_senior_benefits_office.md)      SENIOR BENEFITS

- Revised chapter to match current field processes
- Renamed the section from Senior Benefits Office to Senior Benefits
