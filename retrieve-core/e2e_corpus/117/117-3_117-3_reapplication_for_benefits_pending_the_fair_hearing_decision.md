117-3          REAPPLICATION
FOR BENEFITS PENDING THE FAIR HEARING DECISION

A client requesting a fair hearing may reapply at
any time and have their eligibility and benefit amount determined based
on the information in the application. This may result in reinstatement
of benefits if benefits were note continued, or this may establish a period
of ongoing eligibility and possible a new benefit level even though benefits
had been continued at a prior level.

If a client who is receiving continued benefits
chooses to reapply and the reapplication establishes ineligibility or
a lower benefit level, benefits must continue to be issued at the previous
level until a hearing decision is made. A new application will also assist
the client in establishing facts and ongoing information necessary for
calculating the correct amount of benefits after the hearing decision
is rendered.
