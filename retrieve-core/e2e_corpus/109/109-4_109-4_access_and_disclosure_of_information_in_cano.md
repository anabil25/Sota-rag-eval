109-4          ACCESS
AND DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION IN CANO

Information contained in CANOCase Notes
is considered confidential client information. To disclose any information
contained in CANOCase Notes including
clients requesting access to their case or case file, refer to Administrative
Procedures Manual [section 100-3](../100/100-3_confidentiality.md)
for further guidance on confidentiality and disclosure of information.
