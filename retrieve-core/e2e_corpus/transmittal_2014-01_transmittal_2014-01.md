Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2014-01

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: April 21, 2014

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised. Highlights of the revisions are described below.

If you have any questions, please contact Field Services at [dpafield@alaska.gov](mailto:dpafield@alaska.gov) or Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[100-6](../100/100-6_interpreter_services.md)      Interpreter Services

- 100-6B Adds information about utilizing ASL vendors available in the community, if necessary, and to contact Field Services for information on paying for the service.

[100-8](../100/100-8_health_insurance_portability_and_accountability_act_(hipaa).md)      Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act

- 100-8B Updates the link to the Notice of Privacy Practices online.
- 100-8I Adds Clarification that client information may be exchanged via email between DPA and non-DPA Work Services contractors using only certain and specific identifiers.

[100-10](../100/100-10_ethical_conduct.md)      Ethical Conduct

- 100-10C Adds guidelines for handling and assigning cases which involve current or former DPA employees.

[103-3](../103/103-3_case_numbering_on_eis_and_aries.md)      Case Numbering on EIS

- 103-3C(10) Adds GA (burial) in the list of programs to be registered under unique case numbers.
- 103-3D Adds information about registering GA (burial) as a stand-alone case; MAGI Medicaid as a stand-alone case.

[104](../104/104_statewide_process_management.md)      Statewide Process Management

- 104 Renames the section from Caseload Management to Statewide Process Management; this section will eventually house the current Statewide Process Management Guide (SPMG) and the guide on DPAweb.

[104-1](../104/104-1_first_contact_resolution.md)      First Contact Resolution

- 104-1 Replaces the case maintenance information with first contact resolution guidelines; some of the case maintenance information was incorporated in MS 107 (Changes).

[104-2](../104/104-2_recertification_and_review.md)      Recertification and Review

- 104-2(5) Aligns the procedure with MS 103-2A; recertification/reviews received after there is a one month break in service is registered as a request for service.

[104-3](../104/104-3_tools_for_effective_workload_management.md)      Tools for Effective Workload Management

- 104-3 Updates the section title from "caseload management" to "workload management"; adds ET Resources on DPAweb to the list of workload management tools.

[104-5](../104/104-5_alerts.md)      Alerts

- 104-5 Adds additional instruction and guidance for utilizing system and manually generated alerts in EIS.

[105-1](../105/105-1_verification_of_information.md)      Verification of Information

- 105-1(B) Updates the verification requirements for Medicaid under MAGI rules and clarifies requirements for other programs.

[105-14](../105/105-14_systematic_alien_verification_for_entitlements_-_electronic_verification_process.md)      SAVE - Submitting an Initial Verification

- 105-14B Reflects changes to the SAVE system's new design which became effective on 2/23/14.

[107](../107/107_changes.md)      Changes

- 107-1 Incorporates information from previous MS 104-1 (Case Maintenance) about reports of change.
- 107-2B Updates and clarifies the responsibility of Work Services case managers (DPA and non-DPA) for reporting changes to eligibility staff.
- 107-2D Clarifies the assignment of due dates for setting alerts.

[113](../113/113_quality_assessment.md)      Quality Assessment

- 113 Deletes case manager references throughout chapter 113; updates to Food Stamp review processing, updates to providing QA feedback and review findings; changes in notification of errors and storing client confidential documents by using SharePoint; updates to error feedback and how errors are challenged; updates to QARC purpose and responsibilities; changes to refusal and failure to cooperate processing that involve special reviews; updates to Fraud process; and updates to Q-Form process.

[115-9](../115/115-9_disability_determination_service.md)      DDS Referral Process

- 115-9 Clarifies and updates the process for referring cases to DDS and sending files and/or documentation.

[125](../125/125_interim_assistance_medical_screening_process.md)      Interim Assistance Medical Screening Process

- 125 This is a new section; explains the process for field offices in sending medical records to the IA Medical Reviewer and obtaining decisions.

[126-2](../126/126-2_senior_benefits_office.md)      Senior Benefits Office

- 126-2A Clarifies that SB applications are registered as full application rather than request for service.

[Addendum 107-A](../addenda/addendum_107_a_-_reserved.md)      Change Flowchart

- Updates the flowchart for processing changes in field offices.
