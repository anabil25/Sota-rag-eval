Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2019-03

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: December 2, 2019

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have any questions, please contact Field Services at [dpafield@alaska.gov](mailto:dpafield@alaska.gov) or the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 109-10](../109/109-10_case_managers_use_of_cano.md)      CASE MANAGER USE OF CANO

- Added "Job quit disqualification determination" as an allowable case note entry for case managers due to a change in policy

[MS 111-1](../111/111-1_determining_the_amount_of_a_program_loss.md)      DETERMINING THE AMOUNT OF A PROGRAM LOSS

- Clarified the process for submitting a SNAP GEN 95 claim, ensuring staff print all FSAD screens before and after corrections are made

[MS 117-1 (B)](../117/117-1_fair_hearing_requst.md#117-1_B._HOW_TO_REQUEST_A_FAIR_HEARING)      HOW TO REQUEST A FAIR HEARING

- Revised section to replace emailing the Contracted Service Quality Assurance Unit (CSQA) with submitting the fair hearing via Fair Hearing Referral I-Sight due to a change in process
- Added link and instructions to the "Fair Hearing Referral" on the DPAweb

[MS 117-2](../117/117-2_continuted_benefits_pending_a_fair_hearing_decision.md)      CONTINUED BENEFITS PENDING A FAIR HEARING DECISION

- Revised section to match the continued benefits policy
- Removed sub-sections A and B and renumbered sub-sections throughout

[MS 117-16](../117/117-16_fair_hearing_process_flowchart.md)      FAIR HEARING PROCESS FLOWCHART

- Added I-Sight (fair hearing referral) entry and submittal to the flow chart based on the updated fair hearing referral process

[ADDENDUM 118-B](../addenda/addendum_118_b_-_returned_mail_guide.md)      RETURNED MAIL GUIDE

- Revised with the April 2019 returned mail guide
