125-3          WHERE TO DIRECT [IA](#)Interim Assistance QUESTIONS

In situations where there are [IA](#)Interim Assistance-related questions on a case, staff will direct them to the Statewide Needs Assistance Group ([SNAG](#)Statewide Needs Assistance Group), who will provide support to keep the case moving and assist in processing the [IA](#)Interim Assistance-related case.
