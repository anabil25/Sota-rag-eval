116-8          CASE
FILES

Both DPADivision of Public Assistance
and an NFAPNative Family Assistance Program maintain
their own case files regardless of the eligibility system used in administering
public assistance programs. Case files will not be transferred between
DPADivision of Public Assistance and an NFAPNative Family Assistance Program when households move from a DPADivision of Public Assistance area to an NFAPNative Family Assistance Program
area, or vice versa.
