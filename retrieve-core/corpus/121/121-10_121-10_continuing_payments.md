121-10        CONTINUING
PAYMENTS

Once eligibility has been established and benefits
authorized, the recipient is entitled to receive a payment on a regular
and continuing basis as long as eligibility exists. For SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program and General Relief, eligibility
is authorized only during a specific period of time. (See the program
policy manuals for more information).
