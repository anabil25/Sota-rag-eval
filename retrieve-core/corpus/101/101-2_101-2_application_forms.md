101-2          FORMS

101-2 A.      GEN 50C FORM

The Application for Services (GEN 50CApplication for Services) is an initial application for Alaska Temporary Assistance, Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program, Adult Public Assistance, Medicaid (including Long Term Care), and General Relief Assistance.

101-2 B.      GEN 72 FORM

The Eligibility Review Form (GEN 72Eligibility Review Form) is a review application for the Alaska Temporary Assistance Program, Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program, Adult Public Assistance, Medicaid, and Senior Benefits programs. This form can be used as an application in certain circumstances. Refer to the appropriate policy manuals for specific program information.

101-2 C.      MED 4 FORM

The MED 4Application for Adults and Children with Long Term Care Needs application form is a Medicaid application for adults and children with long-term care needs who want to apply for nursing home, home and community-based services, disabled child living in at home (TEFRATax Equity and Fiscal Responsibility Act) coverage, and for children applying for or receiving SSISupplemental Security Income.

101-2 D.      FFM (FEDERALLY FACILITATED MARKETPLACE) FORM

FFMFederally Facilitated Marketplace is an electronic MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income (Modified Adjusted Gross Income) based Medicaid application for adults and children submitted to the Division through Federally Facilitated Marketplace for any Medicaid category (including Long Term Care and Adult Public Assistance related Medicaid).

101-2 E.      ARIES SSP (SELF SERVICE PORTAL) FORM

SSPSelf-Service Portal is an electronic application submitted through [my.alaska.gov](https://my.alaska.gov/) portal for any Medicaid category (including Long Term Care and Adult Public Assistance related Medicaid).

101-2 F.      FSP 95 FORM

[SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Interim Report Form ([FSP 95](#)SNAP Interim Report Form) is a periodic report, completed at the midpoint of the [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program certification period, for households approved for 12 or 24 month [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program certification periods.
