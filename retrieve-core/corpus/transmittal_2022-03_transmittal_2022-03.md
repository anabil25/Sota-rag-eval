Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2022-03

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: December 1, 2022

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have questions, please contact Division Operations Support Team at [hss.dpadost@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpadost@alaska.gov) or the Policy and Program Development Team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

ENTIRE MANUAL

- Change all references from [DHSS](#)Department of Health and Social Services to [DOH](#)Department of Health, including any email addresses and websites

[MS 100-3 I](../100/100-3_confidentiality.md#100-3_I._GUARDIANSHIP_AND_CONSERVATORSHIP)      GUARDIANSHIP AND CONSERVATORSHIP

- Added new subsection regarding communication with an individual who has a full legal guardianship or conservatorship

[MS 100-4 I](../100/100-4_civil_rights.md#100-4_I._CIVIL_RIGHTS_ASSURANCE)      CIVIL RIGHTS ASSURANCE

- Added new Civil Rights Assurance subsection to ensure that DOH and its agencies providing assistance to families do not discriminate against race, color, national origin, disability, age, sex (including pregnancy, sexual orientation, and gender identity), or religion

[MS 100-6 A](../100/100-6_interpreter_services.md#100-6_A._LIMITED_ENGLISH_PROFICIENCY_(LEP))      LIMITED ENGLISH PROFICIENCY (LEP)

- Clarified that use of minor, friend, or family member as interpreter are only used on emergency basis

[MS 101-1](../101/101-1_definitions.md)      DEFINITIONS

- Removed reference to request for service since no longer used

[MS 101-4](../101/101-4_receipt_of_an_identifiable_application.md)      RECEIPT OF AN IDENTIFIABLE APPLICATION

- Updated to include that Acknowledgment of Understanding is also part of the GEN 72 and it must be initialed to be considered identifiable

[MS 103-2](../103/103-2_identifiable_applications.md)      IDENTIFIABLE APPLICATIONS

- Removed reference to request for service since no longer used

[MS 103-3 C](../103/103-3_case_numbering_on_eis_and_aries.md#103-3_C._CASE_NUMBERING_RULES_FOR_DPA_PROGRAMS)      CASE NUMBERING RULES FOR DPA PROGRAMS

- Updated case numbering for Waiver Medicaid with other programs

[MS 103-3 D](../103/103-3_case_numbering_on_eis_and_aries.md#103-3_D._CASE_NUMBERING_AT_A_GLANCE)      CASE NUMBERING AT A GLANCE

- Updated case numbering for Waiver Medicaid with other programs

[MS 104-2 (5)](../104/104-2_recertification_and_review.md)     RECERTIFICATION AND REVIEW

- Removed reference to request for service since no longer used

[MS 104-5 C](../104/104-5_alerts.md#104-5_C._ALERT_MANAGEMENT)      ALERT MANAGEMENT

- Updated the required action on case table instructions for two different EIS generated alerts

[MS 106-5](../106/106-5_instant_eligibility_verification_system_(ievs).md)      INSTANT ELIGIBILITY VERIFICATION (IEVS)

- Clarified that IEVS is used for minor children to obtain SAVE verification for alien status and PFD verification

[MS 108-4](../108/108-4_how_eis_notices_work.md)      HOW EIS NOTICES WORK

- Removed reference to request for service since no longer used

[MS 109-3](../109/109-3_documenting_sensitive_information.md)      DOCUMENTING SENSITIVE INFORMATION

- Removed reference to 6-part folder for confidential information since no longer used
- Revised section to clarify that the confidential indicator in ILINX is used when documenting sensitive information

[MS 109-10](../109/109-10_case_managers_use_of_cano.md)      CASE MANAGERS USE OF CANO

- Clarified document verification from a case manager

[MS 111-5 E](../111/111-5_recoupments_repayments_and_allotment_reductions.md#111-5_E._EXPUNGEMENT_OF_BENEFITS)      RECOUPMENTS, REPAYMENT, AND ALLOTMENT REDUCTIONS

- Reduced the timeline for benefit expungement from 12 months to 9 months for clients who have not used their SNAP benefits

[MS 112-4 B](../112/112-4_referral_of_suspected_fraud_cases.md#112-4_B._CATEGORICALLY_INELIGIBLE_HOUSEHOLDS)      CATEGORICALLY INELIGIBLE HOUSEHOLDS

- Updated actions taken by Fraud Unit on Out of State EBT Transactions

[MS 115-9](../115/115-9_disability_determination_service.md)      DISABILITY DETERMINATION SERVICE

- Revised section to remove reference to requiring blank MED 2 forms as this violates HIPAA. We should be asking for completed MED 2 forms for each provider, school, and school district

[MS 117-4 A](../117/117-4_pre_hearing_conference.md#117-4_A._SCHEDULING_THE_PRE-HEARING_CONFERENCE)      SCHEDULING THE PRE-HEARING CONFERENCE

- Clarified that pre-hearing notice must be sent to the client if they cannot be contacted for a pre-hearing conference

[MS 121-14](../121/121-14_stop_payment_and_forgery_procedures.md)      STOP PAYMENT AND FORGERY PROCEDURES

- Revised payment processing procedure to include BIRU unit

[MS 121-14 A](../121/121-14_stop_payment_and_forgery_procedures.md#121-14_A._STOP_PAY_PROCEDURES)      STOP PAY PROCEDURES

- Updated information on the Stop Payment Request form

[MS 121-14 B](../121/121-14_stop_payment_and_forgery_procedures.md#121-14_B._FORGERY_PROCEDURES)      FORGERY PROCEDURES

- Revised payment processing procedure to include BIRU unit

[MS 121-15](../121/121-15_returned_warrants___checks.md)      RETURNED WARRANTS / CHECKS

- Updated return warrants/checks procedure

[MS 121-16](../121/121-16_canceling_outstanding_payments.md)      CANCELING OUTSTANDING PAYMENTS

- Updated procedure for canceling outstanding payments

[MS 122-15](../122/122-15_quest_cardholder_support.md)      QUEST CARDHOLDER SUPPORT

- Updated name of Customer Service Support to FIS
- Updated new link where AK Quest cardholders can obtain information about their EBT account(s) and set up information

[MS 122-15 A](../122/122-15_quest_cardholder_support.md#122-15_A._ACCESSING_ACCOUNT_INFORMATION_WITH_EBT_ACCOUNT)      ACCESSING ACCOUNT INFORMATION WITH EBT ACCOUNT

- Updated EBT account access information to include that cardholders can locate "All Point" Surcharge-fee ATMs on the DPA Quest card page
- Removed information on bilingual service since not available through FIS

MS 122-15 B      SUBSCRIBING TO DEPOSIT NOTIFICATIONS

- Removed section about cardholders subscribing to deposit notification since this option is no longer available

MS 122-15 C      PAYING BILLS ONLINE

- Removed section about cardholders viewing online history payment since not available through FIS

[MS 122-15 D](../122/122-15_quest_cardholder_support.md#122-15_B._GETTING_CARDHOLDER_SUPPORT)      GETTING CARDHOLDER SUPPORT

- Updated Customer Service support center for cardholders
- Renumbered the section from subsection D to subsection B

[MS 126-2 A](../126/126-2_senior_benefits_office.md#126-2_A._APPLICATION_PROCESS)      APPLICATION PROCESS

- Removed reference to request for service since no longer used
- Removed reference to the HowWe Guide as this resource is no longer available
