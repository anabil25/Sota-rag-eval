109-10         CASE MANAGERS USE OF CANOCase Notes

CANOCase Notes is not intended to replace other tools to ensure clear communication between Work Services case managers and DPADivision of Public Assistance caseworkers. Work Services case managers have read-access and write-access to [CANO](#)Case Notes, but the use of [CANO](#)Case Notes is limited to the entry of case specific information needed by the caseworker to take action on the case.

See [WSPM MS 1007](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/workservices/WSP Manual/1007/penalties_and_disqualifications.htm#1007________PENALTIES_AND_DISQUALIFICATIONS) for additional information.

CANOCase Notes entries by case managers are limited to:

- Notifications of Job Start and OJTOn the Job Training placements
- Requests to lift or impose penalties
- Requests to start or end exemptions from work activities
- Notifications of extension decisions
- Notification of home visits conducted and decision to increase an ATAPAlaska Temporary Assistance Program penalty amount to 75% or full family sanction
- Job quit disqualification determination

Note:

Client specific entries made in CANOCase Notes before the implementation of Client Notes will remain in CANOCase Notes and will not be deleted.

REPORTING CHANGES

Case managers who receive information that potentially affects a client's [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance program eligibility must forward this information to [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance as it is considered a Report of Change. Paystubs, completed work statements, and all other verification of changes must be sent to [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance via the [Secure Document Portal](https://dpaforms.ilinx.com/ILINXFlex/public-eform?param=WEUwT0U5d0g5QTV2aFZiZlI2Z0VEdyUzRCUzRCAg).

However, if a Work Services case manager obtains income verification from a collateral contact from an employer that contains enough information to take action on the case, the [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance caseworker is required to use this verification. If not, the [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance caseworker can contact the employer again for additional information or pend the case for the client to provide the necessary verification.

Note:

Case managers will upload reports of change ([GEN 55](#)Report of Change) and income verification ([GEN 155](#)Employment Statement form and [WS 155](#)Work Services Employer Collateral Contact Form) through the [Secured Document Portal](https://dpaforms.ilinx.com/ILINXFlex/public-eform?param=WEUwT0U5d0g5QTV2aFZiZlI2Z0VEdyUzRCUzRCAg).
