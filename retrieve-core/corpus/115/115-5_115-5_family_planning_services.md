115-5          FAMILY
PLANNING SERVICES

Family planning services are made available to members
of MAGIModified Adjusted Gross Income Medicaid households
and to new mothers who were on the Pregnant Woman program without regard
to age or marital status.

Family planning services are paid for out of Medicaid
funds. The Medicaid recipient has I.D. cards or coupons to present to
the family planning health care provider. For more information, see the
Medicaid manual.

If a Medicaid recipient inquires about family planning
services, provide him or her with a copy of Alaska
Medicaid Recipient Services booklet. This booklet provides information
about the type of supplies Medicaid will cover.
