104-6          [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program INTERIM REPORT

The [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Interim Report forms are date stamped and registered the same day they are received in the [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance office. All [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Interim Report ([IR](#)Interim Report) forms ([FSP 95](#)SNAP Interim Report Form) will be acted on promptly in accordance with the timeliness standards described in the [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program manual.

The following procedures are for the correct processing of the [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Interim Report:

Note:

See [SNAP MS 604-1 (A)](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/604/604-1.htm#604_1_a_length_of_certification_period) for [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program certification period length policy.

See [SNAP MS 604-2](http://dpaweb.hss.state.ak.us/manuals/fs/604/604-2.htm) for [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Interim Report policy.

- The Interim Report Form ([FSP 95](#)SNAP Interim Report Form) and the [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Interim Report notice (X054) will be sent to the [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program household 45 days before the middle of the [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program certification period and will include a postage paid return envelope. This will be sent in the 5th month for a case certified for 12 months and in the 11th month for a case certified for 24 months. If the [FSP 95](#)SNAP Interim Report Form is not returned 20 days after it is sent, the household will be sent the automated Non-Receipt of [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Interim Report notice (X052).

- If [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance receives the [FSP 95](#)SNAP Interim Report Form timely, it will be registered on the RERE screen under the REC INTERIM ([FSP 95](#)SNAP Interim Report Form) field.

- If there is information on the [FSP 95](#)SNAP Interim Report Form that requires further clarification, a cold call is attempted. An interview must not be scheduled or conducted at the time of processing the Interim Report. If verification is needed, [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program benefits continue through the established certification period, and a soft pend notice (N020) is sent to the household.

- If [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance receives the [FSP 95](#)SNAP Interim Report Form timely, requested mandatory verification, and the household provided the information more than 30 days after the case closed, the household will be required to file a new application for [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program using the Application for Services ([GEN 50C](#)Application for Services).

- If the [FSP 95](#)SNAP Interim Report Form is not returned to [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance 30 days after it was sent, the [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program case will close with adequate notice. The system generated notice [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Closed ‒ Interim Report (X053) will be sent to the household.

- When the [FSP 95](#)SNAP Interim Report Form is received outside of the required midpoint of the [SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program certification period, it must be treated as a report of change. Caseworkers may not input the [FSP 95](#)SNAP Interim Report Form on the RERE screen if the [FSP 95](#)SNAP Interim Report Form is received outside of the 5th/6th month of a 12-month certification period or the 11th/12th month for a 24-month certification period.

Note:

[SNAP](#)Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program recipients are required to complete the Eligibility Review Form ([GEN 72](#)Eligibility Review Form) at the 12 or 24-month recertificaton period.
