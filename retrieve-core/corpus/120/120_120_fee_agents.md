120            FEE
AGENTS

The Division of Public Assistance (DPADivision of Public Assistance) has volunteers called Fee Agents
to help low-income individuals and families apply for financial and medical
assistance in rural communities and villages without DPADivision of Public Assistance
offices. Fee agents provide a valuable service to members of their community
and to DPADivision of Public Assistance. The availability
of fee agents in the rural areas gives clients access to DPADivision of Public Assistance programs and services.
