Administrative Procedures Manual

[Click here to see this page in full context](#)

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2021-01

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: April 1, 2021

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have questions, please contact Division Operations Support Team at [dpadost@alaska.gov](mailto:dpadost@alaska.gov) or the Policy and Program Development Team at [dpapolicy@alaska.gov](mailto:dpapolicy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 100-4 A](../100/100-4_civil_rights.md#100-4_A._CIVIL_RIGHTS_POLICY)      CIVIL RIGHTS POLICY

- Updated the term handicap to disability

[MS 100-4 B](../100/100-4_civil_rights.md#100-4_B._CIVIL_RIGHTS_COMPLAINT_DEFINITION)      CIVIL RIGHTS COMPLAINT DEFINITION

- Updated the term handicap to disability

[MS 100-4 C](../100/100-4_civil_rights.md#100-4_C._RECEIVING_CIVIL_RIGHTS_COMPLAINTS)      RECEIVING CIVIL RIGHTS COMPLAINTS

- Added information that a response must be sent to the client when a Civil Rights complaint is received that includes how the agency reached a decision

[MS 101-4](../101/101-4_receipt_of_an_identifiable_application.md)      RECEIPT OF AN IDENTIFIABLE APPLICATION

- Added reference to the policy manuals for program specific policy regarding acceptable signatures

[MS 103-2](../103/103-2_identifiable_applications.md)      REQUEST FOR SERVICE

- Revised to include updated process
- Removed old subsection D "Storing Request for Service Applications" and subsection E "Retaining and Destroying Request for Service Applications
- Added new subsection C "Applicant Provides the Completed Application." The old subsection C "Applicant Fails to Complete the Application Process" is renumbered to subsection D

[MS 106-5](../106/106-5_instant_eligibility_verification_system_(ievs).md)      INSTANT ELIGIBILITY VERIFICATION SYSTEM (IEVS)

- Updated to include new sources
- Added information on how a caseworker should act on information obtained in IEVSInstant Eligibility Verification System

[MS 107-2 B](../107/107-2_accepting_change_reports.md#107-2_B._PROCEDURE_FOR_PROCESSING_REPORTS_OF_CHANGE)      PROCEDURE FOR PROCESSING REPORTS OF CHANGE

- Updated the process when a report of change is received by the office to align with current workflow process
- Added reference link to Administrative MS 104-4I for additional information when a report of change is received

[MS 107-2 C](../107/107-2_accepting_change_reports.md#107-2_C._STANDARD_ALERTS_AND_TASKS_USED_IN_EIS_AND_ARIES)      FLOWCHART FOR PROCESSING REPORTS OF CHANGE

- Removed reference to Addendum A as it does not match the current workflow process
- Renumbered subsection D "Standard Alerts and Tasks Used in EISEligibility Information System and ARIESAlaska's Resource for Integrated Eligibility Services" to subsection C

[MS 109-10](../109/109-10_case_managers_use_of_cano.md)      CASE MANAGERS USE OF CANO

- Added link to WSPWork Service Procedure MS 1007

[MS 114](../114/114_case_reviews.md)      CASE REVIEWS

- Renamed section from "Quality Assurance" to "Case Reviews" due to the Quality Assurance team being renamed to the Statewide Case Review team
- Revised quality assurance terminology to case reviews

[MS 114-1](../114/114-1_supervisory_reviews_and_management_responsibilities.md)      SUPERVISORY REVIEWS AND MANAGEMENT RESPONSIBILITIES

- Renamed section to include Supervisory Case Reviews
- Added additional reference tools for case reviews
- Removed reference to the GEN 57 form as this is not a case review feedback form

[MS 114-2](../114/114-2_procedures.md)      PROCEDURES

- Revised section with updated guidance on case reviews
- Revised quality assurance terminology to case reviews
- Added links to case review guidelines and guides

[MS 116-2](../116/116-2_application_processing.md)      APPLICATION PROCESSING

- Removed paragraph indicating that DPADivision of Public Assistance accepts interviews conducted by NFAPNative Family Assistance Program agencies as this is no longer allowed

[ADDENDUM 107 A](../addenda/addendum_107_a_-_reserved.md)      RESERVED

- Removed the report of changes workflow chart since it does not match the current process for when changes are received by the office
