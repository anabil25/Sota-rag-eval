Administrative Procedures Manual

STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2025-03

MANUAL: Administrative Procedures Manual

DATE: December 1, 2025

Several sections of the Administrative Procedures Manual have been revised.

If you have questions, please contact Division of Operations Support team a [hss.dpadost@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpadost@alaska.gov) or the Policy & Program Development team at [hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov](mailto:hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov).

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

[MS 103-6](../103/103-6_retention_of_case_records.md)      RETENTION OF CASE RECORDS

- Removed section about Work Services sending files to [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance for file retention

[MS 105-3](../105/105-3_documentary_evidence.md)      DOCUMENTARY EVIDENCE

- Removed note regarding paying out of state birth certificates on behalf of [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance clients

[MS 105-7](../105/105-7_collateral_contact.md)      COLLATERAL CONTACT

- Clarified when Work Services Case Managers obtain income verification from a collateral contact [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance staff must use this source of verification in the eligibility determination, if enough information is obtained for the determination. Otherwise, [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance staff can directly contact the employer for additional information
- Added that Work Services case managers complete and upload [GEN 155](#)Employment Statement form and [WS 155](#)Work Services Employer Collateral Contact Form into Secure Document Portal for income verification obtained by collateral contact

[MS 105-13](../105/105-13_partial_list_of_verification_sources.md)      PARTIAL LIST OF VERIFICATION SOURCES

- Replaced WinStar reference with [ACSESS](#)Alaska Child Support Enforcement Services System system for Child Support

[MS 108-2](../108/108-2_content_of_a_notice.md)      CONTENT OF A NOTICE

- Clarified denial notices sent to households do not need to include a list of household members
- Added income calculation requirement to include conversion factors in all eligibility determination notices

[MS 109-10](../109/109-10_case_managers_use_of_cano.md)      CASE MANAGERS USE OF CANO

- Revised that Work Services Case Managers upload the [GEN 55](#)Report of Change, [GEN 155](#)Employment Statement form, and the new employer income verification [WS 155](#)Work Services Employer Collateral Contact Form into the Secure Document Portal when income information is obtained from collateral contact
- Removed requirement that case managers must send an alert when [CANO](#)Case Notes entry is created

[MS 111-7](../111/111-7_payments_received_in_dpa_offices.md)      PAYMENTS RECEIVED IN DPA OFFICES

- Clarified two [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance staff in the same office are required to sign the [GEN 38](#)Public Assistance Claim Remittance unless staffing does not allow it, in which case it can be signed by a second [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance employee in a different [DPA](#)Division of Public Assistance office

[MS 115-9](../115/115-9_disability_determination_service.md)      DISABILITY DETERMINATION SERVICE

- Revised to align with the current process
